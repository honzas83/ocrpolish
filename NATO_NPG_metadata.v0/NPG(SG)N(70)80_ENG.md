> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, October 19, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to conduct a final review of preparations for the Ottawa meeting, including oral status reports, the transportation of classified documents, and the timing of the Spring Ministerial meeting. Additionally, the meeting will serve as an initial exchange of views on future work. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

Ministerial meeting) and
