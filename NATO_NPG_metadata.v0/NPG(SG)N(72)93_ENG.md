> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice regarding the next meeting of the Staff Group, scheduled for Monday, 23rd October 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to review any outstanding matters concerning the upcoming Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting.

NOTICE NPG(Staff Group)N(72)93
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 23rd October,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to review any
matters concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting which may still be
outstanding.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
