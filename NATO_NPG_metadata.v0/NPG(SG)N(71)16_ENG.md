> # Notes of a Meeting Held on Monday, 1st February, 1971
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on Monday, 1st February, 1971, to establish a work programme for the period leading up to the May Ministerial Meeting. The group agreed that essential studies forming the basis for the Ministerial discussion should be submitted by 1st March, 1971. Documentation expected by this date includes the North Norway Study, the Greek Study, and SACTIANT's Illustrative Studies and Sample Release Requests, with contributions from SACEUR expected in the first week of March. The group also postponed discussion of the North Norway Study and noted papers on national participation in military nuclear planning circulated by the Italian Representative and the International Staff. Additionally, the group received a briefing on Exercise WINTEX 71 from the IMS Nuclear Policy Team, with a second briefing scheduled for the following day. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next meeting on Monday, 8th February, 1971, in Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held on Monday, 1st February, 1971
WORK PROGRAMME
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a Work Programme for the period up to the NPG Ministerial
Meeting in May, handed out by the International Staff;

(b) took note of a statement by the Chairman who explained the Work Programme
and emphasised that if Ministers were to have a meaningful discussion of issues
at the end of May, it was essential that the studies forming the basis of this
discussion should arrive not later than 1st March, 1971; studies arriving after
this date could be used in the continuing process of the Staff Group’s work and
would be discussed at the following Ministerial meeting;

(c) noted that the following basic documentation would have been received by the
target date indicated by the Chairman: North Norway Study; the Greek Study;
SACTIANT’s Illustrative Studies and Sample Release Requests. Those from SACEUR
were expected during the first week of March;

(d) took note of various restrictions to the Work Programme imposed by holiday
periods;

(e) discussed the basic approach to the studies and took note of various views
expressed; agreed to continue discussion at the next meeting;

(f) agreed to discuss a draft Agenda for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in May at
the next meeting of the Staff Group.

NORTH NORWAY STUDY
2. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to postpone discussion of this Study until their next meeting.

NATIONAL PARTICIPATION IN MILITARY NUCLEAR PLANNING
3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) noted that the Italian Representative had circulated a paper on national
participation in Military Nuclear Planning;

(b) noted that the International Staff would also circulate a paper on this
matter in time for the next meeting.

BRIEFING ON EXERCISE WINTEX 71
4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a Briefing on Exercise WINTEX 71 given by the IMS Nuclear
Policy Team; this briefing was followed by questions and discussion;

(b) took note that a further briefing would take place on the next day, Tuesday,
2nd February, 1971.

NEXT MEETING
5. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 8th February, 1971, at 3 p.m.
