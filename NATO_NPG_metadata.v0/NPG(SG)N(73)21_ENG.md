> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 19, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft agenda for the Ankara meeting, the draft report on the Illustrative Study on demonstrative use, and a possible preliminary report on Exercise WINTEX 73. The notice references several working papers and previous documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)2, NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)나4, and NPG/Study(71)4. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th March, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal matters for discussion will be:

(a) the draft agenda(1) for the Ankara meeting;
(b) the draft report(2) on the Illustrative Study on demonstrative use(3) and
suggested changes(4);

(c) a possible preliminary report on Exercise WINTEX 73.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
