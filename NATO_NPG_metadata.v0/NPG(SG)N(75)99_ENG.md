> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the date and time of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled for Monday, November 24, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss advance preparations for the organizational aspects of Phase III and to review procedures in the CNI field. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

20th November, 1975

NOTICE

NPG(Staff Group)N(75)99

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 24th November,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting are to discuss:

(a) advance preparations of the organisational aspects of Phase III;

(b) procedures in the CNI field.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
