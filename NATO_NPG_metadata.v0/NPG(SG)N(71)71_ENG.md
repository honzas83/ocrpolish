> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, July 1, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the dates for the next Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting and the arrangements for handling illustrative studies at that Ministerial meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It also references a previous document, NPG(Staff Group)N(71)69, dated June 25, 1971.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 1st July, 1971,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be to continue the
discussion of:

(a) the dates of the next NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(b) arrangements for handling illustrative studies at the next meeting of
Ministers(1).

(Signed) M.H.G WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)69, dated 25th June, 1971
