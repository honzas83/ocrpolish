> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on 15th September, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened on September 15, 1976, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to address several key agenda items. The group noted an address by Dr. R. Schreffler regarding a new nuclear force. They discussed the approval of Terms of Reference for the NIKE HERCULES Study and the circulation of related documentation. The meeting included a review of acronyms used in SHAPE Nuclear Planning, such as the General Defence Plan and Nuclear Operations Plan. There was a discussion on the classification of nuclear consultation messages, where the United States, Germany, Italy, and the Netherlands expressed a view that the report should not be referred to Ministers, a position not fully shared by Turkey. The group also made preparations for the autumn ministerial meeting, discussing contributions on TNF improvements from Germany, the United Kingdom, and Norway, and the own position of the United States regarding cruise missiles. The meeting concluded by scheduling future study meetings for Phase III, the Technology Study Panel, and the Technology Study Panel, and setting the next Staff Group meeting for September 20, 1976.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on 15th
September, 1976

ADDRESS BY DR. R. SCHREFFLER.
1. The STAFF GROUP: noted with appreciation the address by Dr. R. Schreffler,
the first chairman of the Staff Group, on his work on a new nuclear force.

DOCUMENTATION
2. The STAFF GROUP: noted a statement by the Chairman that the Terms of
Reference for the NIKE HERCULES Study had been approved by NPG Permanent
Representatives under the silence procedure and that they would be circulated
shortly as Annex A to NPG/D(76)8.

ACRONYMS
3. The STAFF GROUP: took note of a statement by the SHAPE representative on the
acronyms currently in use in SIAPE Nuclear Planning: GDP – SACEUR's General
Defence Plan; NOP – Nuclear Operations Plan; SSP – SACEUR's Scheduled Strike
Programme;

CLASSIFICATION OF NUCLEAR CONSULTATION MESSAGES
5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) had a preliminary exchange of views on NPC(Staff Group)WP(76)7;
(b) noted that the United States supported by Germany, Italy and the Netherlands
considered that the report should not be referred to Ministers, a view which was
not fully shared by the Turkish representative;

(c) noted that the International Staff would issue a Corrigendum containing the
amendments proposed by the United States and SIAPE and agreed to return to this
subject at its next meeting.

6. The STAFF GROUP:

Agenda(1) noted a statement by the Canadian representative that the written
contributions on TNF improvements provided by Germany, the United Kingdom and
Norway(2) could serve as a starting point for Ministerial discussion;
