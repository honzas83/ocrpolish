> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, December 2, 1968, at 2:45 p.m. The primary subject for discussion during this meeting will be ADM Time Factors. The notice was issued in Brussels, Belgium, and signed by M.H.G. Wilson. It serves as an official notification for the staff group members of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO).
