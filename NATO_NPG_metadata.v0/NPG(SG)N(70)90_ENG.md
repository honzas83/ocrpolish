> # Farewell Luncheon for Mr. Gordon Smith
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a farewell luncheon in honour of Mr. Gordon Smith, hosted by the Nuclear Planning Directorate. The event is scheduled for 26th November 1970, at 12 o'clock, at the restaurant "L'Ecu de France" located at 24 rue de la Violette, Brussels. To facilitate transportation and avoid parking difficulties in the city center, NATO bus transportation has been arranged to leave NATO Headquarters at 11.45 and return from the restaurant at 14.15, with sufficient capacity for all participants.

24th November, 1970

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(70)90

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
FAREWELL LUNCHEON FOR MR. GORDON SMITH
Note by the Secretary

The farewell lunch hosted by the Nuclear Planning Directorate in honour of Mr.
Gordon Smith will take place at the restaurant "L'Ecu de France", 24 rue de la
Violette (close to the "Grand Place") on 26th November, 1970 at 12 o'clock.

2. To avoid difficulties in finding parking spaces down-town, NATO bus
transportation has been arranged, leaving NATO Headquarters, main entrance, at
11.45 and returning from the restaurant at 14.15. The capacity of the bus will
allow all participants to travel in it.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels
