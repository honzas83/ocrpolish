> # Documentation for Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 30th July 1976. It informs the group that a Military Committee Paper concerning the Work Programme for the NIKE HERCULES study is being circulated under the reference NPG/D(76)9. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It also references a document with the code CIICM-78-76 dated 29th July 1976.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Military-Operations-and-Capabilities/Air-and-Missile-Defense — The document refers to the 'NIKE HERCULES Study', and Nike Hercules was a surface-to-air missile system designed for air and missile defense.
