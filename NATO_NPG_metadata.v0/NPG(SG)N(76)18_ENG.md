> # Notes of Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 23rd February, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group held a meeting on February 23, 1976, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting's agenda included a briefing by Mr. B.L. Judd from the IMS Command, Control and Information Systems Division on Command and Control (C3), specifically focusing on automatic data processing and the potential for computerizing the NATO system to accelerate decision-making. The group also reviewed several documents, including a revised paper on Nuclear Consultation - CNI Procedures and a draft report to the NDC. The Staff Group agreed to modifications for the Nuclear Consultation paper and requested the International Staff to prepare a further revision. Additionally, the group expressed a hope for early information regarding the dates of the NPG Spring Ministerial meeting and scheduled their next meeting for March 8, 1976.
