> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in Rome
> 
> This document, dated 18th October 1974, is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group. It outlines the composition of the International Staff delegation accompanying the Secretary General to the 16th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Ministers to be held in Rome in November. The delegation includes a variety of roles, such as the Deputy Director of Private Office, Assistant Secretary General, Director of Nuclear Planning, and various secretaries, interpreters, and security personnel. Specifically, the list identifies individuals such as Mr. M.C. Lissfelt, Miss E. Borgman Brouwer, Mr. D.C. Humphreys, Mr. R.E. Shearer, and Mr. M.H.G. Wilson. The document also notes that some members of the delegation are accompanied by their wives.
