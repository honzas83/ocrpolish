> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, September 23, 1970, at 10:15 a.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to discuss two key documents: a report by the NATO Military authorities on the 'Provisional Political Guidelines for the Initial Defensive Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons by NATO' and a paper submitted by the Danish Delegation. The notice is signed by J. de Gaye and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document explicitly mentions a meeting to discuss the 'Provisional Political Guidelines for the Initial Defensive Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons by NATO', which directly relates to the deployment and consultation on the use of tactical nuclear weapons in Europe.
