> # The Role of Nuclear Strike Forces
> 
> On April 3, 1969, the Staff Group of the Nuclear Planning Group met at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to discuss the preparation of a paper regarding the role of theatre nuclear strike forces. The group discussed a draft prepared by the Canadian and United Kingdom delegations, alongside an International Staff paper, to determine the form and content of the final document for the meeting of MFG Ministers scheduled for May 29 and 30, 1969. The meeting noted that time was limited and the agenda for the Ministers' meeting was already extensive. During the discussions, the group took note of remarks from the United States Representative concerning the future strategic strike capacity of United States external forces. The United Kingdom Representative was invited to redraft the first part of the Canadian/United Kingdom paper based on these discussions. The meeting concluded by agreeing to continue the discussion at subsequent meetings scheduled for April 8, 10, and a future date to be determined for the Nuclear Planning Group Representatives.

THE ROLE OF NUCLEAR STRIKE FORCES

Notes of a meeting held at NATO Headquarters, Brussels, 20th of Thursday, 3rd
April, 1969

1. The Staff Group:
(a) discussed the sort of paper on the rôle of theatre nuclear strike forces
which should be prepared for the meeting of MFG Ministers to be held on 29th and
30th May, 1969;

(b) noted that time was short and that the agenda for the meeting of the
Ministers was already long;

(c) agreed to discuss the draft prepared by the Canadian and the United Kingdom
Delegations, in conjunction with the International Staff paper(1), with a view
to coming to an early decision as to the form and content of the paper for the
Ministers;

(d) took note of points raised in discussion, and in particular of the remarks
of the United States Representative concerning the future strategic strike
capacity of United States external forces;

(e) invited the United Kingdom Representative to redraft the first part of the
Canadian/ United Kingdom paper in the light of the discussion;

(f) agreed to continue the discussion at the next meeting.

(1) MFG(Staff Group)WP(69)4, dated 5th March, 1969.
