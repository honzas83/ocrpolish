> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 110 Brussels on Monday, 10th September, 1973
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on September 10, 1973. The meeting's primary focus was to review documentation for the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives on October 4, 1975, including draft ministerial agendas and consultation procedures. The group welcomed the new Turkish Representative, Mr. Sungar. They discussed a draft advance press release for a Ministerial Meeting scheduled for November in the Hague, agreeing that the Chairman should re-issue the text with a cover note for member country approval. Additionally, the Staff Group reviewed a list of future tasks to be used for preparing a Program of Work (PO) prior to the Ministerial Meeting. The group also provisionally agreed to a general review of the Nuclear Planning Group's mission and activities on September 28, 1973. Finally, the meeting concluded by scheduling the next meetings for September 17 and 19, 1973.

MEMBERSHIP
1. The STAFF GROUP: welcomed the new Turkish Representative, Mr. Sungar.
DOCUMENTATION
2. The STAFF GROUP: took note of a statement by the Chairman on the status of
the following documents for the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives on 4th
October, 1975:

NPG/WP(73)7, 7th September, 1973 – Draft Ministerial Agenda – distributed on
10th September;

NPG/WP(73)8, 10th September, 1973 – ADM Guidelines – awaiting confirmation by
the United States;

NPG/WP(73)9, 12th September and NPG/WP(73)10, 13th September – Consultation
Procedures and Facilities – to be circulated later in the week.

ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
3. The STAFF GROUP: (a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced
the text of a draft advance press release (1) for the Ministerial Meeting to be
held in the Hague in November;

(b) agreed that the Chairman should re-issue the Press release with a cover note

FUTURE WORK PROGRAMME

4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced a list of future
tasks(1) on the basis of which, and in the light of Staff Group comments, he
would prepare a PO for circulation prior to the Ministerial Meeting;

(b) took note of the points made in discussion which concentrated on:
- approach to be used in bringing the subject before the Permanent
Representatives and the Ministers;

- additions to and deletions from the list of tasks;
- the timing aspects of the future work projects;
(c) noted the Chairman's intention to hold a further discussion of the future
work programme before he finalised the draft PO.

REVIEW OF NPG ACTIVITIES

5. The STAFF GROUP: noted with appreciation the Chairman's proposal to give a
general review of the NPG's mission and activities and provisionally agreed to
schedule the briefing for Friday, 28th September, 1973.

NEXT MEETING

6. The STAFF GROUP: agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 17th September,
1973 at 3 p.m. and to meet again on Wednesday, 19th September at 3 p.m., if
necessary.
