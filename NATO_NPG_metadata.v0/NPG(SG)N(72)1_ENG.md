> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the body. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, January 10, 1972, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The principal object of the meeting will be to discuss Staff Group tasks for 1972, with a particular focus on the work programme leading up to the Nuclear Planning Group Spring Ministerial Meeting.
