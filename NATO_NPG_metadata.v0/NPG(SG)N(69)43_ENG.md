> [!abstract] 
> # Advance Press Release for London NPG Meeting
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Secretary and a revised draft press release regarding the fifth meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) of NATO. The meeting is scheduled to take place in London on May 29th and 30th, 1969, under the Chairmanship of Secretary General Manlio Brosio. The purpose of the meeting is to continue discussions on nuclear planning, specifically focusing on the tactical use of nuclear weapons in the defense of NATO. The Secretary's note specifies that the press release should be released by the NATO Director of Information at 3:00 p.m. Brussels time on Monday, May 26th, 1969, provided no objections are received by Thursday, May 22nd. The NPG currently consists of the Defence Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR LONDON NG MEETING
Note by the Secretary
Attached is the advance press release for the NPG meeting to be held in London
on 29th and 30th May, 1969. It will be considered approved if no objection has
been received to it by the close of business on Thursday, 22nd May. It is
suggested that it should be released by the NATO Director of Information at 3.0
p.m. Brussels time, on Monday, 26th May, with optional simultaneous release in
NFG Capitals.

(Signed) M.H.G. ILSON
CTAN/NATO,
Brussels, 39.

REVISED DRAFT
ADVANCE FEES RELEASE FOR LONDON NG MEETING
As foreshadowed in the Communiqué of the last Ministerial meeting in Bonn the
Nuclear Planning Group of NATO which at present consists of the Defence
Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom and
the United States will hold its fifth meeting in London on 29th and 30th May
under the Chairmanship of Mr. Manlio Brosio, Secretary General of NATO. At this
meeting the Ministers will continue their discussions of nuclear planning with
particular regard to the tactical use of nuclear weapons in the defence of NATO.
