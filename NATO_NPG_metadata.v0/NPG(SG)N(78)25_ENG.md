> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, April 10, 1978, at 3 p.m. The meeting's agenda includes a discussion of the revised draft Permanent Representatives' report on Nike Hercules Adjustments, as well as final preparations for the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions a discussion on 'NIK E HERCULES Adjustments', referring to the Nike Hercules surface-to-air missile system, which was a nuclear-capable system deployed on allied territory as part of NATO's extended deterrence posture.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 10th April, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the revised draft Permanent Representatives' report on NIK E
HERCULES Adjustments(1);

(b) final preparations for the Spring, 1978 NPG Ministerial Meeting;

(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 110 Brussels.
