> # Draft Advance Press Release Concerning the Meeting of Ministers of the Nuclear Planning Group
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Secretary and a draft advance press release concerning the sixth meeting of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) Nuclear Planning Group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on 11th and 12th November 1969 in Washington, D.C., under the chairmanship of Secretary General Manlio Brosio. The note by the Secretary, signed by N.H.G. Wilson, suggests that the press release be issued on Friday, 7th November 1969, at 3 p.m. Brussels local time, with optional simultaneous release in NP3 capitals. The draft press release specifies that the Nuclear Planning Group currently consists of the Defence Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, who will continue their discussions on nuclear planning.

CONFIDENTIAL (Until Released)
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP
Draft Advance Press Release

As decided at its last Ministerial meeting, the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO,
which at present consists of the Defence Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, Germany,
Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will hold its sixth
meeting on 11th and 12th November in Washington, D.C., under the chairmanship of
Mr. Manlio Brosio, Secretary General of NATO. At the meeting, the Ministers will
continue their discussions of nuclear planning.
