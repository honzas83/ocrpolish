> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary regarding the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled for Monday, August 6, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss a proposed addition to the draft report on operational concepts and doctrine, to hold an initial exchange of views on the NATO Military Authorities reports on Early Combined Use of conventional and nuclear capabilities, and to discuss the latest version of the draft report on Consultation Procedures and Facilities. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place
on Monday, 6th August, 1973, at 3 p.m. The principal objects
of the meeting will be:

(a) to discuss a proposed addition to the draft report
on operational concepts and doctrine(1)

(b) to hold an initial exchange of views on the NATO
Military Authorities reports on Early Combined
Use of conventional and nuclear capabilities(2)

(c) to discuss the latest version of the draft report
on Consultation Procedures and Facilities(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)68, 1st August, 1973
(2) NPG/D(73)8, 30th July, 1973
