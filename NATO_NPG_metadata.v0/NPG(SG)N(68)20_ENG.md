> # Terms of Reference for SHAPE Presentation on the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) and an annexed letter from the Secretary General of NATO to the Chairman of the Military Committee. The primary purpose is to provide the terms of reference for a presentation to be given by the Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR) and SHAPE regarding the role of theatre nuclear strike forces. The terms of reference detail the required content, including introductory remarks, the relationship between Allied Command Europe (ACE) and external forces, the ACE threat list, and the complementary nature of General Strike Plan (GSP) and Special Intelligence Command (SIC). The It also covers the role of ACE nuclear strike forces, specifically focusing on nuclear strike (qRA) requirements, on-call programmes, and their relationship to conventional requirements. Additionally, the technical issues regarding ACE strike force planning are to be addressed, including survivability of forces, characteristics of ACE weapons systems, and target coverage. The document specifies that the presentation should be delivered by the end of November 1968, following a decision taken by the Permanent Representatives of the NPG on 24th October 1968.
