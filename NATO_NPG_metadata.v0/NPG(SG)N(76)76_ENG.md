> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, September 6, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the Compilation paper, determine the composition of the Agenda for the Autumn meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers, and continue preparations for the Autumn meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)76
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 6th September,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of the Compilation paper;
(b) to discuss the composition of the Agenda for the Autumn meeting of the NPG
Ministers;

(c) to continue preparations for the Autumn meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
