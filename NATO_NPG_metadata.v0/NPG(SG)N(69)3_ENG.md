> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, dated 13th January 1969, regarding the rescheduling of an upcoming meeting. At the request of some members of the Staff Group, the meeting originally scheduled for 21st January has been moved to Monday, 20th January 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium, under the reference NPG(Staff Group)N(69)3. It references a previous notice, NPG(Staff Group)N(69)1, dated 8th January 1969, and another document, DN(74)18.
