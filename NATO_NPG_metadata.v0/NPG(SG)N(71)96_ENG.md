> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, August 30, 1971, at 3 p.m. The agenda includes several key items: an illustrative study on maritime use in the Mediterranean, unresolved problems regarding tactical nuclear defence, the implementation of special ADM Guidelines, and requests for Military Authorities to comment on the CENTAG Area Study and the Turkish Study. Additionally, the meeting will cover the draft agenda for the Ministerial meeting in October and the status of work in progress on studies of follow-on use. Due to the volume of matters for discussion, the Chairman has indicated that a second meeting in the same week may be necessary. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson of NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Monday, 30th August, 1971,
at 3 p.m. The principal matters for attention will be:

(a) Illustrative study – Maritime use in the Mediterranean(1);
(b) Unresolved problems related to tactical nuclear defence(2);
(c) Implementation of special ADM Guidelines(3);
(d) Invitation to the Military Authorities to comment upon the CENTAG AREA
Study(4) and the Turkish Study(5);

(e) Draft Agenda for the Ministerial meeting in October(6);
(f) Status of work in progress on studies of follow-on use(7).
2. In view of the number of matters for discussion, the Chairman has asked me to
say that a second meeting of the Staff Group will probably be necessary in the
same week.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
