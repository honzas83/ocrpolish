> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, dated August 3, 1977, announcing the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, August 8, 1977, at 5 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to receive a briefing by SHAPE on recent refinements and adjustments to the Multinational Corps' (MNCs) Nuclear Weapons Release Procedures, to conduct a preliminary discussion on the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study, and to consider any other business. The document references the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study (NPG/STUDY/50) and the archive code C-M(2008)0116 (INV).

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

3rd August, 1977

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(77)57

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th August, 1977
at 5 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hear a briefing by SHAPE on recent refinements and adjustments to the
MNCs' Nuclear Weapons Release Procedures;

(b) to have a preliminary discussion of the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study(1);
(c) to consider any other business.

(Signed) N.L.C. PAR'

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG/STUDY/50, dated 1st August, 1977
