> # Draft Ministerial Agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group
> 
> This document consists of a cover note from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group and a draft agenda for an upcoming Ministerial session. The meeting, scheduled for December 11, 1974, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels, aims to discuss several key items. The first item on the agenda is the nuclear aspects of the Nunn Amendment, led by the United States, specifically referring to Section 302(d) of the United States Military Procurement Authorisation Act. Nunn Amendment is a detailed discussion on nuclear weapons policy. The second item concerns the status of selective employment planning, featuring statements from the Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR) and the Supreme Allied Commander Atlantic (SACLANT), based on a SHAPE Background Paper. headlights. The third item is the future work programme, including the timing and place of the next meeting, based on a note by the Chairman. The agenda also includes provisions for any other business and a final communique.
