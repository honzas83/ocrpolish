> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Thursday, September 19, 1974, at 2:15 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 Nuclear Planning Committee (NPC) Ministerial Meeting, to finalize the CNI Study and the draft Permanent Representatives' report on it, and to finalize the draft report on the Air Defence Illustrative Study. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
17th September, 1974
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(74)93
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 19th September,
1974, at 2.15 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPC Ministerial Meeting;
(b) to finalize the CNI Study(1)(2), and the draft Permanent Representatives'
report(3) on it;

(c) to finalize the draft report on the Air Defence Illustrative Study(4).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
