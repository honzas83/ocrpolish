> # SCL'S RFL Meeting Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, dated 7th July 1975, announcing the next meeting of the SCL'S RFL Meeting Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, 5th July 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss follow-up actions resulting from the Ministerial Meeting in Monterey. The document is classified as NATO RESTRICTED.

SCL'S RFL MEETING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the St. IF Group will take place on Wednesday, 5th July 1975
at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to discuss follow-up
action to the Ministerial Meeting in Monterey.
