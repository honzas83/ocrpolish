> # List of International Staff Attending the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Ankara
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on May 3, 1973, provides a list of international staff members from the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) who will be attending the thirteenth meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers in Ankara, Turkey. The list includes personnel from the Secretary General's Private Office, the Executive Secretariat, the NATO Office of Security, the Division of Political Affairs, the Division of Defence Planning and Policy, and the Conference Service. Specifically, it lists individuals such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luns, Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning and Policy D.C. Humphreys, and Director of Nuclear Planning H.B. Seim. The document serves as an official notice for information purposes, signed by M.H.G. Wilson, the Secretary of the NPG Staff Group.
