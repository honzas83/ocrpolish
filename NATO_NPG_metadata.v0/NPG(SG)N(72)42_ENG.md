> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, May 8, 1972, at 5:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to consider the results of the meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives held on May 4, 1972, and to examine the progress of preparations for the Nuclear Planning Committee Ministerial Meeting in Copenhagen. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
