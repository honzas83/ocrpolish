> # Note by the Secretary Regarding the Postponement of the ADM Time Factors Presentation
> 
> This document is a formal note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated September 16, 1968. It informs members that the presentation by the Military Authorities on the subject of ADM Time Factors has been postponed. Consequently, the meeting of the Staff Group originally planned for Wednesday, September 18, is rescheduled to Monday, September 23, 1968, at 2:45 p.m. The note is signed by M.H.G. Willson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
