> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 20th January, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on January 20, 1975, to discuss the Phase II Study on the follow-on tactical use of nuclear weapons. The group endorsed the welcome of the Phase II Study Team and experts from capitals and military headquarters, and noted the handling of ATOM.L information. The Chairman outlined a schedule for the preliminary report, including deadlines for written comments by February 7, presentation to NPG Permanent Representatives on February 20, and final approval by ministers during the Spring 1975 meeting. The Staff Group reviewed remarks from Colonel J.G. Callaway of the United States and discussed key points such as the restoration of deterrence, political control, and the timing of nuclear weapon use relative to conventional forces. Additionally, the group noted concerns raised by Dr. I.J. Shaw of the United Kingdom and the Director of Nuclear Planning regarding potential flaws in the report's conclusions, agreeing that these should be further studied. The meeting concluded with the reminder that the Study Team would brief the NPG Permanent Representatives on February 20, 1975.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 20th
January, 1975

OPENING STATEMENT
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) warnedly endorsed the welcome extended by the Chairman to the members of the
Phase II Study Team and to the experts from Capitals and Military Headquarters;

(b) noted that all those present were cleared for the security classification of
the matters to be discussed;

(c) took note of a statement by the Chairman concerning the handling of ATOM.L
information which might be discussed on the second and third days of the
meeting;

(d) noted the Chairman's request that he be given the texts of any written
briefings or position papers which might be read during the meeting.

PHASE II STUDY
Opening Statement by the Chairman of the NPG Staff Group
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who recalled that it was intended
to submit a preliminary report on Phase II of the work programme on follow-on
tactical use of nuclear weapons to NPG Ministers at the Spring 1975 meeting;

Introduction of the Phase II Study Preliminary Report, and questions to the
Study Team

3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the remarks of the Chairman of the Study Group, Colonel J.G.
Callaway (US), who invited questions;

(b) took note of the questions asked, the answers received and the related
discussions;

(c) observed that there was particular interest in the following points:
(i) the possibility of restoring or enhancing deterrence by follow-on use;
(ii) the maintenance of political control;
(iii) limits and constraints to follow-on use;
