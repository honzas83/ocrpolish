> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1971 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of NATO, H.B. Seim, regarding a proposed advance press release for the upcoming Autumn 1971 Ministerial meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place in Brussels, Belgium, on October 26th and 27th, 1971, under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns. The proposed press release, provided in both English and French, is modeled after previous announcements for meetings in Venice, Ottawa, and Mittenwald. The release is planned for 3 p.m. local time on Friday, October 22nd, 1971, in Brussels and other capitals of participating countries. Delegations are requested to approve the text and release arrangements via the silence procedure by Friday, October 15th, 1971. The Nuclear Planning Group currently consists of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Greece, Italy, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE AUTUMN 1971 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPC Ministerial meeting in Brussels on 26th and 27th
October, 1971, as agreed at a recent Staff Group meeting. The text closely
follows the language used in press announcements that were made just prior to
the Venice, Ottawa and Mittenwald meetings. It is planned that the release be
made in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday, 22nd October, 1971, with
simultaneous additional release in the capitals of those countries who desire to
so.

2. Delegations are requested to submit the proposed text and release
arrangements to capitals for approval by the silence procedure. If no comments
or objections are received by Friday, 15th October, 1971, the text and release
timing will be considered approved.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

As arranged at its previous meeting in Mittenwald, Germany in May 1971, the
Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Belgium, Denmark,
Germany, Greece, Italy, the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the
invitation of the Belgian Government, hold its tenth half-yearly Ministerial
meeting on 26th and 27th October, 1971, in Brussels, Belgium under the
chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

Comme convenu lors de sa précédente réunion à Mittenwald (Allemagne) au mois de
mai 1971, le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement la
Belgique, le Danemark, l'Allemagne, la Grèce, l'Italie, le Royaume-Uni et les
États-Unis tiendra, à l'invitation du Gouvernement belge, sa 10ème réunion
semestrielle au niveau ministériel les 26 et 27 octobre 1971 à Bruxelles
(Belgique) sous la présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secrétaire Général de
l'OTAN.
