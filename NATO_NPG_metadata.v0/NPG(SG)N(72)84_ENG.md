> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, on Thursday, 28th September, 1972
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters on Thursday, 28th September 1972. The meeting began with the welcome of Col. Minet as a Representative of Belgium. The group took note of several working papers, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)18(Revised) regarding the SACILANT Illustrative Study and the NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)19 regarding the Follow-On Use Work Programme and a letter from the United States on the Air Battle Study. Additionally, the group noted that Turkey could agree to the text of a press release (NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)17(Revised)) to be released simultaneously with a Brussels release on 20th October 1972. The meeting concluded by agreeing to hold the next session on Monday, 2nd October 1972, to further examine working papers and discuss the future of the Illustrative Study Programme and the possibility of an NDAC Ministerial Meeting in December 1972.
