> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, April 17, 1972, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to consider the results of the meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives held on April 13, and to further examine the form and content of presentations to be made to Ministers at Copenhagen. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th April, 1972,
at 3.00 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be to consider the
results of the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives held on 13th April, and
to examine further the form and content of the presentations to be made to
Ministers at Copenhagen.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
