> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling a meeting for Monday, May 31, 1976, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to confirm the Turkish amendment to the draft report on CNI Procedures, continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting, and hold a general discussion on the presentational aspects of Newly Developed Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and references several previous documents, including NPG(Staff Group)N(76)48, NPG/WP(76)1, and NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)2.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 31st May, 1976,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to confirm the Turkish amendment(1) to the draft report on CNI
Procedures(2),

(b) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting,
(c) to hold a general discussion of the presentational aspects of Newly
Developed Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation(3), as revised in
Staff Group discussions.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(76)48, 25th May, 1976
(2) NPG/WP(76)1, 28th April, 1976
(3) NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)2, 30th March, 1976
