> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, July 16, 1979, at 5 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with a specific focus on Section IV concerning the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice also allows for the discussion of any other business. The document lists several reference codes for the draft guidelines and selective use of nuclear weapons, including USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126 and DPP/NPD/79/39. The meeting is based in Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 16th July, 1979
at 5 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) any other business.
