> [!abstract] 
> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1973 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of NATO, accompanying a draft advance press release in English and French. The press release is intended for the upcoming fourteenth half-yearly Ministerial meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group, scheduled for 6th and 7th November 1973 in The Hague, under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph Luns. The Chairman suggests that the announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, 2nd November 1973, at 3 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is expected to discuss the draft text and timing of the release at its meeting on 10th September 1973 to develop a proposal for the delegations concerned to forward to their respective capitals for consideration.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE AUTUMN 1973 MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in The Hague on 6th and 7th
November, 1973. The text closely follows the language used in press
announcements issued during the past three years just prior to Ministerial
meetings. As on past occasions, the release might be made a few days before the
meeting takes place; accordingly, it is suggested that an announcement might be
made in Brussels on Friday, 2nd November, 1973 at 3 p.m. local time, with
simultaneous additional release in the capitals of those participating countries
who so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 10th September with a view to developing a
proposal that delegations concerned may forward to capitals for consideration.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Canada, the Federal
Republic of Germany, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom
and the United States, will, at the invitation of the Government of the
Netherlands, hold its fourteenth half-yearly Ministerial meeting in The Hague on
6th and 7th November, 1973, under the chairmanship of Mr. Joseph Luns, Secretary
General of NATO.

Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement le Canada,
les Etats-Unis, la Grèce, l'Italie, la Norvège, les Pays-Bas, la République
Fédérale d'Allemagne, le Royaume-Uni tiendra à l'invitation du Gouvernement des
Pays-Bas, sa 14ème réunion semestrielle au niveau ministériel les 6 et 7
novembre à La Haye sous la présidence de M. Joseph Luns, Secrétaire Général de
l'OTAN.
