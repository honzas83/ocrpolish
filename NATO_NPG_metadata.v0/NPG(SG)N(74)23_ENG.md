> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, March 4, 1974, at 3:0 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to examine the Military Committee's report on its function in the Nuclear Consultation Process, using the International Staff's draft paper as a basis for discussion, and to continue preparations for the upcoming Spring Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references two other documents: NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)3 and NPG/D(74)1.
>

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)23
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 4th March, 1974,
at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) using the International Staff's draft paper(1) as a basis for discussion, to
examine the Military Committee's report(2) on its function in the Nuclear
Consultation Process;

(b) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)3, 25th February, 1974
(2) NPG/D(74)1, 12th February, 1974
