> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 5, 1979, at 5 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, along with any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts of NATO in Brussels. It references several previous documents, including USNATO/DPP/OUT/NS/78-126, NPG(Staff Group)/P(78)6, NPG(Staff Group)/N(78)76, NPG(Staff Group)/N(78)81, NPG/Study/52, and a letter from the Canadian Representative's Letter of 21st November, 1976, as well as NPG(Staff Group)/N(76)03.
