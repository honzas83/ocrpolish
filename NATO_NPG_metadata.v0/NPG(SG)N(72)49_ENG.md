> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 1st June 1972. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th June 1972, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) meeting is to continue discussions regarding the work programme in preparation for the Autumn meeting of NPG Ministers. The notice also references a previous document, NPG(Staff Group)N(72)48.

1st June, 1972

NOTICE

NPG(Staff Group)N(72)49

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th June, 1972,
at 3.00 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue discussion
of the work programme in preparation for the Autumn meeting of NPG Ministers(1).

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(72)48, 1st June, 1972.
