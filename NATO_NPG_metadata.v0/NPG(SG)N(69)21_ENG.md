> # Notes of a Meeting Held at NATO Headquarters on Monday, 24th February, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters on February 24, 1969, to discuss several key items. The group discussed a draft report on NATO's response to the Warsaw Pact's initial tactical use of nuclear weapons, proposing changes to the text and agreeing to continue the discussion at the next meeting. They also noted that the Atomic Demolition Time Factors document required amendments and a revised version would be circulated. Additionally, the group reviewed the revised draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial on May 29-30, and noted that Belgium's representative was not yet able to confirm if Belgium could act as the discussion leader for Item III (Consultation). The group identified a need for discussion leaders for items regarding NATO's response to tactical nuclear weapons and the strategic use of nuclear weapons. They agreed that March 18, 1969, would be as a suitable date for the next NPG meeting at the Ambassadorial level, with an agenda including the revised ADM Time Factors paper and the Draft Agenda for the NPG Ministerial Meeting. Finally, the group scheduled its next meeting for March 3, 1969, to continue discussions on the Warsaw Pact's tactical nuclear use.
