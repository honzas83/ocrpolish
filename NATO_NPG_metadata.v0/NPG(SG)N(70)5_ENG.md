> # Note by the Secretary Regarding the Staff Group Meeting
> 
> This document is a formal note from the Secretary of the Staff Group, dated February 5, 1970, announcing a meeting scheduled for Friday, February 13, 1970, at 10 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss whether a programme should be undertaken to achieve a better understanding of the issues outlined in the cover letter to the report by the Staff Group on further studies of follow-on tactical use. The note references a previous report, IPG/P(70)1, dated January 15, 1970. The document originates from Brussels, Belgium, and is addressed to the US CLE RE (Staff Group). launchers
