> # Draft Agenda for the Ministerial Meeting of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee
> 
> This document consists of a note from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) and a draft agenda for the Ministerial meeting of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee on December 3, 1969. The Chairman, H. B. Seim, proposes to discuss the agenda in a meeting on Monday, November 17, 1969. The draft agenda includes items for consultation on the use of nuclear weapons, provisional political guidelines for the possible initial tactical use of nuclear weapons by NATO, a report by the Nuclear Planning Group, and the membership of the Nuclear Planning Group. The agenda also references several documents to be circulated, specifically NDAC/D(69)1, NDAC/D(69)2, and NDAC/D(69)3.
>
