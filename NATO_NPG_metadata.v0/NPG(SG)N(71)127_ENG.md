> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, November 22, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to determine if any comments or objections have been raised regarding the Working Papers on the Draft Report by the Nuclear Planning Group to the Nuclear Defence Advisory Committee (NDAC) and the paper on Nuclear Aspects of the AD 70s Follow-up action. Additionally, the meeting will continue the review of follow-on action resulting from the Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Ministers held at the Palais d'Égmont on October 26 and 27, 1971.

NPG(Staff Group)N(71)127

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 22nd November,
1971, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to note whether any comments or objections have been raised to the Working
Papers on the Draft Report by the NPG to the NDAC(1) and on the paper on Nuclear
Aspects of the AD 70s Follow-up action(2);

(b) to continue the review of follow-on action(3) resulting from the Meeting of
NPG Ministers held at the Palais d'Égmont on 26th and 27th October, 1971

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

[horizontal line]

(1) NPG/WP(71)13, dated 10th November, 1971
(2) NPG/WP(71)14, dated 11th November, 1971
