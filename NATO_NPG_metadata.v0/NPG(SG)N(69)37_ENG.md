> # Next Meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 17th April 1969, outlining the schedule for upcoming meetings. The meetings are scheduled for Monday 14th April 1969, Tuesday 15th April 1969, and Wednesday 16th April 1969, to continue discussions on the initial use of nuclear weapons by the Warsaw Pact and to discuss the study of the role of theatre nuclear strike forces. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NATO RESTRICTED NOTICE NFG(Staff Group)N(69)37
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETINGS
Note by the Secretary
is agreed by the Staff Group at its meeting on 11th April, the next meetings of
the Staff Group will take place as follows:

(a) Monday 14th April, 1969 at 2.45 p.m., with the object of continuing the
discussion on Initial Use by Warsaw Pact;

(b) Tuesday 15th April, 1969 at 2.45 p.m., with the object of discussing the
study of the role of theatre nuclear strike forces;

(c) Wednesday 16th April, 1969 at 10 a.m. and at 2.45 p.m., with the object of
continuing work as necessary.

(Signed) M.H.G. Wilson
OTAN/NATO, Brussels, 39.
