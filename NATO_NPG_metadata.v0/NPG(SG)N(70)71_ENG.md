> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Fall NPG Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, H.B. Seim, regarding a draft advance press release for the upcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Ottawa, Canada, on October 29th and 30th, 1970. The Chairman suggests that the press release be issued in Brussels at 3 p.m. on Monday, October 26th, 1970, with simultaneous release in national capitals at the discretion of each country. The document notes that an initial discussion of the draft will take place at the NPG Staff Group meeting on September 28th, 1970, to develop an agreed text for national approval via the silence procedure. The draft press release itself specifies that the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, consisting of Canada, Germany, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will hold its eighth half-yearly meeting under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Manlio Brosio.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE FALL NPG MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text of a draft advance press release concerning the forthcoming
NPG Ministerial meeting in Ottawa on 29th and 30th October. The language closely
follows the pattern of the press release made prior to the Venice meeting. It is
suggested that the release might take place in Brussels at 3 p.m. on Monday,
26th October, 1970 with simultaneous release in capitals at the discretion of
the country concerned.

2. As indicated in the Staff Group work programme(1), it is expected that an
initial discussion of this draft will take place at the NPG Staff Group meeting
on Monday afternoon, 28th September for the purpose of developing an agreed text
that may then be sent to capitals for national approval by the silence
procedure.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM
(1) NPG(Staff group)N(70)65(Revised), 21st September, 1970
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
As arranged at its previous Ministerial meeting in Venice, Italy in June 1970,
the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Canada, Germany,
Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom and the United
States, will hold its eighth half-yearly meeting on 29th and 30th October in
Ottawa, Canada under the chairmanship of Mr. Manilo Brosio, Secretary General of
NATO.
