> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary regarding the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled for Monday, February 23, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to receive a briefing on Command, Control and Communications (C3) and to continue discussions regarding Nuclear Consultation - CNI Procedures. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It also references a revised working paper, NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)1(Revised), dated February 17, 1976.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 23rd February,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to receive a briefing on the subject of Command, Control and Communications
(C5);

(b) to continue discussion of Nuclear Consultation - CNI Procedures(1).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
