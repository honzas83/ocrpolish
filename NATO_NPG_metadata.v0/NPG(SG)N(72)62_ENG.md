> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Vice-Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, August 21, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to review reports, studies, and papers received since the last session, and to continue discussions on Consultation Procedures and Facilities. Additionally, the meeting will hold preliminary discussions on the first draft of a Permanent Representatives' Report on the Transition Study, the first draft of a Permanent Representatives' Report on the Implementation of the Special ADM Guidelines, and a United States paper regarding Poseidon. Poseidon refers to a submarine-launched ballistic missile system. The notice is signed by J.P. Mintzel.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions 'Consultation Procedures and Facilities' and 'Implementation of the Special ADM Guidelines', which relate to the mechanisms for sharing nuclear responsibilities and consultation on nuclear use in Europe.
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/British-Nuclear-Policy — The document references a 'U.S. paper on Poseidon', and Poseidon was the US strategic missile system that replaced Polaris, which was the UK's primary nuclear deterrent during this period.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Vice-Chairman
The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Monday, 21st August, 1972,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to take note of various reports, studies and papers received and distributed
since the last meeting of the Staff Group;

(b) to continue the discussion on Consultation Procedures and Facilities(1);
(c) to hold preliminary discussions on:
(i) the first draft of a Permanent Representatives' Report(2) on the Transition
Study(3);

(ii) the first draft of a Permanent Representatives' Report(4) on the
Implementation of the Special ADM Guidelines(5);

(iii) the U.S. paper on Poseidon(6).
(Signed) J.P. MINTZEL
