> # Note by the Secretary Concerning the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Bonn
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated October 2, 1968. It states that members of the Group have proposed amendments to the wording of the press release concerning the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting held in Bonn. Consequently, the Secretary, M.H.C. Wilson, proposes that the NPG Staff Group meet at 2:45 p.m. on Friday, October 4, 1968, to discuss the press release again. The document is issued from Brussels, Belgium, and references the previous press release document NPG(Staff Group)N(68)5.

NPG(Staff Group)N(68)6
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP).
Note by the Secretary
Amendments to the wording of the Press Release (1) concerning the NPG
Ministerial Meeting in Bonn have been proposed by members of the Group.

It will therefore be necessary to discuss the Press Release again and it is
proposed that the NPG Staff Group should meet for this purpose at 2:45 p.m. on
Friday 4th October, 1968.

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON
