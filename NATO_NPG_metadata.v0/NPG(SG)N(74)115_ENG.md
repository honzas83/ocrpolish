> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 27th November 1974. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 2nd December 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss preparations for the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers, scheduled for Tuesday, 10th December 1974, at 5 p.m. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

# Page 1

27th November, 1974

NOTICE

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)115

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 2nd December,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to discuss
preparations for the meeting of NPG Ministers to be held on Tuesday, 10th
December at 5 p.m.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
