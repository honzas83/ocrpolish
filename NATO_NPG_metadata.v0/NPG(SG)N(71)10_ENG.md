> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, January 25, 1971, at 3:00 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes discussions on staff support for military observers, briefings for the Defence Planning Committee and NPG Ambassadors, and the Northern Norway Study. Additionally, the meeting will cover the agenda for a visit to SHAPE (Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe) and national participation in military nuclear planning, specifically if raised by the Italian Representative. The notice was signed by J. de Gaye and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th January,
1971, at 3.00 p.m.

2. Matters for discussion will include:
(a) staff support for military observers (NPG(Staff Group)N(71)9);
(b) briefings for the Defence Planning Committee and NPG Ambassadors;
(c) the Northern Norway Study (NEG/Study(71)1);
(d) the Agenda for the visit to SHAPE;
(e) national participation in military nuclear planning (if raised by the
Italian Representative).

(Signed) J. de GAYE
