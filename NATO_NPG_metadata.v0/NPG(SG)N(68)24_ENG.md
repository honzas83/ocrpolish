> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, dated 5th December 1968, scheduling the next meeting for Monday, 9th December 1968, at 2:45 p.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to continue the discussion of AD/Time Factors. The Secretary, M.H.G. Wilson, signed the document and noted that a revised paper on the matter will be circulated to members before the meeting under the reference NPG(Staff Group)WP(68)5(Revised).
