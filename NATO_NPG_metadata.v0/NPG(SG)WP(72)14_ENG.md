> # Draft Ministerial Agenda
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting scheduled for October 26th and 27th, 1972, in London. The agenda items include briefings on the balance of strategic forces, updating of NATO nuclear planning directives, and studies of follow-on use of nuclear weapons, specifically focusing on the Denmark area. It also covers consultation procedures and facilities, an illustrative study on initial use at sea in the ACLANT area, and forces transition, structure, and deployment in a nuclear environment. Additionally, the agenda includes the implementation of special administrative guidelines and other business, including the future work programme and the final agreed minute and communique. The draft was submitted by the Chairman of the NPG Staff Group, H.B. Seim, from Brussels, and is proposed for discussion by the Staff Group on September 4th, 1972.
>

> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The agenda includes items on 'Updating of NATO Nuclear Planning Directives' and 'Studies of Follow-on Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons' in the Denmark area, as well as 'Initial Use at Sea in the ACLANT area', which directly relate to the mechanisms of sharing nuclear responsibilities and the deployment/use of tactical nuclear weapons on allied territory.

DRAFT MINISTERIAL AGENDA
Note by the Chairman
Attached is an initial draft of an Agenda for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in
London on 26th and 27th October, 1972. To facilitate discussion, all potential
agenda items have been included in this initial draft. It should be understood,
however, that inclusion of a number of the topics (e.g. II and VII) on the final
agenda have not been agreed. It is proposed that the Staff Group discuss this
draft at its meeting on Monday, 4th September.
