> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary on January 29, 1976, announcing the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, February 2, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the session is to continue the discussions regarding the results of the Hamburg meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 2nd February,
