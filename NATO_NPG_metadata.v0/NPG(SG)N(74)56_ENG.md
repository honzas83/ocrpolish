> # List of International Staff Attending the 15th Meeting of NPG Ministers in Norway
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a detailed list of International Staff members accompanying the Secretary General at the 15th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers. The meeting is scheduled to take place in Bergen, Norway, on June 11th and 12th, 1974. The list includes personnel from the Private Office, Executive Secretariat, Division of Political Affairs, Division of Defence Planning and Policy, NATO Office of Security, and the Registry. Specifically, it lists individuals such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Lums, Deputy Directeur du Cabinet Mark C. Lissfelt, and Assistant to the Secretary General E. Borgman Brouwer. It also identifies staff from the Nuclear Planning Directorate and the Cosmic Registry, ensuring the coordination of the staff accompanying the high-level ministerial meeting.
