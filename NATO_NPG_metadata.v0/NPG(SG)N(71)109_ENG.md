> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, October 4, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue preparations for the Ministerial Meeting, taking into account the decisions made during the meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives held on September 27 and October 1, 1971. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

29th September, 1971
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(71)109
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 4th October, 1971
at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue preparations
for the Ministerial Meeting, in the light of decisions taken at the meetings of
the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives held on 27th September and
1st October, 1971.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
