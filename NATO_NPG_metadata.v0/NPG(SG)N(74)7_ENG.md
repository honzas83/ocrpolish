> # Notice of the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, January 21, 1974, at 3:00 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to discuss an initial draft of a Permanent Representatives' report regarding the conceptual analysis of the nuclear aspects of deterrence, to continue discussions on arrangements for the Study of the Implications of Technological Development, and to continue preparations for the upcoming Spring NPG Ministerial meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)7
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 21st January,
1974, at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss an initial draft(1) of a possible Permanent Representatives'
report on the question of making a conceptual analysis of the nuclear aspects of
deterrence;

(b) to continue discussion of arrangements for the Study of the Implications of
Technological Development;

(c) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)1, 17th January, 1974
