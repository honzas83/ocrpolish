> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, September 16, 1974, at 11 a.m. and 2.30 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to finalize the draft report on the Air Defence Illustrative Study, continue discussions on the CNI Study and the draft Permanent Representatives' report on it, and continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references several working papers, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)8(Revised), NPG(Staff Group)WP(74) been revised, and NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)10(Revised).

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 16th September,
1974, at 11 a.m. and at 2.30 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to finalize the draft report on the Air Defence Illustrative Study(1);
(b) to continue discussion of the CNI Study(2), and of the draft Permanent
Representatives' report(3) on it;

(c) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
