> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, September 20, 1976, at 3 p.m. While the meeting time remains as previously stated in a prior note, the Secretary informs the members that the Presentation Room is no longer available at the start time. Consequently, the meeting will begin in Room 3 and move to the Presentation Room later in the afternoon. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson of NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 20th September,
1976 at 3 p.m. as stated in my note dated 16th September(1). Please note however
that the Presentation Room will not now be available at that time and therefore
our meeting will start in Room 3 and we shall move to the Presentation Room
later in the afternoon.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(76)82, 16th September, 1976
