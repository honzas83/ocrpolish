> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document serves as a notice for the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled for Monday, 17th September 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue preparations for the Ministerial meeting. This includes the consideration of a revised draft of the report on early combined use and further discussion of the future work programme, provided time permits. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Monday, 17th September at 3
p.m. The object of the meeting will be to continue preparations for the
Ministerial meeting, including consideration of a revised draft of the report on
early combined use(1) and further discussion of the future work programme(2) if
time permits.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
