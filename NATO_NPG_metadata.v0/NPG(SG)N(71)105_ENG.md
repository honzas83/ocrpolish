> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, September 20, 1971, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss suggestions arising from instructions received from capitals on the subject of recently issued Working Papers and to determine if the draft Ministerial agenda has been approved via the silence procedure. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson of NATO.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 20th September,
1971, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to discuss any suggestions arising from instructions which members may have
received from capitals on the subject of the Working Papers recently issued;

(b) to note whether the draft Ministerial agenda(1) has been approved by the
silence procedure.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
(1) NPG/WP(71)11, 10th September, 1971
