> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 7th May, 1973
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on May 7, 1973. The meeting's agenda included welcoming new members from the Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE), specifically Colonel von Sandart and Lieut. Col. D.L. Groetken. The group noted the upcoming departure of Mr. S.R.C. Jones from the United Kingdom delegation. There was a discussion regarding a possible invitation for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministers to hold their Autumn meeting in the Netherlands. Additionally, the SHAPE representative provided an update on the distribution of the Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR) presentation on the SHAPE Illustrative Study at Ankara. The Chairman mentioned the distribution of initial framework drafts for the Agreed Minute and Final Communiqué to save time during the Ankara meetings. Finally, the group addressed the transport of classified documents to and from Ankara by Mr. K. Bittner and scheduled their next meeting for May 21, 1973.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 7th
May, 1973

WELCOME
1. The STAFF GROUP:
endorsed the warm welcome extended by the Chairman to Colonel von Sandart and
Lieut. Col. D.L. Groetken from SHAPE who would be concerned with Nuclear
Planning and would be attending NPG Staff Group meetings.

DEPARTURE
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) noted that some members of the Staff Group would be departing during the
Summer; the first departure being that of Mr. S.R.C. Jones, United Kingdom
Delegation, on 9th June;

(b) invited the Secretary to organise a lunch party to take place during the
latter half of May(1).

AUTUMN MEETING OF NPG MINISTERS
3. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Netherlands representative who said that he
hoped for an early decision from his Ministry concerning a possible invitation
to NPG Ministers to hold their Autumn meeting in the Netherlands.

(1) Subsequently fixed for Monday, 21st May, 1973, 12.30 for 1 p.m.

PRESENTATION BY SACEUR AT ANKARA
4. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the SHAPE representative who said that copies of
SACEUR's presentation on the SHAPE Illustrative Study at Ankara were being
distributed to Delegations and the International Staff.

INITIAL FRAMEWORK DRAFTS OF THE AGREEED MINUTE AND FINAL COMMUNIQUE
5. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who said that he would distribute, with
the object of saving time in Ankara, initial framework drafts of the Agreed
Minute and Final Communiqué; he emphasised that these initial drafts were in no
way an attempt to pre-judge the results of the meeting's.

TRANSPORT OF CLASSIFIED DOCUMENTS TO AND FROM ANKARA
6. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) noted that Mr. K. Bittner would carry classified documents to and from
Ankara;

(b) invited the International Staff to circulate instructions on this matter(1).
NEXT MEETING OF STAFF GROUP
7. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 21st May, 1973, at 3 p.m.
