> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, May 26, 1983, at 2:45 p.m. The primary agenda items for the meeting include the discussion of WINTEX-CIMEX 83, specifically reviewing a draft report by Permanent Representatives and a draft report to the Committee on Nuclear Planning (COEC). The notice also includes a provision for any other business. The document is issued from Brussels and is signed by D.J. Roberts.
