> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 13th December, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on December 13, 1976. The meeting's primary focus was the future work programme, which involved a preliminary exchange of views on a draft report by Permanent Representatives regarding future work priorities, as detailed in NPG(Staff Group) P(76)9. The Staff Group agreed to resume the discussion of this paper based on instructions at their next meeting. Additionally, the group agreed to discuss a status report on Theatre Information Systems (TIES) at the next meeting. The next meeting of the Staff Group is scheduled for Monday, January 10, 1977, at 3 p.m. in Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 13th
December, 1976

FUTURE 'ORK PROGRAMME

1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) had a preliminary and personal exchange of views on the note by the Chairman
in NPG(Staff Group) P(76)9 containing a draft report by Permanent
Representatives on future work priorities;

(b) agreed to resume discussion of this paper, on the basis of instructions, at
its next meeting;

(c) requested delegations to circulate any comments they might have prior to the
meeting.

THEATRE INFORMATION SYSTEMS

2. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to discuss the question of a status report on TIES at its next meeting on
the basis of instructions.

NEXT MEETING

3. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 10th January, 1977 at 3 p.m.

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
