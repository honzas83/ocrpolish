> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Tuesday, November 24, 1970, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the document are to notify members of the meeting time and outline the principal matters for discussion. The agenda includes the review of draft paragraphs on nuclear matters for inclusion in the Defence Policy Committee (DPC) Communiqué and a possible exchange of views on matters to be addressed by the Nuclear Defence Advisory Committee (NDAC). The notice was signed by I.H. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
