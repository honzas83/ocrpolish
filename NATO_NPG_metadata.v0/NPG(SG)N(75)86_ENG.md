> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 13th October, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on October 13, 1975. The group reviewed a draft report by Permanent Representatives regarding the arrangements for Phase III of the follow-on use work programme and requested a revised draft from the International Secretariat. Additionally, the Staff Group had a preliminary exchange of views on the outline for future Nuclear Planning Group work to be submitted to Permanent Representatives by the Secretary General. The group agreed to resume this discussion on October 20. Finally, the group was informed by the Netherlands Representative that their authorities questioned the need for holding meetings of the Technology Study Group outside of NATO Headquarters.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR PHASE III OF THE FOLLOW-ON USE WORK PROGRAMME
1. THE STAFF GROUP:
(a) reviewed the draft report by Permanent Representatives in NPG(Staff
Group)N(75)11;

(b) requested the International Secretariat to circulate a revised draft in the
light of the discussion for consideration at the next meeting.

FUTURE WORK PROGRAMME
2. THE STAFF GROUP:
(a) had a preliminary exchange of views on the outline suggested in NPG(Staff
Group)N(75)63 for the FO to be submitted to Permanent Representatives by the
Secretary General on future NPG work;

(b) agreed to resume discussion of this outline at its meeting on 20th October.
TECHNOLOGY STUDY
3. THE STAFF GROUP:
was informed by the Netherlands Representative that his Authorities questioned
the need for holding meetings of the Technology Study Group outside NATO
Headquarters.
