> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling a meeting for Tuesday, November 2, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to review a revised presentation on new and refined Nuclear Consultation Procedures, to continue discussions regarding the International Staff note on the possible revision of the Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in Allied Command Europe (ACE), and to discuss arrangements for the NPC London Meeting if necessary. The meeting will include the Assistant Secretary General for Defence Plans and Policy, and other interested persons with a need to know are welcome to attend.

27th October, 1976
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)98
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 2nd November,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to review the revised version of the presentation on new and refined Nuclear
Consultation Procedures;*

(b) to continue the 'discussion on the International Staff note(1) concerning
the possible revision of the Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike
Forces in ACE(2);

(c) if necessary, to discuss arrangements for the NPC London Meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(76)90, 13th October, 1976
(2) DPC/D(70)59(Revised), 21st December, 1972 (CTS)
* It is understood that the Assistant Secretary General for Defence Plans and
Policy will be present for this item. Other interested persons with a need to
know will be welcome.
