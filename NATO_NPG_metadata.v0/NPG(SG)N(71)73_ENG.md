> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on behalf of the Chairman. The purpose of the notice is to inform the members of the Staff Group that their next scheduled meeting has been postponed. The new meeting date is set for Monday, 12th July, 1971, at 3 p.m. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
