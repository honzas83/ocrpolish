> [!abstract] 
> # Reference List of Follow-On Use Work Programme Documents
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), serves as a comprehensive reference list of the principal documents relating to the follow-on use work programme. The document is signed by Chairman H.B. Seim and consists of two annexes. Annex A lists documents by reference number, including the document series (NPG/D) and the study series (NPG/Study), covering various regions such as North Norway, Macedonia, Western Thrace, and the CENTAG area. Annex B organizes these same documents by subject, categorizing them into work programme arrangements, status reports, and follow-on use studies. The list includes classification levels such as Secret (S), Confidential (C), and Cosmic Top Secret (CTS), and mentions comments from SHAPE and SACLANT. The purpose of the document is to ensure NPG members have a current record of the distribution of documents related to the follow-on use work programme.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The document is a reference list of documents relating to the 'follow-on use work programme', which includes specific 'Follow-on Use Studies' for various regions (North Norway, Macedonia, Western Thrace, NORTHAG, CENTAG) and mentions 'Letter/yield code' for specific studies, indicating internal mechanisms for strike planning and yield calculations.
