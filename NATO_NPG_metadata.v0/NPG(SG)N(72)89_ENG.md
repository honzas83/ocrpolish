> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, October 16, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to examine any outstanding matters concerning the forthcoming Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting and to continue discussions regarding the future activities of the Staff Group. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 16th October,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to examine any matters concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting
which may still be outstanding;

(b) to continue the discussion of the future activities of the Staff Group.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
