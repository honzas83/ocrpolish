> # Briefing on the Functions and Activities of the Nuclear Planning Group
> 
> A briefing regarding the functions and activities of the Nuclear Planning Group will be conducted by the Director of Nuclear Planning in conference room 16-A on Friday, September 28, 1973, at 10:15 a.m. The session will include a question period following the presentation. Attendance is open to all national representatives, members of the International Staff, the International Military Staff, and Military Headquarters, provided they have a need to know and are cleared to Cosmic Top Secret. The notice was issued by M.H.G. Wilson, the Secretary, from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary
A briefing on the functions and activities of the Nuclear Planning Group will be
given by the Director of Nuclear Planning in conference room 16-A, at 10.15 a.m.
on Friday, 28th September, 1973.

The briefing will be followed by a question period.
All national representatives, members of the International Staff, the
International Military Staff and Military Headquarters, who have a need to know
and are cleared to Cosmic Top Secret, will be welcome.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
