> # Further Studies of Follow-On Tactical Use
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 12th January 1971. It informs the Staff Group that a study on the limited battle area in Norway, including support by SACLANT Forces, will shortly be circulated. The Secretary notes that several other studies regarding Follow-On Tactical Use will become available in the coming months. To ensure security and economy, the document proposes a limited distribution list for these studies, as detailed in the attached Annex. The distribution list includes various NATO member states such as Belgium, Canada, Denmark, Germany, Germany, Greece, Italy, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, and several NATO bodies like IMS, SACEUREP, SHAPE, and SACLANT. The recipient is requested to agree to the proposed number of copies allotted to them by 15th January 1971.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
FURTHER STUDIES OF FOLLOW-ON TACTICAL USE(1)
Note by the Secretary
At their meeting held on 11th January, 1971, the Staff Group took note that the
study on the limited battle area in Norway including the support by SACLANT
Forces (discussion leaders Norway and Canada) would shortly be circulated. The
Staff Group also noted that a number of other studies would become available
during the next few months.

2. For reasons of security and economy the number of copies of these studies to
be circulated should be kept to a minimum. Attached at Annex is a suggested
distribution list for all the studies dealing with Follow-On Tactical Use.

3. If no word is received by close of business on Friday, 15th January, it will
be assumed that you are able to agree to the proposed number of copies allotted
to you.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
