> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Wednesday, March 2, 1977, at 10:30 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue the discussion of the draft Terms of Reference for the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study and to address any other business. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references a previous working paper, NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)2, dated February 16, 1977.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 2nd March,
1977 at 10.30 a.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of the draft Terms of Reference for the Maritime
Nuclear Warfare Study(1);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)2, 16th February, 1977
