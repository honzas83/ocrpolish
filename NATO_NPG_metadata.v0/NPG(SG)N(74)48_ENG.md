> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) notifying members that the next meeting will take place on Monday, May 6, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to consider comments or instructions regarding the draft Permanent Representatives' reports and to continue preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The document references two specific working papers: NPG/WP(74)4 concerning the Military Committee Function and NPG/WP(74)5 concerning the Warsaw Pact Doctrine and Strategy.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 6th May, 1974 at
3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be: (a) to consider any
comments or instructions that may have been received concerning the draft
Permanent Representatives' reports(1); (b) to continue preparations for the
Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG/WP(74)4, 22nd April, 1974 (Military Committee Function)
NPG/WP(74)5, 30th April, 1974 (Warsaw Pact Doctrine and Strategy)
