> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the rescheduling of a meeting. The meeting, originally scheduled for July 30, 1976, is now set to take place on Monday, August 2, 1976, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the terms of reference for the NIKE HERCULES Study. The notice references several documents, including a US letter dated July 15, 1976, a Military Committee Paper, and an addendum to NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)5 dated June 24, 1976.
>

> #Military-Operations-and-Capabilities/Air-and-Missile-Defense — The document explicitly mentions a meeting to discuss the 'terms of reference for the NIKE HERCULES Study'. The Nike Hercules was a surface-to-air missile system designed for air and missile defense.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 2nd August, 1976,
at 3 p.m., NOT on Friday 30th July as stated in my note NPG(Staff Group)N(76)67,
dated 27th July, which is cancelled. The principal object of this meeting will
be to discuss the terms of reference for the NIKE HERCULES Study(1)(2)(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(2) Military Committee Paper
