> # Draft Agenda for the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee Meeting
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for a meeting of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee scheduled for November 14, 1968. The agenda items include a report by the Nuclear Planning Group, specifically addressing the possible tactical use of nuclear weapons in the Southern region of the Allied Command Europe (A.C.L.), to be introduced by Minister Gul. Additionally, it outlines the the development of tentative guidelines for the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) military authorities, to be introduced by ministers Healey and Schröder. The agenda also includes consultations on the European Community policies on strategic nuclear forces and a discussion on strategic nuclear forces to be introduced by Secretary Clifford. The document originates from Brussels, Belgium, and is marked as CLEAN/NATO.
