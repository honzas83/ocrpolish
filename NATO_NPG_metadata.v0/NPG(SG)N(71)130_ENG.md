> # Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice regarding the next meeting of the Staff Group, scheduled for Monday, 29th November 1971, at 3 p.m. in the Presentation Room of the NATO Situation Centre. The meeting will include a briefing on Crisis Management provided by a member of the Council Operations Directorate. Additionally, the meeting will be included with a visit to the Situation Centre. The Secretary requests that any member wishing to bring additional colleagues to the meeting, due to limited space, must inform the Secretary via extension 2410 no later than midday on Monday, 29th November.

NPG(Staff Group)N(71)130
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 29th November,
1971, at 3 p.m. in the Presentation Room of the NATO Situation Centre. A member
of the Council Operations Directorate will give a briefing on Crisis Management
and there will be a visit to the Situation Centre.

2. There will be space for a limited number of persons in addition to normal
Staff Group attendance. Any member who wishes to bring a colleague or colleagues
with him is requested to inform the Secretary (Ext. 2410) not later than midday
on Monday, 29th November.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
