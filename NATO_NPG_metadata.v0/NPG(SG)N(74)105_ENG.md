> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels on Monday, 14th October, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group met on October 14, 1974, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to coordinate administrative and strategic planning. The meeting covered the distribution of several papers, including reports on the CNI Study and Air Defence, and a note on the Future Work Programme. The group discussed the necessity of close consultation and coordination between the United States and the NPG regarding the Future Work Programme. Additionally, they addressed a proposed change to the CNI Study by the Italian Ambassador, which was decided against, though the Secretary General would mention the matter in Rome. The Chairman emphasized the need for internal cooperation and liaison within NATO Headquarters for matters concerning the NPG. The United States representative indicated that proposals for the date and place of the Spring 1975 meeting of NPG Ministers in the United States would be provided shortly. Finally, the German representative offered air transport from Bonn to Rome for participants of the upcoming ministerial meeting, with flight details provided for November 6-9, 1974.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels
on Monday, 14th October, 1974
SECURITY
1. The STAFF GROUP:
noted that all persons present were cleared for the level of classification of
the matters to be discussed.

DOCUMENTATION
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) noted that the following papers would be distributed during the course of
the coming week:

(i) NPG/D(74)10, 15th October, 1974: Permanent Representatives' report on the
CNI Study;

(ii) NPG/D(74)11, 15th October, 1974: Permanent Representatives' report on the
Illustrative Study on Air Defence;

(iii) PO/74/140, 16th October, 1974: Note by the Secretary General on the Future
Work Programme;

(iv) NPG/A(74)5, 16th October, 1974: Agenda for the Ministerial Meeting;
(b) noted that the background paper being prepared by SHAPE on the subject of
Selective Employment Planning would, when available, be circulated under
reference NPG/D(74)9.

ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
3. The STAFF GROUP: took note of a statement by the Chairman who reminded the
representatives of the countries participating in the Rome NPG Ministerial
Meeting that replies concerning the text and timing of the Advance Press
Release(...) should be received by him not later than Friday, 18th October,
1974.

FUTURE WORK PROGRAMME
4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of and agreed to a strengthening of "the last sentence of
paragraph 3 of the Future Work Programme (to the effect that close consultation
and coordination between the U.S. and the NPG will be required);

(b) invited the International Staff to change paragraph 6, concerning the
implementation of Special ADM Guidelines, in accordance with the views of the
Permanent Representatives expressed at their meeting on 10th October, 1974.

STUDY ON COMMUNICATING NATO'S INTENTIONS
5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) recalled that the Italian Ambassador had proposed a change to the CNI
Study(2) at the meeting of Permanent Representatives on 10th October;

(b) noted that no change would be made to the Study but that the matter would be
mentioned by the Secretary General in his comments in Rome on the agenda item;

(c) took note of a statement by the Chairman who requested that Staff Group
representatives should, when possible, give him advance warning of interventions
by their Ambassadors.

CO-OPERATION IN NUCLEAR MATTERS
6. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of the remarks of the Chairman who stressed the need for co-operation
and liaison within the Headquarters with regard to any questions which could
concern the NPG; he earnestly requested members who, during the course of their
activities in other committees or bodies, came across matters of interest to the
NPG or activities to which the NPG could usefully make a contribution, to inform
him or the Staff Group.

SPRING 1975 MEETING OF NPG MINISTERS
7. The STAFF GROUP:
noted with appreciation the remarks of the United States representative who said
that he hoped shortly be able to inform the Staff Group of his authorities
proposals regarding the place and date of the Spring 1975 meeting of NPG
Ministers to be held in the United States.

AIR TRANSPORTATION FROM BONN TO ROME
8. The STAFF GROUP:
noted with appreciation the remarks of the German representative who said that
the offer by his authorities of air transport from Bonn to Rome and return was
still open, and that names of passengers should be given to him before 25th
October; the latest information regarding the flights was that the aircraft
would leave Bonn Military airport at 4 p.m. on Wednesday, 6th November, arriving
Rome about 6 p.m., and would return to Bonn on Saturday, 9th November, departing
at 4 p.m. and arriving about 6 p.m.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP
9. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 21st October, 1974, at 3 p.m.
1110 Brussels.
