> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of an upcoming meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, January 12, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to make final preparations for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting in Hamburg. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
