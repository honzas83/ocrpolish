> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, September 15, 1975, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to consider a draft paper on CII 'Warning Messages' by the ad hoc working group, review the Country Request paper, and discuss the German and United States Phase III Scope papers. Additionally, the group will hear initial comments on the proposed draft Ministerial Agenda. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Tilton and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th September,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to consider a draft paper on CII 'Warning Messages by the ad hoc working
group(1),

(b) to consider the Country Request paper(2),

(c) to discuss the German United 'in' 'dom and United States Phase III Scope
papers

(d) to hear initial comments on the proposed draft Ministerial Agenda(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. TILTON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) U. Delegation paper NP 10/3 dated 2nd September, 1975

(2) FPC(Staff Group)WP(75)8, 1st September, 1975

(3) IPC(Staff Group)WP(75)9, 10th September, 1975
