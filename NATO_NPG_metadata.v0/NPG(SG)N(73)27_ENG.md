> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, 2nd April, 1973, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The principal subject for discussion during this meeting will be future Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) activities. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and references another document, NPG(Staff Group)N(73)28.
