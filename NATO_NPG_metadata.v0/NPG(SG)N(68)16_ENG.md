> # Note by the Secretary regarding the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Meeting
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated October 20, 1968. The primary purpose of the meeting scheduled for Monday, November 4, 1968, at 2:45 p.m. is to discuss time factors. The note is signed by M.H.G. NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group is scheduled to take place on Monday, 4th
November, 1968, at 2.45 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to
discuss Time Factors.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
