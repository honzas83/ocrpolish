> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, June 19, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to further discuss the NATO Military Authorities' proposals for short-term measures to improve NATO's TNF. The agenda also includes any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references previous documents NPG/Study/52 and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)40.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document is a notice for a Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) meeting to discuss 'short-term measures to improve NATO's TNF', where TNF refers to Tactical Nuclear Forces, which falls under the deployment and sharing of nuclear weapons on allied territory.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th June, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) further discussion of the NATO Military Authorities' proposals for short-
term measures to improve NATO's TNF(1);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG/Study/52, 23rd March, 1978 and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)40, 7th June, 1978
