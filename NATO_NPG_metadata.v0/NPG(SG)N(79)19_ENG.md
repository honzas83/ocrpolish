> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the 25th Meeting of I.F.G. Ministers in Florida
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), outlines the composition of the International Staff delegation accompanying the Secretary General to the 25th Meeting of I.F.G. Ministers. The meeting is scheduled to take place at Homestead AFB, Florida. The delegation list includes high-ranking officials such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luis, Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning and Policy W.F. Munford, and Director for Nuclear Planning R.E. Shearer. Additionally, the delegation comprises the Director of the NATO Office of Security, the Deputy Director for Nuclear Planning, and various support staff including interpreters and secretaries. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts, Secretary of the IPG Staff Group, and serves as a formal record of the delegation's composition for the 25th Meeting of I.F.G. Ministers.
