> # 20th Meeting of NPG Ministers - Transport of Classified Documents to London
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the transport of classified documents for the upcoming 20th meeting of NPG ministers in London. Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section, is designated to carry the classified papers, departing on Tuesday, 16th November 1975, and returning on the same day. Delegations are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and submit their papers by mid-day on Monday, 15th November. The notice further specifies that papers should be packaged, clearly marked with the delegation or person's name and a serial number, and kept as light as possible to avoid burdening the courier with unclassified material.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

20th MEETING OF NPG MINISTERS - TRANSPORT OF CLASSIFIED DOCUMENTS TO LONDON

Note by the Secretary

Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section (Tel. ext.
2481), will carry classified papers for the London meeting, departing Tuesday,
16th November, and will return with them on 16th November. Delegations wishing
to take advantage of this arrangement are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and
to send him their papers not later than mid-day on Monday, 15th November.

2. Papers should be packaged and clearly marked with the name of the delegation
or person and a serial number. Packages should be as light as possible. Please
do NOT burden the Courrier with unclassified material.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
