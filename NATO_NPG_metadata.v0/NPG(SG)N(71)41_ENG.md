> # Illustrative Studies and Examples of Release Requests
> 
> On March 24, 1971, the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened to discuss and finalize a further version of the International Staff's draft paper on Illustrative Studies and Examples of Release Requests. The group reached agreement on the text after making several changes. The International Staff was invited to circulate the agreed version as a Nuclear Planning Group Working Paper for discussion by NPG Permanent Representatives at their meeting scheduled for April 20. Members were further encouraged to send this Working Paper to their respective capitals for immediate comments. The Staff Group noted that minor comments could be handled at its meetings on April 5 and 19, allowing Permanent Representatives to focus on major issues during their subsequent meeting. Additionally, the group agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, April 5, 1971, at 3 p.m.

25th March, 1971
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(71)41
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held on Wednesday, 24th March, 1971
ILLUSTRATIVE STUDIES AND EXAMPLES OF RELEASE REQUESTS
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of and discussed in detail a further version(1) of the
International Staff's draft paper on Illustrative Studies and Examples of
Release Requests, and, after making a number of changes, reached agreement on
the text;

(b) invited the International Staff to circulate the agreed version in the form
of a Nuclear Planning Group Working Paper(2) for discussion by NPG Permanent
Representatives at their meeting to be held on Tuesday, 20th April;

(c) invited members to send this Working Paper to their capitals with a view to
obtaining comments as soon as possible; noted in this respect that it should be
possible for the Staff Group to discuss and deal with minor comments at its
meetings on 5th and 19th April, thus giving the Permanent Representatives the
possibility of concentrating upon the matters of major importance at their
meeting next day.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP
2. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 5th April, at 3 p.m.
