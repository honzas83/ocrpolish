> # Notes of a Meeting Held at NATO Headquarters on Monday, 24th March, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group met at NATO Headquarters on March 24, 1969, to address several key agenda items. Regarding Atomic Demolition Time Factors, the group took note of changes proposed by the United Kingdom and the United States, agreed to modifications of document NPG/D(69)1(Revised), and decided that the paper should be referred to the Italian Authorities. The group also instructed the International Staff to prepare draft terms of reference for future work on this matter. Additionally, the group discussed the role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces, prioritizing the study and accepting a proposal by the United Kingdom and Canadian representatives to prepare a fuller version of their joint outline paper. Finally, the group coordinated the dates for the next meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives on April 26, 1969, and the next NPG Staff Group meetings on March 31 and April 10, 1969.

1. The STAFF GROUP, taking into account the views expressed by the NPG Permanent

modifications to the document;

Permanent Representatives' document, reflecting: the
decisions made by them at their meeting on 18th march,
viz :
(i) took note of the document, subject to certain
amendments;
(ii) agreed to refer it to the Italian Authorities;
(iii) instructed the Staff Group to prepare draft terms
of reference for future work on this matter.
(e) agreed that this version of the document should be
circulated as soon as possible to the Staff Group for
verification. If no comments were received during the
following week, the document would be considered as final
