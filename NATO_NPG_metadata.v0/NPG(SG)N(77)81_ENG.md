> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, December 12, 1977, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to consider reports from the Nuclear Planning Group Ad Hoc Group meetings, specifically the Maritime Study Group and the High Level Group, and to discuss the agenda and preparations for the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. Additionally, the notice mentions a farewell ceremony for Mr. Michael Legge in the Chairman's office at 5 p.m. following the meeting. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
1st December, 1977
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(77)81
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 12th December,
1977 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) reports on NPG Ad Hoc Group meetings (Maritime Study Group and High Level
Group);

(b) the Agenda for the Spring, 1978, NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(c) other preparations for the Spring, 1978 Meeting;
(d) any other business.
2. After the meeting, there will be a farewell ceremony at 5 p.m. in the
Chairman's office, with appropriate refreshments, to mark the departure of Mr.
Michael Legge.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
