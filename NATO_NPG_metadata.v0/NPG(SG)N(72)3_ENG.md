> [!abstract] 
> # Farewell Luncheon For Mr. Da Rin
> 
> A notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announces a farewell luncheon in honor of Mr. Da Rin, who is departing from the Staff Group. The event is scheduled to take place on Wednesday, January 19, 1972, at 12:45 PM for a 1:00 PM start in the Salon des Ambassadeurs at NATO Headquarters. The notice specifies that drinks will be served prior to the luncheon and that members will be informed of their individual contributions by Mr. Serio or the Secretary. Members of the Staff Group are requested to confirm their attendance with either Mr. Serio or the Secretary via the provided internal telephone extensions.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
FAREWELL LUNCHEON FOR MR. DA RIN
Note by the Secretary
As agreed by the members of the Staff Group at their meeting held on 10th
January, 1972, a luncheon party will be given in honour of Mr. Da Rin, who is
shortly leaving the Staff Group.

This luncheon will take place in the Salon des Ambassadeurs, NATO Headquarters,
on Wednesday, 19th January, 1972, at 12.45 for 1 p.m. Drinks will be served
before luncheon. Members will be informed of the amount of their contribution by
Mr. Serio or myself as soon as possible.

Members of the Staff Group are requested to inform Mr. Serio (2043) or myself
(2410), whether they will be able to take part in this luncheon.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
