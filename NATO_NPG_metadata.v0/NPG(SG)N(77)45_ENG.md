> # 21st Meeting of NPG Ministers - Transport of Classified Documents to Ottawa
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, detailing the logistics for the transport of classified papers for the upcoming 21st meeting of NPG ministers in Ottawa. Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section, will serve as the courier, departing on Tuesday, 7th June, and returning on Sunday, 12th June. Delegations are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and submit their classified papers by the closing time on Friday, 3rd June, 1977. The notice specifies that papers should be be packaged and clearly marked with the name of the delegation or person and a serial number, and that packages should be kept as light as possible. Furthermore, it is explicitly stated that unclassified material should not be burden the courier.

Note by the Secretary

Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters
Cosmic Control Section (Tel. ext. 2481), will carry classified
papers for the Ottawa meeting, departing Tuesday, 7th June,
and will return with them on Sunday, 12th June. Delegations
wishing to take advantage of this arrangement are requested to
contact Mr. Tuccimei and to send him their papers not later
than closing time on Friday, 3rd June, 1977.

2. Papers should be packaged and clearly marked with
the name of the delegation or person and a serial number.
Packages should be as light as possible. Please do NOT
burden the Courier with unclassified material.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
