> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary regarding the rescheduling of the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is now set for Monday, 24th October 1977, at 5:00 p.m. in Room I-352, rather than the previously agreed 3:00 p.m. The purpose of the meeting is to comment upon a matter raised at the last meeting, and the Chairman will provide appropriate quantities of solid and liquid fuels. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson in Brussels.
