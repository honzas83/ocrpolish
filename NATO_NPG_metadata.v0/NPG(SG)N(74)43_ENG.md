> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, April 22, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue the examination of a revised text for a possible Permanent Representatives' Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons, while incorporating suggested modifications. Additionally, the meeting will focus on discussing a summary of possible future work for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) and continuing preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by N.L.C. Park and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 22nd April, 1974
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue examination of a revised text(1) of a possible Permanent
Representatives' Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy
and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons(2), taking into
consideration some suggested modifications separately circulated(3);

(b) to discuss a summary of possible future NPG work(4);
(c) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting.
(Signed) N.L.C. PARK
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
