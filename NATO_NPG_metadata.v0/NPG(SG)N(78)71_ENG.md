> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Future Meetings
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of future meetings. The first meeting is scheduled for Monday, 23rd October 1978, at 3 p.m., with the primary objective of following up on the Brussels Ministerial meeting and addressing any other business. A second meeting is scheduled for Thursday, 26th October 1978, at 10 a.m., to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, referencing document USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
FUTURE MEETINGS
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 23rd October,
1978 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) follow-up to the Brussels Ministerial meeting;
(b) any other business.

2. There will also be a meeting on Thursday, 26th October at 10 a.m. at which
the principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1);
(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

(1) USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126, 13th September, 1978
