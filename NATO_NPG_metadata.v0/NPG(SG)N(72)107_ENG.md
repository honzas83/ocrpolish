> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, November 27, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to review preparations for the forthcoming Nuclear Planning Group Advisory Committee (NDAC) meeting on December 6 and to continue discussions on current work. Additionally, the meeting will be preceded by a farewell luncheon party for Colonel J.L. Dawson, which will take place in the Salon des Ambasseurs at 12:30 for 1 p.m.

22nd November, 1972
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(72)107
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 27th November,
1972 at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to review
preparations for the forth-coming NDAC Meeting on 6th December, and to continue
discussions on current work.

2. The meeting will be preceded by a farewell luncheon party for Colonel J.L.
Dawson. This will take place in the Salon des Ambasseurs at 12.30 for 1 p.m.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
