> # NPG (Staff Group) (68)4
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Planning Group (Staff Group) meeting minutes record the Nuclear Planning Group's decisions regarding upcoming meetings, the Southern Region Study, and the amended schedule for the upcoming Ministerial meeting in Bonn. The document notes that the Secretary General intended to mention the Canadian Fighter Bomber Paper in his introductory remarks. It also mentions that no paper on the theatre contribution to Strategic Deterrence would be sent to Military Authorities until after the Bonn conference. Regarding the Southern Region Study, the group agreed with Italy's proposal to not put forward a cover note. The document outlines the schedule for the NPG at Ambassadors level, discussing defensive systems, battlefield use, demonstrative use, and use at sea. Finally, the group agreed to meet again on September 27, 1968, to discuss the Press Announcement and other matters concerning the Bonn meeting.

III. SOUTHERN REGION STUDY

3. The GROUP :
noted that Italy, as discussion leader, preferred not to put forward a cover
note to her study, and agreed with this proposal.

IV. NEXT MEETING OF THE NPG AT AMBASSADORS LEVEL

4. The GROUP, in view of the fact that two of the four papers remaining to be
discussed by the Ambassadors were not yet available,

(a) agreed that on Thursday, 26th September, the NPG should discuss :
(1) the Guideline papers on defensive systems.
(2) Battlefield use
(b) agreed (1) that on Monday, 30th September, the NPG should discuss :
(1) Demonstrative use
(2) Use at Sea.

V. NEXT MEETING OF THE NPG (STAFF GROUP)

5. The GROUP :
agreed to meet on Friday, 27th September, 1968, with a view to discussing the
Press Announcement and other matters concerning the Bonn meeting.
