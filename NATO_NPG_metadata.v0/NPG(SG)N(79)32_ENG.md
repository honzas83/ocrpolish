> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, September 3, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines and specifically address Section IV regarding the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice also lists several reference documents from 1978 and 1979, including those from USNATO and the DPP, to provide context for the discussions. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document is a notice for a meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group to discuss the 'General Political Guidelines' and specifically 'Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons', which directly relates to the consultation and mechanisms for the use of nuclear weapons within the NATO alliance.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 3rd September,
1979 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
