> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, January 14, 1982, at 4 p.m. The meeting's agenda includes the consideration of the NATO/WP Force Comparison, preparations for the Spring 1982 NPG Ministerial Meeting, and any other business. The notice is issued by D.J. Roberts on behalf of the North Atlantic Council.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

ORIGINAL: ENGLISH 11th January, 1982
NOTICE NPG(Staff Group)N(82)2

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 14th January,
1982 at 4 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be: (a) NATO/WP
Force Comparison; (b) preparations for Spring 1982 NPG Ministerial Meeting; (c)
any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
