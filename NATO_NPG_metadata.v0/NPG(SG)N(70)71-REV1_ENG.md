> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Fall NPG Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, H. B. Seim, regarding a proposed advance press release for the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting scheduled for October 29th and 30th, 1970, in Ottawa, Canada. The text of the press release follows the pattern of a previous release made prior to the Venice meeting. The Chairman suggests that the release be issued in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Monday, October 26th, 1970, with simultaneous releases in national capitals at the discretion of each country. Delegations are requested to forward the draft to their respective capitals for approval via the silence procedure, with a deadline of Friday, October 16th, 1970. The draft press release itself specifies that the Nuclear Planning Group, consisting of Canada, Germany, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will hold its eighth half-yearly Ministerial meeting under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Mr. Manlio Brosio.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE FALL NPG MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text of a proposed advance press release concerning the
forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Ottawa on 29th and 30th October, as
agreed at the Staff Group meeting on 28th September. The language closely
follows the pattern of the press release made just prior to the Venice meeting.
It is suggested that the release might be made in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time
on Monday, 26th October, 1970 with simultaneous release in capitals at the
discretion of the country concerned.

2. Delegations are requested to forward the draft to capitals for approval by
the silence procedure. Accordingly, if no objections or comments are received by
Friday, 16th October, 1970 the text and timing of release will be considered
approved.

(Signed) H. B. SEIM
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
As arranged at its previous ministerial meeting in Venice, Italy in June 1970,
the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Canada, Germany,
Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom and the United
States, will hold its eighth half-yearly Ministerial meeting on 29th and 30th
October in Ottawa, Canada under the chairmanship of Mr. Manlio Brosio, Secretary
General of NATO.
