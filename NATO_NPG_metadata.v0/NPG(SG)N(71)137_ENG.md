> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, December 20, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are twofold: first, to continue discussions regarding follow-up actions from the Brussels Meeting, with a specific focus on the Air Study; and second, to hold an initial discussion on the necessity of updating nuclear policy documents in light of improvements to United States naval forces. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(71)137

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 20th December,
1971, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion of follow-up action to the Brussels Meeting(1),
with special reference to the Air Study;

(b) to hold an initial discussion on the requirement to up-date nuclear policy
documents in the light of United States naval forces improvements.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)122, 3rd November, 1971
