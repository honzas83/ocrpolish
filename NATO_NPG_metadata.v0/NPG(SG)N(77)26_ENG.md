> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Wednesday, March 30, 1977, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to hold a preliminary discussion on NG(Staff Group)WP(77)3 (Observations on Exercise WINTEX 77), to review the Status of Work Report (NG/D(77)1), and to initiate discussions on the subjects to be covered in the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Agenda for June 1977. Additionally, the meeting will address any other business. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

24th March, 1977

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(77)26

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 30th March,
1977 at 3.00 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hold a preliminary discussion on NG(Staff Group)WP(77)3, 23rd March, 1977
(Observations on Exercise WINTEX 77);

(b) to review NG/D(77)1, 16th February, 1977 (Status of Work Report);
(c) to initiate discussions on the subjects to be covered in the NG Ministerial
Agenda for June, 1977;

(d) to address any other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
