> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 27th July 1976. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 30th July 1976, at 10:30 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the terms of reference for the NIKE HERCULES Study. The notice references a US letter dated 15th July 1976 and a Military Committee Paper. The document was issued in Brussels, Belgium, and has been declassified for public disclosure.
