> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 8th October, 1973
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened on October 8, 1973, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to prepare for the Ministerial Meeting scheduled for November 6-7, 1973. The group reviewed and finalized several Working Papers, including those on Operational Concepts and Doctrine, Implementation of Special ADM Guidelines, and Consultation Procedures and Facilities. The meeting also addressed the status of the Follow-on Use Work Programme and the Early Combined Use paper, the latter of which required agreement between Germany and the Netherlands. The group discussed reports on Aspects of Deterrence and Communicating NATO's Intentions, though these were not yet deemed ready for Ministerial review. Additionally, the meeting covered an upcoming briefing on Atomic Demolition Munitions (ADMs) scheduled for October 22, and a preliminary study on Warsaw Pact Politico-military Strategy and Military Doctrine. Administrative arrangements, including a press release and participant lists for the meeting in The Hague, were also discussed. Finally, the the group cancelled the tentatively planned meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives on October 18 and scheduled their next Staff Group meeting for October 15, 1973.
