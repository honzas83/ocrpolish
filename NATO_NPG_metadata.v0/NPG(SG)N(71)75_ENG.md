> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling a meeting for Monday, July 19, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss Working Papers NPG/WP(71)4 and NPG/WP(71)5 based on instructions from Capitals, and to prepare necessary amendments for the examination of these papers by the NPG Permanent Representatives at their meeting on July 22. Additionally, the meeting aims to confirm the dates for the Autumn NPG Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson in Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th July, 1971,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be :

(a) to discuss the Working Papers NPG/WP(71)4 and NPG/WP(71)5 in the light of
instructions received from Capitals and to prepare amendments where necessary
with a view to the examination of these Working Papers by the NPG Permanent
Representatives at their meeting to be held on 22nd July;

(b) to confirm if possible the dates of the Autumn NPG Ministerial Meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels
