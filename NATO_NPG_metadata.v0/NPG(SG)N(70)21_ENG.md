> # Nuclear Planetary Group (Staff Group) Memorandum
> 
> This memorandum from the Nuclear Planetary Group (Staff Group) notifies members of an upcoming meeting on Tuesday, March 24, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the document are to coordinate discussion on two main subjects: matters arising from the nuclear briefing for the Military Committee scheduled for the previous day, Monday, March 23, and an initial exchange of ideas regarding recently arrived LD studies. The document is signed by Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANETARY GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
MEMORANDUM
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the staff group will take place on Tuesday, 24th March,
1970, at 3 p.m.

2. The principal subjects for discussion will be :
(a) matters arising from the nuclear briefing for the Military Committee to be
held on the previous day (Monday 23rd March, at 9.30 a.m. and 2.30 p.m.);

(b) initial exchange of ideas concerning the recently arrived LD studies.

(Signed) ... WILSON

NATO 1110 Brussels
