> # Reference List of Follow-On Use Work Programme Documents
> 
> This document, issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a comprehensive reference list of the principal documents relating to the follow-on use work programme that have been circulated to NPG members to date. The list is intended to supersede a previous list distributed in October 1972. The document is organized into two main sections: Annex A, which lists documents by reference number, and Annex B (though not provided in the first part of the text). Annex A is further divided into a Document Series and a Study Series, detailing the reference numbers, dates, and classifications of various reports and studies. These studies cover various strategic areas including North Norway, Macedonia, Western Thrace, NORTHAG, CENTAG, Turkish Thrace, North-Western Turkey, the Mediterranean Maritime Area, and the Denmark area, as well as the ACLANT area. It also includes reports on forces transition, structure, and deployment, and a study on the transition from conventional to nuclear posture. The list includes contributions and comments from organizations such as SHAPE, SACLAN, and the Military Committee.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The document is a reference list of documents relating to the 'follow-on use work programme', which includes specific studies on nuclear use in various regions (e.g., North Norway, Macedonia, Western Thrace, CENTAG, NORTHAG, Turkish Thrace, Mediterranean Maritime Area, ACLANT, and Denmark). It also explicitly mentions 'yield code' and 'Study of Transition from Conventional to Nuclear Posture', indicating detailed strike planning and war planning.
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document lists studies and reports on the nuclear posture and 'follow-on use' of nuclear weapons across various NATO member territories (European and maritime areas), which is the core of extended deterrence and tactical nuclear sharing in Europe.
