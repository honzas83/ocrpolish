> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1974 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, R.E. Shearer, proposing a draft advance press release for the upcoming sixteenth half-yearly Ministerial meeting of the NPG in Rome on November 7-8, 1974. The Chairman suggests that the press release be issued in Brussels on Monday, November 4, 1974, at 3 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is requested to discuss the draft text and timing of the release, and members are asked to obtain approval from their national authorities by October 18, 1974. The draft press release itself, provided in both English and French, identifies the participating nations as Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, the United Kingdom, the United States, Greece, Italy, and Portugal, and notes that the meeting will be chaired by NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE
AUTUMN 1974 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Rome on 7th and 8th
November, 1974. The text closely follows the language used in former press
announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. As on past occasions the
release might be made a few days before the meeting takes place; accordingly, it
is suggested that an announcement might be made in Brussels on Monday, 4th
November, 1974 at 3 p.m. local time, with simultaneous additional release in the
capitals of those participating countries who so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 30th September. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Rome meeting are requested to obtain, after their
initial discussion, the approval of their national authorities for the text of
the announcement and timing of the release, informing me no later than Friday,
18th October, 1974. Information on whether national authorities plan to make a
simultaneous release in the capital would also be appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Belgium, Denmark,
the Federal Republic of Germany, Greece, Italy, Portugal, the United Kingdom and
the United States, will, at the invitation of the Government of Italy, hold its
sixteenth half-yearly Ministerial meeting in Rome on 7th and 8th November, 1974,
under the chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. LUNS, Secretary General of NATO.

Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement la Belgique,
le Danemark, les Etats-Unis, la Grèce, l'Italie, le Portugal, la République
Fédérale d'Allemagne, le Royaume-Uni tiendra à l'invitation du Gouvernement de
l'Italie, sa 16ème réunion semestrielle au niveau ministériel les 7 et 8
novembre à Rome sous la présidence de M. Joseph Luns, Secrétaire Général de
l'OTAN.
