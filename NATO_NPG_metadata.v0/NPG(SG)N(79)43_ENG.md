> # List of Names
> 
> This document serves as a directory of personnel within the North Atlantic Council and associated nuclear planning directorates. It includes a list of names and titles for various officials, such as the Secretary General, Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning and Policy, and the Director for Nuclear Planning. The list also mentions roles within the NRC Press Service and the Nuclear Planning Directorate, and officials from the Department of Energy and Department of Defence. It provides a comprehensive overview of the personnel responsible for nuclear planning and security within the organization.
