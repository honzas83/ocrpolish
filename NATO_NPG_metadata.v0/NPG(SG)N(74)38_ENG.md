> [!abstract] 
> # Change In Time Of Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding a scheduling change for the next meeting. Due to a timing conflict with the EWG, the meeting scheduled for Monday, April 8, 1974, has been moved to 10 a.m. The Chairman, H.B. Seim, notes that the principal objects of the meeting will remain as previously set forth in the earlier notice NPG(Staff Group)N(74)37. The document was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CHRONO
75

5th April, 1974

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(74)38

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

CHANGE IN TIME OF NEXT MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Due to a timing conflict with the EWG, it is necessary to change the time of the
NPG Staff Group meeting on Monday, 8th April, 1974 to 10 a.m. The principal
objects of the meeting will be as set forth in my earlier Notice(1).

(Signed) H.B. SEIM

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

This document consists of one page.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)37, 3rd April, 1974
