> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, November 8, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss a draft report by the Nuclear Planning Group to the Nuclear Defence Advisory Committee (NDAC) for the December 8, 1971, ministerial meeting, and to review the draft agenda for that same meeting. Additionally, the meeting will address the substance of a draft report on the nuclear aspects of AD 70 follow-up action and review follow-on actions resulting from the Meeting of NPG Ministers held at the Palais d'Egmont on October 26 and 27, 1971. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

3rd November, 1971

NOTICE

NPG(Staff Group)N(71)123

135/11

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th November,
1971, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to discuss a draft(1) of a report by the NPG to the NDAC to be placed on the
Agenda for the meeting of NDAC Ministers to be held on 8th December, 1971;

(b) to discuss the draft(2) Agenda for the NDAC Ministerial Meeting on 8th
December, 1971, including the manner in which a report on the nuclear aspects of
AD 70 Follow-up action can best be submitted to Ministers;

(c) to discuss the substance of the draft report(3) on the nuclear aspects of AD
70 Follow-up action;

(d) to review follow-on action(4) resulting from the Meeting of NPG Ministers
held at the Palais d'Egmont on 26th and 27th October, 1971.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels
