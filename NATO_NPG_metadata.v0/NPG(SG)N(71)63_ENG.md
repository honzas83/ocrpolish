> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, June 14, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to conduct an initial discussion on the nuclear aspects of the follow-on work related to the AD 70s Study and to finalize arrangements for the Staff Group's visit to SHAPE on Wednesday, June 16, 1971, including the list of attendees. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 14th June, 1971,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be :

(a) to hold an initial discussion on the nuclear aspects of the follow-on work
on the AD 70s Study(1);

(b) to note final arrangements for the Staff Group visit to SHAPE on Wednesday,
16th June, 1971, and the names of those attending.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)60, dated 1st June, 1971
