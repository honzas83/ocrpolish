> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, June 5, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the revised draft Permanent Representatives report regarding follow-up actions from the Frederikshavn Ministerial Meeting. The notice also includes a provision for any other business. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from Brussels, Belgium.

(a) discussion of the revised draft Permanent Representatives report on follow-
