> [!abstract] 
> # Bus Transportation Between NATO Headquarters and Melsbroek
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO). It outlines the arrangements for bus transportation between NATO Headquarters in Brussels and Melsbroek. The service is available to members of the International Staff, Delegations, and Military Staffs, as well as their wives. Those wishing to use the service are instructed to assemble at the NATO Press Entrance with their luggage at 1:45 p.m. on Monday, June 2, 1980. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

Arrangements have been made for NATO buses to take members of the International
Staff, Delegations and Military Staffs and their wives from the NATO

2. Persons wishing to avail themselves of this service should assemble at the
