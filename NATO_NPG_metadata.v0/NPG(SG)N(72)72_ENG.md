> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Thursday, September 7, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to review the revised version of the draft report on the Denmark Study and to examine the International Staff's first draft of a possible report on the SACLANT Illustrative Study on Initial Use at Sea. The notice is signed by M.H.J. Wilson. It references two working papers: NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)12(Revised) and NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)16.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 7th September,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to review the revised version of the draft report on the Denmark Study(1):
(b) to examine the International Staff's first draft of a possible report on the
SACLANT Illustrative Study on Initial Use at Sea(2).

(Signed) M.H.J. WILSON
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)12(Revised), 6th September, 1972
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)16, 4th September, 1972
