> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, February 10, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary agenda items for discussion include the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, the AD: Time Factors paper, and Consultation. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium, under the NATO framework. It also references a previous document, NPG/D(68)12, dated December 20, 1968, though the text contains a typo stating 1969.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
As agreed on 3rd February, the next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on
Monday, 10th February, 1969, at 2.45 p.m. The principal matters to be discussed
will be the rôle of theatre nuclear strike forces, the AD: Time Factors paper
(1), and Consultation.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
OTAN/NATO
