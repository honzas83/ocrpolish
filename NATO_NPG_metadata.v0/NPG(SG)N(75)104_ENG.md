> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 9th December 1975. It states that no further meetings of the Staff Group are scheduled for the remainder of December 1975. The next meeting is scheduled for Monday, 5th January 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of this meeting will be to discuss preparations for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting in Hamburg.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
9th December, 1975
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(75)104
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
No meetings of the Staff Group are foreseen during the remainder of this month.
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th January,
1976, at 3 p.m. The principal matter to be discussed will be the preparations
for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Hamburg.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1910 Brussels.
