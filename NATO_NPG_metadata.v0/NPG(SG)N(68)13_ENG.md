> # ADM Time Factors
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)N(68)13, provides a time flow chart as an annex to a note by Chairman R.G. Dreitner. The chart outlines the process for the selective release of ADM (Atomic Demolition Munitions) Atomic Demolition Munitions, detailing four distinct phases of communication and decision-making. Phase I involves the request from the Corps to the Army Group and the transmission of the SU-1 message to the SHOC (Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe) Operations Center.promises. Phase II covers internal message processing at SHOC, staffing by the NCC (National Command Center), and the SACEUR (Supreme Allied Commander Europe) decision briefing.
