> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1973 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group has prepared a draft advance press release, available in English and French, concerning the thirteenth half-yearly Ministerial meeting scheduled for May 15th and 16th, 1973, in Ankara, Turkey. The meeting will be held under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph Luns. The Staff Group proposes that the press release be issued in Brussels on Tuesday, May 8th, 1973, at 3 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries at their discretion. Staff Group members from participating countries—Canada, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States—are requested to seek approval from their national authorities for the draft text and the suggested timing. National authorities are also asked to provide information on whether simultaneous releases in their respective capitals will be planned. The document is signed by H.B. Seim and originated from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE SPRING 1973 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Ankara, Turkey on 15th and
16th May, 1973, which the Staff Group has agreed should now be sent to national
authorities of the countries concerned for their concurrence. The text closely
follows the language used in press announcements issued during the past three
years just prior to Ministerial meetings. The Staff Group proposes that the
attached press release be made in Brussels on Tuesday, 8th May, 1973 at 3 p.m.
local time, with simultaneous additional release in the capitals of
participating countries at their discretion.

2. Staff Group members of countries that will be represented at the Ankara
meeting are requested to seek the approval of their national authorities for the
draft text and the suggested release timing, and to inform the International
Staff by Thursday, 26th April, 1973. Information on whether or not it is planned
to make a simultaneous release in the capitals would also be appreciated.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
As arranged at its previous meeting in London on October, 1972, the Nuclear
Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Canada, Germany, Italy, the
Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at
the invitation of the Government of Turkey, hold its thirteenth half-yearly
Ministerial meeting in Ankara, Turkey on 15th and 16th May, 1973, under the
chairmanship of Mr. Joseph Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

----------------
Comme convenu lors de la précédente réunion à Londres au mois d'octobre 1972, le
Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement le Canada, les
États-Unis, l'Italie, la Norvège, les Pays-Bas, la République Fédérale
d'Allemagne, le Royaume-Uni et la Turquie, tiendra à l'invitation du
Gouvernement turque, sa 13ème réunion semestrielle au niveau ministériel les 15
et 16 mai à Ankara sous la présidence de M. Joseph Luns, Secrétaire Général de
l'OTAN.
