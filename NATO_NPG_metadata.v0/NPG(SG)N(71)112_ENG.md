> # SHAPE Preliminary Review of CENTAG Area Follow-on Use Study
> 
> This document is a note from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated October 12, 1971, regarding the SHAPE Preliminary Review of the CENTAG Area Follow-on Use Study. The Chairman, H.B. Seim, informs the Staff Group members that comments from the Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE) on the CENTAG Follow-on Use Study have been received and distributed to all NPG delegations. He notes that NPG Ambassadors will have the opportunity to comment on the SHAPE paper at the NPG Permanent Representatives' meeting on Thursday, October 14, 1971, under Agenda Item II. Furthermore, it is intended that the Staff Group will meet on Monday, October 18, 1971, to address any issues arising from the discussions at the Permanent Representatives' session. The document originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
