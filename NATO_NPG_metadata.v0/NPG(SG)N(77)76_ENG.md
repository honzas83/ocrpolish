> [!abstract] 
> # NPG Ministerial Meetings
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) within the North Atlantic Council, Atlantic Council, is a notice providing reference information for Staff Group members. It contains a comprehensive list of all 22 Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meetings held from Spring 1967 to Autumn 1977, detailing the host city and country for each meeting. Additionally, it includes a note on the number of times each participating country has acted as host, hosting statistics for the Federal Republic of Germany, United Kingdom, United States, Italy, Netherlands, Turkey, and others. The document also features a a diagram showing the participation of permanent members (Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, United Kingdom, and United States) and rotating members (Canada, Netherlands, Norway, Belgium, Belgium, Denmark, Greece, and Turkey) for the period 1977-1980 based on the agreement on NPG membership. It references the agreements MDAC/D(59)4 and NDAC/D(69)4.

For easy reference by Staff Group members, I attach
