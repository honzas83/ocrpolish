> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary regarding the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of the North Atlantic Council. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, July 31, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary agenda items for the meeting include the consideration of a draft Permanent Representatives' report on short-term measures and any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
