> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1978 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated 7 March 1978, regarding a proposed advance press release for the upcoming 23rd NPG Ministerial Meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place in Frederikshavn, Denmark, on 18 and 19 April 1978, under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns. The Chairman suggests that the announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, 14 April 1978, at 4:00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is requested to discuss the draft text and timing of the release at its meeting on 20 March, and members are asked to obtain national authority approval of the text and timing by 31 March 1978. The attached draft press release, in both English and French, lists the participating ministers of defence from Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, the United Kingdom, the United States, Italy, and Turkey.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE SPRING 1978 MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text, in English and French, of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Frederikshavn, Denmark, on
18th and 19th April, 1978. The text closely follows the language used in former
press announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. As on past occasions, the
release could be made a few days before the meeting takes place; accordingly, it
is suggested that an announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, 14th April,
1978, at 4.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous additional release in the
capitals of those participating countries which so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 20th March. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Ministerial meeting are requested after their
initial discussion to obtain their national authorities' approval of the text of
the announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director, Nuclear
Planning no later than Friday, 31st March, 1978. Information on whether national
authorities plan to make a simultaneous release in their capitals would also be
appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

ATTACHMENT to NPG(Staff Group)N(78)17
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the
United Kingdom and the United States will hold its twenty-third Ministerial
Meeting in Frederikshavn, Denmark, on 18th and 19th April, 1978, under the
chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

PROJET DE COMMUNIQUE ANNONCANT LA REUNION
Le Groupe des plans nucleaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
de la Defense de la Belgique, du Danemark, des Etats-Unis, de l'Italie, de la
Republique Federale d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie tiendra sa
vingt-troisieme reunion au niveau ministerielles les 18 et 19 avril 1978 a
Frederikshavn, Danemark, sous la presidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secreteur
General de l'OTAN.
