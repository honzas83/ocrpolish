> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, May 3, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting, discuss the future Nuclear Planning Group work programme, and continue discussions regarding the presentation of newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
27th April, 1976
NOTICE NPG(Staff Group)N(76)36
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 3rd May, 1976, at
3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(b) to discuss the future NPG work programme;
(c) to continue the discussion on the presentation of Newly Developed Procedures
in the Field of Nuclear Consultation(1);

(d) to discuss any other matters.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)2, 30th March, 1976
