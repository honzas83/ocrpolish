> [!abstract] 
> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, June 7, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the results of the Ministerial Meeting at Mittenwald and subsequent actions, hold an initial discussion on the nuclear aspects of the follow-on work regarding the AD 70s Study, and to note the arrangements for the Staff Group's visit to SHAPE on Wednesday, June 16, 1971. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE NPG(Staff Group)N(71)59
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 7th June, 1971,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be :

(a) to discuss the results of the Ministerial Meeting at Mittenwald and future
actions resulting therefrom;

(b) to hold an initial discussion on the nuclear aspects of the follow-on work
on the AD 70s Study;

(c) to note arrangements for the Staff Group visit to SHAPE on Wednesday, 16th
June.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO 1110 Brussels
