> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 21st March, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on March 21, 1977. The meeting covered several key agenda items, including a briefing by Mr. P. Osborn, a United States expert on nuclear matters, regarding nuclear operations against rear areas. The group also discussed nuclear consultation in the context of WINTEX 77 and requested the International Staff to prepare a draft paper for a subsequent meeting on March 30. Additionally, the group was advised to read a paper on SAIP prepared by the United States authorities, referenced as JENATO-CTS-77-2. The meeting also confirmed that a two-day meeting of the Full Study Panel on IEV technology study would begin on March 28, 1977. Finally, the group agreed to postpone its next meeting until March 30, 1977, at 3:00 p.m.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 21st
March, 1977

SECURITY

1. The STAFF GROUP: took note that all persons present were cleared to the
security classification of the matters to be discussed.

NUCLEAR OPERATIONS AGAINST REAR AREAS

2. The STAFF GROUP: (a) concurred with the welcome extended by the Chairman to
Mr. P. Osborn, an expert on nuclear matters from the United States; (b) noted
with great interest a briefing given by Mr. Osborn, and his replies to questions
posed during the ensuing general discussion; (c) warmly supported the thanks
extended to Mr. Osborn by the Chairman.

WINTEX 77

3. The STAFF GROUP: (a) took note of the remarks by the Chairman concerning
nuclear consultation in WINTEX 77; (b) noted the views expressed in the ensuing
discussion; (c) invited the International Staff to prepare, in the light of the
suggestion made, a draft paper in time for discussion on Wednesday, 30th March.

4. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of the remarks of the Chairman who strongly recommended that all

referenced- JENATO-CTS-77-2 (COSMIC TOP SECRET).

NPG FULL STUDY PANEL MEETING – IEV TECHNOLOGY STUDY
5. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of the remarks of the US Representative who confirmed that the two-day
meeting of the Full Study Panel would begin at 10.00 a.m. on Monday, 28th March,
1977 in the NATO Headquarters.

NEXT MEETING
6. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed, in view of the Full Study Panel meeting on 28th March, to postpone its
next meeting until Wednesday, 30th March, 1977, at 3.00 p.m.
