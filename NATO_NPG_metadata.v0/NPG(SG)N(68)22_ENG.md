> [!abstract] 
> # List of Abbreviations
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a comprehensive list of abbreviations to be used during a SHAPE presentation scheduled for December 3, 1968, at 9:45 a.m. The list includes technical and operational terms related to nuclear planning and missile systems, such as Circular Error of Probability (CEP), Inter Continental Ballistic Missile (ICBM), and various levels of Quick Reaction Alert (QRA). The document also provides French translations for these abbreviations, such as 'Ecart circulaire probable' for CEP and 'Missile balistique inter-continental' for ICBM. Additional copies of the list will be available at the Documents Counter in the Conference Hall on the day of the presentation.
>
