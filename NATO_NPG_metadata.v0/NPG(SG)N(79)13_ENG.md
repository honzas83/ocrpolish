> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, March 26, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary agenda item is the discussion of a draft report by Permanent Representatives regarding the Study of the Politico-Strategic Aspects of Maritime Nuclear Warfare in the NATO Area. The Secretary emphasizes the urgency of the matter, noting that a 'white' working paper must be produced at the meeting. Additionally, the meeting will include a farewell ceremony in the Chairman's office for Mr. Tugay Özceri, accompanied by refreshments. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 26th March, 1979
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on the Study of
the Politico-Strategic Aspects of Maritime Nuclear Warfare in the NATO Area(1);
(The urgency of the matter is such that we must produce a "white" working paper
at this meeting.)

(b) any other business.
2. After the meeting, there will be a farewell ceremony in the Chairman's
office, with appropriate refreshments, in honour of Mr. Tugay Özceri.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
