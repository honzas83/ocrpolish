> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, May 22, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to conduct follow-up work regarding the Frederikshavn Ministerial Meeting and to address any other business. Additionally, the notice mentions a farewell ceremony for Captain George Helm, which is to take place at 5 p.m. in the Chairman's office with refreshments. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and references the document NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)4(Revised).

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP(STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 22nd May, 1978 at
3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) follow-up work to the Frederikshavn Ministerial Meeting(1);

(b) any other business.

2. After the meeting, there will be a farewell ceremony at 5 p.m. in the
Chairman's office, with appropriate refreshments, to mark the departure of
Captain George Helm.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)4(Revised) – to be circulated.
