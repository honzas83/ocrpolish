> # Notes of a Meeting Held on Monday, 18th January, 1971
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on January 18, 1971, to address several agenda items. The group noted the arrangements for a farewell luncheon for Mr. A.W.G. Le Hardy and discussed the NATO Senior Officers' Defence Weapons Orientation Course at Oberammerau. Regarding Exercise WINTEX 71, the group planned a series of briefings on the exercise's setting and Selective Release Procedures. The meeting also covered the circulation of the Supreme Allied Commander Europe's (SACEUR) General Strike Plan briefing and staff support for military observers attending Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) and Nuclear Defence Advisory Committee (NDAC) ministerial meetings. Additionally, the group agreed to consider the size of delegations at NPG ministerial meetings at a future date and discussed proposed briefings for the Defence Planning Committee and NPG Ambassadors. A visit to SHAPE was proposed for the third week of March. The group also reviewed progress on tactical nuclear weapon studies, including the ACLANT Study involving the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, Canada, and Norway, and the Mediterranean and Transition studies led by Italy. Finally, the group discussed national participation in military nuclear planning and scheduled their next meeting for January 25, 1971.
