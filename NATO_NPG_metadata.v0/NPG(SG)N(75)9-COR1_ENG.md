> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(75)9, dated 22nd January, 1975
> 
> This document is a corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(75)9, dated 22nd January, 1975, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of the North Atlantic Council. It requests holders of the previous notice to delete paragraph 10 on pages 5 and 6 and replace it with a new section detailing specific technological improvements. These improvements include Rap-artillery, homing missiles, extended range missiles, and multiple/media launching capabilities. Additionally, it covers improvements that reduce the vulnerability of tactical forces and systems, such as tactical mobility and equipment survivability. It also addresses improvements in command and control, specifically tactical satellite communications, and non-nuclear precision-guided munitions like scatterable mines and guided field artillery projectiles. The document further notes that the technology study group will need to obtain additional technological information to meet its requirements. Finally, it instructs that existing paragraphs 11, 12, and 13 on pages 6 and 7 be re-numbered to 10, 11, and 12.
