> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document serves as a notice for the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of the North Atlantic Council, scheduled for Friday, May 2, 1975, at 10 a.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the revised version of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on the phase II report, specifically referencing NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)5(Revised). The document also includes a comprehensive list of members of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), categorized by delegations from Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Germany, Italy, Turkey, United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Greece, Norway, and the Netherlands. It further lists NATO military authorities from the International Military Staff (IMS), Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE), and the Supreme Allied Command Atlantic (SACLANT), and members of the International Staff, including the Chairman, Vice Chairmen, and Secretary.
