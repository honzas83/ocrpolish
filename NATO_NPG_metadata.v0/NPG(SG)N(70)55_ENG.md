> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated June 25, 1970. It announces the cancellation of the meeting originally scheduled for June 29, 1970, and reschedules the next meeting for Monday, July 6, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the first draft of the Special ADM Guidelines, which were prepared by the International Staff in response to a request from the Staff Group during their meeting on June 12, 1970. The document references the draft guidelines in document J70(Staff Group)P(70)3.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
It has been found necessary to cancel the meeting of the Staff Group scheduled
for Monday, 29th June. The next meeting of the Staff Group will now take place
on Monday, 6th July, 1970 at 3 p.m.

The principal object of the meeting is unchanged and will be to discuss the
first draft(1) of the Special ADM Guidelines, prepared by the International
Staff in response to a request made by the Staff Group at their meeting, held on
12th June, 1970.

(Signed) M. G. MILSON
(1) J70(Staff Group)P(70)3 - to be circulated
