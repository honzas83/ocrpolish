> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, April 25, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting scheduled for June, examine an International Staff paper regarding the New Technology Study, and review the status of the Draft Press Release. Additionally, the meeting will address any other business. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th April, 1977
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be :

(a) to continue the discussion of the draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial
meeting in June (1);

(b) to examine an International Staff paper on the New Technology Study (2);
(c) to note the situation regarding the Draft Press Release (3);
(d) to address any other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels

(2) to be circulated
(3) NPG(Staff Group)N(77)25, 24th March, 1977
