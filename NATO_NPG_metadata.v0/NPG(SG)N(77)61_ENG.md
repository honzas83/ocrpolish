> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Tuesday, August 23, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study, to hold a preliminary discussion on a draft agenda for the next meeting of NPG Ministers, and to consider any other business. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references two other documents: the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study (NPG/Study/50) and a draft agenda (NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)9).

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

17th August, 1977

NOTICE NPG(Staff Group)N(77)61

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 23rd August,
1977 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study(1);

(b) to hold a preliminary discussion of a draft(2) of a possible agenda for the
next meeting of NPG Ministers;

(c) to consider any other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

[horizontal line]

(1) NPG/Study/50, dated 1st August, 1977
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)9, dated 17th August, 1977
