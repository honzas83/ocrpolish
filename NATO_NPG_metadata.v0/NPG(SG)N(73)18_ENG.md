> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, March 5, 1973, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The principal matters for discussion include the finalization of the report on the ACLANT Study, the draft agenda for the Ankara Meeting, a draft status report on the Follow-on Use work programme, and a draft report on the Illustrative Study on demonstrative use. The notice references several working papers and studies, including NP(Staff Group)WP(73)1, NP(Staff Group)WP(73)2, NP(Staff Group)WP(73)3, NP(Staff Group)WP(73)4, and NP/Study(71)4. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th March, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal matters for discussion will be:

(a) Finalization of the report(1) on the ACLANT Study;
(b) the draft Agenda(2) for the Ankara Meeting;
(c) a draft status report(3) on the Follow-on Use work programme;
(d) a draft report(4) on the Illustrative Study on demonstrative use(5).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
