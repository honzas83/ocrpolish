> # Video-Cassette "NATO and Warsaw Pact Comparison of Forces"
> 
> On 25th November 1982, J.D. Martin, Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), issued a notice inviting members of the NPG Staff Group and the Defence Review Committee to a screening of a video-cassette titled "NATO and Warsaw Pact Comparison of Forces". The video was produced by the NATO Information Directorate. The screening is scheduled for 7th December 1982 at 9:30 a.m. in Studio Ismay in Brussels, Belgium, to allow for comments from the invited members.

the agis of the NATO Information Directorate. The screening has been arranged to
