> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, January 23, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft agenda for the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting and to handle other preparations for the same event. Additionally, the meeting will include any other business. Following the meeting, a farewell ceremony for Mr. Maurice Wilson will be held at 5 p.m. in the Chairman's office, with refreshments provided. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 23rd January,
1978 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) the draft Agenda for the Spring, 1978, NPG Ministerial Meeting(1);
(b) other preparations for the Spring 1978 Meeting;
(c) any other business.

2. After the meeting, there will be a farewell ceremony at 5 p.m. in the
Chairman's office, with appropriate refreshments, to mark the departure of Mr.
Maurice Wilson.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
