> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, September 9, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting and to continue discussions on the CNI Study and the draft Permanent Representatives' report on it. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references two working papers: NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7(2nd Revise) and NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)10(Revised).

5th September, 1974
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(74)87
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 9th September,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial meeting;
(b) to continue discussion of the CNI Study(1), and of the draft Permanent
Representatives' report(2) on it.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7(2nd Revise), 28th July, 1974
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)10(Revised), 13th August, 1974
