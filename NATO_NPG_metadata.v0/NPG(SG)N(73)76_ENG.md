> # Notice NGP(Staff Group)N(73)76: Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a notice for its next meeting scheduled for Monday, August 20, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the third revision of a draft report by NGP Permanent Representatives on Consultation Procedures and Facilities, and to consider the first draft of a possible report by NGP Permanent Representatives on the SHAPE report on Early Combined Use and related Military Committee comments. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references several working papers and reports, including NGP(Staff Group)WP(73)7, NGP(Staff Group)WP(73)9, and NGP/D(73)8.

NOTICE NGP(Staff Group)N(73)76
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 20th August, 1973
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the third revision of a draft report by NGP Permanent
Representatives on Consultation Procedures and Facilities(1);

(b) to consider the first draft of a possible report by NGP Permanent
Representatives (2) on the SHAPE report on Early Combined Use and related
Military Committee comments(3).

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
1110 Brussels.
