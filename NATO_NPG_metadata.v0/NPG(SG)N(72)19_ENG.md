> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, February 21, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to briefly review the Nuclear Planning Group organization and procedures, continue discussions regarding the initiation of Phase II of the Follow-on Use Programme, and review an initial draft of a Permanent Representatives report on the Turkish Follow-on Use Study. The notice references several documents, including NPG(Staff Group)N(72)18, NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)1(3rd Revision), and NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)2.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions the 'Follow-on Use Programme' and the 'Turkish Follow-on Use Study', which relate to the deployment and sharing of nuclear weapons on allied territory (specifically Turkey) and the mechanisms for their use.

NPG(Staff Group)N(72)19
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 21st February,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal objects will be to review briefly NPG organization
and procedures(1); to continue the discussion of the initiation of Phase II of
the Follow-on Use Programme, taking into account the revised paper(2) prepared
by the International Staff; and an initial draft of a Permanent Representatives
report on the Turkish Follow-on Use Study(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(72)18, 15th February, 1972
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)1(3rd Revision) – to be circulated
(3) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)2 – to be circulated
