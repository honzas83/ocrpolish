> # Notes of a Meeting Held at NATO Headquarters on Wednesday, 23rd April, and on Thursday, 24th April, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held meetings on April 23 and 24, 1969, at NATO Headquarters. The primary focus of the first agenda item was the role of nuclear strike forces, where the group discussed a reference paper and its addendum and agreed upon a revised version for circulation by the International Staff. Additionally, the group scheduled three upcoming meetings for Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives: one on April 30 to discuss the initial use of nuclear weapons by the Warsaw Pact and the Ministerial meeting agenda, and a second on May 6 to discuss the role of nuclear strike forces. A third meeting was scheduled for May 13 to discuss a paper on tactical guidelines provided by Germany and the United Kingdom. No date was fixed for the next meeting of the Staff Group itself.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at NATO Headquarters on Wednesday, 23rd April, and on

THE ROLE OF NUCLEAR STRIKE FORCES
References: NPG(Staff Group)WP(69)6, dated 18th April, 1969, and its addendum,
dated 21st April, 1969

1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) discussed in detail the reference paper and its addendum;
(b) agreed upon a revised version in the light of the views expressed and
invited the International Staff to circulate it as soon as possible.

FORTHCOMING MEETINGS OF NFG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) recalled that the next meeting of NIG Permanent Representatives was
scheduled for Wednesday, 30th April, at 4.0 p.m., and that the principal items
for discussion would be Initial Use by the Warsaw Pact(1), and the Agenda for
the Ministerial meeting;

(b) noted that Tuesday, 6th May, at 4.0 p.m., appeared to be the next possible
date for a second meeting of the NFG Permanent Representatives, and agreed that
the role of nuclear strike forces should be discussed at this meeting;

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WF(69)2(3rd revise), dated 15th april, 1969
