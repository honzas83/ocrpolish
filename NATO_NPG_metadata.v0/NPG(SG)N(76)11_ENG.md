> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 9, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to conduct an initial examination of the International Staff's Working Paper on CNI procedures, taking into account previous papers on the matter. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)1, DPP/NPD/75/59, and NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)7(Revised).

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 9th February,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal object will be to undertake an initial examination
of the International Staff's Working Paper(1) on CNI procedures, taking into
account previous papers on this matter(2)(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
