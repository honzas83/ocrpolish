> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, June 24, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting and to discuss an initial draft of a study on communicating NATO's intentions to an enemy, other countries, and the public. Additionally, the notice mentions an informal reunion in Room I-534 at 5.30 p.m. to bid farewell to Dr. Rolf Hofstetter. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 24th June, 1974
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974
NPG Ministerial Meeting(1);
(b) to discuss an initial draft of a study on
Communicating NATO's Intentions to an enemy, to
other countries and to the public, and on the
related procedural matters(2).
2. Members are reminded that after the meeting there
will be an informal reunion in Room I-534 at 5.30 p.m. to bid
farewell to Dr. Rolf Hofstetter.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)68, 19th June, 1974
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7, 20th June, 1974
