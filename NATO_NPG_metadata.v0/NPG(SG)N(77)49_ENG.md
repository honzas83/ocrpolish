> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Friday, 17th June, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on June 17, 1977, to address several key items. The group carried out a detailed review of the Agreed Minute of the Ottawa Ministerial Meeting, focusing on procedures for conducting tasks, the formal tasking of NATO military authorities, the need for a meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives to prepare for the upcoming Autumn Ministerial Meeting, and the agenda for that meeting. Additionally, the Staff Group requested the Vice-Chairman to circulate a note with specific recommendations regarding the procedures for the tasks mentioned in the Agreed Minute. The group also requested delegations to propose that the Autumn Ministerial Meeting be held on October 13 and 14, 1977. Finally, the meeting notes record that the next meeting is scheduled for July 4, 1977, in Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Friday, 17th
June, 1977 at 10.30 a.m.

I. FOLLOW-UP TO THE OTTAWA MINISTERIAL MEETING

The STAF GROUP:

(1) carried out a detailed review of the Agreed Minute(1) of the Ottawa
Ministerial Meeting, with particular reference to:

(a) the procedures to be allowed for conducting the various tasks referred to;

(b) the need for formal tasking of the NATO military authorities in this
respect;

(c) the requirement for a meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives in the near
future to prepare the Autumn Ministerial Meeting;

(d) the agenda for the Autumn Ministerial Meeting;

(2) requested the Vice-Chairman to circulate a note, in the light of the
discussion, making specific recommendations with regard to the procedures to be
followed in respect of each of the tasks referred to in the Agreed Minute.
