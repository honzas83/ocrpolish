> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, announcing that the next meeting will take place on Wednesday, April 23, 1969, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the role of theatre nuclear strike forces and the agenda for the Ministerial meeting scheduled for May. The notice references several supporting documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(69)6 and NPG/N(69)1. The document was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from the NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group will take place on
Wednesday, 23rd April, 1969 at 3 p.m. The principal items for discussion will
be:

(a) The rôle of theatre nuclear strike forces(1)
(b) The Agenda for the Ministerial meeting in May(2)
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
OTAN/NATO,
Brussels, 39.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(69)6, dated 18th April, 1969 and
its addendum, dated 21st April, 1969.
(2) NPG/N(69)1, dated 3rd March, 1969.
