> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, 15th December 1969, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary subject of discussion for this meeting will be further studies on follow-on tactical use. The notice references the document NPG(Staff Group)N(69)87(Revised), dated 12th December 1969, as a basis for the discussion.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document explicitly mentions that the principal subject of discussion for the upcoming meeting will be 'further studies on follow-on tactical use', which refers to the tactical employment of nuclear weapons within the NATO alliance.
