> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, acting on behalf of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), announcing a meeting scheduled for Tuesday, July 6, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to examine the draft terms of reference for studies aimed at improving the effectiveness of NATO's TNF. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It also references an addendum to NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)5, dated June 24, 1976.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 6th July, 1976,
