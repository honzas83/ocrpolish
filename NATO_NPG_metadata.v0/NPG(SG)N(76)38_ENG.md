> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, May 10, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting and to further discuss the presentation of newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation. Additionally, the meeting will address any other matters as required. The notice refers to document NPG(Staff Group)P(76)2 dated March 30, 1976, and was issued from Brussels, Belgium.
>

> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions the 'Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)' and the discussion of 'Newly Developed Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation', which directly relates to the mechanisms for sharing nuclear responsibilities and consultation on nuclear use in Europe.

NPG(Staff Group)N(76)38
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 10th May, 1976,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(b) to continue the discussion on the presentation of Newly Developed Procedures
in the Field of Nuclear Consultation(1);

(c) to discuss any other matters.
(Signed) M.H.G. 'ILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)P(76)2, 30th March, 1976
