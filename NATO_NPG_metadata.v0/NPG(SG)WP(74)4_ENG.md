> # Draft Ministerial Agenda
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting scheduled for June 11th and 12th, 1974, in Bergen, Norway. The agenda includes briefings on the balance of strategic forces and United States research and development regarding tactical nuclear weapons, both led by the United States. It also features a statement by the Norwegian Minister of Defence on alliance nuclear issues and a discussion on Warsaw Pact politico-military strategy and military doctrine for the tactical use of nuclear weapons, led by the Netherlands. Additionally, the agenda covers the function of the Military Committee in the nuclear consultation process and the future work programme, concluding with any other business, an agreed minute, and a communiqué. The draft was circulated by Chairman H.B. Seim to provide a basis for Staff Group discussion on March 25th, 1974.
>
