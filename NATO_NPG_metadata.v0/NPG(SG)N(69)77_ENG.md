> # Notes of the Meeting Held at NATO Headquarters on Monday, 20th October and Tuesday, 21st October, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held meetings on October 20 and 21, 1969, at NATO Headquarters. The group discussed and amended a draft advance press release for a ministerial meeting scheduled for November 11 and 12, and agreed to its revised wording and publication timing, pending confirmation from the United States Delegation. Additionally, the group noted the circulation of a United States and German document regarding the role of theatre strike forces, which was to be discussed by MFG Permanent Representatives on October 27. Some representatives proposed transmitting the Cosmic Top Secret document via NATO-WIDE to countries that might struggle to send it to their capitals in time for the comments deadline. The International Staff was invited to take the necessary action for this transmission. Furthermore, the group discussed a revised working paper on the draft work programme for further studies on the follow-on tactical use of nuclear weapons. The International Staff was tasked with preparing a new Nuclear Planning Group working paper for discussion by the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent representatives on October 27, 1969, and to arrange for its transmission via NATO-WIDE to all relevant countries. No date was fixed for the next meeting of the Staff Group.
