> # Nike-Hercules Study
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a notice on March 2, 1977, regarding the distribution of the SHAPE Nike Hercules Study. The document states that a limited number of copies of the study were distributed on a preliminary basis to the registries of delegations. It further clarifies that the advance distribution to the Staff Group was arranged with the Military Committee on the condition that Permanent Representatives' consideration of the study be withheld until the Military Committee's views are received. Additionally, the document requests that all unneeded copies of the study be returned to the International Staff Atomal Registry and emphasizes that under no circumstances should these documents be destroyed.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Military-Operations-and-Capabilities/Air-and-Missile-Defense — The document explicitly references the 'NIKE-HERCULES STUDY', referring to the Nike Hercules, which was a surface-to-air missile system used for air and missile defense.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NIKE-HERCULES STUDY
Note by the Secretary

Staff Group members are reminded that as of 2nd March, 1977, a limited number of
copies of the SHAPE NIKE HERCULES Study(1) were distributed, on a preliminary
basis, to registries of delegations.

2. Advance distribution to the Staff Group was arranged with the Military
Committee on the understanding that Permanent Representatives' consideration of
this study would be withheld pending receipt of Military Committee views.

3. Recipients are requested to return to the International Staff Atomal Registry
all unneeded copies of this document. Under no circumstances should any of these
documents be destroyed.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

(1) SHAPE/ATOMAL/231/76, 18th February, 1977
