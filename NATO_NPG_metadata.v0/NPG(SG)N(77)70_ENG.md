> # 22nd Meeting of NPG Ministers - Transport of Classified Documents to Bari
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, providing logistical arrangements for the transport of classified documents to Bari for the 22nd meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) ministers. Mr. D. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section, is designated as the courier to carry the papers, departing on Monday, October 10, 1977, and returning on Friday, October 14, 1977. Delegations are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and submit their papers by midday Friday, October 7, 1977. The notice further specifies that papers should be packaged, clearly marked with the delegation or person's name and a serial number, and kept as light as possible to avoid burdening the courier with unclassified material.

22nd MEETING OF NPG MINISTERS - TRANSPORT OF
CLASSIFIED DOCUMENTS TO BARI

Note by the Secretary

Mr. D. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section (Tel. Ext.
2481), will carry classified papers for the Bari meeting, departing Monday, 10th
October, and will return with them on Friday, 14th October. Delegations wishing
to take advantage of this arrangement are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and
to send him their papers not later than closing time on midday Friday, 7th
October, 1977.

2. Papers should be packaged and clearly marked with the name of the delegation
or person and a serial number. Packages should be as light as possible. Please
do NOT burden the Courrier with unclassified material.

(Signed) N.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
