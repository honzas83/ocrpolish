> # Staff Group Meeting Minutes
> 
> The NATO Staff Group met to discuss and accept Danish proposals subject to some modifications. The International Staff was invited to prepare a revised version of the working paper for immediate distribution, which would be discussed by the MTG Permanent Representatives at their meeting on October 13, 1970. Additionally, the Staff Group agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, October 12, 1970, at 3 p.m. The agenda for the upcoming meeting includes arrangements for the Ottawa meeting, the results of the Permanent Representatives meeting held on October 6, 1970, regarding the AIM paper, and a final review of the Provisional Political Guidelines paper before its discussion by the Permanent Representatives the following day. The document also notes the location of the document's origin as Brussels, Belgium.

4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 12th October, 1970 at 3 p.m.

(iv) any unfinished business.
