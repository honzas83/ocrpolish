> [!abstract] 
> # Implementation of Special ADM Guidelines
> 
> This document, issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), outlines proposed modifications to the previously circulated Working Paper on Implementation of Special ADM Guidelines (NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)11). The Chairman, H.B. Seim, suggests these changes for discussion at the Staff Group's meeting on Monday, 28th August 1972. The modifications include revisions to paragraphs 3 and 4, as well as specific textual deletions and insertions in paragraphs 6, 7, 8, and 11(c). Specifically, paragraph 3 is updated to reflect that Permanent Representatives approved a document in September 1971 based on a Military Committee report, and that a further progress report from the Military Committee was received by 1st August 1972. The document also references several other reports and documents, including NPG/D(71)14, MCM-51-71, and MCM-53-72.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document discusses the 'Implementation of Special ADM Guidelines'. In the context of NATO nuclear planning, 'Special ADM' (Administrative) guidelines typically refer to the administrative and procedural arrangements for the storage, custody, and control of US nuclear weapons forward-deployed in Europe, which is a central component of Extended Deterrence and Tactical Nuclear Sharing.
