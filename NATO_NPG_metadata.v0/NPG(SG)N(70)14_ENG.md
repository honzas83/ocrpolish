> # Draft Agenda for the Meeting of Ministers of the Nuclear Planning Group
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for a meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group ministers to be held in Venice, Italy, on June 8th and 9th, Venice, 1970. The agenda items include discussions on the strategic use of nuclear weapons, specifically the role of theatre nuclear strike forces and a briefing on the strategic balance of forces, with Germany and the United States leading these discussions. The document also outlines the tactical use of nuclear weapons, including Atomic Demolition Munitions and follow-on tactical use studies, featuring reports from Germany, Turkey, and various military authorities. The agenda is accompanied by a keynote by the Secretary, H.H.G. Wilson, who proposes that the initial discussion of this agenda should take place at the meeting of the Staff Group on March 16th, 1970. The meeting is part of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group's coordination of nuclear strategy and planning.
