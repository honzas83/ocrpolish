> [!abstract] 
> # Field Trip to Germany on 15th and 16th November, 1978
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a field trip to the Federal Republic of Germany on November 15 and 16, 1978. The visit is conducted by invitation of the German and United States authorities. The notice specifies that organizational details regarding the trip will be provided to Staff Group members on a personal basis. The document is signed by R.E. Shearer and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
