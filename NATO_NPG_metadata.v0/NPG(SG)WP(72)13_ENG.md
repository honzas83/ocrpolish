> # Updating of NATO Nuclear Planning Directives
> 
> This working paper, authored by Chairman H.B. Seim, outlines a proposal to update NATO nuclear planning directives in response to the POSEIDON modernization programme. The document includes a draft report for the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee and the Defence Planning Committee, suggesting that only the Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in ACE requires updating. The review process considered several directives, including the Provisional Political Guidelines for the Initial Defensive Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons by NATO and the General Guidelines for Consultation Procedure on the Use of Nuclear Weapons. The Nuclear Planning Group concludes that the other directives remain appropriate and only the Concept Paper needs revision. The proposed changes are based on revisions suggested by the United States and contributions from SHAPE. The document recommends that the NPG Ministers approve the draft report and forward it to the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee for endorsement and onward transmittal to the Defence Planning Committee for approval.

UPDATING OF NATO NUCLEAR PLANNING DIRECTIVES

Draft Report by the Permanent Representatives

PURPOSE

This report submits for NPG Ministerial consideration a draft report for the
Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee and the Defence Planning Committee concerning
updating of relevant NATO nuclear planning directives in the light of the
POSEIDON modernization programme.

DISCUSSION

2. The Ministers will recall that the Ministers of the Defence Planning
Committee, during consideration of a report on United States Naval Forces
Improvements(1) in December 1972, noted that the Nuclear Planning Group might
wish to consider whether an updating of the relevant NATO nuclear planning
directives, in particular the Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike
Forces in ACE(2), would be appropriate as a consequence of the introduction of
the POSEIDON weapon system. In this context, a review of the following
directives has been completed:

(1) DFC/D(71)32(Revised), 30th November, 1971

(2) DFC/D(70)59, 10th December, 1970, hereafter referred to as the Concept Paper

UPDATING OF NATO NUCLEAR PLANNING DIRECTIVES
Draft Report by the Nuclear Planning Group

PURPOSE
This report summarizes the result of the Nuclear Planning Group’s review of
relevant NATO nuclear planning directives to see whether updating may be
appropriate as a consequence of the introduction of the POSEIDON weapon system.

DISCUSSION
2. During consideration of a report on United States Naval Forces
Improvements(1) at their meeting on 8th December, 1972, the Ministers of the
Defence Planning Committee noted that the Nuclear Planning Group might wish to
consider whether an updating of the relevant NATO nuclear planning directives,
in particular the Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in
ACE(2), would be appropriate in the light of the POSEIDON modernization
programme. In this context, a review of the relevant directives has been
completed by the Nuclear Planning Group.

CONCLUSION
3. The Nuclear Planning Group has concluded that only one of the directives,
namely, the Concept Paper(3), is in need of updating. Proposed changes to
accomplish this updating are at Annex.
