> [!abstract] 
> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(74)4
> 
> This document is a corrigendum issued by the North Atlantic Council and the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 23rd January, 1975. It serves to correct a typographical error on the cover page of Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(74)4, dated 13th January, 1975, which pertains to articles related to Nuclear Defence Policy. The corrigendum instructs holders of the notice to delete the reference 'NPG(Staff Group)N(74)4' and replace it with 'NPG(Staff Group)N(75)4'. The document was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
