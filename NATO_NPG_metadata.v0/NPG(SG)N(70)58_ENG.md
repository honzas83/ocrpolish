> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, July 15, 1970, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. In the absence of the Chairman, the meeting will be chaired by Mr. Braband. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss a revised draft of the Special ADM Guidelines, which was prepared by the International Staff in light of previous discussions held on July 6, 1970. The notice is signed by M.I.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th July, 1970
at 3 p.m. In the absence of the Chairman, the meeting will be chaired by Mr.
Braband. The principal object of the meeting will be to discuss a revised
draft(1) of the Special ADM Guidelines, prepared by the International Staff in
the light of the Staff Group discussion on 6th July, 1970.

(Signed) M.I.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
