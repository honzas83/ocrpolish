> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, June 29, 1970, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the first draft of the Special ADM Guidelines, which were prepared by the International Staff in response to a request made by the Staff Group during their meeting on June 12, 1970. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 29th June, 1970
at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting is to discuss the first draft(1)

to a request made by the Staff Group at their meeting held on 12th June, 1970.
