> # Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, September 22, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary agenda items for the meeting include further studies on the tactical use of nuclear weapons, referencing document NPG/D(69)5, and the determination of dates for the meetings of NPG Permanent Representatives. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and mentions that a paper by the International Staff will be circulated for the meeting.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 22nd September,
1969, at 2.45 p.m.

The principal matters to be discussed will be :
(a) Further Studies on the tactical use of nuclear weapons (vide NPG/D(69)5)
(1);

(b) Dates of the meetings of NPG Permanent Representatives.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

(1) paper by International Staff to be circulated
