> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in Denmark
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of NATO, lists the members of the International Staff delegation accompanying the Secretary General to the 23rd Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers in Denmark. The delegation includes high-level officials such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luis, Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning & Policy W.F. Munford, and Director of Nuclear Planning R.E. Shearer. Additionally, the technical and support staff, including interpreters, security personnel, and a cosmic control and courier, are listed. The note was signed by D.J. Roberts, Secretary of the NPG Staff Group, and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
