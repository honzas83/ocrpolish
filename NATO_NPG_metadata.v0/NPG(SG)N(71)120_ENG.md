> [!abstract] 
> # Telephone Communications During the 10th Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group in Permanent Session
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a detailed list of telephone extension numbers for the 10th Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group in Permanent Session. The meeting took place on October 26th and 27th, 1971, at the Palais d'Egmont in Brussels, Belgium. It includes the main telephone exchange number for the Palais d'Egmont (13.99.70) and specific extensions for the Office of the Secretary-General, the Office of the Assistant Secretary-General, the Office of the Executive Secretary, the Director of Nuclear Planning, and various national delegations including Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Greece, Italy, and the United Kingdom and United States. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson of NATO.
