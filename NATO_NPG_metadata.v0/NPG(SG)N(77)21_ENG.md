> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Friday, March 11, 1977, at 11 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the discussion regarding the draft Terms of Reference for the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references the document NPG(Staff Group)WF(77)2(2nd Revise), dated March 3, 1977.

> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The document explicitly mentions the 'Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study', which relates to the development of war plans and execution of nuclear operations at sea.
