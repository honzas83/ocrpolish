> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on February 14, 1973. The notice announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, February 19, 1973, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss matters concerning the upcoming Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting and the future activities of the Staff Group. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

# Page 1

14th February, 1973

NOTICE

NPG(Staff Group)N(73)13

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th February,
1973, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to discuss matters
concerning the next NPG Ministerial meeting and future activities of the Staff
Group.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
