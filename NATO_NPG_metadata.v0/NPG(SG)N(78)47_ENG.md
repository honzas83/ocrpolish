> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, July 3, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to further discuss the NATO Military Authorities' proposals for short-term measures to improve NATO's TNF. The agenda also includes any other business. The notice references several previous documents, including NPG/Study/52, NPG(Staff Group)N(78)40, and a letter from the United Kingdom Representative. The document was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 3rd July, 1978 at
3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) further discussion of the NATO Military Authorities' proposals for short-
term measures to improve NATO's TNF(1);

(b) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG/Study/52, 23rd March, 1978, NPG(Staff Group)N(78)40, 7th June, 1978 and
the United Kingdom Representative's letter of 9th June, 1978
