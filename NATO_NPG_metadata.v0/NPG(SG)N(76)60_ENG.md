> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, July 12, 1976, at 5 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to receive a briefing by SHAPE (Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe) on proposed changes to nuclear weapons release procedures and to prepare for the subsequent meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives scheduled for Wednesday, July 14, 1976. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It also references documents NPG/WP(76)5 and its addendum dated July 8, 1976.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 12th July, 1976
at 5 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hear a briefing by SHAPE on a proposed change to the nuclear weapons
release procedures;

(b) to prepare for the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives on Wednesday,
14th July, 1976(1).

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG/WP(76)5, dated 29th June, 1976 and Addendum dated 8th July, 1976
