> # Meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives
> 
> This document is a notice from the Vice-Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 9th February 1978. It refers to a Staff Group meeting held on 30th January 1978, where it was announced that no meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives could be scheduled before 5th April 1978. As no objections have been received, the Vice-Chairman, J.-P. Mentzel, confirms that a meeting on 5th April 1978 at 3.30 p.m. is considered acceptable to NPG Delegations. The agenda for this upcoming meeting will be be discussed at a future Staff Group meeting. The document was issued in Brussels, Belgium.

MEETING OF NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES
Note by the Vice-Chairman
At the Staff Group meeting on 30th January, 1978 it was announced that no NPG
Permanent Representatives' meeting could be scheduled before 5th April, 1978,
and therefore that this date should be considered for such a meeting.

2. Since no objections have been received so far, a meeting on 5th April, 1978
at 3.30 p.m. is considered acceptable to NPG Delegations.

3. The Agenda for the subject meeting will be discussed at one of the next Staff
Group meetings.

(Signed) J.-P. MENTZEL
1110 Brussels.
