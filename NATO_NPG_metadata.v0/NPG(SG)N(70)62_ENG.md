> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Thursday, September 3, 1970, at 3 p.m. in the Bradley Room, F Wing, First Level, with the permission of the United States Military Representative. The meeting's agenda includes reviewing a paper on Atomic Demolition Munitions in light of national views received, and determining the programme of meetings for the Staff Group and the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives in preparation for the meeting of Military Planning Group Ministers in Canada at the end of October. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Thursday, 3rd September,
1970, at 3 p.m., in the Bradley Room, F Wing, First Level, by the kind
permission of the United States Military Representative.

The main subjects for discussion will be:
(a) To review the paper on Atomic Demolition Munitions(1) in the light of
national views received;

(b) to consider what should be the programme of meetings of the Staff Group and
the NPG Permanent Representatives in preparation for the meeting of MPG
Ministers in Canada at the end of October.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(70)3(3rd revise), 24th July, 1970.
