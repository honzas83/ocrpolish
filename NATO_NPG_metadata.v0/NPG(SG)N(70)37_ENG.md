> # Draft Agenda for the Meeting of Ministers of the Nuclear Planning Group to be held in Venice on 8th and 9th June, 1970
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for a meeting of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) ministers in Venice, Italy, scheduled for June 8-9, 1970. The agenda items include discussions on the strategic use of nuclear weapons, specifically a briefing on the balance of strategic forces led by the United States and the role of theatre nuclear strike forces. The meeting will also cover the tactical use of nuclear weapons, including Atomic Demolition Munitions (ADMs) with reports from the Permanent Representatives, Germany, and the Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR/SHAPE), as well as follow-on tactical use studies with reports from Turkey and the Permanent Representatives. The document is a note by the Chairman, H. B. Seim, and mentions that the draft agenda will be circulated as an NPG Working Paper for approval by the Permanent Representatives on May 12, 1970.
>
