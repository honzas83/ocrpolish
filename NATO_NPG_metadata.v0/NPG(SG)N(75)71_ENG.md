> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Friday, September 19, 1975, at 9 a.m. The meeting's objectives are to consider the revised version of the Country West proper, to examine the United Kingdom's draft paper on C1 warning messages, and to further examine the paper on the compilation of nuclear constitution procedures. The notice was signed by N.P.C. Tilton and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several other documents, including NPG(Staff Group)NP(75)8 and a United Kingdom delegation paper.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 19th September,
1975, at 9 a.m. The objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to consider the revised version of the Country he west proper(1),
(b) to further examine the United Kingdom draft paper on C I warning
Messages(2).

(c) to further examine the paper on compilation(3) of nuclear constitution
procedures.

(Signed) N.P.C. TILTON
NATO, 110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)NP(75)8, 16th September, 1975
(2) U: Delegation paper NP 10/3 dated 2nd September, 1975, and revised
paragraphs 4, 5 and 6 (to be circulated)

(3) NPG(Staff Group)NP(75)7(revised), 4th September, 1975
