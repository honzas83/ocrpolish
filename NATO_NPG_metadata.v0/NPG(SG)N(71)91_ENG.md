> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, August 23, 1971, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to address unresolved problems related to tactical nuclear defence and to further discuss preparations for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting scheduled for October. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(71)91

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP(STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Monday, 23rd August, 1971,
at 3 p.m. The principal matters for attention will be:

(a) Unresolved problems related to tactical nuclear defence(1);

(b) Further discussion of preparations for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in
October.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)80(Revised), 17th August, 1971
