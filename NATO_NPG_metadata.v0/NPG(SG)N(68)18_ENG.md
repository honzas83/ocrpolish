> [!abstract] 
> # The Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in AOE
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Salty Group) and a draft of the terms of reference for a proposed SHAPE presentation. The presentation is intended to cover the role of theatre nuclear strike forces in the Atlantic Ocean Area of Europe (AOE), including the general role of these forces and the evolving role of weapons systems under revised strategy. The terms of reference detail the own relationship between ACE and Maternal forces, the role of AOE nuclear strike forces, and detailed technical issues regarding strike force planning, such as survivability, target coverage, and probability of successful strike. The Secretary, M. H.G. Wilson, specifies that the draft was prepared in consultation with Canada, the United States, the International Military Staff, and SHAPE. If no objections are received by November 5, 1968, the terms of reference will be communicated to SHAPE to schedule a presentation for late November 1968.
