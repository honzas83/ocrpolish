> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, January 18, 1971, at 3:00 p.m. The agenda for the meeting will include discussions on the question of notetakers and a further review of tasks for the Permanent Representatives and for the Staff Group during the coming months. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.
