> # Notice Regarding Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on 25th April 1969. The notice informs the members of the Staff Group that the Chairman has decided not to call a meeting for the scheduled week of 28th April to 2nd May, 1969. The communication is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NFG(Staff Group)N(69)40
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
I am directed by the Chairman to inform you that he does not intend to call a
meeting of the Staff Group during the week 28th April to 2nd May, 1969.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
