> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 27, 1972, at 11:00 a.m. and potentially 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to complete the examination and discussion of draft reports by Permanent Representatives on three specific topics: the Illustrative Study on Battlefield Use, Consultation Facilities and Procedures, and the Mediterranean Maritime Study. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several working papers, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)5(Revised), NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)7(Revised), and NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)8.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday 27th March, 1972,
at 11.0 a.m. and, if necessary, at 3.0 p.m. The principal object will be to
complete the examination and discussion of the draft reports by Permanent
Representatives on the Illustrative Study on Battlefield Use(1), Consultation
Facilities and Procedures(2) and the Mediterranean Maritime Study(3)

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
