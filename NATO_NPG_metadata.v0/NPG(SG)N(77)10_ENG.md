> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 14, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the draft Terms of Reference for the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study and to discuss participation in WINTEX 77 and the subsequent report to be prepared. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references previous documents NPC(Staff Group)WP(76)9 and NPG(Staff Group)N(77)11, as well as CMCN-6-77.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

3rd February, 1977

NOTICE
NFG(Staff Group)N(77)10

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 14th February,
1977 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of the draft Terms of
Reference for the Maritime Nuclear Warfare
Study(1)(2);

(b) to continue discussion of participation in
WINTEX 77, and of the subsequent report to be
prepared.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPC(Staff Group)WP(76)9, Annexes B and C, 6th December,1976
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(77)11, 2nd February, 1977, with CMCN-6-77
attached
