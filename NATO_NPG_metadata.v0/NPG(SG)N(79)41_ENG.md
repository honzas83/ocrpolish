> # Notice of the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, October 22, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss preparations for the Autumn 1979 Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting and to further consider the draft report by Permanent Representatives on CIMEX-79. The notice also allows for the discussion of any other business. The document references several previous communications, including NPG(Staff Group):P(79)4, NPG(Staff Group):P(79)38, NPG(Staff Group):P(72)6, and NPG(Staff Group):P(79)5(Revised).

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 22nd October,
1979 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Autumn 1979 NPG Ministerial meeting(1);
(b) further consideration of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on
:INTEx:/CIMEX-79(2);

(c) any other business.
