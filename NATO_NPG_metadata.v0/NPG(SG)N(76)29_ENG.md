> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1976 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated April 6, 1976, regarding a proposed advance press release for the 19th NPG Ministerial Meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place at NATO Headquarters in Brussels, Belgium, on June 14 and 15, 1976, under the chairmanship of Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns. The Chairman's note requests that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and timing of the release, which is suggested for June 11, 1976. Staff Group members from participating countries are asked to obtain approval from their national authorities for the text and timing, and to inform the Director of Nuclear Planning by May 7, 1976. The draft press release, provided in both English and French, lists the participating nations as Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE
SPRING 1976 MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting of NATO Headquarters,
Brussels, on 14th and 15th June, 1976. The text closely follows the language
used in former press announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. As on past
occasions the release could be made a few days before the meeting takes place;
accordingly, it is suggested that an announcement be made in Brussels, on
Friday, 11th June, 1976 at 3.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous additional
release in the capitals of those participating countries who so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 12th April. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Ministerial meeting are requested to obtain,
after their initial discussion, the approval of their national authorities for
the text of the announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director,
Nuclear Planning, no later than Friday, 7th May, 1976. Information on whether
national authorities plan to make a simultaneous release in the capitals would
also be appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER

Brussels.
1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, the Netherlands,
Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States, will hold its
nineteenth Ministerial Meeting at NATO Headquarters, Brussels, on 14th and 15th
June, 1976, under the chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. LUNS, Secretary General
of NATO.

PROJET DE COMMUNIQUE ANNONCANT LA REUNION

Le Groupe des plans nucleaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
de la Défense du Canada, des Etats-Unis, de l'Italie, de la Norvege, des Pays-
Bas, de la Republique Federale d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie,
tiendra sa 19eme reunion au niveau ministeriel les 14 et 15 juin au siege de
l'OTAN a Bruxelles, sous la presence de M. Joseph M.A.H. LUNS, Secrétaire
General de l'OTAN.
