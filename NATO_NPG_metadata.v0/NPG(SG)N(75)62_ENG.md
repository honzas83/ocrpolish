> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, August 25, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss scope papers concerning Phase III of the Follow-on Use Study and to examine any other business. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and references a previous document, PO(75)69, dated June 4, 1975.

NFG(Staff Group)N(75)62

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th August,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss scope papers concerning Phase III of the Follow-on Use Study(1);

(b) to examine any other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

(1) PO(75)69, paragraph 1, 4th June, 1975
