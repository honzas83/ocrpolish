> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on May 15, 1974. It informs members that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, May 20, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue preparations for the 1974 Spring Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
