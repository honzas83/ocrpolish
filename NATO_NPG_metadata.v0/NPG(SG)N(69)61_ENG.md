> # Revised Draft Agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a revised draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) ministerial meeting to be held near Washington on November 11th and 12th, 1969. The agenda is based on discussions from the Staff Group meeting on August 29th. The meeting's primary focus is the divided into three main sections: tactical use of nuclear weapons, strategic use of nuclear weapons, and consultation on the use of nuclear weapons. Specifically, the agenda includes tentative political guidelines for the initial tactical use of nuclear weapons by NATO, with a report by the United Kingdom and Germany. It also addresses follow-on tactical use and the draft terms of reference for future work. The strategic use section focuses on the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, featuring a report by the United States and Germany. The consultation section includes reports by Belgium and a United States paper, with collation by the Netherlands. The agenda concludes with other business, the future work programme, an agreed minute, and a communique.
