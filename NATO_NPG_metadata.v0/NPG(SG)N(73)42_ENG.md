> [!abstract] 
> # Downgrading of Document Classification
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), dated 29th May 1973, regarding the change in security classification for a previous document. Specifically, the Notes of a meeting held on 23rd May 1973, referenced as NPG(Staff Group)N(73)41, are to be downgraded from NATO SECRET to NATO CONFIDENTIAL effective from the date of the notice. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
