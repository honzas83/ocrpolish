> # Notes of a Meeting Held on Monday, 8th December, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on December 8, 1969, to review and refine the draft decision sheet of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee meeting from December 3. The group agreed to additions proposed by Turkish and Danish representatives regarding landing operations and the safeguarding of constitutional rights and responsibilities of Norway and Denmark. The meeting also addressed the Provisional Political Guidelines for the initial defensive tactical use of nuclear weapons by NATO, noting a request from the Secretary General to the Chairman of the Military Committee. The Staff Group invited the Chairman to inform NATO military authorities about Turkish suggestions concerning the use of tactical nuclear weapons against amphibious forces. Additionally, the group discussed the scheduling of the next NPG ministerial meeting in coordination with the Defence Planning Committee Spring ministerial meeting, requesting the Italian representative to coordinate this. The meeting concluded with a discussion on further studies of follow-on tactical use, reviewing draft working principles and a list of specific studies and projects presented by the International Staff.

PROVISIONAL POLITICAL GUIDELINES FOR THE INITIAL DEFENSIVE
TACTICAL USE OF NUCLEAR WEAPONS BY NATO

2. The STAFF GROUP :
(n) noted a statement by the Chairman who said that the Secretary General would
be sending a formal request to the Chairman of the military Committee regarding
the transmittal of, and action to be taken upon, the Provisional Political
Guidelines (1);

(b) invited the Chairman bring to the attention of the NATO military
Authorities, through appropriate channels, the suggestions made by the Turkish
Authorities regarding the use of tactical nuclear weapons against amphibious
forces (2).

DATES OF NEXT MEETING OF NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP IN MINISTERIAL SESSION

3. The STAFF GROUP :
invited the Italian Representative to draw to the attention of his authorities
the need to fix the dates of the next NPG ministerial meeting, taking into
account the dates of the Defence Planning Committee Spring ministerial meeting.

FURTHER STUDIES OF FOLLOW-ON TACTICAL USE

4. The STAFF GROUP :

and a draft list of specific studies and projects (3);

(b) took note of the various suggestions and points of view expressed during the
subsequent discussion of the paper;

(c) noted the views expressed regarding the programme of work on this matter
during the next two months;
