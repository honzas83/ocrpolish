> [!abstract] 
> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1974 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, H.B. Seim, regarding a proposed advance press release for the upcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting in Bergen, Norway, scheduled for 11th and 12th June 1974. The Chairman requests that Staff Group members from participating countries obtain approval from their national authorities for the text and timing of the release, with a deadline for response by 24th May 1974. The proposed release is suggested to be made in Brussels on Friday, 7th June 1974, at 3 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries if desired. The draft press release itself, provided in both English and French, identifies the participating nations as Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom, and the United States, and notes that the meeting will be held under the chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE
SPRING 1974 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting in Bergen, Norway, on 11th
and 12th June, 1974. The text closely follows the language used in former press
announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. As on past occasions the
release might be made a few days before the meeting takes place; accordingly, it
is suggested that an announcement might be made in Brussels on Friday, 7th June,
1974 at 3 p.m., local time, with simultaneous additional release in the capitals
of those participating countries who so desire.

2. Staff Group members of the countries participating in the Bergen meeting are
requested to obtain the approval of their national authorities for the text of
the announcement and timing of the release, informing me no later than Friday,
24th May, 1974. Information on whether national authorities plan to make a
simultaneous release in the capital would also be appreciated.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM
1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Canada, the Federal
Republic of Germany, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom
and the United States, will, at the invitation of the Government of Norway, hold
its fifteenth half-yearly Ministerial meeting in Bergen on 11th and 12th June,
1974, under the chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of
NATO.

Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement le Canada,
les Etats-Unis, la Grèce, l'Italie, la Norvège, les Pays-Bas, la République
Fédérale d'Allemagne, le Royaume-Uni tiendra, à l'invitation du Gouvernement de
la Norvège, sa 15ème réunion semestrielle au niveau ministériel les 11 et 12
juin à Bergen sous la présidence de M. Joseph Luns, Secrétaire Général de
l'OTAN.
