> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place in the Presentation Room on Monday, September 20, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting include hearing and discussing a briefing on the presentation of newly developed procedures, continuing discussions on Part I of the Compilation paper, and continuing discussions on the paper regarding the Classification of Nuclear Consultation Messages. Additionally, the meeting will focus on preparations for the Autumn Ministerial meeting, with a specific emphasis on the Agenda. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place in the Presentation Room on
Monday, 20th September, 1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting
will be:

(a) to hear and discuss a briefing on the Presentation of Newly Developed
Procedures(1);

(b) to continue discussion of Part I of the Compilation paper(2);
(c) to continue discussion of the paper on Classification of Nuclear
Consultation Messages(3);

(d) to continue preparations for the Autumn Ministerial meeting, with special
reference to the Agenda.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
