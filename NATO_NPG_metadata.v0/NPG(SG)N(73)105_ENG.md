> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has scheduled its next meeting for Monday, October 15, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the Permanent Representatives' report regarding the Status Report on the Follow-on Use Work Programme, to address timing aspects of the Future Work Programme, and to conduct initial discussions on whether to hold a meeting of the Nuclear Deterrence Advisory Committee (NDAC) in December. Additionally, the meeting will involve an initial exchange of views on the Conceptual Analysis of Deterrence. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th October,
1973 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of the Permanent Representatives' report on the
Status Report on the Follow-on Use Work Programme(1);

(b) timing aspects of the Future Work Programme(2);
(c) initial discussion on whether to hold a meeting of the NDAC in December;
(d) initial exchange of views on Conceptual Analysis of Deterrence(3).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
