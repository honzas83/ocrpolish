> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Thursday, 12th September, 1974
> 
> The NATO Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on 12th September 1974 in Brussels to address several key strategic documents and studies. The group discussed the third revision of a Working Paper regarding the communication of NATO's intention to use nuclear weapons, agreeing that the summary section required more clarity and that certain substantive points should be moved to the main text. Additionally, the group reviewed a draft Permanent Representatives' report on the CNI Study and agreed to continue discussions on this matter at the next meeting. The meeting also covered a draft report by Permanent Representatives on the Air Defence Illustrative Study, noting a growing consensus on its form and content and planning to finalize the draft at the next session. The group scheduled its next meeting for 16th September 1974. Finally, the meeting notes record a statement by the United States representative regarding an upcoming meeting of the Study Group on the Implications of Technological Developments on 20th September 1974.

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Thursday,

SECURITY
1. The STAFF GROUP:
took note that all persons present were recognised as being appropriately
cleared for the level of classification of the subject matter.

COMMUNICATING NATO'S INTENTION TO USE NUCLEAR WEAPONS
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Vice-Chairman who recalled the meeting of
the open-ended working group on 10th September, and introduced the resulting
third revision of the Working Paper(1); he pointed out the changes in the text
and invited initial comments, pending a further discussion next Monday when he
hoped that representatives would have instructions;

(b) took note of the views expressed in the ensuing discussion which covered in
particular the nature and scope of the paper and its present form and content;

(c) agreed that Part VI of the paper (the Summary) required to be more clear-
cut, and that a number of substantive points in the annex should be transferred
to the main text;

DRAFT PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES' REPORT ON THE CNI STUDY

3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) discussed the draft Permanent Representatives' report(1) on the CNI Study,
in the light of the foregoing debate, and took note of the views expressed;

(b) agreed to continue the discussion at the next meeting.

DRAFT REPORT BY PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES ON THE AIR DEFENCE ILLUSTRATIVE STUDY

4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who recalled that at a recent
meeting the Staff Group had discussed and agreed, with amendments, paragraphs
1-14 of the draft report(2) but had decided to postpone discussion of paragraphs
15-20 until further examination of the CNI Study had been made;

(b) took note of the views expressed in the subsequent discussion which covered
all points of the draft report, and noted a growing consensus on its form and
content;

(c) agreed to finalize this draft report at the next meeting.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP

5. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 16th September, 1974, at 11 a.m. and
in the afternoon.
