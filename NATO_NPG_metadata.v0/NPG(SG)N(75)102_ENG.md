> # Press Release for the Eighteenth NPG Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note from the Chairman of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, R.E. Shearer, dated December 4, 1975, requesting approval for a draft press release. The purpose is to announce that the 18th NPG Ministerial Meeting, which was originally scheduled for November 20-21, 1975, in Hamburg, Germany, was postponed to allow for the participation of the new United States Secretary of Defense. The meeting is now scheduled to take place on January 21 and 22, 1976, in Hamburg, under the Chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns. The draft press release, provided in both English and French, lists the participating countries as Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The Chairman requests that Staff Group members seek their authorities' approval for the text to be released on December 8, 1975, at 3:00 p.m. local time.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

PRESS RELEASE FOR THE LIGHTEENTH NGF MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Staff Group members will recall that on 12th November, 1975, the following press
statement was made in conjunction with the decision to postpone the 18th NFG
Ministerial Meeting:

"The members of NATO's Nuclear Planning Group have agreed to postpone the
Ministerial meeting, which was scheduled to take place in Hamburg, Germany, on
November 20-21, 1975, in order to permit the participation of the new United
States' Secretary of Defense. No decision on the date for re-scheduling the
meeting has yet been taken, but it will probably take place in Germany in
January."

2. Since it has now been agreed to hold the conference on 21st and 22nd January
in Hamburg, the participating countries may wish to consider the release of a
press statement as outlined at Annex, on the occasion of the forthcoming
December Ministerial meetings. Staff Group members of the seven participating
countries are requested to seek their authorities' approval of the attached
suggested text to be released on Monday, 8th December, at 3:00 p.m. local time,
with simultaneous additional release in the capitals of those participating
countries who so desire.

3. If I receive no objections to time and text by noon on Monday, 8th December,
1975 I shall advise the NATO Press Service to release the statement. Information
on whether national authorities plan to make a simultaneous release would be
appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER.

Brussels.

ANNEX to NPG(Staff Group)N(75)102
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the
United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the invitation of the Government
of the Federal Republic of Germany, hold its eighteenth Ministerial Meeting in
Hamburg, on 21st and 22nd January, 1976, under the Chairmanship of Mr. Joseph
M.A.H. LUNS, Secretary General of NATO. This meeting will take the place of the
Ministerial NPG originally planned for last month which had to be postponed.

PROJET DE COMMUNIQUE ANNONCANT LA REUNION

Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
de la Défense de la Belgique, du Danemark, des Etats-Unis, de l'Italie, de la
République Fédérale d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie tiendra, à
l'invitation du gouvernement de la République Fédérale d'Allemagne, sa 18ème
réunion au niveau ministériel les 21 et 22 janvier, 1976 à Hambourg, sous la
présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. LUNS, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN. Cette réunion
tiendra lieu de réunion ministérielle du Groupe des plans nucléaires prévue
auparavant pour le mois dernier et qui avait dû être ajournée.
