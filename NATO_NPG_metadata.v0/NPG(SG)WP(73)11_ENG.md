> # Draft Ministerial Agenda
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting scheduled for November 6th and 7th, 1973, in The Hague. The agenda items include a briefing on the balance of strategic forces led by the United States, discussions on operational concepts and doctrine, and early combined use. It also covers consultation procedures and facilities, the implementation of special administrative guidelines, and the future work programme. The document was issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) and is intended to provide a basis for Staff Group discussion. The agenda lists several supporting documents, including reports by Permanent Representatives and reports from the SHAPE (Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe) and the Military Committee.
>
