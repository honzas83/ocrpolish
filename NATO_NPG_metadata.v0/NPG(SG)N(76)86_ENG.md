> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, October 4, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss a briefing on the presentation of new and refined procedures, continue discussions on the classification of nuclear consultation messages, and prepare for the Autumn Ministerial meeting with a specific focus on the agenda. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
29th September, 1976
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(75)65
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 4th October, 1976
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hear again and discuss a briefing on the Presentation of new and refined
Procedures(1);

(b) to continue discussion of the paper on Classification of Nuclear
Consultation Messages(2);

(c) to continue preparations for the Autumn Ministerial meeting, with special
reference to the Agenda.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)2, 31st May, 1976
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)7, 2nd September, 1976
