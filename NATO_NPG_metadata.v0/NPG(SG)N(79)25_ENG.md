> # Notice of the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding an upcoming meeting on Monday, June 25, 1979, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with a specific focus on Section IV concerning the Selective Use of 'Nuclear' Weapons. The notice also allows for the other business to be addressed. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and includes several references to previous documents, such as CUT/US/79-125 and various IPG(Staff Group) papers. The meeting is organized under the North Atlantic Council's framework within NATO.
>

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th June, 1979
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of 'Nuclear' Weapons(2);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
