> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, April 5, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to continue discussions on the inclusion of Country Request Procedures in the compilation of nuclear consultation procedures and to discuss the procedural handling of a United States paper on improving the effectiveness of NATO's Theatre Nuclear Force Posture. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document discusses the 'inclusion of Country Request Procedures in the compilation of nuclear consultation procedures' and a US paper on 'improving the effectiveness of NATO's Theatre Nuclear Force Posture', both of which relate to the mechanisms for sharing nuclear responsibilities and consultation on nuclear use in Europe.

30th March, 1976

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)25

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th April, 1976
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion of the inclusion of Country Request Procedures in
the compilation of nuclear consultation procedures(1);

(b) to discuss the procedural handling of the US paper on improving the
effectiveness of NATO's Theatre Nuclear Force Posture(2).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(76)17, 23rd February, 1976
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(76)22, 12th March, 1976
