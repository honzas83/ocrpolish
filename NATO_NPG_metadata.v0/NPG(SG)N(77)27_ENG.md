> [!abstract] 
> # SACEUR's Dual-Capable Aircraft Study
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 25th March 1977. It informs the Staff Group members that a limited number of copies of SACEUR's Dual-Capable Aircraft Study (SHAPE/040/77) were distributed to the registries of delegations on a preliminary basis. The Secretary emphasizes that the distribution was arranged with the Military Committee on the condition that Permanent Representatives' consideration of the study would be withheld until the Military Committee's views are received. Additionally, the document requests that any unnecessary copies of the study be returned to the International Staff Cosmic Registry and explicitly forbids the destruction of these documents. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document refers to 'SACEUR's Dual-Capable Aircraft Study', which concerns aircraft capable of delivering both conventional and nuclear weapons, a central component of NATO's extended deterrence and tactical nuclear sharing in Europe.

Note by the Secretary

Staff Group members are reminded that as of 25th March, 1977, a limited number
of copies of SACEUR's Dual-Capable Aircraft Study(1) were distributed, on a
preliminary basis, to registries of delegations.

2. Limited advance distribution to the Staff Group was arranged with the
Military Committee on the understanding that Permanent Representatives'
consideration of this study would be withheld pending receipt of Military
Committee views.

3. Recipients are requested to return to the International Staff Cosmic Registry
any copies of this document which they do not require. Under no circumstances
should any of these documents be destroyed.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
