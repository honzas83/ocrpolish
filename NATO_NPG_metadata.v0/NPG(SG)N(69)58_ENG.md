> [!abstract] 
> # Visit by the Staff Group to Germany
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated July 8, 1969. It informs members that the projected field trip by the Staff Group to Germany has been postponed until later in the year. The postponement is attributed to the absence of several members who are either on mission or on vacation. The notice was signed by W.H.G. Willson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
