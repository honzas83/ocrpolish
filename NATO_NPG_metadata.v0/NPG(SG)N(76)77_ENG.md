> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 6th September, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on September 6, 1976. The group discussed the modification of an International Staff paper on Compilation, agreeing to a number of changes and requesting a new version of Part I of the paper. Regarding the Ministerial Agenda, the group noted remarks on the preparation of the agenda for the Autumn NPG Ministerial Meeting and invited the International Staff to circulate a draft agenda for the information of Capitals. The group also addressed the scheduling of the Autumn NPG Ministerial Meeting, noting requests from the United Kingdom and the Netherlands regarding preferred dates in November 1976, with the UK representative proposing to check with his authorities for a final decision. Finally, the group agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, September 13, 1976.

Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels,
on Monday, 6th September, 1976
SECURITY
1. The STAFF GROUP:
took note that all persons present were cleared
for the security classification of the matters
to be discussed.
COMPILATION
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who
recalled that the International Staff paper(1)
on Compilatio had been discussed briefly at the
last meeting and that some members had been in
favour of examining this paper in further detail
before its publication as an NPG Working Paper;
(b) took note of the further remarks of the Chairman
who said that, as a result of a meeting between
himself and the UK and US representatives, the
text of the paper had been modified as shown in
the paper distributed by the UK representative at
the table;

MINISTERIAL AGENDA

3. The STAFF GROUP:

Capitals and further discussion by the Staff

DATE OF THE AUTUMN NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING

4. The STAFF GROUP:

(a) took note of a statement by the UK representative
who said that his Minister of Defence, in the
light of the inability of the United States to
attend a meeting in the week beginning
22nd November had now indicated that he would
prefer the meeting to take place in the week
beginning 15th November and preferably on
Tuesday 16th and Wednesday 17th;

(b) noted a request from the Netherlands represent-
ative that the dates should be Wednesday 17th
and Thursday 18th November because his Defence
Minister would be much occupied by the debate on
the Netherlands Defence Budget taking place that.
week in The Hague;
