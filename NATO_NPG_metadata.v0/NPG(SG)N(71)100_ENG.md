> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a notice for the next meeting scheduled for Monday, September 6, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss the draft Ministerial Agenda, AD 70 Follow-up Action, and the CENTAG Area Study. Additionally, the meeting will aim to address possible requests to the NATO Military Authorities for comments on the Turkish and CENTAG Area Studies. The document references several previous documents, including NPG(Staff Group)N(71)87, NPG(Staff Group)N(71)98, and NPG/Study(71)6.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 6th September,
1971, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be to discuss:

(a) the draft Ministerial Agenda(1);
(b) AD 70 Follow-up Action(2);
(c) CENTAG Area Study(3) (initial discussion only);
(d) Possible requests to the NATO Military Authorities for comments on the
Turkish and CENTAG Area Studies.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)87, 13th August, 1971
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)98, 30th August, 1971
(3) NPG/Study(71)6, 1st August, 1971
