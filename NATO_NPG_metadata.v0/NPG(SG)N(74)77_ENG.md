> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Thursday, July 18, 1974, at 10 a.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss a revised draft study on Communicating NATO's Intention to use Nuclear Weapons, as well as to potentially discuss a draft Permanent Representatives' report on S.A. CEUR's Illustrative Air Defence Study if time permits. Additionally, the notice mentions a farewell lunch party in honour of Mr. H.B. Seim following the morning session. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and is marked as NATO Restricted.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 18th July,
1974, at 10 a.m. and also in the afternoon if necessary. The principal object of
this meeting will be to discuss the revised draft of a study on Communicating
NATO's Intention to use Nuclear Weapons(1), and if time permits, to hold an
initial discussion of a draft Permanent Representatives' report on S.A. CEUR's
Illustrative Air Defence Study(2).

2. Members are reminded that a farewell lunch party in honour of Mr. H.B. Seim
will take place after the morning session.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
