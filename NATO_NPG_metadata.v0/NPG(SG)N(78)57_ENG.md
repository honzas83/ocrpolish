> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1978 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, R.E. Shearer, regarding a proposed advance press release for the upcoming twenty-fourth NPG Ministerial meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place at the Palais d'Égmont in Brussels, Belgium, on October 18 and 19, 1978. The Chairman suggests that the announcement be made in Brussels on Monday, October 16, 1978, at 4.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is requested to discuss the draft text and timing of the release, and members are asked to obtain national authority approval of the text and timing by September 29, 1978. The draft press release, provided in both English and French, identifies the participating nations as Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE AUTUMN 1978 MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Attached is the text, in English and French, of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting to be held at the Palais
d'Égmont in Brussels on 18th and 19th October, 1978. The text closely follows
the language used in former press announcements prior to NPG Ministerial
meetings. As on past occasions, the release could be made a few days before the
meeting takes place; accordingly, it is suggested that an announcement be made
in Brussels on Monday, 16th October, 1978, at 4.00 p.m. local time, with
simultaneous additional release in the capitals of those participating countries
which so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on Monday, 28th August. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Ministerial meeting are requested after their
initial discussion to obtain their national authorities approval of the text of
the announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director, Nuclear
Planning no later than Friday, 29th September, 1978. Information on whether
national authorities plan to make a simultaneous release in their capitals would
also be appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the
United Kingdom and the United States will hold its twenty-fourth Ministerial
Meeting in Brussels, Belgium, on 18th and 19th October, 1978, under the
chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

PROJET DE COMMUNIQUE ANNONCANT LA REUNION
Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
de la Défense de la Belgique, du Danemark, des Etats-Unis, de l'Italie, de la
République Fédérale d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie tiendra sa
vingt-quatrième réunion au niveau ministériel les 18 et 19 octobre 1978 à
Bruxelles, Belgique, sous la présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secrétaire
Général de l'OTAN.
