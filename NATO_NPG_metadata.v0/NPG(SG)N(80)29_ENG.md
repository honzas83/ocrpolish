> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Tuesday, May 27, 1980, at 3 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes preparations for the Spring 1980 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting and a discussion of the draft transmittal note for the General Political Guidelines. Additionally, the notice mentions that a short ceremony will take place in the Chairman's office immediately following the meeting to mark the completion of the first draft of the General Political Guidelines, to which all Staff Group members are invited. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts of NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 27th May, 1980
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Spring 1980 NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(b) discussion of the draft transmittal note for the General Political
Guidelines(1);

(c) any other business.
2. The completion of the first draft of the General Political Guidelines will be
marked by a short ceremony which will take place in the Chairman's office
immediately after the meeting and to which all Staff Group members are invited.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) DPP/NPD/80/57 (to be circulated)
