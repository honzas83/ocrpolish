> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1972 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, H.B. Seim, originating from NATO headquarters in Brussels. It contains a proposed draft advance press release, provided in both English and French, regarding the upcoming twelfth half-yearly Ministerial meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO. The meeting is scheduled to take place in London on October 26th and 27th, 1972, under the Chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph Luns. The note suggests that the press release be issued in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday, October 20th, 1972, and simultaneously in the capitals of member states that wish to do so. The NPG consists of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The Chairman suggests that the Staff Group discuss the draft at its next meeting on Monday, September 18th, 1972.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE
AUTUMN 1972 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in London on 26th and 27th
October, 1972. The text closely follows the language used in press announcements
issued during the past two years just prior to Ministerial meetings. It is
suggested that the release be made in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday,
20th October, 1972 with simultaneous additional release in the capitals of those
countries who desire to do so. It is suggested that the Staff Group discuss the
draft at its next meeting on Monday, 18th September, 1972.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
As arranged at its previous meeting in Copenhagen in May, 1972, the Nuclear
Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Belgium, Denmark, Germany,
Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the invitation
of the British Government, hold its twelfth half-yearly Ministerial meeting on
26th and 27th October, 1972, in London, under the Chairmanship of Mr. Joseph
Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

Comme convenu lors de la précédente réunion à Copenhague au mois de mai 1972, le
Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement l'Allemagne, la
Belgique, le Danemark, les Etats-Unis, l'Italie, le Royaume-Uni et la Turquie,
tiendra à l'invitation du Gouvernement anglais, sa 12ème réunion semestrielle au
niveau ministériel les 26 et 27 octobre à Londres sous la présidence de M.
Joseph Luns, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN.
