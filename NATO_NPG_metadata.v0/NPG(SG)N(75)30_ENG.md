> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Friday, 11th April, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened on April 11, 1975, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to address several key items. The group discussed a draft advance press release, with the United States representative suggesting a release date of June 9th rather than June 13th, and the United Kingdom representative suggesting minor text changes. Regarding the draft ministerial agenda, the United Kingdom proposed adding Selective Employment Planning as a separate item, while the United States representative expressed concerns about the agenda's length and suggested moving the Illustrative Study to the autumn 1975 agenda and placing the WINTEX 75 item immediately after the item on CNI. The group also reviewed a first draft of a report by Permanent Representatives on Phase II of the work programme on Follow-on Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons, emphasizing its importance for future NPG activities. Additionally, the Staff Group agreed that a visit to the Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE) for briefings on nuclear planning would be beneficial and should occur before S!APEX. Finally, the group scheduled its next meetings for April 21st and April 25th, 1975.

SECURITY

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the United States representative who said that
his authorities would prefer 9th June as a release date rather than 13th June as
suggested in the Staff Group working paper(1);

(b) noted the remarks of the United Kingdom representative regarding minor
changes to the text of the release.
