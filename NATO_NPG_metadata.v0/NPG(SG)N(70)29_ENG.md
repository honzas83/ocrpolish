> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Thursday, April 16, 1970, at 10 a.m. in Brussels. The meeting's primary objectives are to discuss the draft agenda for the meeting of Ministers in Venice, including the potential preparation of a Status Report on Follow-on Studies. Additionally, the group will determine the date for the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives and review the revised draft of the general statement on Air Defence Main System (ADMS). The note is signed by H.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 16th April,
1970 at 10 a.m., continuing in the afternoon if necessary, and if a conference
room is available.

2. The principal subjects for discussion will be:
(a) the draft agenda for the meeting of Ministers in Venice(1), including the
question as to whether a Status Report on Follow-on Studies should be
prepared(2).

(b) the date of the next meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives;
(c) the revised draft of the general statement on ADMS(3);
(Signed) H.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
