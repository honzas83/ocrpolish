> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1972 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, H.B. Seim, regarding a proposed advance press release for the upcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Copenhagen on May 18th and 19th, 1972. The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, consisting of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will hold its eleventh half-yearly Ministerial meeting under the Chairmanship of Mr. Joseph Luns, Secretary General of NATO. The note requests that delegations obtain approval or comments on the proposed text and release arrangements from their respective capitals by May 2nd, 1972. It is planned that the press release would be made in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday, May 12th, 1972, with simultaneous additional releases in the capitals of member states who wish to do so. The document includes the draft press release text in both English and French.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE SPRING 1972 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Copenhagen on 18th and
19th May, 1972, which the Staff Group has agreed should now be sent to capitals.
The text closely follows the language used in press announcements issued during
the past two years just prior to Ministerial meetings. It is planned that the
release would be made in Brussels at 3 p.m., local time on Friday, 12th May,
1972, with simultaneous additional release in the capitals of those countries
who desire to do so.

2. Delegations are requested to obtain approval of or comments on the proposed
text and release arrangements from capitals no later than Tuesday, 2nd May,
1972. Information on whether a simultaneous additional release would be made in
the capital would also be appreciated.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
As arranged at its previous meeting in Brussels in October, 1971, the Nuclear
Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Belgium, Denmark, Germany,
Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the invitation
of the Danish Government, hold its eleventh half-yearly Ministerial meeting on
18th and 19th May, 1972, in Copenhagen, Denmark under the Chairmanship of Mr.
Joseph Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

Comme convenu lors de la précédente réunion à Bruxelles au mois d'octobre 1971,
le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement l'Allemagne,
la Belgique, le Danemark, les Etats-Unis, l'Italie, le Royaume-Uni et la
Turquie, tiendra à l'invitation du Gouvernement danois, sa dixième réunion
semestrielle au niveau ministériel les 18 et 19 mai à Copenhague (Danemark) sous
la présidence de M. Joseph Luns, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN.
