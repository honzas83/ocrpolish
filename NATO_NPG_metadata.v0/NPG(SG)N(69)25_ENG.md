> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 17, 1969, at 4:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the International Staff's draft report on the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, as detailed in document NGF(Staff Group)N(69)4. The notice was signed by J. de Gaye and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
