> # Farewell Lunch Party for Departing Dean
> 
> On 16th July 1976, the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a notice regarding a farewell lunch party for the departing Dean, Colonel Jan P.H. van Valderen. The event is scheduled for Monday, 26th July 1976, at the Salon des Ambassadeurs, starting with drinks at 12:30 p.m. and lunch at approximately 1:00 p.m. All members of the NPG Staff Group are invited to attend. The cost per person is 350 Francs, which covers drinks and the guest of honor's share of the lunch. Members intending to attend are requested to notify the Secretary by Thursday, 22nd July, and pay their share of the cost.

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)63

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
FAREWELL LUNCH PARTY FOR DEPARTING DEAN

Note by the Secretary

I have been instructed by the Chairman to say that a lunch party will take place
on Monday, 26th July, 1976 to mark the departure of our Dean, Colonel Jan P.H.
van Valderen.

2.  The party will take place in the Salon des Ambassadeurs beginning with
drinks at 12.30 and with lunch at about 1 p.m.

3.  All members of the NPF Staff Group are invited.
The cost will be 350 F. per person which will include drinks and the members'
share of the lunch of our departing guest.

4.  Members intending to be present are requested to inform me not later than
Thursday, 22nd July. Their share of the cost should be paid to me when
convenient.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
