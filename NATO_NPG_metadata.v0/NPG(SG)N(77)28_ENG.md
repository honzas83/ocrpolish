> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday, 30th March, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened on March 30, 1977, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to address several administrative and strategic items. The meeting began with confirmation of security clearances for all attendees and the welcoming of new members and visitors, including Gp. Capt. Victor McNabney and members of the Technology Study Full Panel. The group discussed the status of various documents, including the NIKE-HERCULES Study, SACEUR's Dual-Capable Aircraft Study, and the Theatre Information and Target Systems, while noting that the PIT Report and the Technology Study Full Panel report would be circulated once approved. Regarding Exercise WINTEX 77, the group discussed a draft report and invited the International Staff to prepare a revised version with assistance from the UK representative. The group also deferred a decision on the necessity of the Status of Work Report and discussed the possible content of the Ministerial Agenda for June 1977. Finally, the group scheduled future meetings for the NPG Permanent Representatives and postponed their own next meeting to April 6, 1977.
