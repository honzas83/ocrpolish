> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 21st June, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on June 21, 1976, at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The group noted the circulation of several documents, including an Agreed Minute from a ministerial meeting on June 14-15, 1976, Norwegian views on a specific matter, and a United States paper on top-down release. The meeting's focus was on follow-up actions from the Ministerial Meeting, including the preparation of a paper by the International Staff regarding Terms of Reference for studies on Nike Hercules Sites and dual-capable aircraft. It was agreed that this paper would be submitted to Permanent Representatives on July 7 or 14, 1976. Additionally, the group addressed a request from the Canadian Defence Minister for training of Ministers in nuclear decision-making, which will be covered by an 'omibus' presentation. The group also completed discussions on newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation, noting that Denmark withdrew its reservations. The International Staff and NATO Military Authorities will prepare a text for a presentation to be ready by early September. Finally, the group scheduled its next meeting for June 28, 1976.
