> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, June 21, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions on procedural arrangements for handling subsequent stages of the follow-on use work programme. The notice refers to a previous document, NPG(Staff Group)N(71)65, dated June 16, 1971.
