> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, September 17, 1979, at 3 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes a discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines, with a specific focus on Section IV regarding the Selective Use of Nuclear weapons. Additionally, the meeting will cover preparations for the Autumn 1979 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting and any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. The document references several previous communications and working papers, including USNATO/DPD/CUT/NS/73-126 and NPG(Staff Group)WP(79)4.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday. 17th September,
1979 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear weapons(2);

(b) preparations for the Autumn 1979 NPG Ministerial meeting(3);

(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
