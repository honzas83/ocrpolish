> # Note by the Secretary Regarding the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) notifying members of an upcoming meeting on Monday, December 3, 1979, at 7:30 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines and specifically address Section 27 regarding the Selective Use of 'Nuclear' weapons. The note is signed by D.J. Robinson and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents, including P6(Staff Group)(79)2, DDP/79/53, DDP/79/71, and HPC(Staff Group)(79)27, to provide context for the upcoming discussions.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 3rd December,
1979 at 7.30 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section 27. Selective Use of 'Nuclear' weapons(2);

(b) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBINSON
