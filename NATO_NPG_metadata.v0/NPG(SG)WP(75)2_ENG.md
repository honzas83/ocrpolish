> # Draft Agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Meeting in Ministerial Session
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for a meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group in Ministerial Session to be held in the United States on June 16th and 17th, 1975. The agenda items include a briefing on the balance of strategic forces, a discussion on the follow-on use work programme phase II, and the details of communicating NATO's intention to use nuclear weapons. Additionally, the items cover an illustrative study on initial air defence use, preliminary observations on VINTEX 1975, and a discussion of the Nunn Amendment. The meeting concludes with the planning of the future work programme, any other business, and the finalization of an agreed minute and communique.
