> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, December 17, 1979, at 2:30 p.m. The agenda for the meeting will include a follow-up to Ministerial meetings, a discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines, specifically focusing on Section IV regarding the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons, and any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th December,
1979 at 2.30 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) follow-up to Ministerial meetings;
(b) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

IIATO,
1110 Brussels.
