> # 18th Meeting of NPG Ministers - Transport of Classified Documents to Germany
> 
> This document is a notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group regarding the 18th Meeting of NPG Ministers. It outlines the logistical arrangements for the transport of classified papers to Hamburg, Germany, between January 20th and 22nd, 1976. Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section, will serve as the courier. Delegations wishing to use this service are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and submit their papers by mid-day on Monday, January 19th. The notice specifies that papers should be packaged, clearly marked with the delegation's name and a serial number, and kept as light as possible, explicitly instructing that unclassified material should not be included to avoid burdening the courier.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

18th MEETING OF NPG MINISTERS - TRANSPORT OF
CLASSIFIED DOCUMENTS TO GERMANY

Note by the Secretary

Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section (Tel. ext.
2481), will carry classified papers for the Hamburg meeting, departing 20th
January, and will return with them on 22nd January. Delegations wishing to take
advantage of this arrangement are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and to send
him their papers not later than mid-day on Monday, 19th January.

2. Papers should be packaged and clearly marked with the name of the delegation
or person and a serial number. Packages should be as light as possible. Please
do NOT burden the Courier with unclassified material.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
