> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, April 8, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to examine a revised text of a possible Permanent Representatives' Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons, to discuss a possible advance press release for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting, and to consider whether a review of the status of implementation of the ADM Guidelines toward the end of 1974 should be ruled out. Additionally, the meeting will continue preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by H.B. Seim and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Chairman

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th April, 1974
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to examine a revised text(1) of a possible Permanent Representatives' Report
on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for
the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) to discuss a possible advance press release for the Spring NGP Ministerial
meeting(3);

(c) to consider whether a review of the status of implementation of the ADM
Guidelines toward the end of 1974 should be ruled out(4);

(d) to continue preparations for the Spring NGP Ministerial Meeting.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NGP(Staff Group)WP(74)5(Revised), 1st April, 1974
(2) NGP/Study/45, 8th March, 1974
(3) NGP(Staff Group)WP(74)6, 2nd April, 1974
(4) NGP(Staff Group)N(74)35, 29th March, 1974
