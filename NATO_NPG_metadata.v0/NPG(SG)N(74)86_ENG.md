> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Wednesday, September 4, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 Nuclear Planning Committee (NPC) Ministerial meeting, to discuss a draft agenda for said meeting, and to determine if a final report on WINTEX 73 should be prepared by Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Permanent Representatives for Ministerial consideration. Additionally, the meeting will address the continued discussion of a draft Permanent Representatives Report on the Air-Defence Illustrative Study. The notice was signed by N. Park and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 4th September,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be :

(a) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPC Ministerial meeting;
(b) to discuss a draft agenda(1) for the Autumn meeting;
(c) to further discuss whether a final report on WINTEX 73 should be prepared by
NPG Permanent Representatives for Ministerial consideration(2);

(d) to continue discussion of a draft of a Permanent Representatives Report on
Air-Defence Illustrative Study(3).

(Signed) N. PARK
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)9, 23rd July, 1974
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)80, 23rd July, 1974
