> # Probability of Arrival and Damage Expectancy
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on 5th February 1969, serves as a notice to correct a specific error in a previous communication. Specifically, it refers to document NPG(Staff Group)N(69)10 dated 31st January 1969. The notice instructs the recipient to delete the term 'surface-to-surface' and replace it with 'surface-to-air' in sub-paragraph 2(d)(3), line 2 of page 4. This correction is signed by M.H.G. Wilson, the Secretary, and originated from Brussels, Belgium, under the auspices of NATO.
>
