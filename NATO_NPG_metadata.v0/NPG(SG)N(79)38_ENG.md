> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1979 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding a proposed advance press release for the 26th Ministerial Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) to be held in The Hague on November 13 and 14, 1979. The Chairman, J.D. Martin, suggests that the announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, November 9, 1979, at 4:00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is requested to discuss the draft text and timing, obtain national authority approval of the text and timing, and inform the Director of Nuclear Planning. The draft press release itself, provided in both English and French, lists the participating Ministers of Defence from Belgium, Canada, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, and notes that the meeting will be chaired by NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE AUTUMN 1979 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text, in English and French, of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NFC Ministerial meeting to be held in The Hague on
13th and 14th November, 1979. The text closely follows the language used in
former press announcements prior to NPC Ministerial meetings. As on past
occasions, the release could be made a few days before the meeting takes place;
accordingly, it is suggested that an announcement be made in Brussels on Friday,
9th November, 1979 at 4.00 p.m., local time, with simultaneous additional
release in the capitals of those participating countries which so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at a future meeting. Staff Group members are requested after
their initial discussion to obtain their national authorities approval of the
text of the announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director,
Nuclear Planning accordingly. Information on whether national authorities plan
to make a simultaneous release in their capitals would also be appreciated.

(Signed) J.D. MARTIN
JATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
The Nuclear Flanking Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Belgium, Canada, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Greece,
Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States
will hold its twenty-sixth Ministerial Meeting in The Hague on 13th and 14th
November, 1979, under the chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary
General of NATO.

PROJET DE COMMUNIQUE ANNONCANT LA REUNION
Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
de la Défense de la Belgique, du Canada, du Danemark, des États-Unis, de la
Grèce, de l'Italie, de la Norvège, des Pays-Bas, de la République Fédérale
d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie tiendra sa vingt-sixième réunion au
niveau ministériel les 15 et 14 novembre 1979 à La Haye, sous la présidence de
M. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN.
