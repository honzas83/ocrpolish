> [!abstract] 
> # Notice FGT(Staff Group): (79)18
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a new meeting scheduled for Thursday, April 12, 1979, at 10 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss preparations for the upcoming Spring 1979 IFC Ministerial Meeting. The notice also includes a list of reference documents, including IFC/(79)7, IFC(Staff Group): (79)12, and IFC(Staff Group): (79)17. The document is signed by D.J. Robbins of HACO, Brussels.

NOTICE FGT(Staff Group): (79)18

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEW MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 12th April,
1979 at 10 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Spring 1979 IFC Ministerial Meeting(1);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBBINS

HACO, 1110 Brussels.
