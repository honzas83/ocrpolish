> # Draft Agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Meeting in Ministerial Session
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for a Nuclear Planning Group meeting held in Hamburg on November 20th and 21st, 1975. The agenda items include a briefing on the balance of strategic forces, led by the United States, and discussions on nuclear weapons employment policy, specifically focusing on the United States Nuclear Weapons Employment Policy and SACEUR Selective Employment Planning. It also covers force posture issues raised by nuclear powers regarding other countries, and the third phase of the follow-on use work programme. Follow-on use work programme - Phase III is also listed. Additionally, the agenda includes nuclear consultation processes, including country requests for the use of nuclear weapons and communicating NATO's intention to use nuclear weapons. The meeting concludes with any other business, including future meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group and the future work programme, and concludes with thees a draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Group meeting in Hamburg on November 20th and 20th, 1975.
>
