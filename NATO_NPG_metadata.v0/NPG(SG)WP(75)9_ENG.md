> # Draft Ministerial Agenda
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for a Nuclear Planning Group meeting in Ministerial Session to be held in Hamburg on November 20th and 1e5, 1975. The agenda is a consolidation of proposals made by Staff Group members during a meeting on September 8th, 1975, and was submitted by the Chairman, R.E. Shearer, for discussion at the upcoming Staff Group meeting on September 15th. The proposed agenda items include employment policy, specifically the United States Nuclear Weapon Employment Policy, SACEUR presentation and discussion of Strategic Employment Plans, and a review of the Phase III Scope Paper. Force posture issues to be raised by nuclear powers or other countries, and procedural matters such as the Country Request Paper and CNI, and any other business including future meetings and the work programme.
>
