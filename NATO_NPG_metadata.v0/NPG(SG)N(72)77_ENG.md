> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, September 18, 1972, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to review progress on the preparation of documents for the upcoming Ministerial Meeting in October. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
