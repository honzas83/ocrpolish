> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, June 26, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to further discuss the NATO Military Authorities' proposals for short-term measures to improve NATO's TNF. The agenda also includes any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document is a notice for a meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group to discuss 'short-term measures to improve NATO's TNF' (Tactical Nuclear Forces), which directly relates to the deployment and sharing of nuclear weapons on allied territory.
