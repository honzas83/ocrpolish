> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Wednesday, May 21, 1980, at 9:30 a.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to prepare for the Spring 1980 NPG Ministerial meeting and to review Sections III to IX of the draft General Political Guidelines. The notice also lists several reference documents, including NPG(Staff Group)N(80)24 and various DPP/NPD documents from 1979 and 1980, to be used for these discussions. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts.

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place
on Wednesday, 21st May, 1980 at 9.30 a.m. The principal subjects
to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Spring 1980 NPG Ministerial meeting(1);
(b) review of Sections III to IX of the draft General
Political Guidelines(2);
(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
