> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary regarding the scheduling of the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is set to take place on Monday, July 28, 1975, at 10:30 a.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the meeting is to conduct an informal discussion on the Future Work Programme, with a specific focus on the Phase III terms of reference. The document references a previous communication, PO(75)69, dated June 4, 1975.
