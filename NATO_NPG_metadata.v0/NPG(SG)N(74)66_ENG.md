> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday, 5th June, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on June 5, 1974, to review documentation and finalize arrangements for the upcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting in Bergen. The meeting's documentation included a report by the Permanent Representatives on the Military Committee Function in the Nuclear Consultation Process, a revised final agenda for the Bergen meeting, a draft final communiqué, and a framework for the agreed minute. Additionally, the Staff Group took note of the Chairman's remarks regarding the Draft Report to the Defense and Political Committee (DPC) on Exercise HILEX-6, specifically focusing on the section concerning consultation and decision-making. The document serves as a record of the notes from this preparatory meeting.

DOCUMENTATION

1. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who drew
attention to the following papers which had been
circulated:

(a) NPQ/D(74)5, 29th May, 1974: Report by the Permanent
Representatives on the Military Committee Function
in the Nuclear Consultation Process;
(b) NPQ/A(74)3(Revised), 30th May, 1974: Final Agenda
for the meeting of NPG Ministers in Bergen;
(c) DPP/NPD/74/63, 30th May, 1974: Draft Final Communiqué
for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Bergen;
(d) DPP/NPD/74/64, 30th May, 1974: Framework for the
Agreed Minute for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Bergen.

2. The STAFF GROUP:
took note also of the Chairman's remarks concerning
AC/237-WP/90, 21st May, 1974, the Draft Report to the
DPC on Exercise HILEX-6, with particular
reference to Section II (c) - Consultation and
Decision-Making.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE MEETING OF NPG MINISTERS IN BERGEN
