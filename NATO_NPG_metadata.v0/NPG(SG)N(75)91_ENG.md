> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 21st October 1975. It specifies that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 22nd October 1975, at 4 p.m. The primary objective of the agenda is to address the arrangements for Phase III. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
