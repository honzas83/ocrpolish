> [!abstract] 
> # Visit to SHAPE on Tuesday, 19th June
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding a transportation arrangement for a visit to SHAPE (Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe). The note specifies that a bus has been arranged to transport Staff Group members between NATO Headquarters and SHAPE on Tuesday, 19th June. Members intending to use the bus service are requested to assemble at the Press Entrance at 9 a.m., with the return journey scheduled between 4 p.m. and 4.30 p.m. Additionally, the Secretary, D.J. Roberts, requests that members wishing to avail themselves of this service inform the Secretary's office via extension 2410.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
VISIT TO SHAPE ON TUESDAY, 19TH JUNE
Note by the Secretary
As mentioned at the meeting on 11th June, a bus has been arranged to transport
Staff Group members between NATO Headquarters and SHAPE on Tuesday 19th June.
Those intending to take the bus are requested to assemble at the Press Entrance
at 9 a.m. The bus will leave SHAPE between 4 p.m. and 4.30 p.m. for the return
journey to NATO Headquarters.

2. Staff Group members wishing to avail themselves of this service are asked to
inform the Secretary (Ext. 2410).

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
