> # Next Meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of upcoming meetings. The first meeting is scheduled for Monday, April 19, 1971, at 3:00 p.m., with the primary objectives to discuss draft reports on the status of work in progress on studies of follow-one use and the illustrative studies and examples of release request, incorporating comments received from Capitals. The Additionally, the group will discuss a draft report by Permanent Representatives on follow-on issues prepared by the International Staff. If the scheduled time on Monday is not sufficient for full discussion, a second meeting is scheduled for Tuesday, April 20, 1971, at 10:15 a.m. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETINGS
Note by the Secretary
1. The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th April,
1971 at 3.00 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be :

(a) in the light of comments received from Capitals, to discuss further the
draft report on Status of Work in progress on Studies of Follow-one Use
(NPG/WP(71)1, 30th March, 1971);

(b) in the light of comments received from Capitals, to discuss further the
draft report on Illustrative Studies and Examples of Release Request
(NPG/WP(71)2, 1st April, 1971;

(c) to discuss a draft report by Permanent Representatives on Follow-on Issues,
prepared by the International Staff (1).

2. If time should not be sufficient on Monday afternoon for full discussion of
those matters it will be proposed that the Staff Group should meet again on
Tuesday morning, 20th April, at 10.15 a.m.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)46 – to be circulated
