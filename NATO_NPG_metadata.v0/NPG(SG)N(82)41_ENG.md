> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary regarding the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, December 13, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss the follow-up to the Ministerial Meeting and to address any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
