> # Planning Timetable 1975 (Revised)
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on March 21, 1975, outlines a revised planning timetable for the year 1975. The revision is necessary due to the shift of the next Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting from mid-May to June 16-17, 1975. The timetable includes key dates for Staff Group meetings to discuss the WINTEX 75 draft report, Phase II draft report, and the Ministerial Agenda. It also specifies the distribution of NPG Working Papers and the dates for Permanent Representatives' Meetings to take final action on reports to Ministers. The document is signed by Richard E. Shearer and serves to supplant the previous timetable found in the Annex to NPG(Staff Group)N(74)119.
