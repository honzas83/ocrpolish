> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting on Monday, February 14, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions on the initiation of Phase II of the Follow-on Use Programme, incorporating a revised paper prepared by the International Staff. The notice references the revised paper NEF(Staff Group)WP(72)1 (2nd Revision) dated February 11, 1972, and is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 14th February,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal object will be to continue the discussion of the
initiation of Phase II of the Follow-on Use Programme, taking into account the
revised paper(1) prepared by the International Staff.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
(1) NEF(Staff Group)WP(72)1 (2nd Revision), 11th February, 1972
