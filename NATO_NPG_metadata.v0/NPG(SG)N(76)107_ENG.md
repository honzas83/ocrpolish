> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of their next meeting. The meeting originally scheduled for Monday, December 6, 1976, has been postponed. It is now proposed that the next meeting of the Staff Group be held on Monday, December 13, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of this meeting will be to resume the discussion of the future work programme of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group).
