> # Notice of a Meeting Held on Monday, 12th October, 1970
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group met on October 12, 1970, to review preparations for a Ministerial meeting in Ottawa, including delegate arrival arrangements by Canadian authorities. The group discussed a draft concept for the role of theatre nuclear strike forces in the Allied Command Europe (ACE), noting a need for an amendment to the United States' figures. The agenda for the Ottawa meeting was reviewed, with specific mentions of the CENTAG Study and reports from Italy and Greece. The group also addressed modifications to the Provisional Political Guidelines (DPC/DC(69)58), specifically regarding the use of Atomic Demolition Munitions and a proposed text change by the United Kingdom. The Staff Group noted that Permanent Representatives would discuss a draft report on these guidelines. Additionally, the group discussed a report from the Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR) to the Secretary General on illustrative studies and release messages. A suggestion by the United Kingdom for a visit to CINCENT and NORTHAG was considered, and the group agreed to cancel the Permanent Representatives meeting scheduled for October 20, 1970, and set their next meeting for October 19, 1970.
