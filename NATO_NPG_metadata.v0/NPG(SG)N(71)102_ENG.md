> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 7 September 1971, announcing that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 10 September 1971, at 10:00 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss a paper produced by the International Staff regarding the CENTAG Area Study. The notice references two other documents: NPG(Staff Group)N(71)101 and NPG/Study(71)6. The document was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
