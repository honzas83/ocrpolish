> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a restricted notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated June 2, 1969. It serves as a notice for the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, scheduled for Wednesday, June 4, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers' meeting held the previous week in London, United Kingdom. The notice is signed by I.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group will take place on
Wednesday, 4th June, 1969 at 2.45 p.m. The principal item for discussion will be
the follow-on action resulting from the meeting of NPG Ministers held last week
in London.
