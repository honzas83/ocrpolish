> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, November 9, 1970, at 2:30 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss matters arising from the Ottawa meeting and to make preparations for the Nuclear Planning Advisory Committee (NDAC), Defense Planning Committee (DPC), and Council meetings scheduled for December 2nd, 3rd, and 4th, 1970, including the distribution of papers. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 9th November,
1970 at 2.30 p.m.

The principal matters for discussion will be:
(a) matters arising out of the Ottawa meeting;
(b) preparations(1) for the NDAC/DPC/Council meetings on 2nd, 3rd and 4th
December, 1970 including the distribution of papers.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(70)84, dated 5th November, 1970.
