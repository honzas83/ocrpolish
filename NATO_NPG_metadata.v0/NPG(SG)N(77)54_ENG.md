> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Tuesday, 26th July, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on July 26, 1977, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The group endorsed the Chairman's welcome to new members and noted that SHAPE would provide briefings on Nuclear Weapons Release Procedures and the Nuclear Operations Plan. Regarding new weapons technology, the group reached a consensus that a formal report would be premature and should await progress in the TMD modernisation field and the Long-Term Programme or NATO Defence Planning (LTDP). The United States representative expressed broad acceptance of the draft report but awaited specific guidance. Representatives from Canada, Germany, and the Netherlands supported a shorter paper at a later date. The group invited the United States to provide a timeframe for the TN Improvement Programme and requested the Chairman to consult with LTDP Study Groups. The meeting also addressed the follow-up to the Ottawa Ministerial meeting, noting that comments on the Maritime Nuclear Safeguards Study would be forwarded to the NPG. It was noted that a draft letter from the Secretary General to the Chairman of the Military Committee regarding NIKE HERCULES was under preparation. Finally, the group discussed the proposed dates for the 1976 Spring Ministerial meeting in Denmark, with several delegations favoring a date between April 15th and May 15th, 1977.
