> # Note by the Secretary Regarding the Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> This document is a formal note from the Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, dated March 29, 1979, notifying the Staff Group of their next meeting scheduled for Monday, April 2, 1979, at 10 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to prepare for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Permanent Representatives meeting on April 5 and the Spring 1979 NPG Ministerial meeting. Additionally, the agenda includes further discussion of issues raised in 'NATO/HEC/79' and any other business. The note is signed by I.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

INC/AD FLATNG GROUP (CFAPF GROUP)

NOTE BY THE SECRETARY

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 2nd April, 1979
at 10 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the NPG Permanent Representatives meeting on 5th April(1);

(b) preparations for the Spring 1979 NPG Ministerial meeting(2);

(c) further discussion of issues raised in 'NATO/ HEC/79 (time permitting)(3);

(d) any other business.

(Signed) I.J. ROBERTS

BRUSSELS. 1979
