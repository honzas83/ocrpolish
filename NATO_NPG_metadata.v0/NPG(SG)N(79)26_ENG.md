> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Meeting Notice
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting to be held on Monday, July 2, 1979, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines and specifically Section IV regarding the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice includes several references to previous documents, including US/ATO/DPD/OUT/IC/78-126 and various PG(Staff Group) and PG(Study/52) documents. The meeting is organized under the North Atlantic Council, North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO).

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 2nd July, 1979 at
3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
