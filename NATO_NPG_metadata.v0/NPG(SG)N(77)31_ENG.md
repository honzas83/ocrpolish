> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, April 18, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on observations from Exercise WINTEX 77, to discuss the draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial meeting in June, and to initiate discussions on the New Technology Study. Additionally, the meeting will address any other business. Following the session, attendees will have the opportunity to view a British video-taped television film on Cruise Missiles. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD

13th April, 1977

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(77)31

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 18th April, 1977,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion on the observations on Exercise WINTEX 77(1);
(b) to discuss the draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial meeting in June(2);
(c) to initiate discussion on the New Technology Study(3);
(d) to address any other business.

2. After the meeting, opportunity will be given to see a British video-taped
television film on Cruise Missiles.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(2) NPG(Staff Group)WF(77)4, 1st April, 1977
