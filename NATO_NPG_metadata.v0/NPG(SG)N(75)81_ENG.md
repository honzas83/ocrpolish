> # Draft Agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Meeting
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Acting Chairman and a draft agenda for a meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Permanent Representatives at NATO Headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. The meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, October 15, 1975, at 4:00 p.m. The Acting Chairman, J.-L. Métze, suggests that Staff Group members discuss the draft agenda at their meeting on Monday, October 6, 1975, to confirm the date and time. The draft agenda includes five main items: the agenda for the ministerial meeting (Document NTG/WP(75)4), country requests (Document MFG/WP(75)5), communicating NATO's intentions regarding varying messages (Document NPG/WP(75)6), the coalition of consultation procedures (Document NPG/WP(75)7), and any other business.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document is a draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG), the body responsible for nuclear sharing and consultation in Europe. The agenda items specifically mention 'Communicating NATO's Intention' and 'Coalition of Consultation Procedures', which are central to the mechanisms of extended deterrence and the nuclear umbrella.
> #Internal-Alliance-Dynamics/Political-Consultation-Mechanisms — The document outlines the agenda for a meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives and the NPG Staff Group, focusing on the process of aligning foreign policies and consensus building through formal consultation procedures (Item IV: 'Coalition of Consultation Procedures').
