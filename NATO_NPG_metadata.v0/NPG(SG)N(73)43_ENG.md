> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, June 4, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to continue the review of future Nuclear Planning Group work, with a specific focus on preparations for the Autumn 1973 meeting. Additionally, the meeting will address the distribution of basic Nuclear Planning Group documentation to Portugal. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 4th June, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue the review of
future NPG work(1), with particular reference to the preparations for the Autumn
1973 meeting. The distribution(2) of basic NPG documentation to Portugal will
also be mentioned.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)45, 30th May, 1973
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)44, 29th May, 1973
