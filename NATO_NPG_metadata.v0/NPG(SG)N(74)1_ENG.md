> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) First Meeting in 1974
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the first meeting of the year 1974. The meeting is scheduled for Monday, January 7, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions regarding arrangements for future Nuclear Planning Group activities and to continue preparations for the upcoming Spring Nuclear Planning Committee Ministerial meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
FIRST MEETING IN 1974
The first of the 1974 meetings of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 7th
January, 1974 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussions of arrangements for future NPG activities;
(b) to continue preparations for the Spring NPC Ministerial meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 110 Brussels.
