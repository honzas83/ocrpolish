> [!abstract] 
> # Postponement of Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 5th August, 1971. It informs members that due to a likely meeting of the Council on Monday, 9th August, and potentially Tuesday, 10th August, the Chairman proposes to postpone the next meeting of the Staff Group until Tuesday, 10th August, 1971, at 10:15 a.m. The notice was signed by J. de Gaye, the Acting Secretary, and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NPF(Staff Group)N(71)62
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
POSTPONEMENT OF NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary
As it appears almost certain that there will be a meeting of the Council on
Monday afternoon, 9th August, 1971, possibly to be continued during the
afternoon of the following day, the Chairman proposes to postpone the next
meeting of the Staff Group until Tuesday, 10th August, 1971, at 10.15 a.m.

(Signed) J. de GAYE
NATO, 1110 Brussels
