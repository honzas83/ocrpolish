> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, February 17, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the future work programme of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG), as well as any other business requiring attention. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references a previous document, NPG(Staff Group)N(74)119, dated December 13, 1974.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th February,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to discuss the NPG
future work programme(1), and any other business which requires attention.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
