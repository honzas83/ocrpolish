> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1972 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a working paper from the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated March 5, 1972, containing a draft advance press release for the upcoming Spring 1972 Ministerial meeting. The note by the Chairman, H.D. Seim, suggests that the press release be issued in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday, May 12, 1972, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating nations. The draft press release, provided in both English and French, announces that the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, consisting of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will meet in Copenhagen, Denmark, at the invitation of the Danish Government. The meeting is scheduled for May 18 and 19, 1972, under the Chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO. The document also indicates that the Staff Group is intended to discuss this initial draft at an early meeting.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE SPRING 1972 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is an initial draft in English and French of a proposed press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Copenhagen on 18th and
19th May, 1972. The text closely follows the language used in press
announcements issued during the past two years just prior to Ministerial
meetings. It is suggested that this release be made in Brussels at 3 p.m. local
time on Friday, 12th May, 1972, with simultaneous additional release in the
capitals of those countries who desire to do so.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss this initial draft at an early
meeting.

(Signed) H.D. SEIM.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
As arranged at its previous meeting in Brussels in October' 1971, the Nuclear
Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Belgium, Denmark, Germany,
Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the invitation
of the Danish Government, hold its eleventh half-yearly Ministerial meeting on
18th and 19th May, 1972, in Copenhagen, Denmark under the Chairmanship of Mr.
Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

Comme convenu lors de la précédente réunion à Bruxelles au mois d'octobre 1971,
le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement l'Allemagne,
la Belgique, le Danemark, les États-Unis, l'Italie, le Royaume-Uni et la
Turquie, tiendra à l'invitation du Gouvernement danois, sa dixième réunion
semestrielle au niveau ministériel les 18 et 19 mai à Copenhague (Danemark) sous
la présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN.
