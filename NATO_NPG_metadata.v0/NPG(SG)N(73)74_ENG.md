> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document serves as a notice for the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), scheduled for Monday, August 13, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the NATO Military Authorities reports regarding the Early Combined Use of conventional and nuclear capabilities, and to initiate a discussion on the Military Committee's report concerning the implementation of Special ADM Guidelines. The notice was issued by M.H.G. Wilson from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions a discussion on the 'Early Combined Use of conventional and nuclear capabilities' and the 'implementation of Special ADM Guidelines'. ADM (Air Deliverable Munitions) in a NATO context typically refers to tactical nuclear weapons, and 'Combined Use' refers to the sharing and coordination of these assets among allies.
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The meeting's purpose is to discuss reports on the 'Early Combined Use of conventional and nuclear capabilities' and 'Special ADM Guidelines', both of which pertain to the internal mechanisms for planning the execution and use of nuclear weapons.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 13th August,
1973, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion on the NATO Military Authorities reports on Early
Combined Use of conventional and nuclear capabilities(1);

(b) to hold an initial discussion on the Military Committee's report(2) on the
implementation of Special ADM Guidelines.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
