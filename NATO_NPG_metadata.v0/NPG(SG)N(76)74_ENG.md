> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting on Monday, August 30, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to reach an agreement on the final text for the Nike Hercules Terms of Reference paper, continue preparations for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting scheduled for Autumn 1976, and discuss the next steps in processing the Compilation paper. The notice includes several references to previous documents, including contributions from the German and Canadian delegations. The document was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 30th August, 1976
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to reach agreement on a final text for the NIKE HERCULES Terms of Reference
paper (1),(2);

(b) to continue preparations for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Autumn
1976(3),(4),(5);

(c) to discuss the next steps in processing the Compilation paper(6),(7),(8).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
