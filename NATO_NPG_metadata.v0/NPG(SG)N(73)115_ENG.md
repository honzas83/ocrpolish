> # Draft Agenda for the NDAC Ministerial Meeting on 7th December, 1973
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the draft agenda for the upcoming Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee (NDAC) Ministerial Meeting on December 7, 1973, in Brussels. The Chairman, H.B. Seim, proposes that the draft agenda be discussed at the Staff Group meeting on November 19, 1973, to reach a consensus on a final version for circulation. The draft agenda itself consists of three main items: a report on Nuclear Planning Group activities during 1973, based on documents NDAC/D(73)1, NPG/D(73)7 (Ankara Agreed Minute), and NPG/D(73)16 (The Hague Agreed Minute), any other business, and the preparation of a communique to be addressed as part of the DPC communique. The document originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

DRAFT AGENDA FOR THE NDAC MINISTERIAL
MEETING ON 7TH DECEMBER, 1973

Note by the Chairman

Attached is a draft of a possible agenda for the NDAC Ministerial Meeting on 7th
December, 1973. Since all NDAC countries are now represented in the Staff Group,
I propose that the draft be addressed at our meeting on Monday, 19th November,
with a view to reaching consensus on a final agenda that can be circulated
within the next week or so.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
