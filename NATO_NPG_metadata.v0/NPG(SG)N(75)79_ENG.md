> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting to be held on Monday, October 1, 1975. The agenda items for this meeting include the discussion of CNI 'arming messages', the agenda for the meeting of the Permanent Representatives on October 15, 1975, the initial discussion of the Future Work Programme, and the advance press release for the autumn meeting. Additionally, the note mentions that the subsequent meeting of the Staff Group on October 13, 1975, is intended to cover the first draft of the Phase III Report and the first draft of the Future Work Programme, as well as CNI procedures if necessary. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday 1st October 1975.
The principal matters to be discussed are as follows:

(a) CNI 'arming Messages(1). The paper should then become an NPG Working Paper;
(b) the agenda for the meeting of the Permanent Representatives to be held on
15th October 1975;

(c) Future work Programme - Initial discussion;
(d) Advance Press Release for the autumn meeting(2);
(e) any other matters.

?

For the information of members it is hoped that, at the following meeting of the
Staff Group to be held on Honda, 13th October it will be possible to discuss:

(a) the first draft of the Phase III Report(3);
(b) the first draft of the Future Work Programme(4);
(c) if deemed necessary, CN1 Procedures.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
