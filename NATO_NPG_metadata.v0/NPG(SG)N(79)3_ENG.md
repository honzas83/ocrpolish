> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Thursday, 18th January 1979, at 10 a.m. The meeting will be held in the Chairman's office (I.352) in Brussels, Belgium. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with the rest of the agenda including any other business. The notice includes references to several previous documents, including USNATO/DDP/OUT/NS/78-126, NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)8, NPG(Staff Group)N(78)76, and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)81. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 18th January,
1979 at 10 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1);
(b) any other business.
2. The meeting will be held in the Chairman's office (I.352), unless members are
advised to the contrary.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
