> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on January 30, 1974, announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 4, 1974, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss an initial draft of a Permanent Representative's report regarding arrangements for the Study of the Political and Military Implications of Technological Developments, and to continue preparations for the upcoming Spring Nuclear Planning Committee (NPC) Ministerial meeting, including the determination of dates. The notice is signed by N.L.C. Park and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)13

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 4th February,
1974, at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss an initial draft(1) of a possible Permanent Representative's
report on arrangements for the Study of the Political and Military Implications
of Technological Developments;

(b) to continue preparations for the Spring NPC Ministerial meeting, including
discussion of dates.

(Signed) N.L.C. PARK

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)2, 30th January, 1974
