> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, January 8, 1979, at 3 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes a briefing on targeting, an initial discussion of the draft Permanent Representatives' report on the status of TNF modernisation, and a discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines. The Secretary, D.J. Roberts, requests that any individuals other than regular Staff Group members who intend to attend the meeting provide their names and security clearance certificates. The document references several other documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)9, USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126, NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)8, NPG(Staff Group)N(78)76, and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)81.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th January, 1979
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) briefing on targeting;
(b) initial discussion of the draft Permanent Representatives' report on the
status of TNF modernisation(1);

(c) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(2);
(d) any other business.

2. The - briefing under item (a) above will be CTS ATOMAL; if anyone other than
regular Staff Group members intend to be present at the meeting, they are
requested to send me their names together with a copy of their security
clearance certificate.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
