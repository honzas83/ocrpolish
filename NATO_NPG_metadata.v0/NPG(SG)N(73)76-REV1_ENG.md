> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled a meeting for August 20, 1973, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the third revision of a draft report by NPG Permanent Representatives on Consultation Procedures and Facilities, and to consider the first draft of a possible report by NPG Permanent Representatives on the Military Committee report regarding the implementation of Special ADM Guidelines. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and references several working papers and reports, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)7, NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)9, and NPG/D(73)9.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 20th August, 1973
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the third revision of a draft report by NPG Permanent
Representatives on Consultation Procedures and Facilities(1);

(b) to consider the first draft of a possible report by NPG Permanent
Representatives(2) on the Military Committee report on implementation of Special
ADM Guidelines(3).

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
