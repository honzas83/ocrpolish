> # Future Meetings of Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a detailed schedule of proposed meetings for the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives. These meetings are intended to prepare for the NPG Ministerial Meeting scheduled for October 26th and 27, 1972. The first proposed meeting is set for September 14, 1972, to present the Denmark Follow-On Use Study. The second, provisional meeting on September 29, 1972, is dedicated to the consideration and approval of draft reports to Ministers. The third meeting is a preliminary review of the ACLANT Illustrative Study and a final exchange of views on Ministerial Agenda topics. The notice also specifies that it supersedes the previous Staff Group Notice NPC(Staff Group)N(72)56. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson of NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NGC(Staff Group)N(72)61

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
FUTURE MEETINGS OF NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES

Note by the Secretary

For convenience and forward planning, the Chairman
has instructed me to recall the discussion held in the Staff
Group on 3rd July on the dates for meetings of the NPG Permanent
Representatives in preparation for the NPG Ministerial Meeting on
26th and 27th October, 1972. The proposed dates are as follows:

(a) Thursday, 14th September, 1972, at 3.30 p.m.
Presentation of the Denmark Follow-On Use Study
by the Study team. (The Danish Authorities have confirmed that this date, which
was at first

doubtful, will now be convenient to them).

(b) Friday, 29th September, 1972, at 10.15 a.m.
Consideration and approval of draft reports to
Ministers. This meeting on 29th September depends,
however, on the progress achieved in preparing
the draft reports and must therefore, for the time
being, be considered as provisional. (A meeting
on 28th September would conflict with the 20th
Anniversary of AGARD.)

(c) Thursday, 12th October, 1972, at 3.30 p.m.
Preliminary review of the ACLANT Illustrative
Study; final exchange of views on Ministerial
Agenda topics.

2. This notice supersedes the Staff Group Notice
NPC(Staff Group)N(72)56, of 14th June, 1972.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
