> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Tuesday, August 17, 1971, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary focus will be the discussion of Special ADM Guidelines and a draft status report on studies regarding Follow-on use. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents, including NPG/D(71)9, NPG(Staff Group)N(71)83, and NPG(Staff Group)N(71)84.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Tuesday, 17th August, 1971,
at 3 p.m. The principal matters for attention will be:

(a) Special ADM Guidelines(1)(2);
(b) Draft status report of work in progress on studies on Follow-on use(3).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
