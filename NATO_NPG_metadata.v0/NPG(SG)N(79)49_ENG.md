> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Thursday, December 20, 1979, at 9:45 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to conduct further discussion on the revised draft of Section IV(1) of the General Political Guidelines. Additionally, the meeting will address any other business. The notice is issued by D.J. Roberts on behalf of the North Atlantic Council, North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO), in Brussels, Belgium.
