> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 15, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft report to the Nuclear Planning Group (NDAC), to continue discussions on Country Request Procedures, and to hold a final discussion on Nuclear Consultation - CNI Procedures. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents, including NPG(Staff Group)N(76)16, NPG(Staff Group)N(76)17, and NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)1(2nd Revise) with its Corrigendum.

NPG(Staff Group)N(76)21

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th March, 1976
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the draft report to the NDAC(1);

(b) to continue the discussion of Country Request Procedures(2);

(c) to hold a final discussion on Nuclear Consultation – CNI Procedures(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

and its Corrigendum, 9th March, 1976
