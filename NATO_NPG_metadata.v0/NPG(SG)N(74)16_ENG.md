> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, February 11, 1974, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to resume discussions on the initial draft of a Permanent Representative's report regarding the arrangements for the Study of the Political and Military Implications of Technological Developments. Additionally, the meeting will focus on continuing preparations for the upcoming Spring Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting. The notice was signed by N.L.C. Park and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 11th February,
1974, at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to resume discussion, as appropriate, of the initial draft(1) of a possible
Permanent Representative's report on arrangements for the Study of the Political
and Military Implications of Technological Developments;

(b) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial meeting.
(Signed) N.L.C. PARK
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)2, 30th January, 1974
