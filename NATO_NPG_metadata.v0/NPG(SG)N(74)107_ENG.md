> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, October 21, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss possible procedures for the coordination of work related to the Nunn Amendment. The notice references a previous document, NPG(Staff Group)N(74)96, dated September 20, 1974.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 21st October,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to discuss possible
procedures for co-ordination of work in connection with the Nunn Amendment(1).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
