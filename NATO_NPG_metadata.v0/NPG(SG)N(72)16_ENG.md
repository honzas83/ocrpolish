> # NPG(Staff Group)N(72)16
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group met to discuss a revised draft and its general principles and detailed wording, agreeing to several changes. While most countries, including the United States, found the compromise draft acceptable, some representatives insisted that the report should be submitted to NPG Permanent Representatives rather than directly to Ministers. The International Staff is tasked with the prepare a fourth revised text reflecting these divergent views. Additionally, the representatives of Italy, Belgium, Canada, and Turkey were invited to meet separately to further examine their positions and to prepare texts for insertion into the next draft. The meeting also addressed the 'Studies of Follow-on Tactical Use – The Turkish Study', noting that the SHAPE review on the study of the Turkish Thrace and Northwestern Turkey Area had been received and requesting a first draft report by NPG Permanent Representatives. The Staff Group also expressed concern regarding the remaining time to complete the current work programme according to the schedule and agreed that additional meetings may be necessary. The next meeting of the Staff Group is scheduled for Monday, 21st February, 1972.

THE WORK PROGRAMME OF THE STAFF GROUP
4. The STAFF GROUP:
