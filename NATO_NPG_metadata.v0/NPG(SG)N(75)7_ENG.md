> [!abstract] 
> # NPG Study Meetings - 20, 21 and 22 January, 1975: Attendance
> 
> This document is a formal note by the Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, M.H.C. Wilson, detailing the attendance for the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) study meetings scheduled for January 20, 21, and 22, 1975. The meetings took place at NATO Headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. The document lists representatives from various member states, including Belgium, Canada, Denmark, Germany, Germany, Greece, Italy, Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States. It also includes personnel from the International Staff, the International Military Staff, Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE), and the Supreme Allied Command Atlantic (SACLANT). The list serves as anatomical record of the participants from military and civilian experts across the allied nations.
