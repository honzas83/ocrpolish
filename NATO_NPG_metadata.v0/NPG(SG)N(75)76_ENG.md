> [!abstract] 
> # Status of Current Work
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a detailed status update on the current tasks and next actions for its members as of September 29, 1975. The list includes items such as the draft ministerial agenda, compilation of documents for Permanent Representatives, Permanent Representative meeting dates, country requests, and future Nuclear Planning Group ministerial meetings. Additionally, it outlines the timeline for the Phase III paper, CNI warning and procedures, and force posture issues. The document is signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
