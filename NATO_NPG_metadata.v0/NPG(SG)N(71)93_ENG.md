> # Implementation of Special ADM Guidelines
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 23rd August 1971. It suggests the insertion of a new first paragraph into a revised draft paper to be discussed at the meeting on 30th August. The proposed paragraph defines the purpose of the report as summarizing the current status of implementation of the Special Political Guidelines for the Fossile Use of Atomic Demolition Munitions (ADM) and other related measures. The notice also mentions that if the proposed addition is accepted, existing paragraphs in the revised draft will be renumbered and minor editorial changes will be made. The document references the Special ADM Guidelines (DPC/D(70)60) and a revised draft paper (NPG(Staff Group)N(71)83(Revised)).

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
IMPLEMENTATION OF SPECIAL ADM GUIDELINES
Note by the Chairman
At its meeting on 30th August, the Staff Group is expected to discuss a revised
draft paper (1) on the implementation of special ADM Guidelines. On that
occasion, I suggest that the Staff Group also consider inserting the following
as a new first paragraph of the revised draft:

"PURPOSE.
1. This report summarises the current status of implementation of the Special
Political Guidelines for the Fossile Use of Atomic Demolition Munitions (1) and
certain other measures related to ADMS.

(1) DPC/D(70)60, 10th December, 1970, hereafter referred to as Special ADM
Guidelines."

2. If this proposed additional paragraph is acceptable, all present paragraphs
in the revised draft should be renumbered and some minor editorial changes
should be made in the first sentence of the present first paragraph.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.
NATO, 1110, Brussels
