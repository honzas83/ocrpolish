> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Tuesday, April 8, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The agenda for the meeting will focus on three principal matters: determining the date of the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives, the finalization and release of the Atomic Demolition Munition Time Factors paper, and the discussion of the role of theatre nuclear strike forces. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several working papers, including NPG(staff Group)WP(69)5 and NPG(staff Group)WP(69)4, as well as a draft from Canada and the United Kingdom.

4th April, 1969
NOTICE
NPG(staff Group)N(69)34
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
As agreed by the staff Group, its next meeting will take place on Tuesday, 8th
April, 1969 at 2.45 p.m. The principal matters for attention will be:

(a) the date of the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent
representatives;

(b) the release of the atomic Demolition Munition Time Factors paper(1) as a
final document;

(c) the rôle of theatre nuclear strike forces(2).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
OTAN/NAТО, Brussels, 39.
(1) NPG(staff Group)WP(69)5, dated 28th March, 1969.
(2) NPG(staff Group)WP(69)4, dated 5th March, 1969, and the Canadian/United
Kingdom draft (revised version to be circulated.)
