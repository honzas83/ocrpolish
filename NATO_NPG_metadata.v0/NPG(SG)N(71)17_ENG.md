> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) to schedule the next meeting for Monday, February 8, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions regarding the various activities of the Staff Group during the period leading up to the meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Ministers in May. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th February,
1971, at 3 p.m.

The principal object of the meeting will be to continue discussion of the
various activities of the Staff Group during the period leading up to the
meeting of NPG Ministers in May.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
