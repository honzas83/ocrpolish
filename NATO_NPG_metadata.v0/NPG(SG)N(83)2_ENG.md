> [!abstract] 
> # NPG Ministerial Meetings
> 
> This document, a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, serves as a reference for Staff Group members. It contains a detailed chronological list of 32 Ministerial meetings held between Spring 1967 in Washington, United States, and Autumn 1982 in Brussels, Belgium. The list includes the location and the timing of theunited states, Federal Republic of Germany, United Kingdom, United States, Italy, Canada, Denmark, Norway, Belgium, and Turkey. It also provides a tally of the number of times each participating country has acted as host, and notes the dates when certain countries, such as Portugal, Luxembourg, and Spain, began participating at the Ministerial level. The document was issued by NATO in Brussels, Belgium, on 21st January 1985.
