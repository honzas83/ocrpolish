> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1973 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, H.B. Seim, regarding a proposed advance press release for the Spring 1973 Ministerial meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place in Ankara, Turkey, on May 15th and 16th, 1973, under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph Luns. The Chairman suggests that the press release be issued in Brussels on Tuesday, May 8th, 1973, at 3 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The draft text of the press release, provided in both English and French, identifies the participating nations as Canada, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The Staff Group is intended to discuss the draft text and timing of the release at its meeting on March 26th to develop a proposal for the delegations to forward to their respective capitals for consideration.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE SPRING 1973 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Ankara on 15th and 16th
May, 1973. The text closely follows the language used in press announcements
issued during the past three years just prior to Ministerial meetings. As on
past occasions, the release might be made a few days before the meeting takes
place; accordingly, it is suggested that an announcement might be made in
Brussels on Tuesday, 8th May, 1973 at 3 p.m. local time, with simultaneous
additional release in the capitals of those participating countries who so
desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 26th March with a view to developing a proposal
that delegations concerned may forward to capitals for consideration.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

As arranged at its previous meeting in London in October, 1972, the Nuclear
Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Canada, Germany, Italy, the
Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at
the invitation of the Government of Turkey, hold its thirteenth half-yearly
Ministerial meeting in Ankara, Turkey on 15th and 16th May, 1973, under the
chairmanship of Mr. Joseph Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

Comme convenu lors de la précédente réunion à Londres au mois d’octobre 1972, le
Groupe des plans nucléaires de l’OTAN qui comprend actuellement le Canada, les
États-Unis, l’Italie, la Norvège, les Pays-Bas, la République Fédérale
d’Allemagne, le Royaume-Uni et la Turquie, tiendra à l’invitation du
Gouvernement turque, sa 13ème réunion semestrielle au niveau ministériel les 15
et 16 mai à Ankara sous la présidence de M. Joseph Luns, Secrétaire Général de
l’OTAN.
