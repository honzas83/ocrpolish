> # Nuclear Planning Group (St.1F Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, announcing the next meeting of the Staff Group on Monday, April 27, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary subjects for informal discussion during this meeting will include ancillary problems connected with Follow-on studies and the NORTHAG study, provided it is received in time. The note was signed by M.I.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
