> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 8th August, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on August 8, 1977. The agenda items included a briefing from SHAPE on recent refinements and adjustments to the Nuclear Planning Group's Nuclear Weapons Release Procedures. The group also discussed communications security during the WINTEL 77 exercise, referencing memorandum IASN-1/7-77, and agreed to determine how to bring this matter to the attention of Ministers. Additionally, the group reviewed a recommendation by SACLANT regarding the participation of headquarters in exercises, as detailed in the draft report AC/237-11/12(Revised). The meeting concluded with the agreement to defer consideration of a draft report by Permanent Representatives on the study of nuclear weapons release in the NATO area from August 16th to August 25th due to some delegations not receiving instructions in time. Future meetings were scheduled for August 16th and 23rd, 1977.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 8th
August, 1977 at 1.00 p.m.

I. NUCLEAR WEAPONS RELEASE PROCEDURES
The STA`F GROUP:
(1) received a briefing from SHAPE on recent refinements and adjustments to the
NPGs' Nuclear Weapons Release Procedures;

(2) noted the replies given by the SHAPE Representatives to a number of
questions put to them.

II. COMMUNICATIONS SECURITY DURING WINTEL 77
The STAF GROUP:
(1) had its attention drawn by the Chairmen to the IIE memorandum on this
subject - IASN-1/7-77;

(2) agreed to consider at the next meeting how the matter might best be brought
to the attention of Ministers.

III. PARTICIPATION OF HEADQUARTERS IN EXERCISES
The STAF GROUP:
(1) had its attention drawn by the Chairmen to the recommendation by SACLANT in
paragraph 5 of the Council Operations and Exercises Committee's revised draft
report on the participation of Headquarters in Exercises
(AC/237-11/12(Revised));
