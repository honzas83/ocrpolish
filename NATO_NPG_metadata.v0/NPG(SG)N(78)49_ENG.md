> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, July 10, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to further discuss the NATO Military Authorities' proposals for short-term measures to improve NATO's TIIF. The agenda also includes any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents, including NPG/Study/52 and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)40, as well as a letter from the United Kingdom Representative.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 10th July, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) further discussion of the NATO Military Authorities' proposals for short-
term measures to improve NATO's TIIF(1);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG/Study/52, 23rd March, 1978, NPG(Staff Group)N(78)40, 7th June, 1978 and
the United Kingdom Representative's letter of 9th June, 1978
