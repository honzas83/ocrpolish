> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Cocktail Party for the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the International Staff Nuclear Planning Directorate. It serves as an invitation to a cocktail party for the members of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, scheduled for August 19, 1973, at 5:30 p.m. The purpose of the event is to bid farewell to Mr. John Eisele and Colonel Cul Vencetanos. The notice is associated with the North Atlantic Council and the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
The Director and members of the International Staff Nuclear Planning Directorate
requested the pleasure of the company to hold a cocktail party for the members
of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group at the Nuclear Planning Group Staff
Group at 19th August, 1973, at 5.30 p.m., to bid farewell to Mr. John Eisele and
to it. Colonel Cul Vencetanos.
