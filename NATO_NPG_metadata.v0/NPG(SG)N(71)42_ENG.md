> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, April 5, 1971, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue preparations for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting. This includes a discussion regarding the Military Committee Report on the Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in the Allied Command Europe (ACE). The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions a discussion on the 'Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in ACE', which directly relates to the deployment and role of nuclear weapons in the European theatre (ACE - Allied Command Europe).

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th April, 1971
at 3.00 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue
preparations for the NPG Ministerial Meeting, including in this respect a
discussion of the Military Committee Report on the Concept for the Role of
Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in ACE(1).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
