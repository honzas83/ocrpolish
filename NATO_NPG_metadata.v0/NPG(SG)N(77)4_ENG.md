> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, January 17, 1977, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to further discuss the Draft Report by Permanent Representatives on Future Work Priorities and to examine any other business. The notice references several documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)9 and NPG(Staff Group)N(77)3, as well as a Draft Status of Work Report to be circulated. The document was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
