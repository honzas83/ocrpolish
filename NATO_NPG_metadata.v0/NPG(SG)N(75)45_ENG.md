> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Tuesday, 20th May and Wednesday 21st May, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on May 20th and 21st, 1975. The meeting focused on several key agenda items, including observations on WINTELX 75 and the agreement on a report by Permanent Representatives to Ministers. The group discussed the future work programme, noting a statement by the Chairman regarding a Staff Group paper on future work and planning for a Secretary General's FO for discussion by Ministers in Monterey. The Staff Group also reviewed a report on the work programme concerning the follow-on tactical use of nuclear weapons, agreeing to changes in a composite draft and requesting the International Staff to prepare a final text for dispatch to Capitals. Additionally, the meeting touched upon arrangements for the upcoming meeting at Monterey.
