> # Mediterranean Maritime Follow-on Use Study
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 7th April 1972, proposing modifications to a draft report on the Mediterranean Maritime Area Follow-on Use Study. The Chairman, H.B. Seim, notes that while a draft report was previously circulated, it was agreed that changes could be made once comments from the NATO Military Authorities (NMA) were received. Having received feedback from SACLAN T and SACEUR, the Chairman suggests that the Staff Group discuss these changes to include them in a corrigendum for the Permanent Representatives' consideration on 15th April. The document provides specific textual substitutions and additions for pages 3, 4, 6, and 7 of the draft report, ensuring that the points regarding the Mediterranean Maritime Study are based in part on NMA comments and that assessments of escalation risk are questioned by SHAPE. The process ensures that military perspectives from SACLAN T and SACLAN T and SACEUR are integrated into the final report.

7th April, 1972

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(72)37

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

MEDITERRANEAN MARITIME FOLLOW-ON USE STUDY

Note by the Chairman

Staff Group members will recall that it was agreed to circulate a draft
report(1) on the Mediterranean Maritime Area Follow-on Use Study before the
comments from the NATO Military Authorities were received, with the
understanding that the Staff Group might make proposals to modify the draft when
the NMA views became available. Comments have now been received from SACLAN T(2)
and SACEUR(3).

2. The Staff Group may therefore wish to discuss the attached changes with a
view to including them in a corrigendum for consideration by the Permanent
Representatives when they address the draft report on 15th April.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

This document consists of 2 pages

(1) NPG/WP(72)/7, 31st March, 1972
(2) NPG/Study/21, 6th April, 1972
(3) NPG/Study/22, 7th April, 1972

NPG(Staff Group)N(72)37

SUGGESTED CHANGES TO NPG/VP(72)/7

TO REFLECT NMA COMMENTS

1. Page 3, paragraph 5, delete the first sentence and substitute the following:

"5. In their consideration of the Mediterranean Maritime Study, the Ministers
may wish to draw on the several possible areas for discussion set out in the
following paragraphs, which are based in part on comments by the NATO Military
Authorities(1).

(1) SACLAN T Review – NPG/Study/21, 6th April, 1972 SACEUR Review –
NPG/Study/22, 7th April, 1972

2. Page 4, paragraph 8, change first sentence to read:

"8. The Study confirms(1) and the comments of the NATO Military Authorities
reinforce(2) a general point ....

(1) Main Study, paragraphs 20, 29, 39, 50

(2) NPG/Study/21, paragraphs 11, 12; NPG/Study/22, paragraphs 11, 15"

3. Page 6, paragraph 15, add the following sentence at the end of the paragraph:

"The Study's assessment of the escalation risk is also questioned by SHAPE(1).

(1) NPG/Study/22, paragraphs 5, 10, 12, 16"

4. Page 7, paragraph 14, insert the following after the first sentence:

"In this context, SHAPE points out that this conclusion is not supported by any
analysis(1).

(1) NPG/Study/22, paragraph 14"
