> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, November 13, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to examine the international staff's report on Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) activities, continue discussions regarding participation at Nuclear Forces Group (NFG) Ministerial Meetings, and begin preparations for the Spring Meeting of NPG Ministers. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson. It also references several documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)20, NPG(Staff Group)N(72)99, and NPG(Staff Group)N(72)102.

NPG(Staff Group)N(72)101
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 13th November,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to examine the international Staff's report on NPG activities(1);
(b) to continue the discussion on participation at NFG Ministerial Meetings(2);
(c) to begin preparations for the Spring Meeting of NPG Ministers(3).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)20, to be circulated
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(72)99, 7th November, 1972
(3) NPG(Staff Group)N(72)102, to be circulated
