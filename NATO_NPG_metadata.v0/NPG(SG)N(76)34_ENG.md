> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Friday, April 23, 1976, at 2:30 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting and to hold an initial discussion on the presentation of newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation. Additionally, the meeting will cover any other matters as required. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on
Friday, 23rd April, 1976 at 2.30 p.m. The principal objects of
this meeting will be:

(a) to continue preparations for the Spring NEF
Ministerial Meeting;

(b) to hold an initial discussion on the presentation
of Newly Developed Procedures in the Field of
Nuclear Consultation(1);

(c) to discuss any other matters.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NFG(Staff Group)WF(76)2, 30th March, 1976
