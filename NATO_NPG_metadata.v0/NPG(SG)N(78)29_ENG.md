> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Tuesday, May 2, 1978, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to discuss the revised draft Permanent Representatives' report on NIKE HERCULES Adjustments and to conduct follow-up actions from the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. Additionally, the meeting will address any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 2nd May, 1978 at
3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the revised draft Permanent Representatives' report on NIKE
HERCULES Adjustments(1);

(b) follow-up to the Spring, 1978 NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)2(Revised), 4th April, 1978
