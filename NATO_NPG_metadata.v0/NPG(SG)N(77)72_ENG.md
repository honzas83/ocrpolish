> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, October 17, 1977, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the follow-up to the Bari Meeting and to consider any other business. The notice was issued by M.H.G. Wilson in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th October,
1977 at 3.00 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the Follow-up to the Bari Meeting;
(b) to consider any other business.

1110 Brussels.
