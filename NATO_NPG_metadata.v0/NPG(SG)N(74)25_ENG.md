> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, March 11, 1974, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions regarding a possible Permanent Representatives' report on the Military Committee Function within the nuclear consultation process and to continue preparations for the upcoming Spring Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 11th March, 1974,
at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of a possible Permanent Representatives' report(1) on
the Military Committee Function in the Nuclear Consultation process;

(b) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)3(Revised), 5th March, 1974
