> [!abstract] 
> # NPG Ministerial Meetings
> 
> This document, issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a comprehensive overview of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meetings. It includes a list of all Ministerial meetings that have taken place from 1967 to 1975, detailing the location and host country for each meeting. The document also provides a summary of the total number of meetings hosted by each participating country as of July 1, 1975. Additionally, it is accompanied by a diagram showing the projected participation of permanent members (Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Italy, United Kingdom, and United States) and rotating members (Canada, Netherlands, Norway, Belgium, Belgium, Denmark, Greece, and Turkey) for the period 1975-1978 based on the agreement on NPG membership. It references the agreement on NPG membership agreement NDAC/D(69)/4.
