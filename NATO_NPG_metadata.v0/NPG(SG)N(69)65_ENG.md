> # Notes of a Meeting Held at NATO Headquarters on 15th September, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters on 15th September 1969. The group agreed to changes in the wording of certain agenda items for the NPG Ministerial Meeting scheduled for 11th and 12th November 1969 near Washington. Additionally, the group discussed the 'Further Studies on follow-on use of tactical nuclear weapons' and requested the International Staff to prepare a new paper on the subject. The meeting also addressed the scheduling of meetings for the NPG Permanent Representatives during October 1969 and noted the upcoming circulation of reports from the United Kingdom, Germany, Belgium, and the United States. Finally, the group scheduled its next meeting for 22nd September 1969.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at NATO Headquarters on 15th September, 1969
REVISED AGENDA FOR THE NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING TO BE HELD NEAR WASHINGTON ON
11TH AND 12TH NOVEMBER, 1969

1. The STAFF GROUP :
(a) agreed to some changes in the wording of Agenda Items I.1 and I.2;
(b) agreed to make no change to the wording of Agenda Items II-VII;
(c) invited the International Staff to circulate the agenda revised in the light
of the discussion, and expressed the hope that the new draft would in due course
be adopted as the final version.

FOLLOW-ON TACTICAL USE
2. The STAFF GROUP :
(a) agreed that the most appropriate title for the matter was "Further Studies
on follow-on use of tactical nuclear weapons (vide paragraph 10 of NFG/D(69)5)";

(b) took note of the draft terms of reference prepared by the International
Staff (1);

MEETINGS OF THE NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES
