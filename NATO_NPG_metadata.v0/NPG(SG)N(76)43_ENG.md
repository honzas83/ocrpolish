> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 17th May, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on May 17, 1976, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting covered several key items: the agenda for the upcoming June Ministerial Meeting, which will include a document on improving the effectiveness of NATO's theatre nuclear forces (NPG/D(76)4), and the newly issued CNI procedures paper (NPG/D(76)5). The Staff Group also discussed the future work programme and the transmission of preliminary national views on improving the effectiveness of NATO's theatre nuclear forces, agreeing to a deadline of May 28 for national inputs. Additionally, the group continued its examination of a paper on the presentation of newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation, with further discussion scheduled for the next meeting. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next Staff Group meeting on May 24, 1976.

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 17th

SECURITY
1. The STAFF GROUP: took note that all persons present were cleared to the
security classification of the matters to be discussed.

AGENDA FOR NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING IN JUNE
2. The STAFF GROUP: took note of a statement by the Chairman who said that the
Ministerial Agenda, shortly to be issued, would include a third reference
document for Item II; this would be the Transmission of Preliminary National
Views on Improving the Effectiveness of NATO's Theatre Nuclear Forces
(NPG/D(76)4).

NUCLEAR CONSULTATION - CNI PROCEDURES
3. The STAFF GROUP: took note of a statement by the Chairman who recalled that
14th May had been the deadline for approval of the CNI paper; this would now be
issued as NPG/D(76)5.

FUTURE WORK PROGRAMME

4. The STAFF GROUP: noted the remarks of the Chairman who said that the PO to
Permanent Representatives on the Future Work Programme would be circulated
shortly.

TRANSMISSION OF PRELIMINARY NATIONAL VIEWS ON IMPROVING THE EFFECTIVENESS OF
NATO'S THEATRE NUCLEAR FORCES

5. The STAFF GROUP:

(a) discussed the International Staff paper(1) and agreed to a number of
changes;

(b) agreed that the deadline for the submission of national inputs should be
28th May;

(c) agreed that any inputs arriving after the deadline but prior to the
Ministerial Meeting would be distributed as addenda to the transmission paper.

PRESENTATION OF NEWLY DEVELOPED PROCEDURES IN THE FIELD OF NUCLEAR CONSULTATION

6. The STAFF GROUP:

(a) continued its examination of the International Staff's paper on the
Presentation of Newly Developed Procedures and agreed to a number of changes in
the text;

(b) agreed to continue the discussion of this paper at the next meeting with a
view to reaching agreement on the main text and the annexes, and to circulating
the paper subsequently as an-NPG Working Paper.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP

7. The STAFF GROUP: agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 24th May, 1976 at
3 p.m.
