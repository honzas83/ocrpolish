> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a notice announcing that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, February 28, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the initiation of Phase II of the follow-on use programme, taking into account recent informal contacts, and to continue the discussion of the initial draft of a report by Permanent Representatives on the Turkish Follow-on Use Study. Additionally, the meeting will hold an initial discussion of the SHAPE Illustrative Study on Battlefield Use. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document discusses the 'follow-on use programme' and the 'Turkish Follow-on Use Study', which relate to the deployment and sharing of nuclear weapons on allied territory (specifically Turkey) as part of NATO's extended deterrence framework.
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The document mentions the 'SHAPE Illustrative Study on Battlefield Use', which indicates internal mechanisms for planning the tactical execution and use of nuclear weapons on the battlefield.

23rd February, 1972
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(72)21
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 28th February,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal objects will be:

(a) to continue the discussion of the initiation of Phase II of the follow-on
use programme, taking into account the results of the informal contacts which
are taking place this week;

(b) to continue the discussion of the initial draft of a report by Permanent
Representatives on the Turkish Follow-on Use Study(1);

(c) to hold an initial discussion of the SHAPE Illustrative Study on Battlefield
Use(2).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)2, 17th February, 1972
(2) NPG/Study/14, 1st February, 1972
