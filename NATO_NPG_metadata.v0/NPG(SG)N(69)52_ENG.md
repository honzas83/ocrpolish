> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled a meeting for June 16, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to conduct a detailed discussion on the draft terms of reference for further analyses of the Air Defense Main (ADM) problem in selected areas of the Allied Command Europe (ACE). Additionally, the meeting will address the arrangements for preparing draft terms of reference for longer-term studies related to the Anglo-German guidelines paper. The agenda also includes the distribution of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) papers to members of the Nuclear Defense Advisory Committee (NDAC). The document is signed by H.G. Wilson, the Secretary, and originates from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

As agreed at its last meeting, the Staff Group will next meet on Monday, 16th
June, 1969, at 2.45 p.m.

The principal subjects for discussion are as follows :

- detailed discussion of the draft terms of reference (1) for the conduct of
further analyses of the ADM problem in certain selected areas of ACE;

- further discussion of the arrangements for preparing draft terms of reference
for the longer term studies related to the Anglo-German guidelines paper;

- distribution of NPG papers to NDAC members.

(Signed): H.G. WILSON
