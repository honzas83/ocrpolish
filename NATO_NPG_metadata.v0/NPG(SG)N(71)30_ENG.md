> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on 4th March 1971. It specifies that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th March 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions regarding activities and preparations leading up to the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting scheduled for the end of May 1971. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
