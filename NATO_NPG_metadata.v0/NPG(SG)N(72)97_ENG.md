> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 31st October 1972, scheduling the next meeting for Monday, 6th November 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the results of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial in London and to examine the necessary follow-up actions. The notice references two other documents: an Agreed Minute (NPG/D(72)18) and a follow-up action document (NPG(Staff Group)N(72)96), both of which are to be circulated.

# Page 1

31st October, 1972

NOTICE
MPG(Staff Group)N(72)97

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 6th November,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to discuss the
results(1) of the NPG Ministerial in London, and to examine the various follow-
up actions required(2).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

(1) Agreed Minute, NPG/D(72)18 – to be circulated
(2) Follow-up action, NPG(Staff Group)N(72)96 – to be circulated
