> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 7th April, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO headquarters in Brussels on April 7, 1975. The group discussed several documents, including POLADS(75)10 regarding HILEX-7, a draft report by the COEC on VINTEX 75, and a document on the nuclear aspects of the Nunn Amendment. The meeting also addressed the NPG Staff Group Planning Timetable, noting the convenience of May 12, 1975, for a meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives and adjusting the schedule for the meeting on Phase II action to June 3, 1975. Additionally, the group discussed reports on WINTEX 75, clarifying that the Military Committee had met privately to discuss the exercise, but not using the Staff Group's informal draft. The Staff Group reviewed a revised report on WINTEX 75, incorporating changes suggested by the United Kingdom representative, and tasked the Chairman to prepare a new version for distribution to Capitals. Finally, the the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) agreed to hold its next meetings on April 11, 21, and 25, 1975.

NPG DIV. FT REPORT ON WINTEX 75
4. The STAFF GROUP:

suggestions made, to prepare a new revised version

the current week, and with a view to re-opening
the discussion on Monday, 21st April, 1975.
