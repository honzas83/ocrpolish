> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, October 2, 1978, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss preparations for the Brussels Ministerial meeting and to address any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts.
