> # List of International Staff Attending the 14th Meeting of NPG Ministers in The Hague
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on October 23, 1973, provides a detailed list of International Staff members accompanying the Secretary General at the 14th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers. The meeting is scheduled to take place in The Hague on November 6th and 7th, 1973. The list includes personnel from various departments, including the Private Office of the Secretary General, the Executive Secretariat, the Division of Political Affairs, the Division of Defence Planning and Policy, the Nuclear Planning Directorate, the NATO Office of Security, the Conference Service, and the Registry. Specifically, it lists individuals such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luns, Deputy Executive Secretary E.G. Luff, and Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning and Policy D.C. Humphreys. The document serves as an administrative record of the attendance of the International Staff at the high-level ministerial meeting.
