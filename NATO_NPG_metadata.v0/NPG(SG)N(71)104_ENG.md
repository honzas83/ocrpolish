> # Notes of a Meeting Held on Friday, 10th September, 1971
> 
> On September 10, 1971, the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group convened to review several working papers and coordinate upcoming meetings. The group welcomed Lieutenant Colonel A.W. Schulte from SHAPE. The meeting addressed several working papers, including those on maritime use in the Mediterranean, tactical nuclear defence, and the draft Ministerial Agenda. The Staff Group also noted the status of regional follow-on studies for the Turkish and CENTAG Area, as well as preliminary comments from SHAPE on the North Norway Area, Greek, and NORTHAG Area studies. Plans were established for a series of meetings of NPG Permanent Representatives in preparation for the October Ministerial Meeting, with dates set for September 27, October 1, and October 14. The group specifically discussed the CENTAG Area Study and requested a revised version of the paper to be issued as a NPG Working Paper. Finally, the Staff Group scheduled its next meetings for September 20 and September 24, 1971.

FUTURE MEETINGS OF NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES

Friday, 1st October, might be suitable to the second meeting of the NPG
Permanent Representatives and invited the Chairman to examine this possibility

examination of the working papers referred to in paragraph 2(a) above (less the
draft agenda, to be approved earlier by the silent procedure);

(c) recalled that the week beginning 4th October was not suitable for a meeting
of NPG Permanent Representatives because of the High Level Meeting taking place
on 5th and 6th October, and the visit of Permanent Representatives to the
Mediterranean; agreed therefore that it was essential that all the working
papers destined to become NPG documents for discussion at the Ministerial
Meeting at the end of October should be cleared without fail on 1st October; for

REGIONAL FOLLOW-ON STUDIES – CENTAG AREA STUDY

4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced the International
Staff's paper(1) on the CENTAG Area Study, and invited comments;

(b) took note of the views expressed and the suggestions made in the ensuing
discussion, and agreed to a number of changes in the text;

(c) invited the International Staff, in the light of the comments made, to
prepare and issue a revised version of the paper in the form of a NPG Working
Paper, which should be sent immediately by Delegations to their capitals, with a
view to obtaining instructions before 1st October (see paragraph 3(c) above)(2).

NEXT MEETINGS OF THE STAFF GROUP

5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 20th September, 1971, at 3 p.m;
(b) agreed provisionally to hold a subsequent meeting on Friday, 24th September,
