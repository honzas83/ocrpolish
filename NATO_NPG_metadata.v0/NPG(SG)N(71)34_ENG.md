> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, March 15, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss national participation in NATO nuclear planning, the agenda for the Meeting of Ministers in May, SACEUR's Illustrative Studies, and the draft advance press release for the May Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It includes references to several previous documents: NPG(Staff Group)N(71)22, NPG(Staff Group)N(71)18, NPG/Study(71)4, and NPG(Staff Group)N(71)33.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th March, 1971
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be to discuss:

(a) National participation in NATO nuclear planning(1);
(b) the Agenda for the Meeting of Ministers in May(2);
(c) SACEUR's Illustrative Studies(3);
(d) the draft advance press release for the May Meeting(4).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels
