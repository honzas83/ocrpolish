> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Friday, 12th June, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss matters arising from a meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Ministers that occurred earlier in the week. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
