> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in Germany
> 
> This document, dated November 4, 1975, outlines the composition of the International Staff delegation accompanying the Secretary General to the 18th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers to be held in Germany in November 1975. The note, signed by F.I.I.G. Wilson, provides an attachment containing a detailed list of the delegation members. The delegation includes high-ranking officials such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luns, Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning & Policy D.C. Humphreys, and Director of Nuclear Planning R.E. Shearer. Additionally, the staff includes Nuclear Planning Staff Officers, interpreters, a Cosmic Control and Courier, and secretarial support. The document serves as an administrative record of the personnel involved in the International Staff delegation for the NPG Ministers' meeting.
