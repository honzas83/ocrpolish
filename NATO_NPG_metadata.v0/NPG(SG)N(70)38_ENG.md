> [!abstract] 
> # Draft Advance Press Release Concerning the Ministerial Meeting at Venice
> 
> This document consists of a note from the Chairman, H. B. Seim, and a draft advance press release regarding the seventh meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO. The meeting is scheduled to take place on June 8th and 9th, 1970, in Venice, under the chairmanship of Secretary General Manlio Brosio. The Chairman's note suggests that the Group may comment on the draft press release during their meeting on the following Monday before it is sent to capitals. It is proposed that the press release be officially released on Tuesday, June 2nd, 1970, at 3 p.m. Brussels time. The Nuclear Planning Group currently consists of the Defence Ministers of Canada, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, the United Kingdom, the United States, Norway, and Turkey.

As decided at its last Ministerial meeting, the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO,

Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United kingdom, and the United States, will
