> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, July 26, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the terms of reference for the NIKE HERCULES Study, which is based on a United States letter dated July 15, 1976. The notice was issued in Brussels, Belgium, and signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
>
