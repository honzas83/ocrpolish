> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, May 5, 1980, at 9:30 a.m. and 2:15 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to prepare for the Spring 1980 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting and to discuss the draft of Sections VIII and IX of the General Political Guidelines. The notice also includes references to working papers and documents related to these agenda items. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th May, 1980 at
9.30 a.m. and 2.15 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Spring 1980 NPG Ministerial Meeting(1);
(b) discussion of the draft of Section VIII and IX of the General Political
Guidelines(2);

(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
