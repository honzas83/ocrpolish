> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 21, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to receive a briefing from Mr. P. Osborn on nuclear operations against rear areas, to discuss WINTEX 77, and to address any other business. The notice was issued by M.H.G. Wilson from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

16th March, 1977

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(77)23

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take plac on Monday, 21st March, 1977
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to receive a briefing from Mr. P. Osborn on nuclear operations against rear
areas;

(b) to discuss WINTEX 77;
(c) to address any other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
