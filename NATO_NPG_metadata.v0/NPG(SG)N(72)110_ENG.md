> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, December 11, 1972, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to hear a briefing by Colonel G.L. Vincent, Director of Council Operations, on the outline of WINTEX 73. To assist members in preparation, the Secretary references two documents: the WINTEX Operation Order (BLUSAND) 1710.2.3/14-7/5501/72 dated March 31, 1972, and AC/237-D/109 dated November 27, 1972. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

5th December, 1972
NOTICE
NFC(Staff Group)N(72)110
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 11th December,
1972, at 3.0 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to hear a
briefing by Colonel G.L. Vincent, Director, Council Operations, on the outline
of WINTEX 73. In this connection members may find the following documents useful
for reference purposes:

(a) WINTEX Operation Order (BLUSAND) 1710.2.3/14-7/5501/72 of 31st March, 1972;
(b) AC/237-D/109 of 27th November, 1972.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
