> # Draft Agenda for the Meeting of Ministers of the Nuclear Planning Group to be held in Venice on 8th and 9th June, 1970
> 
> This document is a second draft agenda for the meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers to be held in Venice, Italy, on June 8th and 9th, 1970. The agenda is revised based on discussions from the Staff Group meeting on March 16th and was submitted by Secretary M.H.G. Wilson. The proposed agenda is divided into several key sections: Strategic Use of Nuclear Weapons, which includes briefings on the Balance of Strategic Forces led by the United States and the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces led by Germany and the United States. The section on Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons covers Atomic Demolition Munitions (ADMs) with Italy as the discussion leader, and further studies on follow-on tactical use, including an interim report by Turkey. The agenda also includes items for any other business, the future work programme, an agreed minute, and a communique. The document also references the document NPG/D(70)1.
