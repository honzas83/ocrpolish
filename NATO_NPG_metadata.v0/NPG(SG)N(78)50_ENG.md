> # Notice MPG(Staff Group)N(78)50: Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, July 17, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to present the draft Phase III report and to address any other business. The notice was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Actina Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th July, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) presentation of the draft Phase III report (hopefully);
(b) any other business.
