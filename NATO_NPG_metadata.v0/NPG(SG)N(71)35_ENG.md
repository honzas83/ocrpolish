> # Canadian Proposal for a Briefing on Nuclear Release Procedures at the May NPG Ministerial Meeting at Mittenwald
> 
> This document outlines a Canadian proposal to include a briefing on nuclear release request procedures in the program for the May Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting in Mittenwald. The proposal suggests that ministers be briefed on the procedures for generating and handling requests for nuclear release, potentially using a shortened version of briefings previously given to the Defense Policy Committee (DPC) regarding SACEUR's selective release procedures or the equivalent for SACLANT. The Canadian member argues that such a briefing would serve as a logical first step in familiarizing ministers with the processes and forms used for nuclear release requests. Furthermore, it is intended as a useful prelude to the ministers' examination of the Illustrative Studies item on the agenda, which requires an appropriate introduction to the language and form of requests for nuclear weapons release. The proposal aims to facilitate these purposes by including a briefing on MNC's nuclear release request procedures in the ministers' programme.

It is proposed that the Ministers attending the NPG Ministerial meeting at
Mittenwald be briefed on the procedures for generating and handling requests for
nuclear release. The briefing could be on the lines of that already given to the
DPC on SACEUR's selective release procedures, or SACLAN'T's counterpart thereof.
In either case the presentation could be abbreviated in view of time pressures
at Mittenwald.

2. Such a briefing would be a logical first step in familiarizing Ministers with
the processes and form in which nuclear release requests would reach them. It
would therefore be a useful prelude to the Ministers' examination of the
Illustrative Studies item which will also be on the agenda at Mittenwald. The
Illustrative Studies will in any event require an appropriate introduction on
the language and form of requests for nuclear weapons release. The proposal for
including a briefing on MNC's nuclear release request procedures in the
Ministers' programme is intended to facilitate these several purposes.
