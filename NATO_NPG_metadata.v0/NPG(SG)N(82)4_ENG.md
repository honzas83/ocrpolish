> [!abstract] 
> # Visit to SHAPE
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding a scheduled visit to SHAPE. The visit is confirmed for Monday, 8th February 1982, with the programme, departure, and arrival times remaining unchanged. Additionally, the Secretary notes that arrangements are being made for a NATO bus to transport members of the Group to and from SHAPE, with further details to be communicated at a later date. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts of NATO in Brussels.
