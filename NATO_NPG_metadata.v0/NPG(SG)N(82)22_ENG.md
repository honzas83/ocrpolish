> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, June 14, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to consider General Political Guidelines, with additional items under any other business. Following the meeting, members are invited to gather in the Chairman's office to bid farewell to Mr. Candan Azer. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 14th June, 1982
at 2.45 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) General Political Guidelines(1);
(b) any other business.
2. After the meeting, Staff Group members are invited to assemble in the
Chairman's office to bid farewell to Mr. Candan Azer.
