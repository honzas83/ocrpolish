> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, June 18, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to conduct an initial exchange of views on the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) reports on operational concepts and doctrine, and to reach an agreement on the dates for the Autumn meeting of NPG Ministers. Additionally, the notice informs members of a farewell luncheon party in honor of Colonel J.B. Spiegelenberg, scheduled for Monday, June 25, 1973, at 12:30 p.m. to 1 p.m. in the Salon des Ambassadeurs. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels.

14th June, 1973
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(73)50

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 18th June, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to hold an initial exchange of views on the NMA reports(1) on operational
concepts and doctrine;

(b) to reach agreement on the dates of the Autumn meeting of NPG Ministers(2).

2. For the information of members, and in accordance with their wish expressed
at the last meeting, a farewell luncheon party in honour of Colonel J.B.
Spiegelenberg has been arranged for Monday, 25th June, 1973, at 12.30 for 1
p.m., in the Salon des Ambassadeurs.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG/D(73)5, 7th June, 1973 and
NPG/D(73)6, 7th June, 1973

(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)47, 1st June, 1973
