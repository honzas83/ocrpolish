> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, June 7, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss General Political Guidelines, with additional time allocated for any other business. Following the meeting, members of the Staff Group are invited to gather in the Chairman's office to bid farewell to Bernard Zepter and Colonel Peter Heck. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several documents including DPP/NFD/82/47, DPP/NFD/82/54, and USKATO/DPD/OUT/HS/82-102.

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 7th June, 1982 at
2.45 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) General Political Guidelines(1);

(b) any other business.

2. After the meeting, Staff Group members are invited to assemble in the
Chairman's office to bid farewell to Bernard Zepter and Col. Peter Heck.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
