> # Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, October 20, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the discussion will be the draft advance press release for the meeting of Ministers of the Nuclear Planning Group to be held near Washington, United States, on November 11 and 12, 1969. Additionally, the meeting will focus on the Draft Work Programme for further studies on the follow-on tactical use of nuclear weapons. The notice is signed by H.H.G. Wilson. It references documents NPG(Staff Group)N(69)75 and NPG(Staff Group)WP(69)8(Revised).
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on monday, 20th October,
1969 at 2.45 p.m.

The principal subjects of discussion will be the draft advance press release for
the meeting of Ministers of the Nuclear Planning Group to be held near
Washington on 11th and 12th November (1), and the Draft Work Programme for
further Studies on the Follow-on Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons (2).

(Signed) H.H.G. WILSON
