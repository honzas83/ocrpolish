> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, July 30, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue preparations for the Autumn Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting. Specifically, the meeting will focus on Operational Concepts and Doctrine, as well as Consultation Procedures and Facilities. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 30th July, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue the
consideration of steps to prepare the Autumn NPG Ministerial Meeting with
particular reference to:

(a) Operational Concepts and Doctrine(1)
(b) Consultation Procedures and Facilities(2).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
