> # Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, October 6, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary subject of discussion for the meeting will be further studies on the tactical use of nuclear weapons. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and was circulated on September 30, 1969.
