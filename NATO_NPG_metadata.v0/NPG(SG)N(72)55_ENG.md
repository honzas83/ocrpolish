> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, June 19, 1972, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to consider the first draft of a possible report to Ministers on Consultation Procedures and Facilities. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references a working paper, NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)9, dated June 15, 1972.
