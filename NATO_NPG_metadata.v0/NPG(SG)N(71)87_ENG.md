> # Draft Ministerial Agenda
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting to be held at Palais d'Égmont in Brussels on October 26-27, 1971. The agenda items include a briefing on the balance of strategic forces led by the United States, regional follow-on use studies for the CENTAG area led by Germany and the United States, and discussions on unresolved problems related to tactical nuclear defence. Additionally, the agenda covers follow-up action from AD 70, nuclear aspects of the Defence Planning Committee (DPC) and the status of the follow-on use study programme. The meeting is part of the process of the NPG Staff Group, the Nuclear Planning Group, detayling the detailed planning and preparatory work for the upcoming ministerial session. The document is signed by H.B. Seim, Chairman of the Staff Group, and references several reports by Permanent Representatives and SACEUR illustrative studies.
