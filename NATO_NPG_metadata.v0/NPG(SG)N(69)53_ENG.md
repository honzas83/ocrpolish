> [!abstract] 
> # Major NATO Commanders' Units on Nuclear-German Guidelines
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated June 12, 1969, originating from Brussels, Belgium. It serves as a transmission of a letter sent by the Secretary General of NATO to the Chairman of the Military Committee, requesting that the Major NATO Commanders provide their comments on the Anglo-German Guidelines paper (MPG/D(69) 2). The note is signed by H.G. Wilson. The primary purpose of the document is to ensure that the Major NATO Commanders are informed of the request for feedback on the nuclear guidelines established between the United Kingdom and Germany.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document discusses the 'Anglo-German Guidelines' regarding nuclear planning, specifically requesting comments from Major NATO Commanders on these guidelines, which relates to the deployment and sharing of nuclear responsibilities in Europe.
