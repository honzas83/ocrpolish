> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 20th October, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on October 20, 1975. The group discussed the future work programme, noting a statement by the Chairman regarding the Secretary General's plan to forward a paper on future Nuclear Planning Group work to Permanent Representatives. The group proposed changes to the form and content of the paper and noted that the final version would include an annex on the technology study. Additionally, the United States representative expressed hope to receive instructions to discuss the Phase III arrangements paper on October 22. The group agreed that finalizing the Phase III paper was urgent for discussion by Permanent Representatives on October 29. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next meeting on Wednesday, October 22, 1975, at 4 p.m. in Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 20th
October, 1975

SECURITY
1. The STAFF GROUP: noted that all persons present were cleared to the security
classification of the matters to be discussed.

FUTURE WORK PROGRAMME
2. The STAFF GROUP: (a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who recalled
that, in line with past practice, the Secretary General planned to forward to
Permanent Representatives a PO on Future NPG Work; the International Staff
Draft(1) gave a possible format and content; he invited members to comment upon
it; (b) took note of the views expressed in the ensuing discussion and proposed
a number of changes to the form and content of the paper; (c) noted that the
final version of the PO would contain an Annex giving a status report on the
technology Study.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR PHASE III

3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the United States representative who said that
he hoped to have instructions from his Authorities which would permit him to
discuss the paper(1) on Phase III arrangements on Wednesday, 22nd October;

(b) agreed that it was most urgent to finalise the Phase III paper in time for
it to be discussed by Permanent Representatives on 29th October.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP

4. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Wednesday, 22nd October, 1975 at 4 p.m.

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
