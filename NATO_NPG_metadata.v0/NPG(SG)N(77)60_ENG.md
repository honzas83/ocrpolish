> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Tuesday, 16th August, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on August 16, 1977. The group took note of a briefing by SHAPE on recent refinements and adjustments to the Supreme Allied Commander Europe's (SACEUR) Nuclear Operations Plan (NOP). The meeting included a briefing officer, Lt. Col. A. Esse, and other SHAPE members, Col. W. Baddaker and Captain P. Moore. Additionally, the Staff Group held a general discussion regarding the possible form and content of the agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers' meeting scheduled for October in Italy. The group agreed to continue these discussions at their next meeting and requested the International Staff to circulate a draft agenda as a green Working Paper. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next session on Tuesday, August 23, 1977.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Tuesday,
16th August, 1977 at 3.00 p.m.

SACEUR'S NUCLEAR OPERATIONS PLAN
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a briefing given by SHAPE on recent refinements and adjustments
to SACEUR's Nuclear Operations Plan (NOP);

(b) took note of the replies given during the ensuing question period;
(c) warmly endorsed the Chairman's thanks to the briefing officer, Lt. Col. A.
Esse, and to the other members of SHAPE (Col. W. Baddaker and Captain P. Moore)
also present.

DRAFT AGENDA FOR THE AUTUMN NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) held a general discussion on the possible form and content of the agenda for
NPG Ministers at their meeting in October in Italy;

(b) agreed to continue their discussion at the next meeting and invited the
International Staff to circulate a draft agenda in the form of a green Working
Paper.

NEXT MEETING
3. The STAFF GROUP: agreed to hold its next meeting on Tuesday, 23rd August,
1977 at 3 p.m.

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
