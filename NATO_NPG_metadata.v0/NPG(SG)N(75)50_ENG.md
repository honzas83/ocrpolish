> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in California
> 
> This document, dated June 3, 1975, outlines the composition of the International Staff delegation accompanying the Secretary General to the 17th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers to be held in California. The delegation includes high-ranking officials such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luns, Deputy Secretary General F. Pansa Cedronio, and Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning & Policy D.C. Humphreys. Additionally, the list identifies staff members from various departments, including the Director of Nuclear Planning, the Deputy Executive Secretary, and officers from the NATO Press Service and the Office of Security. The delegation also includes interpreters and secretarial support staff, such as a Cosmic Control and Courier. The document was issued by the Secretary of the North Atlantic Council,の a NATO organization based in Brussels, Belgium.
