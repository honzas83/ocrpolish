> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, acting on behalf of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), announcing the next meeting of the Staff Group. The meeting is scheduled for Monday, August 1, 1977, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on New Weapons Technology Follow-up Work, to address points arising from the follow-up action to the Ottawa Ministerial Meeting, and to consider any other business. Additionally, the meeting will be followed by a reception for new and departing members of the group.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STATI GROUP)
NEXT MEETING

Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 1st August, 1977
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be: (a) to continue the
discussion of the New Weapons Technology Follow-up Work(1); (b) to pursue any
other points arising from the Follow-up action to the Ottawa Ministerial
Meeting(2); (c) to consider any other business.

2. Following the meeting, the Staff Group will hold a reception for new and
departing members.

(Signed) N.W.G. SHER'EN

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
