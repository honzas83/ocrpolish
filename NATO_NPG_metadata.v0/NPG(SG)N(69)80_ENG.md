> # Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting of the Staff Group on Monday, November 17, 1969, at 2:15 p.m. The purpose of the meeting is twofold: first, to review the outcome of the Nuclear Planning Group meeting that took place near Washington, and second, to prepare a report for submission by the Nuclear Planning Group to the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee. The notice is signed by A.L.G. Wilson. The document originates from Brussels, Belgium, and is associated with NATO.
