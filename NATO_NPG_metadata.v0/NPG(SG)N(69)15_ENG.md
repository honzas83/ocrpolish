> # Draft Agenda for NGP Ministerial Meeting in May 1969
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), outlines a revised draft agenda for the upcoming Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting to be held in London on May 29-30, 1969. The agenda is divided into several key sections: tactical use of nuclear weapons, including tentative political guidelines and NATO's response to a Warsaw Pact initial tactical nuclear strike, and the strategic use of nuclear weapons, specifically the role of theatre nuclear strike forces. Additionally, the agenda includes consultations on the use of nuclear weapons, any other business, the future work programme, and the final agreed minute and communique. The document references several working papers from the United States, Italy, and Germany, and notes that reports by the United Kingdom and Germany, as well as reports by Permanent Representatives, are to be circulated. The meeting's objectives are focus on nuclear strategy and political guidelines for the tactical and strategic deployment of nuclear weapons within the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO).
