> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in London
> 
> This document, dated October 29, 1976, outlines the composition of the International Staff delegation accompanying the Secretary General to the 20th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers to be held in London in November. The notice, issued by the Secretary of the NPG Staff Group, M.H.G. Wilson, originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. The delegation list includes high-ranking officials such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luns, Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning & Policy W.F. Mumford, and Director of Nuclear Planning R.E. Shearer. It also includes various staff officers, interpreters, and secretarial support staff, including members of the the Nuclear Planning Staff and the NATO Press Service. The purpose of the document is to formally record the members of the International Staff who will be attending the meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Ministers.
