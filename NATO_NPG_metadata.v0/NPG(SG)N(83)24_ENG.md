> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1983 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Secretary and a draft advance press release regarding the upcoming thirty-fourth Ministerial meeting of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group (NPG). The meeting is scheduled to take place in Montebello, Quebec, Canada, on 27th and 28th October 1983, under the chairmanship of Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns. The Secretary's note invites Staff Group members to discuss the draft text and the timing of the release, which is proposed for Brussels on Tuesday, 25th October 1983, at 4:00 p.m. local time. Additionally, the document requests information on whether national authorities in participating countries wish to make simultaneous releases in their respective capitals. The draft press release is provided in both English and French.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR
THE AUTUMN 1983 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Secretary

Attached is the text, in English and French, of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting to be held in Canada on 27th
and 28th October, 1983. It is proposed that the release be made in Brussels on
Tuesday, 25th October, 1983 at 4.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous
additional release in the capitals of those participating countries which so
desire.

2. Staff Group members are invited to discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at the meeting on Monday, 10th October. The Director, Nuclear
Planning would welcome information on whether national authorities plan to make
a simultaneous release in their capitals.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
