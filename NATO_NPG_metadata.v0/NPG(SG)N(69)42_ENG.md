> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group
> 
> This document consists of a notice from the Secretary, M.H.G. Wilson, regarding the next meeting of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group scheduled for Monday, May 19, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the advance press release for the upcoming London NPG Ministerial meeting. The attached draft press release, suggested by the United States delegation, states that the Nuclear Planning Group, comprising the Defense Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will meet in London on May 29-30, 1969, under the chairmanship of Secretary General Manlio Brosio. The draft release also suggests a distribution time of 3:00 p.m. Brussels time on Monday, May 26, with simultaneous release in NPG capitals and Washington, D.C.
>

>

ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR LONDON NPG MEETING
(Suggested by the U.S. delegation)
As decided during its last Ministerial meeting, in Bonn, 10th – 11th October,
1968, the NATO Nuclear Planning Group, which consists of the Defense Ministers
of seven NATO countries, will hold its next meeting in London on 29th – 30th
May, under the chairmanship of kr. Manlio Brosio, Secretary General of NATO.

At present, the Nuclear Planning Group composed of the Defense Ministers of
Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United
States. In the forthcoming meeting in London, the Ministers will continue their
discussions of nuclear planning for the defense of NATO.

The U.S. suggests release by NATO Director of Information at 3.CC p.m. Brussels
time, Monday, 26th May, and optional simultaneous release in NPG capitals. The
U.S. plans to make simultaneous release in Washington.
