> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on 19th and 20th September, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on September 19 and 20, 1974. The meeting's primary focus was included security clearances for all present members and the review of several key documents. Specifically, the the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) reviewed the latest version of the CNI Study, composed of NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7(4th Revise) and two addenda, and agreed to finalize the the same and circulate it as an NPG Study. Additionally, the group discussed a draft Permanent Representatives' report on the CNI Study, NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)10(Revised), and considered whether it should be forwarded to the Defence Policy Committee (DPC). The group requested the International Staff to revise paragraphs 3 and 4 of the draft report. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next meeting on September 23, 1974. The group also took note of a letter from the United States Permanent Representative to the Secretary General and all Permanent Representatives regarding the United States Procurement Authorisation Act.

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on 19th and

SECURITY
1. The STAFF GROUP:
noted that all persons present were cleared for the security classification of
the matters to be discussed.

DOCUMENTATION
2. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who drew attention to the following
papers which were about to be distributed:

(a) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)95, 20th September, 1974: Future Work Programme;
(b) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)96, 20th September, 1974: Copy of a letter from the
United States Permanent Representative to the Secretary General and all
Permanent Representatives concerning the United States Procurement Authorisation
Act, the last section of which is of particular interest to the Staff Group.

3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced the latest version

(b) requested the International Staff to prepare a revised version of paragraphs

(c) agreed to consider the draft report in detail at its meeting on 23rd
September.
