> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, August 27, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines and specifically address Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It includes several references to previous documents, including USNATO/DDP/OUT/NS/78-126 and various NPG(Staff Group) working papers and notices, as well as DPP/NPD documents related to nuclear planning.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 27th August, 1979
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
