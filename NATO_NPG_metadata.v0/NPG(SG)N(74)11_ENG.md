> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, January 28, 1974, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss a revised draft of a Permanent Representative's report regarding the conceptual analysis of the nuclear aspects of deterrence, to continue discussions on the arrangements for the Study of the Implications of Technological Development, and to continue preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)11
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 28th January,
1974, at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss a revised draft(1) of a possible Permanent Representative's
report on the question of making a conceptual analysis of the nuclear aspects of
deterrence;

(b) to continue discussion of arrangements for the Study of the Implications of
Technological Development;

(c) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)1(Revised), 22nd January, 1974
