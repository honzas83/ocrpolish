> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 10th April, 1972
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened on April 10, 1972, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to review several working papers and coordinate preparations for the Permanent Representatives' meeting scheduled for April 13, 1972. The meeting began with a welcome to new members, including Mr. Francesco Spinelli of Italy and Lt.Col. Onno Oldigs of the International Military Staff Nuclear Policy Team. The group discussed and agreed to modifications for several working papers, including the Turkish Follow-on Use Study (NPG/WP(72)1), the Status of Work in Progress (NPG/WP(72)2), and the Draft Agenda for the May Ministerial Meeting (NPG/WP(72)3). Additionally, the group addressed the lack of consensus regarding the initiation of Phase II (NPG/WP(72)4) and agreed that further efforts should be made at the Staff Group and Permanent Representative level before involving Ministers. They also reviewed and amended working papers on Consultation Procedures and Facilities (NPG/WP(72)5), an Illustrative Study on Battlefield Use (NPG/WP(72)6), and the Report on the Mediterranean Maritime Area Study (NPG/WP(72)7). The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next session on April 17, 1972.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 10th

WELCOME
1. The STAFF GROUP:
warmly endorsed the words of welcome addressed by the Chairman to Mr. Francesco
Spinelli, the new Italian Representative on the Staff Group, and to Lt.Col. Onno
Oldigs, the new member of the International Military Staff Nuclear Policy Team.

DOCUMENTATION
2. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who drew attention to the following
documents which had recently been issued:

(a) NPG/A(72)3, dated 51st March, 1972
This is the Agenda for the meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives to be
held on Thursday, 13th April, 1972;

(b) NPG/Study/21, dated 6th April, 1972
This is the SACLANT review of the Follow-on Use Study of the Mediterranean
Maritime Area;

(c) NPG/Study/22, dated 7th April, 1972
This is the SHAPE review of the same Follow-on Use Study.

3. The STAFF GROUP:

(b) noted a general consensus in favour of presenting this working paper to the
Permanent Representatives without further change.

Status of Work in Progress – NPG/WP(72)2
4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who recalled that the Status Report
was non-controversial in nature, and noted that only Annex B had so far been
received; he requested that Annex A (Denmark Study Status Report) and Annex C
(Transition Study Status Report) should be forwarded to him by the end of the
week;

(b) after discussion agreed to one addition to the text of the working paper;
(c) invited the International Staff to issue an appropriate corrigendum to the
working paper in time for the Permanent Representatives' meeting on 13th April.
