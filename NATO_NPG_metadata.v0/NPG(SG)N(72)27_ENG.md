> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, 13th March 1972, at 11 a.m. and 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the discussion of the draft report on the SHAPE Illustrative Study on Battle-field Use, referencing document NPC(Staff Group)WP(72)5 dated 2nd March 1972. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions a discussion on the 'SHAPE Illustrative Study on Battle-field Use', which refers to the tactical employment of nuclear weapons in the European theater (SHAPE), as opposed to strategic nuclear forces.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 13th March, 1972,
