> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, March 30, 1978, at 10:30 a.m. The agenda for the meeting includes a discussion of the draft Permanent Representatives' report on NIKE HERCULES adjustments, preparations for the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting, and any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 30th March,
1978 at 10.30 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft Permanent Representatives' report on NIKE HERCULES
Adjustments(1);

(b) preparations for the Spring, 1978 NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(c) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
