> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 24th June, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on June 24, 1974. The group took note of a statement by the Chairman regarding two papers: the Agreed Minute of the Bergen meeting (NPG/D(74)7) and a draft study on communicating NATO's intentions (NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7). Regarding preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting, the group reviewed the International Staff's note and planning timetable, expressing general agreement with the content. The United States representative emphasized that agenda items for Ministers should be chosen to stimulate interest, while routine matters should be handled at lower levels. Additionally, the group agreed to hold a special meeting on June 27, 1974, to discuss the draft study on communicating NATO's intentions due to the importance and workload involved. The group also planned to decide on the date for the following week's meeting during the session on June 27.

DOCUMENTATION
1. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who drew attention to two papers:
(a) NPG/D(74)7, dated 14th June, 1974: The Agreed Minute of the Bergen meeting;
(b) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7, 20th June, 1974: A draft study on Communicating
NATO’s intentions (to be distributed next day).

PREPARATIONS FOR THE AUTUMN 1974 NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the remarks of the Chairman who introduced the International
Staff’s note(1) on preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting and
invited comments;

(b) took note of the views expressed in the ensuing discussion and observed that
there was general agreement with the content of the International Staff’s note
and the attached planning timetable;

(c) concurred with the view expressed by the United States representative who

FUTURE MEETINGS OF THE STAFF GROUP
3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) agreed, in view of the importance of the draft Study on Communicating NATO's

meeting of the Staff Group to discuss the draft on Thursday, 27th June, at 3
