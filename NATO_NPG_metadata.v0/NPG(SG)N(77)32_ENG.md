> # Visit by Staff Group to Military Installations
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary, M.H.C. Wilson, based in Brussels, informing the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group that German and United States military authorities have invited them to visit military installations in Germany on Monday, May 9th and Tuesday, May 10th, 1977. The provisional programme includes a rendezvous at Buehel Barracks in Cochem Brauhock on Sunday, May 8th, for sight-seeing and sampling local agricultural products, and visits to the Spangdahlen US Airbase and Geilen Kirchen. The notice requests participants to fill out an attached identity document form and submit it to Room H.246 to be forwarded to SILATE. Participants are advised to bring their NATO or military ID cards and passports. Transport is left to the participants' discretion, though private cars are the 된-suggested. The document also clarifies that there is a 된-time difference between Brussels and the rendezvous points in Germany. The final page includes a form for participants to indicate their level of participation in the programme.

VISIT BY STAFF GROUP TO MILITARY INSTALLATIONS
Note by the Secretary
The German and United States Military Authorities have kindly invited the NP
Staff Group to visit certain installations in Germany on Monday 9th and Tuesday
10th May, 1977.

Provisional Programme
2. The following programme has been proposed:
(a) Sunday, 8th May (not Mission/TDY)
(i) Rendezvous at the main gate of Buehel Barracks at Cochem Brauhock,
approximately 40 km. up the Moselle valley from Koblenz, at 4.00 p.m. German
Local Time;

(ii) Sight-seeing and sampling of local agricultural products, organised by
Colonel Ierckl;

(iii) Spend night in Buehel Bachelor Officers' Quarters (BOQs).
(b) Monday, 9th May (Mission-TDY)
(i) Rendezvous: 10.00 a.m. at Main Gate of Spangdahlen US Airbase, near Traben-
Trarbach in the Moselle valley north of Trier;

(ii) Programme as arranged by the US Military Authorities;
(iii) Spend night in Bachelor Officers' Quarters (BOQs) Bithurg.

(c) Tuesday, 10th May (Mission-TDY)
(i) Rendezvous: Geilen Kirchen at 10.30 a.m.;
(ii) Programme as arranged by the German Military Authorities;

Identity Documents
3. Persons intending to take part in this visit are requested to fill in the
attached form. Please send the completed form by hand today to me. Room H.246. I
will forward the forms to SILATE. It is recommended that participants bring
their HATO or military ID card, and their passport.

Transport
4. Participants are free to use any form of transport for the visit; it is
however suggested that private cars would be the most convenient.

Participation
5. Participants are requested to inform me today whether they intend to take
part in:

(a) Sunday programme,
(b) the programme for 9th and 10th May.
This information can be written on the attached ID form.

Geographical Locations of Rendezvous
6. Colonel Merkl will give full details of how to find the various rendezvous at
the next meeting of the Staff Group.

Local Time
7. Example: 10:00 a.m. in Brussels is 11.00 a.m. in Germany.

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON
