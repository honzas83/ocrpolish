> # Notes of a Meeting Held at NATO Headquarters on Monday, 2nd February, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters on February 2, 1969, to address several key strategic and administrative issues. The group discussed the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, agreeing to prepare a draft paper based on SHAPE replies and emerging issues. Regarding the agenda for the upcoming May ministerial meeting, the group decided to delete the item on Atomic Demolition Munitions and requested a revised draft agenda from the International Staff. The meeting also covered the 'ADN Time Factors' paper, noting comments from the United Kingdom and Italy and proposing a detailed discussion at the Permanent Representative level. On the matter of consultation, the group felt that a paper for the May meeting should only be prepared once the United States could provide a contribution, with a suggestion that the Netherlands might prepare draft revised conclusions. Additionally, the group noted a scheduled briefing on Atomic Demolition Munitions by a team from CENTAG under the auspices of SACEUR on February 25, 1969. The meeting concluded by scheduling the next session for February 10, 1969, to continue discussions on theatre nuclear strike forces, the ADN Time Factors paper, and consultation.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at NATO Headquarters on Monday, 2nd February, 1969
THE ROLE OF THEATRE NUCLEAR STRIKE FORCES
1. Continuing from the meeting held on 27th January, the STAFF GROUP pursued its
study of the questions in the Working Paper of 21st January (1)

2. The STAFF GROUP :
(a) noted and discussed the further replies given by the SHAPE representatives;
(b) agreed that the next step was to prepare a draft paper on the role of
theatre nuclear strike forces, in the light of the SHAPE replies, taking into
account the various issues which were emerging in the discussions;

(c) agreed to discuss this matter again at the next meeting and invited members
to circulate their comments and their views regarding issues, or to bring them,
in writing, to the meeting;

(d) expressed the thanks of the Group to Rear Admiral Hart and his advisors.

ADM TIME FACTORS

4. The STAFF GROUP discussed whether the ADN Time Factors paper (1) should be
