> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(72)44
> 
> This document, dated May 15, 1972, serves as a corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(72)44, which was originally dated May 12, 1972. It instructs holders of the notice to delete the last sentence of paragraph 3 and replace it with new instructions regarding the timing for delegations or individuals to collect their packages. Specifically, it states that packages may be collected between 19:00 and 00:01 hours on Wednesday evening, or after 08:00 hours on Thursday morning, as the Conference Secretariat will be open during these times. Additionally, it specifies that delegations will be able to deposit classified material either in the Conference Secretariat or in the safes of their own offices up until midnight on Wednesday.

Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(72)44

Holders of the above-mentioned notice are requested to
make the following change:
Paragraph 3, lines 6 to 8
DELETE last sentence of paragraph 3 and INSERT:
"Delegations or individuals may collect their packages when they wish between
19.00 and 0001 hours that evening, or after 0800 hours

the next morning because the Conference Secretariat will be open
on Wednesday from 1300 to 0001 hours, and will re-open at
0800 hours on Thursday morning. Delegations will be able to
deposit classified material either in the Conference Secretariat
or in the safes of their own offices up until midnight on
Wednesday."
NATO, 1110 Brussels
