> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, April 10, 1972, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to examine comments from Capitals on various Working Papers that will subsequently be discussed by the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives on April 13. The Staff Group aims to resolve as many minor comments as possible to allow the Permanent Representatives to focus on substantive matters. Additionally, the document notes that if comments on the Mediterranean Maritime Study are received from NATO Military Authorities in time, the Chairman may convene an additional meeting of the Staff Group between April 4 and April 7. The notice lists several Working Papers as references, including reports on the Turkish Study, Follow-on Studies, a Draft Ministerial Agenda, and the Initiation of Phase II, as well as pending consultations on Consultation Procedures, an Illustrative Study, and the Report on the Mediterranean Maritime Study.

NOTICE NPG(Staff Group)N(72)36
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 10th April, 1972,
at 3.0 p.m. The principal object will be to examine the comments by Capitals on
the Working Papers(1) which are to be discussed by NPG Permanent Representatives
on Thursday, 13th April. The Staff Group may wish to reach agreement on as many
of the lesser comments as possible so that the Permanent Representatives need
only examine matters of substance.

2. It is hoped that comments on the Mediterranean Maritime Study will be
received from the NATO Military Authorities in time for military views to be
reflected in the report on this Study. If it should happen that such comments
become available during the week 4th-7th April, the Chairman may wish to convene
a meeting of the Staff Group. In this event members will be warned immediately.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
