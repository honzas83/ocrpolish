> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 29, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss any outstanding matters concerning the forthcoming meeting of Ministers in The Hague. The notice is signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originates from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.
