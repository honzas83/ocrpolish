> [!abstract] 
> # Corrigendum to Working Paper NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)17(Revised)
> 
> This document is a corrigendum issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on 21st September 1972, requesting a correction to Working Paper NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)17(Revised) dated 19th September 1972. The correction specifically instructs holders of the previous working paper to insert the word 'britannique' into line 5 of the French text on page 2. This ensures the accuracy of the French translation within the official NATO nuclear planning documentation.
