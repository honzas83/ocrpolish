> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary to the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of their next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Thursday, June 27, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the session is to discuss an initial draft of a study concerning the communication of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's (NATO) intention to use nuclear weapons. The notice references a working paper, NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7, dated June 20, 1974, as the basis for the discussion.
