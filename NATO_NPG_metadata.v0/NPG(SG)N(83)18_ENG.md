> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting to be held on Friday, July 15, 1983, at 10 a.m. The meeting will be chaired by Mr. D.A. Nicholls, the Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning and Policy. The primary agenda items for the meeting include considerations concerning nuclear tasks and any other business. The notice also references a letter from the Netherlands Permanent Representative dated June 28, 1983, regarding nuclear tasks. The document was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
A meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 15th July, 1983 at 10
a.m. This meeting will be chaired by the Assistant Secretary General for Defence
Planning and Policy, Mr. D.A. Nicholls, and the principal subjects to be
considered will be:

(a) considerations concerning nuclear tasks(1);
(b) Any Other Business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) Letter from the Netherlands Permanent Representative dated 28th June, 1983
