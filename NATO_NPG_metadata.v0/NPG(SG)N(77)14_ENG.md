> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, February 28, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the discussion of the draft Terms of Reference for the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study. Additionally, the meeting will cover any other business. The notice was signed by V. G. Wilson of NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 28th February,
1977 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of the draft Terms of Reference for the Maritime
Nuclear Warfare Study(1);

(b) any other business.
