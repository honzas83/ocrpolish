> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 11, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the Agenda for the Autumn Ministerial Meeting and to consider matters related to the Future Work Programme. The notice was issued by M.H.G. Wilson from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

7th October, 1976
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)88
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 11th October,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of the Agenda for the Autumn Ministerial Meeting(1);
(b) to consider matters relative to the Future Work Programme.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)8, 9th September, 1976
