> # Note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 16th May 1975, detailing the upcoming schedule for two meetings. The first meeting is scheduled for Tuesday, 20th May 1975, at 10 a.m. to discuss the Future Work Programme. The second meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, 21st May 1975, at 10 a.m. to discuss the report on Ehtac II. The note references several documents for the Ehtac II discussion, including NPG(Staff Group)P(75)5 and its subsequent corrigenda. The document was issued in Brussels, Belgium, and signed by N.H.C. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Note by the Secretary

The next two meetings of the Staff Group will take
place as follows
(a) Tuesday, 20th May, 1975 at 10 a.m.
to discuss the Future Work Programme(1)

(b) Wednesday, 21st May, 1975, at 10 a.m.
to discuss the report on Ehtac II(2)

(Signed) N.H.C. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(75)41, 15th May, 1975

dated 15th May, 1975
- for paragraphs 15 to 24, refer to Corrigendum No 2 to
