> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled for Monday, March 3, 1969, at 2:45 p.m., as agreed upon during the previous meeting held on February 24. The primary objective of the meeting will be to continue discussions on the Warsaw Pact Initial Use. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium, within the context of OTAN/NATO operations.
