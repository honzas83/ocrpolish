> [!abstract] 
> # NPG Organization and Procedures
> 
> The Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice to members of the Staff Group to prepare for a review of the organizational and procedural aspects that distinguish the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) from other NATO activities. This review is intended to place the consideration of initiating Phase II in a proper context. To facilitate this review, the Chairman suggests that Staff Group members scan and bring copies of several key documents, including the NDAC/NFG Charter, the Hague NPG Minute, the NPG Membership Arrangements, and the NDAC Decision Sheet. The document is signed by H.B. Seim and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Chairman
