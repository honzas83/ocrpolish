> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) confirming that the next meeting will take place on Monday, August 9, 1971, at 5 p.m. The Chairman intends to continue discussions regarding preparations for the October Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting, with a specific focus on the PO/226 question and the Military Committee report on Special ADM guidelines. The notice was signed by J. de Gaye and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary
It is confirmed that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on
Monday, 9th August, 1971, at 5 p.m.

2. The Chairman proposes to continue the discussion on preparations for the
October NPG Ministerial meeting, with particular emphasis on the PO/226
question(1) and the Military Committee report on Special ADM guidelines(2).
