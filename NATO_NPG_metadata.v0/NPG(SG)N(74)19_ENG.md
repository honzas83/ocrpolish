> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, February 18, 1974, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on a possible Permanent Representatives' report regarding arrangements for the Study of the Political and Military Implications of Technological Developments, to hold an initial exchange of views on the Military Committee's report on its function in the Nuclear Consultation Process, and to continue preparations for the upcoming Spring NPG Ministerial meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NG(Staff Group)N(74)19

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 18th February,
1974, at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of a possible Permanent Representatives' report(1) on
arrangements for the Study of the Political and Military Implications of
Technological Developments;

(b) to hold an initial exchange of views on the Military Committee's report(2)
on its function in the Nuclear Consultation Process;

(c) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)2(Revised), 12th February, 1974
(2) NPG/D(74)1, 12th February, 1974
