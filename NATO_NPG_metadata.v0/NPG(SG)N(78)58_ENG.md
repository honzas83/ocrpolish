> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, issued on August 17, 1978, announcing the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Thursday, August 24, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary agenda items include the discussion of a draft Permanent Representatives' report on short-term measures and any other business. Additionally, the notice invites Staff Group members to the Chairman's office on Monday, August 28, 1978, at 5 p.m. to bid farewell to Mr. Cem Ergin. The document is signed by N.L.C. Park and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 24th August,
1978 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) draft Permanent Representatives' report on short-term measures(1);
(b) any other business
2. Staff Group members are invited to the Chairman's office on Monday, 28th
August, 1978 at 5 p.m. to say goodbye to Mr. Cem Ergin.

(Signed) N.L.C. PARK
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
