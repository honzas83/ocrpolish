> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, October 12, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to conduct a final review of the Provisional Political Guidelines paper before its discussion by the Permanent Representatives the following day, and to study the Chairman's note regarding potential changes to the original Provisional Political Guidelines. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several documents, including NPG/WP(70)6(Revised), NPG(Staff Group)N(70)77, and DFC/D(69)58.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 12th October,
1970 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to take a final look at the Provisional Political Guidelines paper(1) before
its discussion next day by the Permanent Representatives;

(b) to study the Chairman's note(2) concerning eventual changes to the original
Provisional Political Guidelines(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG/WP(70)6(Revised), dated 6th October, 1970
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(70)77, dated 7th October, 1970
