> # Further Studies of Follow-On Tactical Use (1)
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on Monday, December 15, 1969, to address the topic of Further Studies of Follow-On Tactical Use (1). The group discussed a revised version of the International Staff draft paper and requested a revised version to be circulated as a working paper for distribution to Capitals. The Staff Group expressed hope that countries and NATO Military Authorities would provide definite offers of participation. The goal is to submit an agreed paper to the NPG Permanent Representatives by the end of January. Additionally, the group welcomed the representatives of Norway. Regarding a planned field trip to Germany, the group took note of a statement by the United States Representative concerning a field demonstration near Heidelberg, organized by HQ EUCOM. The trip is scheduled for January 13 to 15, 1970, with departure from Melsbrook airfield. the group noted that participants from each country, one or two persons, and from the International Staff, I&S, and SHAPE, are expected. Accommodation and meals are to be organized by HQ EUCOM and paid for by participants. Names of participants for the trip must be submitted to the Chairman before December 20. Finally, the group agreed that the next meeting will be place on January 8 or 9, 1970.

Notes of a meeting held on ionday, 15th December, 1969
FURTHER STUDIES OF FOLLOW-ON TACTICAL USE (1)
1. The STAFF GROUP :
(a) discussed the revised version of the International Staff draft paper (1);
(b) invited the International Staff to prepare, in the light of the views
expressed, a revised version of the paper, to be circulated as a working paper
as soon as possible;

(c) agreed to send the Working paper to Capitals, with a view to obtaining
instructions in time for the next meeting of the Staff Group;

(d) expressed the hope that at the same time it might be possible for countries
and NATO Military Authori-tie to make definite offers of participation;

(e) agreed that it should be the aim of the Staff Group to submit an agreed
paper to the NPG Permanent Representatives towards the end of January.

MEMBERSHIP
2. The STAFF GROUP :
welcomed the Representatives of Norway who were present at the meeting.
