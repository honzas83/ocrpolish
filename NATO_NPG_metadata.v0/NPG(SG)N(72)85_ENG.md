> # Revision of the Draft Report on Consultation Procedures and Facilities
> 
> This document, issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides revised language for certain paragraphs of the Draft Report by the Permanent Representatives on Consultation Procedures and Facilities. The Chairman, H.B. Seim, notes that not all members were able to to agree to the modifications of the original Working Paper during the meeting. If confirmation is received from all Staff Group members that the modifications are acceptable, a corrigendum will be issued to the original Working Paper. This would allow the Permanent Representatives to approve the revised Working Paper at their meeting on 5th October 1972. The proposed modifications include deletions in Paragraph 7 regarding the timely delivery of selective release messages to political authorities of non-ATOMAL members and changes to the Annex, paragraph 5, regarding the political consultation among Alliance members. The text highlights the difficulties in exempting nuclear release messages from ATOMAL classification without risking the compromise of vital information, and notes that the COEC is covering some of the communications aspects of these problems.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

REVISION OF THE DRAFT REPORT

ON CONSULTATION PROCEDURES AND FACILITIES

Note by the Chairman

Attached, for the information of Staff Group members, is the revised language
for certain paragraphs of the Draft Report by the Permanent Representatives on
Consultation Procedures and Facilities(1) that was developed by the Staff Group
today. It will be recalled that not all members were in a position at the
meeting to agree to modify the original Working Paper by the insertion of the
revised language. If confirmation that the modification is acceptable to all
Staff Group members is received in time, it is intended to issue a corrigendum
to the original Working Paper. In this case, the Permanent Representatives would
be invited at their meeting on 5th October to approve the Working Paper as
revised by the corrigendum.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.

1110 Brussels.

NPG(Staff Group)N(72)85

PROPOSED MODIFICATIONS TO NPG/I(72)12

1. Paragraph 7, lines 10 and 11: delete

"and with respect to timely delivery to political authorities of non-ATONAL
parts of the selective release messages"

2. Paragraph 7, last sentence: delete

3. Annex, paragraph 5, last sentence: change to read, and add new language:

"any of these arrangements might facilitate political consultation among all
Alliance members, whether cleared for ATOMAL or not, and could also improve the
possibilities of timely delivery of significant parts of the information needed
for that consultation process. On the other hand, there may be serious
difficulties in that it may not be possible for nuclear release messages to be
exempted from ATOMAL classification without risking compromise of vital
information at a critical time, and the utility of the messages could be much
reduced if all ATOMAL information were excluded from them. Moreover, any system
of separate or additional messages could add to the message handling burden. The
COEC is covering some of the communications aspects of these problems and the
Permanent Representatives will keep their political implications under review."
