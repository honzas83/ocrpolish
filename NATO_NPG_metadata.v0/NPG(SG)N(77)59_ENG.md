> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, dated August 11, 1977, announcing the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Tuesday, August 16, 1977. The primary objectives of the meeting are to receive a briefing by the Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE) on recent refinements and adjustments to the Supreme Allied Commander Europe's (SACEUR) Nuclear Operations Plan (NOP), and to consider any other business. The notice was signed by N.L.C. Par and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
