> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 23rd September and Wednesday, 24th September, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held meetings on September 23 and 24, 1974, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The group approved a revised draft report on the SHAPE Illustrative Study on Initial Use - Air Defence and requested its circulation as an NPG Working Paper. They also discussed a draft report on the CNI Study, establishing a drafting group comprising representatives from Canada, Germany, the United Kingdom, and the United States to revise its content. Regarding the Future Work Programme, the group took note of the importance of the Programme and the potential impact of the United States Military Procurement Authorisation Act of 1975, specifically Section 302 (d). The United States representative suggested that the Work Programme should be a personal document of the Secretary General. The group also reviewed a list of distributed documentation, including agendas for the Ministerial Meeting and the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives on October 10, 1974. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next meeting on September 30, 1974.

SECURITY
