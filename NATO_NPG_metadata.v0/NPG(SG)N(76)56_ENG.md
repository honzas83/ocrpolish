> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary, D.J. Roberts, regarding the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, June 28, 1976, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the meeting is to examine a paper prepared by the International Staff on follow-up action to the NPG Ministerial Meeting. The notice references the document NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)5, dated June 23, 1976.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 28th June, 1976
at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to examine the paper
prepared by the International Staff on follow-up action to the NPG Ministerial
Meeting(1).
