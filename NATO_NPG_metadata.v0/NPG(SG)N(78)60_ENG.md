> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note from the Acting Secretary to the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Wednesday, September 6, 1978, at 10:30 a.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to discuss the draft Permanent Representatives' report on short-term measures, the draft agenda for the Brussels Ministerial Meeting, and the draft advance press release for the Brussels Ministerial Meeting. The note also references several working papers and documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)6(2nd revise), NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)7, and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)57. The document was issued in Brussels, Belgium, and signed by N.L.C. Park.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 6th September,
1978 at 10.30 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) Draft Permanent Representatives' report on short-term measures(1) (to enable
the subsequent circulation of a "white" working paper);

(b) Draft agenda for the Brussels Ministerial Meeting(2);
(c) Draft advance press release for the Brussels Ministerial Meeting(3);
(d) Any other business.

(Signed) N.L.C. PARK

1110 Brussels.

(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)7
