> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, March 10, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions regarding the Warsaw Pact Initial Tactical Use. Additionally, if time permits, the Staff Group may examine a draft paper on theatre nuclear strike forces. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium, under the NATO framework.
>

NPQ(Staff Group)N(69)24

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

As agreed at the meeting held on 3rd March, the next meeting of the Staff Group
will be held on Monday, 10th March, 1969, at 2.45 p.m.

2. The principal object of this meeting will be to continue the discussion on
Warsaw Pact Initial Tactical Use (1). If time permits the Staff Group may also
wish to examine the draft paper on theatre nuclear strike forces (2).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

OTAN/NATO
Brussels, 39
