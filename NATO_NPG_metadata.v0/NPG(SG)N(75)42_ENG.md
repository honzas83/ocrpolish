> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, Brussels on Wednesday, 14th May, and Thursday, 15th May, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on May 14 and 15, 1975. The group noted a statement by the SHAPE representative regarding a planned visit to Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE) in early June, which will include briefings on Selective Employment Planning, military views on Communicating NATO's Intentions (CNI), and Num Follow-on Studies. Additionally, the Canadian representative invited attendees for a meeting in Ottawa on June 19, offering administrative assistance. The group also discussed and agreed to changes in the second revision of a Working Paper, requesting the International Staff to prepare a Corrigendum for discussion on May 21. The goal is to reach a final version of the paper by May 21 so that an NPC Working Paper could be circulated for consideration in Capitals and examination by the Permanent Representatives on June 3, 1975. Finally, the group scheduled its next meetings for May 20 and 21, 1975, in Brussels.

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 11.0 Brussels. on Wednesday,

STAFF GROUP VISIT TO SHAPE

1. The STAFF GROUP:
noted with appreciation a statement by the SHAPE representative who said that
his authorities intended to organise a visit to SHAPE by the Staff Group in the
early part of June; he outlined the programme and said that there would be
briefings on Selective Employment Planning, military views on Communicating
NATO's Intentions (CNI) and on Num Follow-on Studies; he hoped to have more
definite information shortly.

MEETING OF THE TECHNOLOGICAL STUDY TEAM (MILITARY IMPLICATIONS) IN OTTAWA

2. The STAFF GROUP:
noted with appreciation a statement by the Canadian representative who invited
those persons attending the meeting in Ottawa on 19th June, to communicate with
him; he would be glad to help them with administrative arrangements.
