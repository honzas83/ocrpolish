> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Friday, April 11, 1975, at 10 a.m. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss the Ministerial Agenda, the Phase II draft report, and future work for the Nuclear Planning Group. The notice references two working papers: NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)2 dated March 17, 1975, and NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)5 dated March 26, 1975. The document was issued by I.H.C. Wilson in Brussels, Belgium.
