> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Thursday, June 19, 1980, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the agenda are to discuss the follow-up to the Spring 1980 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial and to address any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts.
