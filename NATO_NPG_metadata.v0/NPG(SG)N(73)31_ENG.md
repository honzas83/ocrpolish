> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, April 9, 1973, at 11 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to conduct a final review of Working Papers 1 through 5, in accordance with instructions from capitals, before they are considered by the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives on April 16. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 9th April, 1973,
at 11 a.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to make a final review,
in the light of instructions from capitals, of the Working Papers (1) to be
considered by NPG Permanent Representatives on 16th April.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
