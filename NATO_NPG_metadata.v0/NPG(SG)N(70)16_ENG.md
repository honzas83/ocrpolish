> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) notifying members of the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 16, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary subjects for discussion include the draft agenda for the meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Ministers to be held on June 8 and 9, and the timetable of work leading up to that Ministerial meeting. Additionally, the meeting will address questions regarding selective release procedures. The note is signed by N.H.C. from the NATO office in Brussels, Belgium.
