> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, November 3, 1975, at 5 p.m. The primary agenda items for the meeting include any matters arising from the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives held on October 29, 1975, and the arrangements for an upcoming meeting in Hamburg. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 3rd November,
1975, at 5 p.m. The principal points for attention will be:

(a) Any matters arising from the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives held
on 29th October, 1975;

(b) arrangements for the meeting in Hamburg.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
