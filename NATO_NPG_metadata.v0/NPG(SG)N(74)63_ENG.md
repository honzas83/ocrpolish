> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 29th May 1974. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 5th June 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to review preparations for the upcoming Spring meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
