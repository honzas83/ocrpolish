> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1977 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated 24th March 1977, proposing a draft advance press release for the Spring 1977 Ministerial Meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place in Ottawa on 8th and 9th June 1977, under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns. The Chairman suggests that the press release be issued in Brussels on Friday, 3rd June 1977, at 4:00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is requested to discuss the draft text and timing at its meeting on 18th April and obtain approval from national authorities by 6th May 1977. The document includes the draft press release in both English and French, listing the participating Ministers of Defence from Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, the United Kingdom, the United States, Italy, Greece, Norway, and the Netherlands.

SPRING 1977 MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Attached is the text, in English and French, of a proposed press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting in Ottawa on 8th and 9th
June, 1977. The text closely follows the language used in former press
announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. As on past occasions, the
release could be made a few days before the meeting takes place; accordingly, it
is suggested that an announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, 3rd June, 1977,
at 4.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous additional release in the capitals of
those participating countries who so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 18th April. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Ministerial meeting are requested to obtain,
after their initial discussion, the approval of their national authorities for
the text of the announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director,
Nuclear Planning, no later than Friday, 6th May, 1977. Information on whether
national authorities plan to make a simultaneous release in their capitals would
also be appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER

1110 Brussels

ATTACHMENT to NPG(Staff Group)N(77)25
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, Greece, Italy, the
Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom and the United States, will hold its
twenty-first Ministerial Meeting in Ottawa on 8th and 9th June, 1977, under the
chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

Le Groupe des plans nucleaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
