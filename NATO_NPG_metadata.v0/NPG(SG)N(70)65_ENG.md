> # Meeting Schedule and Work Schedule Up to Ottawa
> 
> This document serves as a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated September 7, 1970, outlining the detailed meeting and work schedules leading up to the NPG Ministerial in Ottawa on October 29th and 30th, 1970. The meeting schedule includes tentative agendas for Permanent Representatives and the Staff Group, covering topics such as SACEUR Briefing, AIM Guidelines, and the Draft Concept. Draft Concept work involves the distribution of a US-German draft paper and reviews by Permanent Representatives. The Provisional Guidelines work schedule includes reports from the NMA and Nuclear Planning Group, 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된 관련된는 an NMA report to the Secretary General and input from Denmark. the Provisional Guidelines process involves multiple stages of meeting discussions and distribution of final documents by the International Staff (IS). The AIM Guidelines and the Ottawa Agenda are also scheduled for discussion and an advance press release is scheduled for national approval by silence procedure. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO, Brussels.
