> # Notice of the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by M.H.G. Wilson of NATO in Brussels, Belgium, announcing the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on Monday, November 19, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss preparations for the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee (NDAC) scheduled for December 7, 1973, specifically focusing on the draft agenda, the draft report by the Nuclear Planning Group to the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee, and the draft text for the final combined Ministerial communique. Additionally, the meeting will address the results of a previous meeting held in The Hague and initiate discussions on preparations for the Spring meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Ministers. The notice references several other documents, including NPG(Staff Group)N(73)115, NPG(Staff Group)N(73)116, NPG(Staff Group)N(73)117, and NPG/D(73)16.

14th November, 1973
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(73)118
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th November,
1973 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the preparations for the meeting of the Nuclear Defence Affairs
Committee (NDAC) to be held on Friday 7th December, 1973; in particular the
draft agenda(1), the draft report by the NPG to the NDAC(2), and the draft
text(3) for inclusion in the final combined Ministerial communique;

(b) to discuss the results(4) of the meeting in The Hague;
(c) to hold an initial discussion on the preparations for the Spring meeting of
NPG Ministers.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)115, 13th November, 1973
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)116, 13th November, 1973
