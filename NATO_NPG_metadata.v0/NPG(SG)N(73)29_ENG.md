> # Preparation for the Permanent Representatives' Meeting and Ministerial Meeting in Ankara
> 
> This document schedules two upcoming meetings. The first is a preparation session for the Permanent Representatives' meeting scheduled for May 3rd, taking place on Thursday, April 26th at 3:00 p.m. The second is a scheduled final exchange of views regarding the Ministerial meeting in Ankara, tentatively set for Monday, May 7th at 3:00 p.m. The document is signed by H.B. Seim and originates from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.
