> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, July 24, 1978, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The meeting's primary objectives are to consider a draft report on short-term measures to improve theatre nuclear forces, specifically referencing document NPG(Staff Group)N(78)6, and to present the draft Phase III report. The notice also allows for any other business to be members' consideration.

Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 24th July, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) draft report on short-term measures to improve theatre nuclear forces –
NPG(Staff Group)N(78)6;

(b) presentation of the draft Phase III report (hopefully);
(c) any other business.

(Signed) N.L.C. PARK

1110 Brussels.
