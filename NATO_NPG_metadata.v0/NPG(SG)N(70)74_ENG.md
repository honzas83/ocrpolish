> # Notice of Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held meetings on September 26 and 29, 1970, to address several key agenda items. The group noted personnel changes, including the departure of Group Captain L. Clare and the welcome of Group Captain V.M. Gramer. Discussions were held regarding the text and release date of a draft advance press release for the Ottawa meeting. The group also reviewed the 5th revision of the Atomic Demolition Municipal Guidelines, deciding that these should be separate from provisional political guidelines and be issued as a Nuclear Planning Group Working Paper. Additionally, the group discussed the initial draft of the Permanent Representatives Report on Provisional Political Guidelines and requested a briefing on Safeguards and Restraints from the International Military Staff. The document also outlines administrative arrangements for an upcoming meeting in Ottawa, Canada, including delegation lists, travel logistics, and vaccination requirements. Finally, the group confirmed the draft agenda for the Ottawa meeting and noted a scheduled briefing on STRIKEFORLAN on October 2, 1970, and agreed to meet again on October 5, 1970, in Brussels.
