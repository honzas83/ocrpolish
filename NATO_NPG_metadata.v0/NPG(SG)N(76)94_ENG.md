> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary regarding the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), scheduled for Monday, October 25, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss an International Staff note concerning the possible revision of the Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in Allied Command Europe (ACE), to hear follow-on commentaries on briefings provided by Mr. Cotter, and to discuss future Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) tasking. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th October,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the International Staff note(1) concerning the possible revision
of the Concept for the R6le of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in ACE(2);

(b) to hear follow-on commentaries, if any, on the briefings by Mr. Cotter;

(c) to discuss future NPG tasking.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
