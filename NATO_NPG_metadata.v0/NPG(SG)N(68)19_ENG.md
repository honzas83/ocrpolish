> # Note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated November 7, 1968. It serves as a notification for the upcoming meeting of the Staff Group scheduled for Monday, November 11, 1968, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the discussion of Time Factors. The note is signed by M.I.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium, under the authority of NATO.
