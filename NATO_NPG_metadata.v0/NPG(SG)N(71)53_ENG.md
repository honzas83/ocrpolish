> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, May 3, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to review National comments on the Working Paper on Studies of Follow-on Use and to continue preparations for the Mittenwald Meeting. The review of the Working Paper is intended to prepare for its consideration at the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives on May 6, 1971. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 3rd May, 1971 at
3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to review any National comments on the Working Paper on Studies of Follow-on
Use(1), in preparation for its consideration at the meeting of NPG Permanent
Representatives on 6th May, 1971;

(b) to continue preparations for the Mittenwald Meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
