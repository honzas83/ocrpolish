> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, April 18, 1983, at 2:45 p.m. The primary subjects for consideration during this meeting will be WINTEX-CIMEX 83 and any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
