> # Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice regarding the next informal meeting, scheduled for Thursday, 6th November 1969, at 10:15 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to discuss possible amendments to the Anglo-German paper on Tentative Political Guidelines, as referenced in document NPG/D(69)7. This notice serves as a coordination effort for the members of the Staff Group within the Nuclear Planning Group.
