> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, June 25, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss a draft paper prepared by the International Staff on Consultation Procedures and Facilities. Additionally, the meeting will be preceded by a farewell luncheon party in honor of Colonel J.B. Spiegelenberg at 12:30 for 1 p.m. in the Salon des Ambassadeurs. The notice is signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originated from Brussels.

NPG(Staff Group)N(73)53

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th June, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to discuss a draft
paper(1) prepared by the International Staff on Consultation Procedures and
Facilities.

2. The meeting will be preceded by a farewell luncheon party in honour of
Colonel J.B. Spiegelenberg at 12.30 for 1 p.m., in the Salon des Ambassadeurs.

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)7, 21st June, 1973
