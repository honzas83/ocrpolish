> # Next Meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 15th January 1976. It specifies that there will be no meeting on Monday 19th January, as decided by the Staff Group on 12th January. Additionally, it announces a debriefing session for members of Canada, Greece, Netherlands, and Norway who did not attend the Hamburg Meeting, to be held in the Chairman's office at 10:30 a.m. on Monday 26th January. Finally, it schedules a meeting of the Staff Group for Monday 26th January 1976, at 3 p.m. to discuss the results of the Hamburg Meeting.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

15th January, 1976

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)6

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETINGS

Note by the Secretary

1. In accordance with the decision of the Staff Group taken at its meeting held
on 12th January, there will be no meeting on Monday 19th January.

2. A debriefing will be given by the Chairman to members of those countries not
attending the Hamburg Meeting (Canada, Greece, Netherlands and Norway) in his
office at 10.30 a.m. on Monday 26th January.

3. The Staff Group will meet on Monday, 26th January, 1976, at 3 p.m. to discuss
the results of the Hamburg Meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
