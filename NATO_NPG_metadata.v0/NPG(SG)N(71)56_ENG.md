> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, 10th May, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue preparations for the Mittenwald Meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.
