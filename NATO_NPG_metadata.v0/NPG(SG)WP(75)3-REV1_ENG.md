> # Draft Advance Press Release for the NPG 1975 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) and a draft advance press release. The note, dated May 13, 1975, requests that Staff Group members from participating countries obtain approval from their national authorities for the revised text and timing of the announcement, with a deadline of May 28, 1975. The press release itself specifies that the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, comprising the Defence Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will meet in Monterey, California, under the Chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns, following an invitation from the United States Government. The proposed announcement is intended for release in Brussels on June 9, 1975, at 3:00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries as desired.

Note by the Chairman
Attached to the revised text in English and French of a proposed advance press
release concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Monterey,
California on 16th and 17th June, 1975. The text closely follows the language
used in former press announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. It has
been suggested that an announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, 9th June,
1975 at 3.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous additional release in the
capitals of those participating countries who so desire.

2. Staff Group members of the countries participating in the Monterey meeting
are requested to obtain the approval of their national authorities for the
revised text of the announcement and timing of the release, informing me no
later than Wednesday, 28th May, 1975. Information on whether national
authorities plan to make a simultaneous release in the capital would also be
appreciated.

(Signed) R.L. SHEAR.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of the Defence
Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey,
the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the invitation of the
Government of the United States, hold its seventeenth Ministerial Meeting in
Monterey, California, United States, on 16th and 17th June, 1975 under the
Chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. LUNL, Secretary General of NATO.

Le Groupe des plens nucléaire de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les ministres
de la défense de la Belgique, du Danemark, des États-Unis, de l'Italie, de la
République Fédérale d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie biendarie, à
l'invitation du gouvernement de l'États-Unis, sa 17ème réunion semestrielle au
niveau ministériel les 16 et 17 juin à Monterey, Californie, États-Unis, sous la
présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. LUNL, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN.
