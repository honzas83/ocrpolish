> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Wednesday, May 2, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the agenda are to conduct a follow-up to the Florida NPG Ministerial meeting and to address any other business. The notice was issued by D.J. Roberts from IATO in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 2nd May, 1979
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) follow-up to the Florida NPG Ministerial meeting;
(b) any other business.
