> [!abstract] 
> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(75)102
> 
> This document is a corrigendum to a press release for the eighteenth Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) ministerial meeting. It requests holders of the notice NPG(Staff Group)N(75)102, dated 4th December 1975, to replace the last sentence of page 2 in both the English and French versions with a statement clarifying that the meeting was originally scheduled for 20th and 21st November 1975 but had to be postponed. The document was issued by the North Atlantic Council in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
PRESS RELEASE FOR THE EIGHTEENTH NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING

Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(75)102
dated 4th December, 1975

Holders of the above mentioned notice are requested to make the following
change:

Page 2, English and French versions: Delete all of last sentence and replace by:
"This meeting was originally scheduled for 20th and 21st November, 1975, and had
to be postponed."

"Cette réunion avait été prévue pour les 20 et 21 novembre 1975 et avait dû être
ajournée."

1110 Brussels.
