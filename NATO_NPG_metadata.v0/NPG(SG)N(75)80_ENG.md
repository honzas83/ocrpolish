> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1975 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note from the Acting Chairman, J-P. Mentzel, regarding a proposed advance press release for the 18th Ministerial Meeting of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) scheduled for 20th and 21st November 1975 in Hamburg, Federal Republic of Germany. The note requests that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggest release timing, with a proposed announcement date of Friday, 17th November 1975, in Brussels. Staff Group members from participating countries are asked to obtain approval from their national authorities for the text and timing of the release by 17th October 1975. The draft press release itself, which is provided in both English and French, identifies the participating nations as Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, and notes that the meeting will be chaired by NATO Secretary General Joseph H.A.H. Luns.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE AUTUMN 1975 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Acting Chairman
Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming N.G Ministerial meeting in Hamburg, Federal Republic
of Germany on 20th and 21st November, 1975. The text closely follows the
language used in former press announcements prior to N.G Ministerial meetings.
As on past occasions the release could be made a few days before the meeting
takes place; accordingly, it is suggested that an announcement be made in
Brussels on Friday, 17th November, 1975 at 3.00 p.m. local time, with
simultaneous additional release in the capitals of those participating countries
who so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggest
release timing at its meeting on 6th October. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Hamburg meeting are requested to obtain, after
their initial discussion, the approval of their national authorities for the
text of the announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director,
Nuclear Planning, no later than Friday, 17th October, 1975. Information on
whether national authorities plan to make a simultaneous release in the capitals
would also be appreciated.

(Signed) J-P. MENTZEL.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, at Ministerial level, currently consisting
of the Ministers of Defence of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of
Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the
invitation of the Government of the Federal Republic of Germany, hold its
eighteenth Ministerial Meeting in Hamburg, on 20th and 21st November, 1975,
under the Chairmanship of Mr. Joseph H.A.H. LUNS, Secretary General of NATO.

Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement la Belgique,
le Danemark, les États-Unis, l'Italie, la République fédérale d'Allemagne, le
Royaume-Uni et la Turquie tiendra, à l'invitation du gouvernement de la
République fédérale d'Allemagne, sa 18e réunion semestrielle au niveau
ministériel les 20 et 21 novembre à Hambourg, sous la présidence de M. Joseph
H.A.H. LUNS, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN.
