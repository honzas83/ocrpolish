> # Note by the Secretary regarding the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Meeting
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated September 26, 1968. It announces that the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) will take place on Friday, September 27, 1968, at 2:15 p.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to discuss the Press Announcement and other matters concerning the Ministerial Meeting to be held in Bonn on October 10 and 11, 1968. The note is signed by N.H.O. Willson and originated from NATO Brussels.
