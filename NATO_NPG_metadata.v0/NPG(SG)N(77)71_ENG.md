> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in Bari
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO), lists the composition of the International Staff delegation accompanying Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luns to the 22nd Meeting of NPG Ministers in Bari, Italy, scheduled for October 1977. The delegation includes high-ranking officials such as Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning & Policy W.F. Mumford, Director of Nuclear Planning R.E. Shearer, and Deputy Executive Secretary E.G. Luft. It also includes support staff, including the NATO Press Service, Office of Security, Nuclear Planning Staff Officer, and interpreters. The document serves as an official record of the personnel involved in the_International Staff delegation for this specific ministerial meeting.
