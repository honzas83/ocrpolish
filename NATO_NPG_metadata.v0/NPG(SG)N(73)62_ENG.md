> # Meetings of the NPG Permanent Representatives
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group regarding the scheduling of meetings for Permanent Representatives. The original timetable for preparing for the Autumn 1973 NPG Ministerial meeting, adopted in early June, had anticipated meetings on 27th September and 18th October. However, since the NPG Ministerial meeting has been rescheduled to 6th and 7th November, the Chairman proposes that the Permanent Representatives' sessions be moved to 4th October and 25th October 1973. The meeting on 4th October is intended for final action on reports to the Ministers, and the meeting on 25th October is for the final exchange of views on Ministerial Agenda items. The Chairman, H.B. Seim, indicates that the Staff Group will discuss these proposed changes of dates at its next meeting on Monday, 16th July.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
MEETINGS OF THE NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES
Note by the Chairman
The timetable(1) for preparing for the Autumn 1973 NPG Ministerial meeting,
adopted by the Staff Group for planning purposes in early June, anticipates two
NPG Permanent Representatives' meetings on 27th September and 18th October. This
timetable was developed on the assumption that the NPG Ministerial meeting would
be held in late October. Since it has now been agreed that the Ministers will
meet on 6th and 7th November, it is proposed that the NPG Permanent
Representatives' sessions be re-scheduled as follows:

Thursday, 4th October 3:30 p.m. – Permanent Representatives' meeting to take
final action on reports to the Ministers.

Thursday, 25th October 3:30 p.m. – Permanent Representatives' meeting for final
exchange of views on Ministerial Agenda items.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the proposed changes of dates at
its next meeting on Monday, 16th July.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.
