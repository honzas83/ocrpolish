> # Next Meetings of the NPG St. FF Group
> 
> This document outlines the potential scheduling for the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group on Force Factors (ST. FF GROUP). The group agreed to hold its next meeting on either Thursday, 21st May, in the morning, or Monday, 25th May, in the afternoon, with the final decision to be made at a later date. The communication is directed to NATO officials in Brussels.
