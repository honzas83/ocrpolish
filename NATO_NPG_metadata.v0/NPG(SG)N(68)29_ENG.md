> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, dated 12th September 1968, announcing that the next meeting will take place on Friday, 13th December 1968, at 4 p.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to continue the discussion regarding ADM Time Factors. The notice was signed by M.I.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium, under the authority of OTAN/TATO.
