> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, April 12, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the procedural handling of a United States paper regarding the improvement of NATO's Theatre Nuclear Force Posture, to hold an initial discussion on the presentation of newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation, and to begin discussing the composition of the agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Spring Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document explicitly mentions a discussion on the 'U.S. paper on improving the effectiveness of NATO's Theatre Nuclear Force Posture', which directly relates to the deployment and effectiveness of nuclear weapons in the European theatre.
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The document references the discussion of 'Newly Developed Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation', which concerns the internal mechanisms and execution consultation for nuclear operations.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 12th April, 1976
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion on the procedural handling of the U.S. paper on
improving the effectiveness of NATO's Theatre Nuclear Force Posture(1)(2);

(b) to hold an initial disucssion on the presentation of Newly Developed
Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation(3);

(c) to start discussion of the composition of the Agenda for the NPG Spring
Ministerial Meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(76)22, 12th March, 1976
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(76)27, 6th March, 1976
