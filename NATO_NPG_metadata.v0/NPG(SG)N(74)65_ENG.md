> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday, 5th June, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on June 5, 1974, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to coordinate preparations for the upcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Bergen. The group took note of several documents, including reports on the Military Committee's function in the nuclear consultation process and the final agenda and communiqué for the Bergen meeting. Discussions focused on the logistical arrangements for the upcoming ministerial session, including the press release schedule and the courier for classified documents. The group also addressed the communication of NATO's intentions to enemies, other countries, and the public, agreeing to re-examine the question after the Bergen meeting. Additionally, the group scheduled an initial examination of SACEUR's illustrative study on the defensive tactical use of nuclear air defence weapons for their next meeting on June 17, 1974. A subsequent notice from the Secretary, M.H.G. Wilson, confirmed the next meeting's objectives, which include reviewing the Bergen meeting results and discussing the SACEUR study.

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday,
5th June, 1974

DOCUMENTATION

1. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who drew attention to the following
papers which had been circulated:

(a) NPG/D(74)5, 29th May, 1974: Report by the Permanent Representatives on the
Military Committee Function in the Nuclear Consultation Process;

(b) NPG/A(74)3(Revised), 30th May, 1974: Final Agenda for the meeting of NPG
Ministers in Bergen;

(c) DPP/NPD/74/63, 30th May, 1974: Draft Final Communiqué for the NPG
Ministerial Meeting in Bergen;

(d) DPP/NPD/74/64, 30th May, 1974: Framework for the Agreed Minute for the NPG
Ministerial Meeting in Bergen.

2. The STAFF GROUP:
took note also of the Chairman's remarks concerning AC/237-WP/90, 21st May,
1974, the Draft Report to the DPC on Exercise HILEX-6, with particular reference
to Section II (c) – Consultation and Decision-Making.

took note of a statement by the Chairman who expressed the hope that Ministers

allowing sufficient time after their morning and afternoon sessions for the
drafting group to prepare the drafts of the Agreed Minute and the Final
Communiqué.

public, and on the related procedural matters; he referred to the paper(2)

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th June, 1974
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the results of the Bergen meeting;

(b) to review the future work of the Staff Group;

(c) to hold an initial discussion of SACEUR's Illustrative Air Defence Study(1).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
