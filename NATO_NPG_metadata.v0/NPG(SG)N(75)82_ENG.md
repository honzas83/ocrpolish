> # Note by the Secretary Regarding the Staff Group Meeting
> 
> This document is a formal note from the Secretary of the North Atlantic Council (NAC) to the Staff Group. It outlines the agenda for a meeting scheduled for Friday, October 25, 1975, at 00:00. The primary matters for discussion include CI messages and an initial change of views on specific items, provided time permits and members desire it. Additionally, the document mentions that the coming meeting of the Staff Group is planned for Monday, October 25, 1975, and hopes that certain positions can be announced. The note is signed by T. J. C. IL50 and references several working papers, including PC(Staff Group)WP(5)0(2nd Revise) and PC(Staff Group)WP(5) and PC(Staff Group)WP(75)83.
