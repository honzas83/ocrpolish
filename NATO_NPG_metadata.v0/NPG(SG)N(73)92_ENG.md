> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, September 24, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to examine draft reports prepared for the upcoming Ministerial meeting, taking into account any instructions received from national capitals, and to discuss the first draft of a possible report regarding the Status of the Follow-on Use Work Programme. The notice is signed by M.H.C. Wilson. It references several working papers, including NPG/WP(73)6, NPG/WP(73)8, NPG/WP(73)9, NPG/WP(73)10, NPG/WP(73)11, and NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)13.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 24th September,
1973 at 3 p.m. The purpose of the meeting will be:

(a) to examine the draft reports(1) prepared for the next Ministerial meeting in
the light of instructions which may have been received from capitals;

(b) to discuss the first draft(2) of a possible report on the Status of the
Follow-on Use Work Programme.

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON
