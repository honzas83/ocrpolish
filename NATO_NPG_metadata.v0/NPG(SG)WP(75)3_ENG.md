> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1975 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding a proposed advance press release for the Spring 1975 Ministerial Meeting scheduled for June 16th and 17th, 1975, in Monterey, California. The Chairman, R.E. Shearer, suggests that the announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, June 13th, 1975, at 3:00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is intended to discuss the draft text and timing of the release during its meeting on March 21st. Members of the Staff Group from participating countries are requested to obtain approval from their national authorities for the text and timing, and to inform the Chairman no later than Wednesday, May 28th, 1975. Additionally, the Chairman requests information on whether national authorities plan to make simultaneous releases in their respective capitals.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE
SPRING 1975 MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Attached is the text in English and French of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting in Monterey, California on
16th and 17th June, 1975. The text closely follows the language used in former
press announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. As on past occasions the
release could be made a few days before the meeting takes place; accordingly, it
is suggested that an announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, 13th June, 1975
at 3.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous additional release in the capitals of
those participating countries who so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 21st March. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Monterey meeting are requested to obtain, after
their initial discussion, the approval of their national authorities for the
text of the announcement and timing of the release, informing me no later than
Wednesday, 28th May, 1975. Information on whether national authorities plan to
make a simultaneous release in the capital would also be appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. Shearer

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
