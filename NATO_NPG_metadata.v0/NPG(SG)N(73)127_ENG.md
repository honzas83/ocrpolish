> # Notice of the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Wednesday, December 12, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to review points of interest arising from Ministerial meetings and to continue discussions regarding arrangements for future Nuclear Planning Group activities. Additionally, the notice mentions a vin d'honneur in room I-334 at 5 p.m. following the meeting to mark the departure of Mr. Moray Stewart and to welcome his successor, Mr. N.J. Beaumont. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 12th December,
1973 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to review any points of interest which may have come up during the
Ministerial meetings;

(b) to continue discussion of arrangements for future NPG activities.

2. Members of the Staff Group will recall that, after the meeting, a vin
d'honneur will take place in room I-334 at 5 p.m. to mark the departure of Mr.
Moray Stewart, and to welcome his successor, Mr. N.J. Beaumont.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
