> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, issued by the North Atlantic Council. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, May 3, 1977, at 10:30 a.m. and potentially at 3 p.m. if necessary. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions regarding the draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial meeting in June, to continue the examination of the International Staff paper on the New Technology Study, and to address any other business. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
27th April, 1977
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)F(77)37
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STA. F GROUPE)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 3rd May, 1977 at
10.30 a.m. and at 3 p.m. if necessary. The principal objects of this meeting
will be:

(a) to continue the discussion of the draft agenda for the NP G Ministerial
meeting in June(1);

(b) to continue the examination of the International Staff paper on the New
Technology Study(2);

(c) to address any other business.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)4, 1st April, 1977
(2) NPG(Staff Group)VP(77)5, 22nd April, 1977
