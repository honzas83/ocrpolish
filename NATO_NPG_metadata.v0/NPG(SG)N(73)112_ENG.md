> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, November 12, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to hold a preliminary discussion regarding the results of the recent meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers in The Hague. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 12th November,
1973 at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to hold a preliminary
discussion on the results of the meeting of NPG Ministers in The Hague.
