> # Advance Press Release - Venice Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 21st May 1970. It confirms that the United States Delegation has agreed to the originally proposed date of Friday, 5th June 1970, for the release of the advance press release for the meeting of Ministers of the Nuclear Planning Group in Venice. Consequently, a previous notice suggesting an earlier release date is cancelled. The Chairman, R. B. Seim, specifies that the release of the agreed text will take place in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday, 5th June 1970, with simultaneous release in national capitals at the option of each country concerned. The document references previous communications NPG(Staff Group)N(70)4C and NPG(Staff Group)N(70)46.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE - VENICE MEETING
Note by the Chairman
The United States Delegation has just been advised that Washington can go along
with originally-proposed date – Friday, 5th June – for the release of the
Advance Press Release(1) for the meeting of Ministers of the Nuclear Planning
Group in Venice.

2. Accordingly, the NPG Staff Group Notice(2) reporting the US suggestion that
the release be made one day earlier than planned is hereby cancelled.

3. All Delegations now having indicated their concurrence, the release of the
agreed text(1) will be made in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday, 5th
June, 1970 with simultaneous release in capitals at the option of the country
concerned.

(Signed) R. B. SEIM
NATC, 1110 Brussels
