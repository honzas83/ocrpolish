> # Advance Press Release - Venice Meeting
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on May 22, 1970, serves as a cover note by the Secretary accompanying a French translation of an advanced press release. The press release announces that the Nuclear Planning Group of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO), consisting of ministers of defense from Canada, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will hold its seventh semi-annual meeting on June 8 and 9 in Venice. The meeting is scheduled to be chaired by Manlio Brosio, the Secretary General of NATO. The document also references previous arrangements for release set out in NPG(Staff Group)N(70)46 dated May 20, 1970.
