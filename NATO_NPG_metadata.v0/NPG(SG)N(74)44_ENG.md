> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, April 29, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue the examination of a revised text for a possible Permanent Representatives' Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons, incorporating suggested modifications. Additionally, the meeting will focus on preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The document references several previous documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)5(Revised), NPG/Study/45, and NPG(Staff Group)N(74)41, as well as amendments from the United Kingdom and Canada.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 29th April, 1974
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue, if necessary, examination of a revised text(1) of a possible
Permanent Representatives' Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military
Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons(2),
taking into consideration some suggested modifications circulated
separately.(3);

(b) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting.

(Signed) N.L.C. PARK

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)5(Revised), 1st April, 1974
(2) NPG/Study/45, 8th March, 1974
(3) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)41, 17th April, 1974; amendments circulated by the
United Kingdom Representative on 19th April and by the Canadian Representative
on 22nd April, 1974
