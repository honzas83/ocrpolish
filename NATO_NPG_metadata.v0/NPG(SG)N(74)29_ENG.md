> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Thursday, 14th March, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on 14th March 1974 at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting focused on three primary agenda items. First, the group discussed a report by the Ad Hoc Study Group on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons, agreeing that a draft report for Permanent Representatives would highlight specific aspects of the Study Group's paper. The group planned for this draft to be circulated to capitals for approval by 9th May 1974. Second, the group noted the upcoming circulation of a revised draft report regarding the Military Committee's function in the Nuclear Consultation Process. Third, the group concurred with the Chairman's proposal to circulate a draft agenda for the NPG Spring Ministerial meeting for further discussion. The meeting concluded by scheduling the next Staff Group meeting for 25th March 1974.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Thursday,
14th March, 1974

WARSAW PACT STRATEGY AND DOCTRINE
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who invited initial comments and
discussion of the report by the Ad Hoc Study Group on Warsaw Pact Politico-
Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear
Weapons(1);

(b) took note of the views expressed and of the suggestions made concerning the
possible form and content of a draft report by Permanent Representatives to
Ministers; agreed that the draft report report might high-light certain aspects
of the Study Group's paper but would not need to summarise it;

(c) agreed that it was necessary to plan the work on this matter in such a way
as to ensure that the draft report should be circulated to capitals in time for
examination and approval by Permanent Representatives at their meeting scheduled
for 9th May, 1974;

(d) invited the Chairman to prepare an initial draft in the light of the views
just expressed, in time for discussion at the next meeting of the Staff Group.

(1) NPG/Study/45, 8th March, 1974

MILITARY COMMITTEE FUNCTION IN THE NUCLEAR CONSULTATION PROCESS

2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) noted that the next revision of the draft report(1) on the Military
Committee function in the Nuclear Consultation Process would be circulated
immediately;

(b) agreed to discuss the new revision at the next meeting of the Staff Group.

DRAFT AGENDA FOR THE NPSPRING MINISTERIAL MEETING

3. The STAFF GROUP:
concurred with a statement by the Chairman who proposed to circulate a draft
agenda for the NPG Spring Ministerial meeting for discussion at the next meeting
of the Staff Group.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP

4. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 25th March, 1974 at 3 p.m.

1110 Brussels.
