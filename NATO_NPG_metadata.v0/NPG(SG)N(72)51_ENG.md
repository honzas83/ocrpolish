> # Farewell Luncheon for Departing Members
> 
> A notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announces a farewell luncheon party in honor of Mr. E.V. Cuade, Mr. C. Stephansen, Mr. H.R. Morgan, and Colonel P.E. Montoya. The event is scheduled to take place in the Salon des Ambassadeurs at NATO Headquarters on Friday, 16th June, 1972, from 12:45 to 13:15. All members of the Staff Group are cordially invited to attend. Those who have not yet confirmed their attendance are requested to notify the Secretary by the morning of 13th June. Additionally, the notice mentions that host members will be informed of their financial contributions in due course.

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(72)51
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Farewell Luncheon for Departing Members
Note by the Secretary
A farewell luncheon party, in honour of Mr. E.V. Cuade, Mr. C. Stephansen, Mr.
H.R. Morgan and Colonel P.E. Montoya, will be held in the Salon des
Ambassadeurs, NATO Headquarters, on Friday, 16th June, 1972, at 12.45 for 13.15.

All members of the Staff Group are cordially invited to take part. Those who
have not already informed the Secretary whether they are able to attend are
requested to do so, if possible not later than the morning of 13th June.

Host members will be informed of their contribution in due course.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
