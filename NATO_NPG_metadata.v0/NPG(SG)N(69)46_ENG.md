> # Advance Press Release for London NPG Meeting
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Secretary and an advance press release for the fifth meeting of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) in London, scheduled for May 29 and 30, 1969. The note by the Secretary, signed by M.H.G. Wilson, specifies that the press release will be released by the Head of the NATO Press Service at 3:00 p.m. Brussels time on Tuesday, May 27, 1969, with optional simultaneous release in NPG capitals. The press release itself states that, as foreshadowed in the Communiqué of the last Ministerial meeting in Bonn, the Nuclear Planning Group, consisting of the Defence Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, member states of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO), {the Ministers will continue their discussions of nuclear planning. The meeting is held under the Chairmanship of Mr. Manlio Brosio, Secretary General of NATO.

ANNUAL to NPG(Staff Group)N(69)46
CONFIDENTIAL UNTIL RELEASE
ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR LONDON NPG MEETING
As foreshadowed in the Communiqué of the last Ministerial meeting in Bonn, the
Nuclear Planning Group of NATO which at present consists of the Defence
Ministers of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom and
the United States will hold its fifth meeting in London on 29th and 30th May
under the Chairmanship of Mr. Manilo Brosio, Secretary General of NATO. At this
meeting the Ministers will continue their discussions of nuclear planning.
