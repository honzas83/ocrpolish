> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, January 14, 1974, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions regarding the arrangements for the Study of the Implications of Technological Development and to proceed with preparations for the upcoming Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 14th January,
1974, at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of arrangements for the Study of the Implications of
Technological Development;

(b) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
