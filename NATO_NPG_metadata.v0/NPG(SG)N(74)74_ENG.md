> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, acting on behalf of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), announcing the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Wednesday, July 3, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the discussion of an initial draft of a study on Communicating NATO’s Intention to use Nuclear Weapons, referencing document NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document explicitly mentions a study on 'Communicating NATO’s Intention to use Nuclear Weapons', which relates to the consultation and communication of nuclear use within the Alliance, a core component of Extended Deterrence.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 3rd July,
