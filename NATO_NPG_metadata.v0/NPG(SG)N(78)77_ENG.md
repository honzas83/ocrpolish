> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, November 13, 1978, at 3 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes a discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines and the finalization of the Permanent Representatives' report on TNF Short Term Improvements. The notice also references several documents, including USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126 and NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)8, regarding the political guidelines, and NPG/D(78)9, NPG/WP(78)9, and a United Kingdom Delegation paper NPO/83/8/2, concerning the TNF improvements. The meeting is organized under the auspices of the North Atlantic Council.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 13th November,
1978 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1);
(b) finalisation of the Permanent Representatives' report on TNF Short Term
Improvements(2);

(c) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
