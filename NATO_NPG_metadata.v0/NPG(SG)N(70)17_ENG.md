> # News Article on NPG Activities
> 
> The Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) circulated a news article from Atlantic News (Nouvelles Atlantiques) dated 5th March 1970 for the information of the Staff Group. The article describes the continuation of studies by the Nuclear Planning Group concerning the possible use of tactical and strategic nuclear weapons in a conflict with the forces of the Warsaw Pact. According to military officials, this complex task is expected to take at least two years to complete. The objective of the new study phase is to develop directives for the pursuit and escalation of an atomic conflict should initial defensive tactical nuclear weapon use fail to deter the communist world from stopping hostilities. The work is organized on a regional basis, with the United States participating in strategic studies for the central front and other regions. The United Kingdom is involved in studies concerning the territory defended by the Northern Army Group and the northern flank, including Norway and Denmark. Belgium and the Netherlands are also participating in studies regarding the Northern Army Group's nuclear strategy, while the German Federal Republic is involved in studies concerning its own territory, and Mediterranean countries including Italy, Greece, and Turkey are conducting other studies.

TL'TIC NEWS
5th March, 1970

The Nuclear Planning Group has now arranged for the continuation of the studies
concerning the possible use of tactic.l and strategic nuclear weapons in a
conflict with the forces of the Warsaw Pact. According to the military
officials, this is an extremely complex and delicate task which cannot be
concluded in less than two years. The aim of this new study phase is to
elaborate the directives required for the pursuit and escalation of the atomic
conflict, if the initial and defensive use of a small number of tactical nuclear
weapons should not achieve the desired objective of convincing the leaders of
the communist world that the best solution would be an immediate stop to
hostilities.

The work is being organised on a regional basis. The United States are
participating directly in the strategic studies concerning the central front,
but they also attend the preparation of the studies concerning other regions.
The United Kingdom too, will participate in various studies and particularly in
that one which concerns the territory defended by the Northern Army Group and in
the study related to the northern flank (Norway and Denmark). Belgium and the
Netherlands will of course participate in the study concerning the nuclear
strategy of the Northern Army Group. The German Federal Republic is involved in
the t.o. studies concerning her territory, while other studies are being carried
out by the Mediterranean countries (Italy, Greece, Turkey).
