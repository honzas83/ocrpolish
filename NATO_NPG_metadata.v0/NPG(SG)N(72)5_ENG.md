> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, January 17, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to examine the best way to prepare a report on the initiation of Phase II of the Follow-on Use Studies. Additionally, if a first draft paper is available in time, the group may discuss the factual description of facilities available to the Alliance for consulting on requests for the use of nuclear weapons. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th January,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be:

to examine how best to prepare a report on the initiation of Phase II of the
Follow-on Use Studies. If a first draft paper is available in time, a discussion
might also take place on the factual description of the facilities that are
available to the Alliance for consulting on requests for the use of nuclear
weapons.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
