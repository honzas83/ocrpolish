> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 9th February, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on February 9, 1976, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The session covered several key items, including the confirmation of security clearances for all attendees. The group reviewed the informal record of the 18th Meeting of NPG Ministers in Hamburg and noted the United States' authorization to reproduce additional copies of a paper on improving the effectiveness of NATO's theater nuclear forces. Discussions were held regarding the policy of NATO towards Chemical Warfare and the requirements for improved communications, command and control. The group also addressed the handling of press conferences for Ministerial meetings and the development of specific Nuclear Consultation (CNI) procedures. The Italian representative mentioned that the next meeting of the MIT would take place in Rome, while the German authorities proposed meetings for the PIT and Phase III Study Group in NATO headquarters. Finally, the group agreed to hold its next meeting on February 16, 1976.

SECURITY

took note that all persons present were cleared for the security classification
of the matters to be discussed.

RECORD OF HAMBURG MEETING

2. The ST.FF GROUP:
(a) took note that the informal record of the 18th Meeting of NPG Ministers held
in Hamburg was available for consultation in the Chairman's office;

(b) took note of the remarks of the United States representative who said that
his authorities had authorised the NATO Registry to reproduce additional copies
of the US 'TOMAL paper entitled "Improving the Effectiveness of NATO's Theater
Nuclear Forces".

REMINDER

3. The ST.FF GROUP:
(a) took note of the remarks of the Chairman who recalled that NPG Ministers, at
