> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, December 8, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss the outcomes of Ministerial discussions held on December 3 and 4, 1969, specifically as they relate to Nuclear Planning, and to conduct further studies on follow-on tactical use. The notice is signed by H.C. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium, under the authority of NATO. The document also references previous communications, including DN(74)18 and NPG(Staff Group)N(69)87.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document explicitly mentions 'further studies on follow-on tactical use', which relates to the deployment and use of tactical nuclear weapons in Europe, a core component of Extended Deterrence and Tactical Nuclear Sharing.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th December,
1969, at 2.45 p.m.

The principal subjects of discussion will be :
- the outcome of the Ministerial discussions held on 3rd and 4th December
insofar as they concern Nuclear Planning,

- further studies on follow-on tactical use (1).

(Signed) H.C. WILSON

OTAN/NATO
Brussels, 39

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(69)87, 27th November, 1969.
