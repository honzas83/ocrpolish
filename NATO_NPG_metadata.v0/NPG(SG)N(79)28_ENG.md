> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, July 9, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with a specific focus on Section IV regarding the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice also allows for any other business to be considered. The document lists several reference documents, including USNATO/DPD/OUT/IS/78-126 and NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)8, to provide context for the discussion. The meeting is originated from Brussels, Belgium, and is signed by D.J. Roberts.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 9th July, 1979 at
3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
