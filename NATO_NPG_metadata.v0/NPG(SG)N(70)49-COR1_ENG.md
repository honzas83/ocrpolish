> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(70)49: Security at the Venice Meeting
> 
> This document, dated 1st June 1970, serves as a corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(70)49, which was originally issued on 28th May 1970. It provides specific corrections and updates to the security protocols for the Venice meeting. The updates include instructions for the arrival of ATOMAL documents, specifying that they should be taken to an ATOMAL Registry or Control Point upon arrival at the destination. It also details the reception of ATOMAL couriers, stating that the Italian Authorities are arranging for couriers to be met at TESSNA aerodrome and the railway station, and escorted to the TOH:I Control Point on San Giorgio island, with land and water transport provided. Couriers carrying normal classified documents may also benefit from these arrangements. Additionally, the document modifies the numbering of sub-paragraphs in paragraph 12 of the original notice.
