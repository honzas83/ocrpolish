> # Nuclear Flying Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Flying Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, April 9, 1970, at 3 p.m. The principal subjects for discussion include the initial draft of the general statement on Airborne Delivery Systems (ADHs), in light of comments from the April 6 meeting, the date of the next meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives, and the draft agenda for the meeting of Ministers in Venice, including whether a Status Report on Follow-on studies should be prepared. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR FLYING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 9th April, 1970
at 3 p.m.

2. The principal subjects for discussion will be:
(a) the initial draft of the general statement on ADHs(1), in the light of
comments made at the Staff Group meeting held on 6th April;

(b) the date of the next meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives;
(c) the draft agenda for the meeting of Ministers in Venice(2), including the
question as to whether a Status Report on Follow-on studies should be
prepared(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
