> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled a meeting for August 27, 1973, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The meeting's primary objectives are to hold a final discussion on the draft report on Consultation Procedures and Facilities, as modified by NPG(Staff Group)N(73)79. Additionally, the group will continue discussions on the report regarding the implementation of Special ADM Guidelines based on a revised draft prepared by the International Staff. The meeting will also include a preliminary discussion of a draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in The Hague. Finally, the group will consider the first draft of a report on the advantages and disadvantages of the early combined use of conventional and nuclear capabilities.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take
place on Monday, 27th August, 1973 at 3 p.m. The principal
objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to hold a final discussion on the draft
report(1) on Consultation Procedures and
Facilities, as modified by NPG(Staff Group)N(73)79,
dated 22nd August, 1973;

(b) to continue the discussion of the report on
the implementation of Special ADM Guidelines
on the basis of the revised draft(2) prepared
by the International Staff;

(c) to hold a preliminary discussion of a draft
agenda(3) for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in
The Hague;

(d) to consider the first draft(4) of a possible
report on the advantages and disadvantages of
early combined use of conventional and nuclear
capabilities.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels
