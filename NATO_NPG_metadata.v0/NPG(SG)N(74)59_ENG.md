> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on May 22, 1974. It announces that a meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, May 27, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue preparations for the upcoming Spring meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
