> # Draft Agenda for the May 1971 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document consists of a cover note from H. B. Seim to the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) and a draft agenda for the upcoming May 1971 Ministerial meeting in Mittenwald. The agenda items include reports on the follow-on use of nuclear weapons, specifically mentioning studies on North Norway and Greece, and illustrative studies and sample release requests. The meeting's focus is also on the strategic use of nuclear weapons, including a briefing on the balance of strategic forces and the concept for the role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in ACE. The document serves as a basis for initial discussion at a Staff Group meeting on February 8th, 1971.
>
