> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the cancellation of a scheduled meeting on July 13, 1970. The meeting has been rescheduled for Wednesday, July 15, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting remains unchanged: to discuss the revised draft of the Special ADM Guidelines, which were prepared by the International Staff in response to a request from the Staff Group at their meeting on July 6, 1970. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
It has been found necessary to cancel the meeting of the Staff Group scheduled
for today, 13th July. The next meeting of the Staff Group will now take place on
Wednesday, 15th July, 1970 at 3 p.m.

The principal object of the meeting is unchanged and is to discuss the revised
draft(1) of the Special ADM Guidelines, prepared by the International Staff in
response to a request made by the Staff Group at their meeting held on 6th July,
1970.

(Sig:md) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
