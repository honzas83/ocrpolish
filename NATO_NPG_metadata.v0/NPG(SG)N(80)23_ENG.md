> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, May 8, 1980, at 9:50 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to consider a draft report by Permanent Representatives on Nuclear Play in future WINTEX Exercises. Additionally, the meeting will cover any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 8th May, 1980
at 9.50 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) consideration of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on Nuclear

(b) any other business.
