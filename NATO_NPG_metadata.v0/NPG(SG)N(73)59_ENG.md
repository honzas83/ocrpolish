> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, July 9, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to continue discussions on future Nuclear Planning Group activities, with a specific focus on preparations for the next Ministerial meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 9th July, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue the discussion
of future NPG activities as appropriate, with particular reference to
preparations for the next Ministerial meeting.

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON
