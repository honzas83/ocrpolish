> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 110 Brussels, on Tuesday, 24th May, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group held a meeting in Brussels on May 24, 1977, to address several administrative and technical agenda items. The group noted the schedule for an upcoming lecture by Dr. Edward Teller, scheduled for May 27, 1977. They also discussed travel arrangements for a visit to North-West Canada, including dress code and hotel expenses to be covered by the Canadian authorities. The meeting's Agreed Minute and Communiqué are to be distributed in due course. Additionally, the group agreed to amendments proposed by the German and Italian representatives regarding theatre integral improvements and new technology studies, respectively. Both the Original Paper on theatre integral improvements and the New Technology Study paper were to be issued as Nuclear Planning Committee documents for consideration by Ministers in Ottawa. The group concluded by agreeing to leave the date of its next meeting open.

LECTURE BY DR. EDWARD TELLER

1. The STAFF GROUP:
noted that the talk by Dr. Teller would take place from 9.30 a.m. to 10.30 a.m.
on Friday, 27th May, in room 14.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE VISIT TO NORTH-WEST CANADA

2. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of the remarks of the Canadian representative who said:

(a) informal dress was essential for the trip to the station; Ladies should wear
slacks and walking shoes;

(b) during the trip hotel expenses would be paid by the Canadian Authorities.

AGREED MINUTE

3. The STAFF GROUP:
noted the remarks of the Chairman who said that a tentative first draft of the
Agreed Minute and of the Communiqué would be distributed in due course.

THEATRE INTEGRAL - DUC IMPROVEMENTS

4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) agreed to an amendment proposed by the German member to NN/WP(77)6 and to
some minor editorial changes;

(b) agreed, in the light of the views expressed by the Chairman the previous day
that the Original Paper should be issued as an NPC Document for consideration by
Ministers at their meeting in Ottawa.

NEW TECHNOLOGY STUDY

5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) agreed to an amendment proposed by the Italian representative to NN/WP(77)6;
(b) agreed, in the light of the Permanent Representatives' discussion to
circulate this paper as an NPC Document for consideration by Ministers in
Ottawa.

NEXT MEETING

6. The STAFF GROUP: agreed to leave open the date of its next meeting.
