> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, September 11, 1972, at 5 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to review the revised version of the draft report on the Transition Study. The notice references two other documents: NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)10(Revised) and NPG/Study/23. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
