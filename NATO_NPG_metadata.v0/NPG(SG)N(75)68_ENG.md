> [!abstract] 
> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 110 Brussels, on Monday, 8th September, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on September 8, 1975, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting's agenda included a welcome for Major Russ MacDonald of the United States Delegation. The group took note of a briefing by Dr. Francis Lane from CF Corporation on Nuclear Weapons Simulation of Combat as a tool for assessing battlefield effectiveness. Discussions were held regarding nuclear release requests from NATO member councils, with the group agreeing to reopen the discussion at the next meeting following instructions from capitals. The group also concurred with a proposed work schedule for NPG Permanent Representatives, noting difficulties for the Turkish representative. Preparations for the NPG Ministerial meeting in Germany were discussed, including arrival dates, accommodation, and the requirement for ATOMAL clearance certificates. The United States representative proposed that the ministerial agenda should focus on employment policy and war issues. The group agreed to discuss the communication of NATO's intentions and aids to decision-making in future meetings, and noted current activity in the Military Committee on Air Defence. The next meetings were scheduled for September 15 and provisionally for September 19, 1975.
