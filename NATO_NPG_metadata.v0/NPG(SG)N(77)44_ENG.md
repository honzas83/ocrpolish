> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in Ottawa
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on May 24, 1977, lists the members of the International Staff accompanying the Secretary General to the 21st Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers to be held in Ottawa in June. The notice, signed by M.H.G. Wilson, identifies the personnel involved, including high-ranking officials such as Mr. W.F. Nutford, Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning and Policy, and Mr. R.E. Shearer, Director of Nuclear Planning. The delegation includes representatives from the NATO Press Service, the Office of Security, and various administrative and secretarial staff. The document serves as an official record of the delegation's composition for the upcoming ministerial meeting in Canada.
