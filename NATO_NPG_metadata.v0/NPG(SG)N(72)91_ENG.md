> # Draft Agenda for a Possible NDAC Ministerial Meeting in December, 1972
> 
> This document, authored by H.B. Seim, serves as a note from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) presenting a draft agenda for a possible Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee (NDAC) Ministerial Meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place at NATO Headquarters in Brussels, Belgium, on Wednesday, December 6, 1972, at 9:30 a.m. The proposed agenda includes a report by the Nuclear Planning Group, referencing the Copenhagen and London Agreed Minutes, and a discussion on the updating of NATO Nuclear Planning Directives, specifically referencing the role of Theater Nuclear Strike Forces in the Allied Command Europe (ACE). The agenda also includes provisions for any other business and the preparation of a communique to be addressed as part of the Defence Policy Committee (DPC) Communique.
