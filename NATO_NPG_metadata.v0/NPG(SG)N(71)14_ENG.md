> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 1, 1971, at 3 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes discussions on the Northern Norway Study, national participation in military nuclear planning, and a briefing by the IMS Nuclear Policy Team on WINTEX 71. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics

> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document lists 'national participation in military nuclear planning' as a matter for attention, which relates to the mechanisms for sharing nuclear responsibilities among allies.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 1st February,
1971, at 3 p.m.

Matters for attention will include :
(a) the Northern Norway Study (NPG/Study(71)1);
(b) national participation in military nuclear planning;
(c) a briefing by the IMS Nuclear Policy Team on WINTEX 71.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110, Brussels
