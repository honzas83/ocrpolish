> [!abstract] 
> # MFG Ministerial Meetings
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), serves as a detailed record of the Ministerial meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group (MFG) that occurred between Spring 1967 and Autumn 1979. It includes a chronological list of 26 meetings, specifying the location (city and country) and the time of year and year. The list covers meetings held in various NATO member states, including the United States, Turkey, the Netherlands, the Federal Republic of Germany, the United Kingdom, Italy, Canada, Belgium, Denmark, and Norway. It also notes that some meetings were held at NATO Headquarters without a specific host country. and it provides a summary of the number of times each participating country has acted as host. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
