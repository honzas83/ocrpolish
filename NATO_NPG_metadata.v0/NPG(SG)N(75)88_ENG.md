> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) notifying members of the next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 20, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the Future Work Programme and further arrangements for Phase III. The note is signed by M.H.C. Vilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references previous documents NPC(Staff Group)II(75)83 and NPC(Staff Group)WP(75)11(2nd Revise).

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 20th October,
1975; at 3 p.m. The principal matters for attention are:

(a) Future Work Programme(1),

(b) further discussion, if possible, of arrangements for Phase III(2).

(Signed) M.H.C. VILSON

1110 Brussels.
