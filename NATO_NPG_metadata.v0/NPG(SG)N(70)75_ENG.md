> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 5, 1970, at 2 p.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to discuss the Danish proposal for modifying the guidelines and a proposed Canadian amendment to paragraph 65 of the guidelines. The notice was signed by M.I.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references other documents, including NPG/D(70)6 and a draft dated September 29, 1970.
