> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 7, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to review suggestions from Capitals regarding Working Papers NPG/WP(74)7, NPG/WP(74)8, and NPG/WP(74)6, which are to be discussed by NPG Permanent Representatives on October 10, 1974. Additionally, the meeting will focus on continuing preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson in Brussels, Belgium.

2nd October, 1974
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(74)102
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 7th October,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to take note of any suggestions which may have been received from Capitals
concerning the Working Papers(1) to be discussed by NPG Permanent
Representatives on 10th October and to agree upon corrigenda if necessary;

(b) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG/WP(74)7, 30th September, 1974
NPG/WP(74)8, 25th September, 1974
NPG/WP(74)6, 25th September, 1974
