> # Note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated September 16, 1968. It serves as a follow-up to a previous note regarding the meeting scheduled for Monday, September 23, 1968, at 2:45 p.m. The Secretary informs the members of the Staff Group that, in addition to ADM Time Factors, the meeting's agenda will include a re-examination by NATO Military Authorities of the Supreme Allied Commander Europe's (SACEUR) scheduled programme and a follow-on discussion regarding the Southern Region Report, which was previously discussed in the Nuclear Planning Group on September 17.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Note by the Secretary
Further to my note(1) concerning the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group
(Staff Group) to be held on Monday, 23rd September, 1968, at 2.45 p.m., I have
been instructed to inform the members of the Staff Group that in addition to ADM
Time Factors the following matters will also be discussed:

- re-examination by NATO Military Authorities of SACEUR's scheduled programme;
- follow-on to the discussion which took place in the NPG on 17th September on
the Southern Region Report.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
