> # Notes of a Meeting Held on Friday, 9th January, 1970
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on Friday, 9th January 1970, to welcome Mr. Caspar Stephansen as the new representative for Norway. The group discussed a working paper prepared by the International Staff regarding further studies of follow-on tactical use. The Chairman noted that the goal was to reach agreement on a paper suitable for submission to the Permanent Representatives, with a proposed deadline for a revised version by Monday, 19th January. If the revised paper was completed on time, a meeting of the Permanent Representatives was envisioned for Thursday, 29th January, to ensure the study effort could begin as early as possible. The Staff Group agreed to continue these discussions following their trip to Germany from 12th to 14th January. Finally, the group scheduled its next meeting for Thursday, 15th January 1970, at 3:00 p.m. in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held on Friday, 9th January, 1970
WELCOME TO THE NEW NORWEGIAN MEMBER
1. The STAFF GROUP:
extended a warm welcome to Mr. Caspar Stephansen
who will be representing Norway in the NPG Staff Group.
FURTHER STUDIES OF FOLLOW-ON TACTICAL USE
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) noted a statement by the Chairman on the working paper prepared by the
International Staff(1), in which he made the 'following point :

(i) the aim of the discussion was to reach agreement on a paper suitable for
submission to the Permanent Representatives;

(ii) hopefully a revised version of the working paper could be produced by
Monday, 19th January, at the latest;

(iii) if the revised paper came out in time it should be possible to envisage a
meeting of the Permanent Representatives on Thursday, 29th January;

(iv) every effort should be made to achieve this timetable so that the study
effort could begin as early as possible;
