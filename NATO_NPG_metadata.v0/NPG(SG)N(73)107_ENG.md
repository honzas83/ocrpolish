> [!abstract] 
> # Briefing on Atomic Demolition Munitions (ADMs)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a briefing on Atomic Demolition Munitions (ADMs), which will include a film presentation. The event is scheduled for Monday, October 22, 1973, at 3 p.m. in conference room 15, replacing the usual Monday meeting of the Staff Group. The briefing will be followed by a question period. Attendance is open to national representatives, members of the International Staff, the International Military Staff, and Military Headquarters who have a need to know. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(73)107
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
BRIEFING ON ATOMIC DEMOLITION MUNITIONS (ADMs)
Note by the Secretary
In the place of the usual Monday meeting of the Staff Group a briefing on Atomic
Demolition Munitions (ADMs), including a film, will take place in conference
room 15, at 3 p.m. on Monday, 22nd October, 1973.

The briefing will be followed by a question period.
National representatives, members of the International Staff, the International
Military Staff and Military Head- quarters, who have a need to know, will be
welcome.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
