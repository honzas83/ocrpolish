> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 8, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss any matters arising from the meeting of Permanent Representatives held on October 4, 1973, and to conduct an initial exchange of views regarding the question of communicating NATO's intentions. The notice was signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references previous documents DN(77)3 and NPG(Staff Group)N(73)101.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th October, 1973
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss any matters which may have arisen as a result of the meeting of
Permanent Representatives held on 4th October, 1973;

(b) to have an initial exchange of views on the question of communicating NATO's
intentions(1).

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
