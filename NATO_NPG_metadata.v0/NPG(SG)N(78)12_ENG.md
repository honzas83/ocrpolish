> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Tuesday, February 21, 1978, at 3 p.m. The meeting's agenda includes a report by the Chairman on the second meeting of the High Level Group, a review of the Ministerial Agenda, and a preliminary discussion regarding the agenda for the Meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives on April 5. Additionally, the group will consider a Draft Report to the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) activities for the Nuclear Delegations Advisory Committee (NDAC). The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 21st February,
1978 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) report by the Chairman on the second meeting of the High Level Group;
(b) the Ministerial Agenda(1);
(c) a preliminary discussion of the Agenda for the Meeting of NPG Permanent
Representatives on 5th April(2);

(d) the Draft Report to the NDAC on NPG activities(3);
(e) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
110 Brussels.
