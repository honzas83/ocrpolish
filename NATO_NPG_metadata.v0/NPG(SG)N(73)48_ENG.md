> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Wednesday, June 13, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the review of future Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) work, with a specific focus on preparations for the Autumn 1973 meeting. Additionally, the meeting will address the distribution of basic NPG documentation to Portugal and the scheduling of the Autumn 1973 NPG Ministerial Meeting. The agenda also includes an initial exchange of views on the Nuclear Planning Committee (NMA) reports regarding operational concepts and doctrine. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(73)48
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 13th June,
1973, at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue the
review of future NPG work(1), with particular reference to the preparations for
the Autumn 1973 meeting. The distribution(2) of basic NPG documentation to
Portugal and the dates(3) of the Autumn 1973 NPG Ministerial Meeting will also
be discussed.

2. It is planned also to hold an initial exchange of views on the NMA reports(4)
on operational concepts and doctrine.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)45, 30th May, 1973
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)44, 29th May, 1973
(3) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)47, 1st June, 1973
(4) NPG/D(73)5, 29th May, 1973 and NPG/D(73)6, 30th May, 1973
