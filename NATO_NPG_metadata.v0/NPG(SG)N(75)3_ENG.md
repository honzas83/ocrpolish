> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, January 15, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to reach a decision regarding the report to the Nuclear Planning Group Advisory Committee (NDAC) on NPG activities, to discuss arrangements for subsequent meetings on January 20, 21, and 22 concerning the Technological Study and the Phase III report, to continue the exchange of views on the Nunn Amendment remit, and to review preparations for the Spring 1975 Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.C. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th January,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to reach a decision concerning the report(1) to the NDAC on NPG activities;

(b) to discuss arrangements for the consecutive meetings on 20, 21 and 22
January concerning the Technological Study and the Phase III report(2);

(c) to continue the exchange of views on the Nunn Amendment remit;

(d) to continue the review of preparations for the Spring 1975 Ministerial
Meeting(3).

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)116, 28th November, 1974
(2) NPG/VP(74)9, 4th December, 1974
(3) NPC(Staff Group)N(74)119, 13th December, 1974
