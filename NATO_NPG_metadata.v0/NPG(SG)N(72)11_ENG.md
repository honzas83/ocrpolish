> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting on Monday, February 7, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions on the initiation of Phase II of the Follow-on Use Programme, taking into account a revised paper prepared by the International Staff. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson. It references a revised working paper, NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)1(Revised), dated January 26, 1972.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 7th February,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal object will be to continue the discussion of the
initiation of Phase II of the Follow-on Use Programme, taking into account the
revised paper(1) prepared by the International Staff.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)1(Revised), 26th January, 1972
