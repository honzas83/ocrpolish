> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday, 8th March, 1972
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on March 8, 1972, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting began with farewells to Colonel K.L. Franks and Colonel H.F. Lombard of the Nuclear Policy Section of the Plans and Policy Division of the International Military Staff. The group discussed the scheduling of the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives meeting for May 4, 1972. Arrangements were made for the carriage of classified documents to and from the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Copenhagen. The Staff Group reviewed an initial draft report on the SHAPE Illustrative Study on Battlefield Use of nuclear weapons, requesting the International Staff to redraft specific paragraphs. Additionally, the group noted the upcoming circulation of papers regarding consultation facilities available to the Alliance and the Mediterranean Maritime Study. The meeting concluded by scheduling the next session for March 13, 1972.

**ILLUSTRATIVE STUDY ON BATTLEFIELD USE – INITIAL DRAFT REPORT**

4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced the initial draft(1)
of a possible Permanent Representatives to Ministers on the SHAPE Illustrative
Study on Battlefield Use of nuclear weapons(2); he invited comments;

(b) took note of the general comments made in the subsequent discussion and of
the detailed comments on the International Staff’s draft;

(c) invited the International Staff to redraft paragraphs 2 and 3 of the paper
in the light of the discussion;

(d) agreed to continue the discussion at the next meeting.

**DESCRIPTION OF FACILITIES AVAILABLE TO THE ALLIANCE FOR CONSULTATION**

5. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who said that a paper on consultation
facilities was being prepared by the International Staff with a view to its
circulation during the course of the week beginning 13th March.

**MEDITERRANEAN MARITIME STUDY**

6. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who said that he hoped shortly to
circulate an International staff paper on the Mediterranean Maritime Study.

**NEXT MEETING**

7. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 13th March, 1972 at 11 a.m. and 3
p.m.
