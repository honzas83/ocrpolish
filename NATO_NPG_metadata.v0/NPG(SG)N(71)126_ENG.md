> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, November 15, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to continue the review of follow-on actions resulting from the Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Ministers held at the Palais d'Egmont on October 26 and 27, 1971. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th November,
1971, at 3 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue the
review of follow-on action(1) resulting from the Meeting of NGP Ministers held
at the Palais d'Egmont on 26th and 27th October, 1971.

NATO 1110 Brussels
