> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, February 5, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the examination of the Allied Command Atlantic (ACLANT) Follow-on Study and to discuss a further revision of the draft report on it. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references a previous working paper, NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)1(2nd Revise), dated January 31, 1973.

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(73)10

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th February,
1973, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to continue the
examination of the ACLANT Follow-on Study and to discuss a further revision of
the draft(1) of a possible report on it.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)1(2nd Revise), 31st January, 1973
