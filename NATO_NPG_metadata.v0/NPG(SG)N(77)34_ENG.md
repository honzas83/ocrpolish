> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday, 20th April, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on April 20, 1977, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. The meeting covered several key administrative and strategic items. First, the Staff Group took note of security clearances for all present members. Second, they discussed arrangements for a visit to military installations in Germany. The group also addressed the logistics of the upcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Canada, including the urgency of arrival and departure details for Ottawa. Additionally, the group reviewed a draft advance press release for the Ministerial meeting and noted general agreement with the text. A significant portion of the meeting was focused on the draft agenda for the Ministerial meeting in Canada, specifically regarding the NATO Theatre Nuclear Force Posture, New Technology Study, and the Future Work Programme. The group agreed to seek further instructions from their respective capitals and to re-open the discussion at the next meeting scheduled for April 25, 1977.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday,
20th April, 1977

SECURITY
1. The STAFF GROUP: took note that all persons present were cleared to the
security classification of the matters to be discussed.

STAFF GROUP VISIT TO MILITARY INSTALLATIONS IN GERMANY
2. The STAFF GROUP: took note of the remarks of the Chairman and of the German
Representative concerning arrangements for the Staff Group visit to military
installations in Germany.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING IN CANADA
3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the remarks of the Canadian Representative that details of
arrivals and departures were urgently required in Ottawa;

ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING IN CANADA
4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Canadian Representative who said that his
Authorities agreed with the text of the draft advance press release(1) which
would be published in Ottawa simultaneously with releases in other capitals;

(b) noted general agreement with the text and invited members who had not yet
received instructions from their Authorities to do so, if possible in time for
the next meeting.

DRAFT AGENDA FOR NPG MINISTERIAL IN CANADA
5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the form and content of the draft Ministerial_Agenda(1) and of
the Chairman's proposal that it shall be processed in time to be placed on the
Agenda for the meeting of Permanent Representatives to be held on 5th May, 1977;

(b) took note of the views expressed in the ensuing discussion during which
particular attention was paid to the form and documentary support of the
following items:

(i) Item II. NATO THEATRE NUCLEAR FORCE POSTURE, and the related documents on
NICK-HERCULES, Dual-Capable Aircraft and TINS.

(ii) Item III. NEW TECHNOLOGY STUDY, with particular reference to the PIT report
and the Forwarding Letter.

(iii) Item V. FUTURE WORK PROGRAMME.
(c) agreed to obtain further instructions from capitals and to re-open the
discussion at the next meeting;

(d) invited the International Staff to prepare, in time for the next meeting, in
the light of the suggestions made in the discussion, and of relevant
observations which might be made at the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives
on 21st April, draft papers to support Item II (NATO TNF Force Posture) taking
into account the documents listed under this item.

NEXT MEETING
6. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold the next meeting on Monday, 25th April, 1977 at 3 p.m.
