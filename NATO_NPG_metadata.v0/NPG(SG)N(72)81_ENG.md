> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary, D.A.R. Cooper, regarding the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled for Thursday, 28th September 1972, at 3 p.m. in Conference Room E 5734/5 of the United States Delegation. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to further examine comments received from Capitals on various Working Papers, specifically NPG/WP(72)8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 14, and 15. Additionally, the group will consider proposed modifications to NPG/WP(72)10 based on comments from the Military Committee. Military planning and coordination between member states' capitals and the Military Committee are the central focus of {// a placeholder for NATO, as the context implies NATO's NPG_} NATO's Nuclear Planning Group.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Acting Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place in Conference Room E 5734/5
(third floor) of the United States Delegation on Thursday 28th September, 1972,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be a further examination of
comments received from Capitals on the Working Papers(1) and a consideration of
proposed modifications(2) to NPG/WP(72)10 in the light of the Military Committee
comments.

(Signed) D.A.R. COOPER

(1) NPG/WP(72)8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 14 and 15

(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)18(Revised), 26th September, 1972
