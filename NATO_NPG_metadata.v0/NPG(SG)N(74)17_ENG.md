> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Informal Meeting to Bid Farewell to Colonel Robert M. Krone
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on February 8, 1974. The Director and members of the International Staff Nuclear Planning Directorate request the pleasure of the company of the members of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group for an informal meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place in Conference Room I-334 in Brussels, Belgium, on Monday, February 11, 1974, at 5:15 p.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to bid farewell to Colonel Robert M. Krone. The notice is signed by H.B. Seim.
