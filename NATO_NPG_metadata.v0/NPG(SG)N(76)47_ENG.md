> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 24th May, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on May 24, 1976, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to coordinate preparations for the June Ministerial Meeting. The meeting covered security certificates, noting that briefings for Ministerial Agenda Item III would be classified as COSMIC TOP SECRET ATOMAL, and that access to SIOP extremely sensitive information would not be required. The group discussed the Ministerial Agenda and the upcoming circulation of a revised version and a preliminary version of the Future Work Programme. They also agreed on the text and timing of an advance Press Release. Regarding nuclear consultation, the group addressed a proposal from the Turkish Authorities to amend the draft report on Nuclear Consultation – CNI Procedures, instructing the International Staff to prepare a revised text. Additionally, the group continued its examination of Working Paper NPG(Staff Group)VP(76)2 concerning newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation, requesting the SHAPE representative to provide a suitable text on command levels. The meeting concluded by scheduling the next meeting for May 31, 1976.

SECURITY

1. The STAFF GROUP:
took note that all persons present were cleared to the security classification
of the matters to be discussed.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING IN JUNE

Security Certificates

2. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who said that he had been informed by
the United States Delegation that the briefings to be provided under Ministerial
Agenda Item III would be within the COSMIC TOP SECRET ATOMAL classification; it
would not be necessary for participants in the meeting to possess authorisation
for access to SIOP extremely sensitive information; a letter would be circulated
amending paragraph 7 of EGL(76)59 and paragraph 4 of NPG(Staff Group)N(76)39.

Ministerial Agenda

3. The STAFF GROUP:
noted that the Agenda for the NPG Ministerial Meeting
