> # Outline Programme for the Meeting of NPG Ministers to be held in Venice
> 
> This document provides the detailed itinerary for the 7th NATO Nuclear Planning Group meeting in Venice, Italy, scheduled for June 8th and 9th, 1970. The programme includes arrival arrangements for delegations on Sunday, June 7th, and conference sessions at the Fondazione CINI, including lunches and dinners hosted by the Italian Minister of Defence. It also outlines a social programme for accompanying ladies, including guided visits to Piazza San Marco and a motor-boat tour of Venice. The meeting is circulated by the Secretary, N.H.G. Wilson, from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium, at the request of the Italian Representative. The itinerary covers logistical details such as transport from Tessera Airport and Venice S. Lucia railway station to the hotels, and a boat trip to Burano and Torcello on the final day.

7TH NATO NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP MEETING
(Venice, 8th and 9th June, 1970)
OUTLINE PROGRAMME

Sunday, 7th June
Morning and early afternoon
(1) Delegations arrive, by own arrangements and informally either at TESSERA
civil Airport or at VENICE S. LUCIA 'railway station, at Piazzale Roma, where
they will be received by Italian Defence Staff representatives.

Transfer to Venice by motorboat provided by the Italian Authorities, from
TESSERA to VENICE.

Delegations settle in hotels.
Evening
Cocktail for all participants, hosted by the Chief of the Italian Defence Staff,
at the Circolo Ufficiali di Marina. (Informal)

Monday, 8th June
Morning and afternoon
0900 – 12.30 Conference at the Fondazione CINI
15.00 Lunchon at the Fondazione CINI, SalaFranzo della Forcsteria, hosted by the
Minister of Defence, for Ministers, Secretary General, permanent
Representatives, Chiefs of Staff, Chairman MC and MNCS.

12.30 – 14.45 Buffet luncheon for all other participants at the Fondazione CINI,
Salone del Cenacolo Palladiano.

14.45 – 1700 Conference continues
Evening
21.00 Dinner in VENICE, hosted by the Minister of Defence, for all delegates and
wives, at the Sala delle Colonne, Ca' Giustiniani – by invitation (Dress
informal) Delegates involved in the drafting of the agreed minute will be served
a buffet dinner at the bar of the Fondazione CINI.

(1) Military planes, after landing and disembarkation of passengers, may stay at
the Airport.
