> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, April 19, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to discuss the follow-up to the Colorado Springs Ministerial Meeting and the planning for WINTEX-CIMEX 83. Additionally, the same meeting will address any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th April, 1982
at 2.45 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) Follow-up to the Colorado Springs Ministerial Meeting(1);
(b) Planning for WINTEX-CIMEX 83(2);
(c) Any Other Business.
