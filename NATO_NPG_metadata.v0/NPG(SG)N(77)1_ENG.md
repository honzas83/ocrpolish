> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, January 10, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to prepare a second draft of the Draft Report by Permanent Representatives on Future Work Priorities, to discuss the draft terms of reference for the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study, and to address the question of a TIES status report. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Tilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
