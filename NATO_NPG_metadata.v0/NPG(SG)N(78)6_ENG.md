> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled for Monday, January 30, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the agenda are to discuss preparations for the Spring 1976 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial and to address any other business. The notice was issued by D.J. Roberts of NATO in Brussels, Belgium.
