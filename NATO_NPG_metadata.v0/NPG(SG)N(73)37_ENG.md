> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, May 7, 1973, at 3:30 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss remaining matters concerning a previous meeting in Ankara. Additionally, the notice invites members and their wives to a screening of a short film titled 'Prospect of Turkey' at 2:30 p.m. in the NATO Theatre, arranged by the Information Directorate.

# Page 1

3rd May, 1973
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(73)37
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 7th May, 1973 at
3.30 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to discuss remaining
matters concerning the meeting in Ankara;

2. A short film called "Prospect of Turkey" will be shown in the NATO Theatre at
2.30 p.m. under the kind arrangement of the Information Directorate. Members and
their wives are cordially invited to this film.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
