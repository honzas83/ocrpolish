> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, July 2, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on Consultation Procedures and Facilities, hold an initial discussion on Operational Concepts and Doctrine, and discuss future Nuclear Planning Group activities as appropriate. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references two working papers: NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)7 and NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)8.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 2nd July, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion on Consultation Procedures and Facilities(1)
(b) to hold an initial discussion on Operational Concepts and Doctrine(2)
(c) to discuss future NPG activities as appropriate.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
