> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, February 3, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions regarding Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces, as well as to address ADM Time Factors, Consultation, and the agenda for the upcoming Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting in London in May. The meeting is classified as ATOMAL, requiring special passes for attendees who participated in the meeting on January 27. Any new attendees must inform the Secretary and provide proof of ATOMAL clearance. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
As agreed on 27th January, the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff
Group will be held on Monday, 3rd February, 1969, at 2.45 p.m.

The object of the meeting will be to continue the discussion of the questions
(1) relating to Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces; some time will also be devoted to
ADM Time Factors (2), Consultation, and the Agenda for the NPG Ministerial
meeting to be held in London in May.

The meeting next Monday will be classified ATOMAL. The Secretary will make
arrangements for special passes to be available that day in Office OA 119 for
all those who took part in the meeting on 27th January.

If any different persons wish to attend, they are requested to inform the
Secretary, at the same time supplying proof of ATOMAL clearance.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
