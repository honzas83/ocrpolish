> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, February 16, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to receive an introductory briefing on Command, Control and Communications (C3) and to continue discussions regarding Nuclear Consultation - CNI Procedures. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 16th February,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to receive an introductory briefing on the subject of Command, Control and
Communications (C³);

(b) to continue discussion of Nuclear Consultation – CNI Procedures(1).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
