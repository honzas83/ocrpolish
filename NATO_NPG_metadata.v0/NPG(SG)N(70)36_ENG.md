> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, May 4, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss the draft agenda for the meeting of NPG Ministers in June and to address ancillary problems connected with follow-on studies. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references other documents, such as DN(74)18 and NPG(Staff Group)N(70)37.
