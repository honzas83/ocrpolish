> # Consultation Procedures and Facilities: Classification of Nuclear Consultation Messages
> 
> This document, issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), proposes specific amendments to Working Paper NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)7 dated 2nd September 1976. The amendments were initiated to meet requests made by the United States member of the NPG Staff Group and the SACLANT representative during a meeting on 13th September 1976. The proposed changes include textual insertions in paragraph 8(a), the complete replacement of paragraph 13 to address the applicability of arrangements during periods of hostilities, and deletions in paragraphs 14(c) and 14(d). The document references the previous edition of the document C-M(68)41(2nd Revise) and emphasizes that arrangements should remain applicable in hostilities provided they do not adversely affect operational capability. The proposal is submitted for the consideration of the Staff Group.
