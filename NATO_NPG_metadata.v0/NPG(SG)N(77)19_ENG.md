> # Nike-Hercules Study
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 2nd March 1977. It serves as a follow-up to a previous note, NPG(Staff Group)N(77)18, regarding the preliminary distribution of the SHAPE NIKE-HERCULES Study (SHAPE/ATOMAL/231/76, 18th February 1977). The Secretary informs the registries that they will receive an equal number of copies of a SHAPE cover letter, referenced SHAPE 6440.02.09/5220/5025/77 and dated 28th February 1977, which should be attached to the ATOMAL Study. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Military-Operations-and-Capabilities/Air-and-Missile-Defense — The document explicitly mentions the 'NIKE-HERCULES STUDY', referring to the Nike Hercules, which is a surface-to-air missile system used for air and missile defense.
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The Nike Hercules system was designed to be capable of nuclear-tipped missiles, and the document's origin from the 'NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)' and the 'ATOMAL Study' refers to the context of nuclear weapons deployment and sharing within NATO.
