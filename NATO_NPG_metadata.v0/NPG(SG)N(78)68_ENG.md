> [!abstract] 
> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in Brussels
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of NATO, lists the members of the International Staff delegation accompanying Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luns to the 24th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers in Brussels. The delegation includes high-ranking officials such as Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning & Policy W.F. Mumford, Director of Nuclear Planning R.E. Shearer, and Deputy Executive Secretary E.G. Luff. It also includes support staff, including interpreters, a Cosmic Control and Courier, and secretaries. The note is signed by D.J. Roberts, Secretary of the NPG Staff Group, and is dated October 3, 1978.
