> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 29th January, 1973
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on January 29, 1973, at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting focused on two primary areas: documentation and the ACLANT Area Follow-on Study. Regarding documentation, the Staff Group took note of a SHAPE review of the ACLANT Area Study and comments from SACLANT on the same study. The group discussed a revised version of a possible report by Permanent Representatives, noting that while it had incorporated previous views, it did not yet include the same from the Major NATO Commanders, such as SACEUR and SACLANT. After detailed discussion of the revised working paper, the group agreed to several changes to the text. The International Staff was then invited to prepare a further revised version of the draft report based on the discussion and the SHAPE review of the ACLANT Study. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next meeting on February 5, 1973.

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 29th

DOCUMENTATION
1. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who drew attention to the following
documents which had been, or were about to be, distributed:

(a) NPG/Study/38, 29th January, 1973: SHAPE review of the ACLANT Area Study,
(b) NPG/Study/39, to be circulated shortly: SACLANT comments on the ACLANT Area
Study.

THE ACLANT AREA FOLLOW-ON STUDY
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who referred to the discussions on
the ACLANT Study(1) which had taken place at the last two meetings of the Staff
Group, and who introduced a revised and more complete version(2) of a possible
report by Permanent Representatives; he reminded the Staff Group that although
this new version included the views expressed at the previous meetings, it did
not yet take into account the views of the Major NATO Commanders (those of
SACEUR had just been received, and those of SACLANT were expected shortly; the
Chairman of the Military Committee might also be replying);

(1) NPG/Study/19, 1st December, 1973
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)1(Revised), 25th January, 1973
