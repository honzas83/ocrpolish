> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, August 12, 1971, at 3:00 p.m. The meeting's primary focus will be further discussion of unresolved problems related to tactical nuclear defence and Special ADM Guidelines. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents, including NPG(Staff Group)N(71)80, NPG/D(71)9, and NPG(Staff Group)N(71)83.

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(71)85

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Thursday, 12th August, 1971,
at 3.00 p.m. The principal matters for attention will be:

(a) further discussion of Unresolved Problems Related to Tactical Nuclear
Defence(1);

(b) Special ADM Guidelines(2),(3);

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)80, dated 5th August, 1971
(2) NPG/D(71)9, dated 2nd August, 1971 (MCM-51-71, 29th July, 1971)
(3) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)83, dated 6th August, 1971
