> [!abstract] 
> # Note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) notifying members of an upcoming meeting on Tuesday, October 15, 1968, at 2:45 p.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to discuss matters relating to the outcome of the Ministerial Meeting that took place in Bonn on October 10 and 11, 1968. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) will take place on
Tuesday, 15th October, 1968, at 2.45 p.m. It is proposed to discuss matters
relating to the outcome of the Ministerial Meeting held in Bonn on 10th and 11th
October, 1968.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

OTAN/NATO Brussels, 29
