> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated January 10, 1973. It informs the members of the Staff Group that the Chairman has directed the cancellation of the meeting originally scheduled for Monday, January 15, 1973. The next meeting is instead proposed to be held on Monday, January 22, 1973, at 3 p.m. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
