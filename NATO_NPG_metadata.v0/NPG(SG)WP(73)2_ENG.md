> # Draft Ministerial Agenda
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting scheduled for May 15th and 16th, 1973, in Ankara. The agenda includes a briefing on the balance of strategic forces led by the United States, and a discussion on the follow-on use work programme, specifically focusing on the ACLANT area. The follow-on use work programme discussion is led by the Netherlands and the United Kingdom, and includes reports and studies on tactical nuclear weapons use in the ACLANT area. The agenda also covers consultation procedures and facilities regarding VINTEX 73, as well as an illustrative study on demonstrative use. The Additionally, the agenda includes operational concepts and doctrine, any other business, the future work programme, an agreed minute, and a communique. The document was prepared by the Chairman of the NPG Staff Group, H.B. Seim, and was intended to provide a basis for Staff Group discussion on March 5th.
>
