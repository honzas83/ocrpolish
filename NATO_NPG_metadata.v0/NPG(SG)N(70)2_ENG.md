> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled for Thursday, 15th January 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions on further studies regarding follow-on tactical use. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document explicitly mentions the object of the meeting is to discuss 'further studies on follow-on tactical use', which refers to the tactical employment of nuclear weapons within the NATO alliance's shared strategy.
