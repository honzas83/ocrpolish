> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, issued on June 24, 1977, announcing the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, July 4, 1977, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions regarding the results of the Ottawa meeting, to begin considering the activities of Permanent Representatives in preparation for the Autumn Ministerial meeting, and to discuss any other business. The notice references previous documents NPG/D(77)8 and NPG(Staff Group)N(77)50.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 4th July, 1977 at
3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of the results of the Ottawa meeting(1);
(b) to begin consideration of Permanent Representatives' activities in
preparation for the Autumn Ministerial meeting;

(c) to discuss any other business.
(Signed) N.L.C. PARK
1110 Brussels.
