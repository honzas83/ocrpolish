> # Notice: NPC(Staff Group) N/5
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the North Atlantic Council (NATO) Staff Group, detailing the schedule and agenda for two upcoming meetings. The first meeting is scheduled for Thursday, September 25, 1975, at 2 p.m., to discuss the Ministerial Agenda, communicating NATO's intentions regarding warning messages and procedures, and nuclear consultation procedures. The second meeting is scheduled for September 26, 1975, at 3 p.m., to focus on consultation procedures for country requests and aids to decision making through illustrative studies. Additionally, the Secretary emphasizes that the document's discussions should help prepare the agenda for the meeting of Permanent Representatives to be held on October 9, 1975. The notice is signed by H.G. Wilson in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

25th September 1975
NOTICE: IFC(Staff Group) N/5

A CLEAR PLANNING CLOU (STAFF GROUP)
MEAT MEETINGS
Note by the Secretary
The next meetings of the Staff Group will take place
as follows:

Thursday, 25th September, 1975 at 2 p.m.
The subjects to be discussed are
(a) The Ministerial Agenda(1)
(b) Communicating NATO's Intentions (Warning Messages)(2)
(c) Communicating NATO's Intentions (Procedures)(3)
(d) Nuclear Consultation Procedures - Compilation(4)
Monday, 26th September, 1975 at 3 p.m.
The subjects to be discussed are
(a) Consultation Procedures - Country Requests(5)
(b) Aids to Decision Making - Illustrative Studies(6)
2. The need to prepare an agenda for the meeting of
Permanent Representatives to be held on 9th October should be
borne in mind during the discussions on 25th and 26th September.

(Signed) H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPC(Staff Group) N(75)5; 5th August, 1975
(2) NPC(Staff Group) N(75)7; 4th September, 1975
(3) NPC(Staff Group) N(75)8; 9th September, 1975
(4) NPC(Staff Group) N(75)9; 2nd September, 1975
(5) NPC(Staff Group) N(75)10; 5th August, 1975
(6) NPC(Staff Group) N(75)11; 1st September, 1975
