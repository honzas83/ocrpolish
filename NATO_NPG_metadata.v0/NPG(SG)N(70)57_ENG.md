> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters on Monday, 6th July, 1970
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters on July 6, 1970, to address two primary agenda items. First, the group discussed the draft concept for the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, noting that comments had been received from the Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR), the Supreme Allied Commander Atlantic (SACIANT), the Netherlands, Turkey, and the United Kingdom. The Staff Group urged representatives of countries that had not yet submitted their comments to encourage their capitals to do so without delay. Second, the group reviewed a working paper on specific administrative guidelines and invited the International Staff to prepare a revised version for the next meeting. It was agreed that once a version of the paper was finalized at the Staff Group level, it would be sent to capitals for study before further discussion in early September. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next session on July 15, 1970, at 3 p.m. in Brussels, Belgium.
