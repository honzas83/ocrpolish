> [!abstract] 
> # Additional Proposed Changes to Draft Report on Demonstrative Use Illustrative Study
> 
> This document, authored by H.B. Seim, the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), outlines additional proposed changes to the Staff Group Working Paper and a related Notice regarding a demonstrative use illustrative study. The Chairman intends for the Staff Group to continue discussing the basic draft and proposed changes at their meeting on March 26, 1973, with the goal of producing a version for the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives' meeting on April 16. The proposed amendments include a new section titled 'Implications of Failure to Grant Release' which discusses the military and political consequences of not using nuclear weapons to prevent an enemy amphibious landing in north Norway, noting that such a failure could be perceived as a lack of determination or cohesiveness within the NATO alliance. Additionally, the document provides specific textual changes to NPG(Staff Group)N(73)20, including adjustments to wording regarding the timing and communication of demonstrative use and the requirement to explain NATO's actions to the rest of the world. The document references previous working papers and notices, such as NPF(Staff Group)WF(73)4 and NPG(Staff Group)N(73)23, and was issued from Brussels, Belgium.
