> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, October 18, 1971, at 10:15 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the handling of the Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE) preliminary review of the CENTAG Area Follow-on Use Study, taking into account comments from the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives held on October 13, 1971. The notice references a paper issued by the International Staff, as well as previous studies and reviews. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 18th October,
1971, at 10.15 a.m. The 'principal object of the meeting will be to discuss the
handling of the SHAPE preliminary review(1) of the CENTAG Area Follow-on Use
Study(2), in the light of comments made at the meeting of NPG Permanent
Representatives held on Thursday, 13th October, 1971. A paper on this matter has
been issued by the International Staff(3).

(,signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO 1110 Brussels
(1) NPG/Study(71)13, 12th October, 1971
(2) NPG/Study(71)6, 1st August, 1971
(3) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)116, 15th October, 1971.
