> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, March 5, 1979, at 10 a.m. in Brussels. The agenda for the meeting includes a preliminary discussion of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on the Politico-Strategic Aspects of Maritime Warfare in the NATO Area, preparations for the Spring 1979 NPG Ministerial Meeting, and a discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines. The notice also provides a list of reference documents related to these agenda items, including various working papers and studies. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th March, 1979
at 10 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preliminary discussion of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on
the Politico-Strategic Aspects of Maritime Warfare in the NATO Area(1);

(b) preparations for the Spring 1979 NPG Ministerial Meeting(2);
(c) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(3);
(d) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
