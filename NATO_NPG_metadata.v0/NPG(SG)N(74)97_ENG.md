> # United States Military Procurement Authorisation Act
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the 1975 United States Military Procurement Authorisation Act, which became law on August 5, 1974. The notice highlights Section 302(d) of the law, which mandates a study on the overall concept for the use of tactical nuclear weapons in Europe. Because this matter falls within the commitment of the Nuclear Planning Group, the Chairman suggests that members of the NP Staff Group discuss the issue during their meeting on September 23, 1974. The document includes an annex containing a letter from the United States Permanent Representative to the North Atlantic Council, as well as an attachment, for reference.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

UNITED STATES MILITARY PROCUREMENT AUTHORISATION ACT

Note by the Chairman

The United States Permanent Representative to the North Atlantic Council has
recently circulated a letter concerning certain provisions of direct interest to
NATO contained in the 1975 United States Military Procurement Authorisation Act
which became law on August 5, 1974(1).

2. One of these provisions (contained in Section 302(d) of the law) calls for a
study on the overall concept for use of tactical nuclear weapons in Europe. It
falls, therefore, within the range of matters to which the NP is particularly
committed. Members of the NP Staff Group may wish to discuss this issue at their
meeting on Monday, 23rd September, 1974.

3. For ease of reference, the above mentioned letter and the attachment to it
are reproduced at Annex.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER.

1110 Brussels.

This document consists of one cover note and an Annex of 4 pages.

(1) Letter of 27th August, 1974.
