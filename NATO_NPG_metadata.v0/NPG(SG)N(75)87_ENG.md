> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Friday, October 17, 1975, at 10 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss arrangements for Phase III, as referenced in NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)11(Revised). The notice also includes a provision for any other business. The document was issued by the North Atlantic Council in Brussels, Belgium.
