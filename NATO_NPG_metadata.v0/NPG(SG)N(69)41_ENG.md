> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, May 5, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss a paper regarding NATO's response to the Warsaw Pact's initial tactical use of nuclear weapons, which was revised by the Permanent Representatives at their meeting on April 30, 1969. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

29th April, 1969
NOTICE
NFG(Staff Group)N(69)41
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group is scheduled to take place on Monday, 5th
May, 1969, at 2.45 p.m. The principal subject to be discussed will be the paper
on NATO's Response to the Warsaw Pact's Initial Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons,
as revised by the Permanent Representatives at their meeting on 30th April,
1969.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
OTAN/NATO,
Brussels, 39.
