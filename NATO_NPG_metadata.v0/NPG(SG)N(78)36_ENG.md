> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, May 29, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the revised draft Permanent Representatives report on follow-up action to the Frederikshavn Ministerial Meeting. Additionally, the notice informs Staff Group members of a luncheon in honor of the departing dean, Mr. Woodworth, which will take place on Friday, June 30, 1978, at 1 p.m. in the Secretary General's Dining Room, located next to the Salon des Ambassadeurs. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 29th May, 1978 at
3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the revised draft Permanent Representatives report on follow-
up action to the Frederikshavn Ministerial Meeting(1);

(b) any other business.
2. Staff Group members are informed that a luncheon in honour of the departing
dean, Mr. Woodworth, will be held on Friday, 30th June, 1978 at 1 p.m. in the
"Secretary General's Dining Room" which is next to the Salon des Ambassadeurs.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
1110 Brussels.
