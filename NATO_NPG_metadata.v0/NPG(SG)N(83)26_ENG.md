> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 10, 1983, at 2:45 p.m. The agenda for the meeting will focus on preparations for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting, the draft Ministerial communiqué, and questions and answers relating to the High Level Group's work. Additionally, the meeting will include a time for any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(83)26
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 10th October,
1983 at 2.45 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be: (a)
preparations for the NPG Ministerial Meeting(1); (b) draft Ministerial
communiqué(2); (c) Questions and Answers relating to the HLG's Work(3); (d) Any
Other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
