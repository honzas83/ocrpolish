> [!abstract] 
> # NPG Ministerial Meetings
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, provides a detailed record of the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meetings. It lists all 24 meetings held from Spring 1967 in Washington, United States, to Autumn 1978 in Brussels, Belgium, detailing the location and host country for each session. Additionally, the document includes a summary of the number of times each participating country has acted as host, with the Federal Republic of Germany, United Kingdom, and United States each hosting three times. It also outlines the participation of permanent members (Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Italy, United Kingdom, and United States) and rotating members (Canada, Netherlands, Norway, Belgium, Denmark, Greece, and Turkey) for the period 1979–1982, based on an agreement from 1969. Rotating members are specified to spend 18 months 'in' and 18 months 'out' of the group's activities.
