> # Notes of Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 16th February, 1976
> 
> The NATO Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group held a meeting on February 16, 1976, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. The meeting's primary focus was divided into two main agendas: Command, Control and Communications (C3) and Nuclear Consultation procedures. Regarding C3, the group took note of a briefing by Mr. V.J. Richards from the Infrastructure Directorate, Defence Support Division, and expressed a hope that Mr. B.L. Judd from the IMA Command, Control and Information Systems Division would provide a further briefing at the next meeting. The group also discussed the International Staff paper on Nuclear Consultation procedures, examining the text and its annex in detail and agreeing to several changes and additions. The International Staff was requested to prepare a revised version of the paper for further discussion. Finally, the group agreed to schedule their next meeting for Monday, February 23, 1976, at 5 p.m.
