> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary regarding the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled for Tuesday, October 19, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the Future Work Programme and to continue preparations for the Autumn Ministerial Meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It also references document PO/76/116 dated October 13, 1976, in relation to the Future Work Programme.

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)91
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 19th October,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the Future Work Programme(1);
(b) to continue preparations for the Autumn Ministerial Meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) PO/76/116, 13th October, 1976
