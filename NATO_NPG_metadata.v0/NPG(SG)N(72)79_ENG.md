> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, September 25, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to examine comments received from Capitals on various Working Papers and to continue discussions regarding the Ministerial Agenda. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson. It references several Working Papers, specifically NFG/WP(72)8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 14, and 15, as well as NFG/WP(72)13.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th September,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be to examine any
comments which may have been received from Capitals on the Working Papers(1),
and to continue the discussion on the Ministerial Agenda(2).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
(1) NFG/WP(72)8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 14 and 15
(2) NFG/WP(72)13, 14th September, 1972
