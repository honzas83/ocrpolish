> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 9, 1978, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to conduct a preliminary discussion of the draft Phase III report and to make final preparations for the Brussels Ministerial meeting. The notice also includes a provision for any other business. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references a document from the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) with the code USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126 dated September 13, 1978.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 9th October, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preliminary discussion of the draft Phase III report(1);
(b) final preparations for the Brussels Ministerial meeting;
(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
