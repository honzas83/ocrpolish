> # Notes of a Meeting Held on 21st September, 1970
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on September 21, 1970, in Brussels, Belgium. The meeting's agenda covered three main points: travel arrangements for an upcoming ministerial meeting in Ottawa, Canada, and the review of reports on theatre nuclear strike forces and provisional political guidelines. Regarding the travel, the Canadian Representative noted that Canada could not provide an aircraft for the NPG members' transport to Ottawa. Consequently, the International Staff suggested making a group booking for civil scheduled flights with a 10% rebate. For the theatre nuclear strike forces, the Staff Group expressed appreciation to the German and United States authorities for their report (document NPG/D(70)6) and invited further written comments. The group also agreed to defer the consideration of provisional political guidelines, specifically documents NPG/D(70)7 and 8, until their next meeting on September 23, 1970.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (ST.FF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held on 21st September, 1970
I. MINISTERIAL MEETING IN OTTAWA
1. The ST.FF GROUP:
(a) noted a statement by the Canadian Representative that regretfully his
Authorities were unable to provide an aircraft to convey members of the NPG to
the ministerial Meeting to be held in October, 1970, in Ottawa;

(b) noted that a Group booking could be made by the International Staff for a
return flight on a civil scheduled journey to Canada, a 10% rebate being given
on a normal commercial flight fare; the Group would require a leader for the
organization of the trip.

II. DR.EFT CONCEPT FOR THE USE OF THEATRE NUCLEAR STRIKE FORCES IN AGE
2. The STAFF GROUP:
at the conclusion of its discussion on document NPG/D(70)6, expressed its
appreciation to the German and United States authorities for their excellent
report and invited members who wished to comment upon the report to forward
their comments in writing to the German and United States Representatives.

III. PROVISIONAL POLITICAL GUIDELINES
2. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to defer consideration of documents NPG/D(70)7 and 8 to its next meeting
(to be held on Wednesday, 23rd September, 1970).

1110 Brussels.
