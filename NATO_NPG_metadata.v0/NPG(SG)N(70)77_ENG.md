> # Provisional Political Guidelines for the Initial Defensive Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons by NATO
> 
> This document, a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), outlines proposed revisions to the Provisional Political Guidelines for the initial defensive tactical use of nuclear weapons by NATO. Following remarks from the Secretary General and agreement among Permanent Representatives, it is suggested that paragraph 55 of the Provisional Political Guidelines be updated to reflect the issue of Special Political Guidelines for the Possible Use of Atomic Demolition Munitions (ADMs). The document proposes specific text substitutions for paragraph 55 and paragraph 123 of document DPC/D(69)58 to ensure that NATO Military Authorities follow the specific policy guidance promulgated by the Defence Planning Committee. The proposed changes are intended for Ministerial approval at Ottawa and subsequent submission to the Nuclear Planning Group and Defence Planning Committee. The note is signed by H. B. Seim.

FRC'ISIONAL POLITICAL GUIDELINES FOR THE INITIAL DEFENSIVE
TACTICAL USE OF NUCLEAR WEAPONS BY NATO
Note by the Chairman
In his introductory remarks at the meeting on the 6th October, the Secretary
General pointed out, and Permanent Representatives agreed, that if the special
ADM Guidelines are approved and promulgated in December, it would be appropriate
for the DFC to change paragraph 55 of the Provisional Political Guidelines to
reflect the issue of ADM Guidelines. He also suggested that the Staff Group
prepare a proposed revision of this paragraph for Ministerial approval at Ottawa
and further submission to the NDAC and DFC.

2. To provide a basis for initial Staff Group discussion at our next meeting on
Monday, 12th October, the following modifications of DPC/D(69)58 are suggested:

(a) Paragraph 55, page 17, substitute the following text:
In planning for the use of ADMs, the NATC Military Authorities should follow the
Special Political Guidelines for the Possible Use of Atomic Demolition
Munitions(1).

(b) Paragraph 123, page 34, change last sentence to read:
Planning should follow the guidelines in this paper and other specific policy
guidance promulgated by the Defence Planning Committee.

(1) DFC/D(70).... December, 1970 /This would be the reference number of the
policy document approved and promulgated by the DFC.

This document consists of 1 page. (Signed) H. B. SEIM
