> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Wednesday, 14th May, 1975, at 2:30 p.m., with a continuation on Thursday, 15th May, if necessary. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the latest revised version of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on the Phase II report. The notice was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium, and references a previous document, NPG(Staff Group)P(75)5(2nd revise), dated 9th May 1975.

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 14th May, 1975
at 2.30 p.m. and will be continued on Thursday, 15th May at 2.30 p.m. if
necessary.

2. The principal object of this meeting will be to discuss the latest revised
version of the draft report(1) by Permanent Representatives on the Phase II
report.
