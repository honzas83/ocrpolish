> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, December 6, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the follow-up to the Ministerial Meeting and to address any other business. The notice was issued in Brussels by D.J. Roberts. It references a previous document, NPG/D(82)6, dated December 1, 1982.
