> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Friday, March 21, 1975, at 10 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft agenda for the Spring meeting of NPG Ministers, the draft Advance Press release for the Spring Meeting, the draft report on WINTEX 75, and the Future Work Programme. The notice is signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originated from Brussels. It references several other documents, including NPG(Staff Group)VP(75)2, NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)3, NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)4, and NPG(Staff Group)N(74)119.

NPG(Staff Group)N(75)24

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 21st March, 1975,
at 10 a.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be to discuss:

(a) the draft agenda(1) for the Spring meeting of NPG Ministers;

(b) the draft Advance Press release(2) for the Spring Meeting;

(c) the draft report(3) on WINTEX 75;

(d) the Future Work Programme(4).

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON

1110 Brussels.
