> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, March 8, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on Nuclear Consultation - CNI Procedures and to address any other current matters. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references the document NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)1(2nd Revise) dated February 24, 1976.
>

NPG(Staff Group)N(76)19
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th March, 1976
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of Nuclear Consultation - CNI Procedures(1);
(b) to discuss any other current matters.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
