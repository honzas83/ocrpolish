> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting on Monday, July 1, 1974, at 3:30 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the discussion of an initial draft of a study titled 'Communicating NATO's Intention to use Nuclear Weapons'. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references a working paper, NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7, dated June 20, 1974.
>
