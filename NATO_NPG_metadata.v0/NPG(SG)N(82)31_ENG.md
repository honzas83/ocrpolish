> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, September 27, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss preparations for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting and to address any other business. The notice was issued in Brussels by D.J. Roberts on September 23, 1982, on behalf of the North Atlantic Council.
