> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 25th March, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on March 25, 1974. The group agreed that the Secretary General should invite General Goodpaster and Admiral Cousins to the Spring 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting. They discussed the scheduling of the next meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives, tentatively agreeing on May 20, 1974. The group reviewed and modified a draft agenda for the Ministerial Meeting, noting that a Planning Outline (PO) for the Future Work Programme would be circulated soon. The revised draft agenda was to be circulated as an NPG Working Paper for approval by the eight attending countries under a silence procedure with a cut-off date of May 3, 1974. Consideration of the Warsaw Pact strategy and doctrine was deferred to the next meeting. Additionally, the group discussed a revised draft report on the Military Committee's function in the nuclear consultation process, incorporating changes proposed by the United States Staff Group member. The group agreed to continue these discussions on March 28, 1974, and scheduled several future meetings, including a visit to SHAPE on April 23, 1974.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 25th
March, 1974.

INVITATION TO SACEUR AND SACLANT
1. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed that the Secretary General should send invitations to General Goodpaster
and Admiral Cousins to attend the Spring 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting.

NEXT MEETING OF NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) discussed the possibility of holding the next NPG Permanent Representatives
meeting on Monday, 13th May, but noted that several Ambassadors would not be
able to participate on that date;

(b) agreed, subject to confirmation at the next meeting of the Staff Group, that
the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives would take place on Monday, 20th
May, 1974.

DRAFT AGENDA FOR SPRING 1974 NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING
3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) reviewed a proposed draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial Meeting(1);
(b) agreed to a number of modifications in the wording of agenda items and their
order on the agenda;
