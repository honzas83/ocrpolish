> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(78)19
> 
> This document is a corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(78)19, dated March 14, 1978. It serves to correct the meeting time for the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), changing the start time from 3 p.m. to 10.30 a.m. on Monday, March 20, 1978. The notice was issued by NATO in Brussels, Belgium.
