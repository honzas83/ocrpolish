> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 6, 1978, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to discuss the progress report of Task Force 6 and preparations for the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting. Additionally, the meeting will cover any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
