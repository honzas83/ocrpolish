> # Draft NDAC Communiqué
> 
> On October 29, 1968, the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a note accompanying a suggested draft of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee (NDAC) communiqué. The document is intended for discussion at the Staff Group meeting scheduled for Wednesday, October 30, 1968, at 2:45 p.m. The draft communiqué itself, dated October 25, 1968, notes that the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee met on November 14 (likely an OCR error for October 14) and took note of the work currently in progress within the Nuclear Planning Group. The document originated from Brussels, Belgium, and is associated with the NATO organization.
