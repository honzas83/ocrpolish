> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, January 8, 1973, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to conduct a preliminary exchange of views on the ACLANT Study and to continue discussions on matters that are likely to be examined at the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Ministers. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and references the ACLANT Study NPG/Study/19 dated December 1, 1972.

C.C
2nd January, 1973
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(73)1
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th January,
1973, at 3.0 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hold a preliminary exchange of views on the ACLANT Study(1);
(b) to continue the discussion of matters likely to be examined at the next
meeting of NPG Ministers.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
(1) NPG/Study/19, 1st December, 1972
