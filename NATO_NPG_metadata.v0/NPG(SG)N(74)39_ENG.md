> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Informal Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, dated 9th April 1974, inviting members of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group to an informal meeting. The meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, 17th April 1974, at 5 p.m. in Conference Room I-334. The purpose of the meeting is to bid farewell to Captain Ernie Barrett. The notice was signed by H.B. Seim and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
INFORMAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman

The Director and members of the International Staff Nuclear Planning Directorate
request the pleasure of the company of the members of the Nuclear Planning Group
Staff Group at an informal meeting in Conference Room I-334 on Wednesday, 17th
April, 1974, at 5 p.m. to bid farewell to Captain Ernie Barrett.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM

1110 Brussels.
