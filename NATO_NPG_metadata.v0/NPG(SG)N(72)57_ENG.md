> [!abstract] 
> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) informing members that the next meeting of the Staff Group has been rescheduled from June 26 to Monday, July 3, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to continue discussions regarding Consultation Procedures and Facilities. Additionally, the Secretary notes that a revised paper, identified as NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)9(Revised), will be circulated to the members early the following week.

21st June, 1972

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(72)57

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The Chairman has asked me to inform you that the next meeting of the Staff Group
will take place on Monday, 3rd July, 1972, at 3 p.m., not on 26th June as
originally planned.

2. The object of the meeting will be to continue the discussion on Consultation
Procedures and Facilities.

A revised paper(1) on this matter will be circulated early next week.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)9(Revised) - to be circulated
