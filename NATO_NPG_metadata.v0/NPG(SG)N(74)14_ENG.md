> [!abstract] 
> # Downgrading of Selected NDAC Documents and Related Working Papers
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides notification to its members regarding the downgrading of classification for a selection of North Atlantic Council (NDAC) documents and related working papers from 1969 and 1970. Following a recent review, the NDAC countries agreed to the downgrading action. The Head of the NATO Registry has already notified holders of these documents of the new classification. The document includes an annex listing eight specific documents, including reports by the NPG and decisions of the NDAC, which have been downgraded from NATO SECRET to NATO CONFIDENTIAL. Staff Group members are advised to verify that any copies of these documents in their possession have been appropriately downgraded. The note is signed by H.B. Seim and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NFG(Staff Group)N(74)14
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DOWNGRADING OF SELECTED NDAC DOCUMENTS
AND RELATED WORKING PAPERS
Note by the Chairman
As a result of a recent review of NDAC documents issued in 1969 and 1970, it was
proposed(1) that a number of documents be downgraded, to which the NDAC
countries subsequently agreed. Holders of the documents were then notified(2) of
the new classification by the Head of the NATO Registry. For the convenience of
Staff Group members, the NDAC documents that were affected by this action are
listed at Annex.

2. Staff Group members may wish to verify that copies of these documents, which
may be in their possession, have been appropriately downgraded.

3. No NDAC drafts or working papers were circulated in connection with the
preparation of the NDAC documents in the attached list.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
This document consists of 1 cover page and one page at Annex.
(1) NDAC/N(73)2, 12th December, 1973
(2) DN(74)4, 25th January, 1974
