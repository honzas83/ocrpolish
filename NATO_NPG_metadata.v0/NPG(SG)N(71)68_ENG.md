> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated June 24, 1971. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, June 28, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions regarding the dates for the next NPG Ministerial Meeting, procedural arrangements for subsequent stages of follow-on studies, and the handling of illustrative studies at the next meeting of Ministers. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and references a previous document, NPG(Staff Group)N(71)65, dated June 16, 1971.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP ( STAFF GROUP )
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 28th June, 1971,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be to continue the
discussion of:

(a) the dates of the next NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(b) procedural arrangements for subsequent stages of follow-on studies(1);
(c) arrangements for handling illustrative studies at the next meeting of
Ministers.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
