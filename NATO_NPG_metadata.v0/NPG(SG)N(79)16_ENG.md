> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, April 9, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to prepare for the Inverse Function Group (IFG) Permanent Representatives meeting on April 10 and the Spring 1979 IFG Ministerial meeting. The notice also includes a provision for any other business. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 9th April, 1979
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the IFG Permanent Representatives meeting on 10th April(1);
(b) preparations for the Spring 1979 IFG Ministerial meeting(2);
(c) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
