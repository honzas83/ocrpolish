> [!abstract] 
> # Visit to SHAPE
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding a scheduled visit to SHAPE on 5th October. The Secretary requests that Staff Group members provide the names of their delegation members who will be attending the visit by 28th September, along with confirmation that they are CTS ATCMAL cleared. Additionally, the document outlines transportation arrangements, stating that a bus will be transported from NATO Headquarters to SHAPE, departing from the Press Entrance at 9 a.m. on Monday, 8th October, and returning from SHAPE at approximately 4 p.m. Members who do not intend to use the provided bus are asked to inform the Secretary.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
VISIT TO SHAPE
Note by the Secretary

As requested by the SHAPE Representative at the Staff Group meeting on 24th
September, Staff Group members are asked to send me by 28th September the names
of the members of their delegation who will be going to SHAPE on 5th October,
together with confirmation that they are CTS ATCMAL cleared.

2. A bus has been arranged to transport the Staff Group between NATO
Headquarters and SHAPE. Those wishing to take the bus are asked to assemble at
the Press Entrance at 9 a.m. on Monday, 8th October. The bus will depart from
SHAPE at approximately 4 p.m. Those not intending to take the bus are asked to
inform me.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
