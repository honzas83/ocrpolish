> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, September 25, 1978, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to prepare for the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives on September 28, and to make preparations for the Brussels Ministerial meeting. Additionally, the agenda includes determining the date of the next High Level Group meeting and addressing any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place
on Monday, 25th September, 1978 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects
to be considered will be:

(a) preparation of the meeting of NPG Permanent
Representatives on 28th September(1);
(b) preparations for the Brussels Ministerial meeting;
(c) date of next High Level Group meeting;
(d) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
