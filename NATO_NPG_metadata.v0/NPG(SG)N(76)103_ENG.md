> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, informing the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) that their next meeting will take place on Monday, November 22, 1976, at 4 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to conduct an initial discussion on the results of the meeting of NPG Ministers held in London on November 17 and 18, 1976, and to address any other outstanding business. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

19th November, 1976

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)103

/ NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 22nd November,
1976 at 4 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to have an initial discussion on the results(1) of the meeting of NPG
Ministers held in London on 17th and 18th November, 1976;

(b) to discuss any other outstanding business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG/D(76)12, 18th November, 1976
