> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters on Wednesday, 30th August, 1972
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened at NATO Headquarters on August 30, 1972, to address several key agenda items. The group took note of several papers regarding the draft agenda for the NPG Autumn Ministerial Meeting, a status report on follow-on studies, and comments on the Transition and Denmark studies. Regarding the implementation of Special ADI Guidelines, the group agreed that the matter should be dealt with at the Permanent Representative level, pending final instructions from the United Kingdom. The The group also reviewed the first draft of a report on the Denmark Study, agreeing to changes and requesting a revised version that avoids ATOMAL content. The meeting concluded with a discussion on future work, including the Ministerial Agenda, consultation, and the Poseidon upgrading concept paper, as well as scheduling next meetings for September 5, 6, and 7, 1972.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters on Wednesday, 30th August, 1972

DOCUMENTATION

1. The STAFF GROUP:

took note of a statement by the Chairman who drew attention to the following
papers which had just been, or were about to be, circulated:

(a) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)14, dated 29th August, 1972: This is the draft agenda
for the NPG Autumn Ministerial Meeting;

(b) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)15, dated 30th August, 1972: This is the draft Status
Report on Follow-on Studies, and follows the lines of previous status reports;

(c) NPG/Study/27, dated 29th August, 1972: SHAPF comments on the Transition
Study;

(d) NPG/Study/28, dated 29th August, 1972: SACLANT comments on the Denmark
Study;

(e) IMSWM-211-72, dated 28th August, 1972: This is a revised version of the IMS
Working Memorandum on the Structure and Deployment of NATO Forces for Action in
a Nuclear Environment.
