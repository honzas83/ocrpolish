> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) informing members that the meeting scheduled for Monday, November 20, 1972, at 3 p.m. is postponed. The postponement is due to a private meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives who will meet at 4 p.m. on the same afternoon in the Secretary General's conference room. Consequently, the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, November 27, 1972, at 3 p.m., unless a meeting is needed earlier as a result of the outcomes of the Permanent Representatives' Meeting.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
I have been directed by the Chairman to inform you that the meeting of the Staff
Group, scheduled for Monday, 20th November, 1972, at 3 p.m. is postponed. This
is because a private meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives will take
place at 4 p.m. the same afternoon in the Secretary General's own conference
room.

2. The next meeting of the Staff Group will therefore take place on Monday, 27th
November, 1972, at 3 p.m. unless it appears necessary, as a result of the
Permanent Representatives' Meeting, to hold a Staff Group meeting at an earlier
date.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
