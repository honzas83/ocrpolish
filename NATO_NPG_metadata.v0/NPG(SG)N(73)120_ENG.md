> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, November 26, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to finalize the NPG Activities Report in preparation for the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee (NDAC) meeting on December 7, and to continue discussions regarding preparations for the Spring 1974 meeting of NPG Ministers. Additionally, the meeting will cover other general NPG activities. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 26th November,
1973 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to complete preparations for the meeting of the Nuclear Defence Affairs
Committee (NDAC) on 7th December, and in particular to finalize the NPG
Activities Report(1);

(b) to continue the discussion on preparations for the Spring 1974 meeting of
NPG Ministers(2);

(c) to continue consideration of other NPG activities.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
