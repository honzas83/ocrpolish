> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on February 21, 1973, to inform members that the next meeting will take place on Monday, February 26, 1973, at 3 p.m. The principal matters for discussion during the meeting will include WINTEX 73, the latest draft of the report on the ACLANT Study, and the determination of the date for the next meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 26th February,
1973, at 3 p.m. The principal matters for discussion will be:

(a) WINTEX 73(1)
(b) The latest draft(2) of the report on the ACLANT Study
(c) The date of the next meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives.
