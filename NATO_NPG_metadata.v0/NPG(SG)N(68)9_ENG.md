> [!abstract] 
> # Note by the Secretary
> 
> This document is a note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (State Group) dated 15th October 1968. It serves to notify members of the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Staff Group, scheduled for that afternoon. The Secretary, acting on instructions from the Director of Nuclear Planning, draws attention to a document titled 'Follow-on action to the Ministerial meeting at Bonn on 10th and 11th October, 1968', which is distributed alongside the note to provide a basis for the upcoming discussion. The document is signed by M. H. G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STATE GROUP)
Note by the Secretary
With reference to the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Staff Group(1) due to
take place this afternoon, the Director of Nuclear Planning has instructed me to
draw your attention to his document entitled "Follow-on action to the
Ministerial meeting at Bonn on 10th and 11th October, 1968"(2) which is being
distributed at the same time as this note, and will provide a basis for the
discussion.

(Signed) M. H. G. WILSON
OTAN/NATO, Brussels, 39.
