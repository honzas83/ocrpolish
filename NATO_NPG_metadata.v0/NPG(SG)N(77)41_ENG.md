> # Next Meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of preparatory meetings. The primary goal is to finalize the New Technology Study and the draft report on Theatre Nuclear Force Improvements before the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives scheduled for May 23, 1977. To achieve this, the Staff Group is proposed to meet on May 11, 12, and 13, 1977, with specific sessions at 10:30 a.m. and 2:30 p.m. The first two days will be dedicated to Theatre Nuclear Force improvements, while Friday will be focus on the New Technology Study. Additionally, a conference room has been reserved for May 24 to discuss the results of the Permanent Representatives' meeting and another session on May 27 for a discussion with Dr. Edward Keller. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETINGS

Note by the Secretary

It will be appreciated that little time remains between now and the meeting of
NPG Permanent Representatives flxed for 23rd May, 1977.

2. Among the tasks to be accomplished are the following: (a) the finalisation of
the New Technology Study(1); (b) the draft report by Permanent Representatives
on Theatre Nuclear Force Improvements(2).

These papers must be circulated as IPG Working Papers in both languages and
should be completed not later than Friday, 13th May, so that, hopefully they may
reach Capitals by Monday, 16th May.

3. For these reasons, it is proposed that the Staff Group shall meet at 10.30
a.m. and 2.30 p.m. on Wednesday 11th, Thursday 12th and Friday 13th.

4. The first matter to be discussed will be the TNF improvements which will
probably take up Wednesday and Thursday, leaving Friday for the completion of
the report on the New Technology Study.

5. For your information, a conference room has been reserved on Tuesday, 24th
May so that the results of the Permanent Representatives' meeting on the
previous day may, if necessary, be discussed.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)5, to be circulated
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)6, 29th April, 1977
