> # Schedule of Work for the Preparation of the November 1975 NPG Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, provides a detailed schedule of work for the preparation of the November 1975 NPG Ministerial meeting. The note by Chairman R.E. Shearer informs members of the Staff Group that a schedule has been agreed upon and is attached as an annex. The schedule includes key deadlines for finalizing drafts of the Compilation, Country Request, and Phase III Scope Paper, and meetings of the Permanent Representatives on October 9th and 29th. Specifically, the Permanent Representatives are to take final action on the Compilation and Country Request, and discuss the future of Autumn NPG Ministerial meetings and the Political Assessment on SACEUR Nike Hercules Study. The timeline concludes with the distribution of final documents to capitals on November 6th and 7th, and the Ministerial meeting itself on November 19th and 20th. The document also references a NATO SECRET document FO/75/64 dated June 3, 1975.
