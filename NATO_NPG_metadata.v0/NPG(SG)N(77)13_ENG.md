> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 15th February, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on February 15, 1977. The session began with a welcome to Lt. Colonel Jen Jean Pain of SHAFC. The group discussed nuclear consultation procedures, noting an invitation from the Turkish representative for a presentation on new and refined procedures. They also reviewed the results of a visit to Oberammergau. Regarding maritime nuclear safety, the group reviewed discussions from January 1, 1977, and considered US letters and suggestions for Terms of Reference, ultimately inviting the Nuclear Planning Directorate to prepare a new draft for consideration in Capitals. They further addressed arrangements for the NPG Spring Ministerial Meeting in Ottawa, with the Canadian representative requesting participant numbers for accommodation. Additionally, the US representative proposed a meeting of the Technology Study Full Study Panel to follow the PIT meeting on February 23-24, with a potential start on February 25. The group agreed to skip the meeting on February 21 and scheduled their next meeting for February 28, 1977.

3. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who summarised the results of the
valuable visit to Oberammergau made by members of the Staff Group last week.

TECHNOLOGY STUDY

6. The STAFF GROUP:

took note of a letter handled out by the US representative who said that his
authorities wished to hold a meeting of the Technology Study Full Study Panel
immediately following the meeting of the PIT scheduled for 23rd and 24th
February; he proposed to reserve 25th February for the Full Study Panel, with
the understanding that the meeting might begin during the afternoon of Thursday,
24th February if the PIT did not require the full day to complete its work.

NEXT MEETING

7. The STAFF GROUP:

(c) agreed not to hold a meeting on Monday, 21st February;

(b) agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 28th February, 1977 at 3 p.m.
