> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, September 21, 1970, at 3 p.m. in the Consultation Room of the NATO Situation Centre. The main items for discussion include a first exchange of views on the 'Draft Concept for the Role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in Acc' and a discussion of the report by the NATO Military Authorities on the 'Provisional Political Guidelines for the Initial Defensive Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons by NATO', as well as a paper by the Danish Delegation. The meeting is intended to prepare for a review by the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives the following day.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 21st September,
1970 at 3 p.m. in the Consultation Room of the NATO Situation Centre.

2. The main items for discussion will be:
(a) a first exchange of views of delegations on the "Draft Concept for the Role
of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in Acc" (1) to be reviewed by the Nuclear
Planning Group Permanent Representatives the following day;

(b) a discussion of the report by the NATO Military Authorities on the
"Provisional Political Guidelines for the Initial Defensive Tactical Use of
Nuclear Weapons by NATO" (2), and of the paper by the Danish Delegation on the
same subject (3).

(Signed) J. de GAYE
(1) NPG/D(70)6, dated 2nd September, 1970
(2) NPG/D(70)7, dated 16th September, 1970
(3) To be issued as NPG/D(70)8
