> [!abstract] 
> # Notice: Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) informing members that the meeting originally scheduled for January 20, 1969, has been postponed to Monday, January 27, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The reason for the postponement is to allow SHAPE (Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe) more time to prepare answers to proposed questions. The meeting on January 27 will be classified as ATOMAL, requiring members to provide written confirmation of their ATOMAL clearance. Special passes for the meeting will be available for collection from office No OA 119 in the Main Hall of the NATO Building in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(69)4

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP(STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group has asked me to inform
you that the meeting of the Staff Group scheduled for 20th January (1) is
postponed until Monday 27th January, 1969, at 2.45 p.m.

The reason for this postponement is to give SHAPE further time in which to
prepare the answers to the various questions which have been proposed.

The meeting on 27th January will be classified ATOMAL. Members of the Staff
Group are therefore requested to send written confirmation to the Secretary that
they are ATOMAL cleared. Special passes will then be made available for them for
collection from office No OA 119 in the Main Hall of the NATO Building before
the meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(69)3, dated 13th January, 1969.
