> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, September 1, 1977, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss and finalize the draft report by Permanent Representatives on the status of New Weapons Technology Follow-up Work, to complete the discussion of a draft agenda for the next meeting of NPG Ministers, and to consider any other business. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references two working papers: NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)7(Revised) and NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)9.

25th August, 1977
NOTICE NPG(Staff Group)N(77)53

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 1st September,
1977 at 3.00 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss, and if possible to finalize, the draft(1) report by Permanent
Representatives on the status of New Weapons Technology Follow-up Work;

(b) to complete the discussion of a draft Agenda(2) for the next meeting of NPG
Ministers;

(c) to consider any other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)7(Revised), 23rd August, 1977
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)9, dated 17th August, 1977
