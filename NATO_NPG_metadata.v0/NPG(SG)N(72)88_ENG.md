> [!abstract] 
> # Reference List of Follow-On Use Work Programme Documents
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on 10th October 1972, serves as a comprehensive reference list of the follow-on use work programme. The list is presented in two annexes: Annex A, which lists documents by reference number, and Annex B, which lists documents by subject. The document supersedes a previous list distributed in May 1972. The listed documents include a variety of studies and reports on various geographic areas, including North Norway, Macedonia, Western Thrace, Turkey, and the Mediterranean Maritime Area. It also includes reports on forces transition, structure, and deployment, and comments from SHAPE and SACLAND. Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE) Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR) Supreme Allied Commander Atlantic (SACLAN) and the Military Committee are listed as contributing bodies. The documents are categorized by classification levels such as Secret, Confidential, and Cosmic Top Secret. The list covers a period from 1969 to 1972.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The document is a reference list of 'follow-on use work programme' documents, which includes specific studies on nuclear use in various regions (e.g., 'Follow-on Use Study of North Norway area', 'Follow-on Use Study of CENTAG', 'Study of Transition from Conventional to Nuclear use'). The mention of 'Letter/yield code' for several studies indicates technical strike planning and yield calculations.
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document lists numerous studies regarding the deployment and use of nuclear weapons across various NATO allied territories, including North Norway, Macedonia, Western Thrace, NORTHAG, CENTAG, Turkish Thrace, North-Western Turkey, and the Mediterranean Maritime Area, which is the core of extended deterrence and tactical nuclear sharing in Europe.
