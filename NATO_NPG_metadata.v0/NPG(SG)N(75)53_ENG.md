> [!abstract] 
> # Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> This document is a note from the acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council (NATO) informing the Staff Group that their next meeting will take place on Monday, July 14, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to resume discussions regarding follow-up actions from the Ministerial Meeting in Monterey and to consider preparations for the upcoming Autumn Ministerial Meeting. The note is signed by F.L.C. Park and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 14th July, 1975
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to resume discussion of follow-up action to the Ministerial Meeting in
Monterey(1);

(b) to consider preparations for the Autumn Ministerial Meeting.
