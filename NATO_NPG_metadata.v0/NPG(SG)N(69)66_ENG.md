> # Revised Agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), dated September 17, 1969, presenting a revised agenda for an upcoming Ministerial meeting to be held near Washington on November 11th and 12th, 1969. The agenda includes discussions on the tactical use of nuclear weapons, specifically focusing on political guidelines for initial tactical use by NATO and further studies on tactical use. The meeting's agenda also covers the strategic use of nuclear weapons, specifically the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, with reports from the United States and Germany. It further addresses consultation on the use of nuclear weapons, featuring reports from Belgium and the United States, and a collation by the Netherlands. It concludes with other business, the future work programme, an agreed minute, and a communique. The Secretary, H. L. G. Wilson, specifies that if no further changes are requested, the agenda would be considered final and requested to be circulated on October 10th.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
REVISED AGENDA FOR THE NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP
MINISTERIAL MEETING TO BE HELD NEAR
WASHINGTON ON 11TH AND 12TH NOVEMBER, 1969

Note by the Secretary

Attached is a draft of the agenda for the NPG Ministerial meeting to be held in
November, revised in the light of the discussion which took place at the Staff
Group meeting on 15th September.

If no further changes are proposed, this agenda would be considered as final and
would be circulated on 10th October.

(Signed) H. L. G. WILSON
