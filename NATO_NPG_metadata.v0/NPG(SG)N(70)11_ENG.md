> # Nuclear Planning Group (N.P. Grup) Next Meeting
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (N.P. Grup) has issued a notice to schedule the next meeting of the Staff Group on Monday, March 9, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the the meeting is to feature an informal talk by the International Military Staff on the subject of Selective Release procedures. This presentation serves as an introduction to the exploration of Selective Release procedures by the Staff Group. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO in Brussels, Belgium.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (N.P. GRUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
As agreed at its last meeting, the Staff Group will hold its next meeting on
Monday, 9th March, 1970, at 3 p.m.

2. The principal object of this meeting will be to hear an informal talk given
by the International Military Staff on the subject of Selective Release
procedures, as an introduction to the exploration by the Staff Group of this
subject.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 111C Brussels.
