> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the rescheduling of an upcoming meeting. Due to the absence of several Staff Group members, the meeting originally scheduled for July 7 is cancelled. The next meeting will be an informal session taking place on Monday, July 14, 1980, at 3 p.m. in the Chairman's office. The primary purpose of this meeting will be to prepare for the next Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meetings. Additionally, the meeting will address arrangements for the departure of Staff Group members who are leaving during the summer period.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
Due to the absence of several Staff Group members, there will be no meeting on
7th July. The next meeting, which will be an informal one, will take place on
Monday, 14th July at 3 p.m. in the Chairman's office and will be devoted to
preparations for the next NPG Ministerial meetings.

2. Consideration will also be given at the meeting to arrangements for the
departure of those Staff Group members who are leaving during the Summer.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
