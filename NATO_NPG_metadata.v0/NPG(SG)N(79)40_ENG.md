> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Wednesday, October 17, 1979, at 9:30 a.m. The primary objectives of the agenda include preparations for the Autumn 1979 Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meeting, a discussion on the draft General Political Guidelines, and a specific focus on Section IV concerning the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice also lists several reference documents from the NPG Staff Group and other departments such as the USNATO/DDP/OUT/NS and DPP/IPD/DPP/NPD, providing a comprehensive list of supporting materials for the participants. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts of NATO in Brussels, Belgium.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 17th October,
1979 at 9.30 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Autumn 1979 NPG Ministerial meeting(1);
(b) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(2), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(3);

(c) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, '10 Brussels.
