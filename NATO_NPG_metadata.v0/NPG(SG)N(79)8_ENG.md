> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 12, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with additional time allocated for any other business. The notice includes a list of reference documents, including various NPG and USNATO documents, and a Canadian Representative's letter. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts of NATO in Brussels, Belgium.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 12th February,
