> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, August 18, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the Future Work Programme and to decide on the utilization of the Air Defence Illustrative Study. The notice is signed by R.H.C. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It also references document PO(75)69 dated June 4, 1975, in relation to the Future Work Programme.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL.

8th August, 1975
NOTICE
PCN(Staff Group)N(75)60

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 18th August,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the Future Work Programme(1),
(b) to decide upon the use to be made of the Air Defence Illustrative Study.

(Signed) R.H.C. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) PO(75)69, 4th June, 1975
