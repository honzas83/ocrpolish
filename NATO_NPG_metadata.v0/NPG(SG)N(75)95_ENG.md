> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 3rd November, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on November 3, 1975. The agenda included an exchange of views regarding possible modifications to the Air Defence Study within the draft Future Work Programme in PO/75/119. Additionally, the group heard a statement from the German Representative concerning the administrative arrangements for a ministerial meeting scheduled to take place in Hamburg. The meeting also included a follow-up to the ministerial meeting, featuring personal remarks from the United Kingdom representative and a general exchange of views on CNI - Messages of warning and procedural aspects. The date for the next meeting was to be arranged following the the Ministerial Meeting unless an earlier session was required.
