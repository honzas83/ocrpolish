> # Notes of a Meeting Held on Monday, 27th April, 1970
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group held a meeting on April 27, 1970, in Brussels to address several key agenda items. The group approved a welcome for Colonel Vander Voort from the Pentagon, and noted a farewell cocktail party for members leaving Brussels during the summer. The group agreed on the dates and agendas for the next meetings of the NPG Permanent Representatives, including discussions on the draft report on ADMS and the status report on studies on follow-on use. Additionally, the group discussed a draft addendum to the ADM report and the implications of the NORTAG study, inviting the International Staff to circulate revised paragraphs for approval via silent procedure. The meeting also covered a draft report on the work programme for further studies on the follow-on tactical use of nuclear weapons, with instructions for the International Staff to prepare a revised version. The next meeting of the Staff Group was scheduled for May 4, 1970.

DISTINGUISHED VISITOR
1. The STAFF GROUP:
approved the warm welcome extended to Colonel Vander Voort who had come over
from the Pentagon and was attending the meeting of the Staff Group.

FAREWELL COCKTAIL PARTY
2. The STAFF GROUP:
noted a statement by the Chairman who said that, in view of the considerable
number of the Staff Group members who were leaving Brussels during the summer,
the International Staff intended to give a cocktail party in their honour; this
party would be held on Thursday, 25th June.

NEXT MEETINGS OF THE PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES
3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) agreed that the next meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives should
take place on Tuesday, 12th May, at 10.15 a.m., with the possibility of
continuing at 4 p.m. ;

STATUS REPORT

5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) discussed the draft report by Permanent Representatives on the work
programme for further studies on the follow-on tactical use of nuclear
weapons(1);

(b) invited the International Staff to prepare a revised version of the draft
