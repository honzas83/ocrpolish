> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Thursday, January 25, 1979, at 2:30 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with additional time allocated for any other business. The notice references several previous documents, including USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126, PG(Staff Group)/P(78)8, PG(Staff Group)N(78)76, PG(Staff Group)N(78)81, and NPG/Study/52, as well as a letter from the Canadian Representative. The meeting is based in Brussels, Belgium, and is signed by D.J. Roberts.
