> # Notes of a Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> The NATO Staff Group held a meeting in Brussels on October 6, 1975, to coordinate preparations for the Autumn Ministerial Meeting. The group discussed the scheduling of Permanent Representatives' meetings, including a proposal to postpone the next meeting to October 29. They reviewed the draft agenda for the Hamburg meeting, specifically amending Item V(b) to focus on communicating NATO's intentions to use nuclear weapons and messages of warning. The group also addressed the progress of future work, including subjects for the Information Office to submit to ministers. They approved a draft press release with one amendment and noted the inaccurate French translation. There was a discussion regarding the proposal to hold future Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meetings at NATO Headquarters. Additionally, the group deferred the completion of work on the consultation procedure and discussed updating the illustrative study on spokesman's procedure in light of modifications to the Supreme Allied Commander Europe's (SACEM) 'ISK' warning message. Finally, they reviewed a revised draft report on communications and messages of warning, requesting the International Secretariat to draft a new conclusion paragraph.

AGENCIA PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting, held in the NATO Headquarters, 140 Brussels, on Monday, 6th
October, 1975

FIRST MEETING OF THE PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES
1. The Staff Group
agreed that in view of the meetin of the DPC scheduled for 15th October, the
next meeting of Permanent Representatives might be postponed to 29th October.

DRAFT AGENDA FOR III. ISTRIAL MEETING I. HAMBURG
2. The Staff Group:
(a) noted that since the draft agenda(') would be discussed at the next meeting
of Permanent Representatives, the cover note suggesting approval by the silence
procedure would have to be modified;

(b) agreed that Item V(b) should be amended to read 'Communicating NATO's
Intentions to Use Nuclear Weapons - Messages of Warning'.

FUTURE WORK PROGRESS.
3. The Staff Group:
had an exchange of views on possible subjects which might be included in the IO
to be submitted to ministers at their Autumn meeting.

ADVANCE PAPER FOR THE AUTUMN MINISTERIAL MEETING
4. The Staff Group:
(a) approved the draft press release in 1.7 (Staff Group)N(75)80 subject to one
amendment;

(b) noted that the French translation of this text was inaccurate and would have
to be revised.

FUTURE AUTUMN MINISTERIAL MEETINGS
5. The Staff Group:
(a) noted the position of several delegations with regard to the proposal that
future NEPG Ministerial meetings should be held at NATO Headquarters;

(b) noted that this subject probably would be further discussed by Permanent
Representatives at their next meeting.

COMPILATION OF CONSULTATION PROCEDURE
5. The Staff Group:
agreed that completion of work on this subject should be deferred until after
the Autumn Ministerial Meeting; and that it should, therefore, be deleted from
the Agenda of the next meeting of Permanent Representatives (1).

INCLUSION OF SPOKE MAN'S PROCEDURE
7. The Staff Group:
(a) noted a suggestion by the United Kingdom Representative concerning the
possible updating of the illustrative study on this subject in view of the
recently introduced modification to SACEM's "ISK" warning message;

(b) following an exchange of views, requested the Chairman to consult with
individual delegations as to how the modified procedure should best be explained
to Ministers.
