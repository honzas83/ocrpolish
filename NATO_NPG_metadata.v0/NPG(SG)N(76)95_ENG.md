> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Tuesday, 19th October, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on October 19, 1976, at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting covered several key agenda items, including the note that the United States had approved Working Paper NPG/1P(76)5. The group discussed the possibility of wider distribution for Part I of the New Technology Study, specifically the Military Implications Team (MIT) Report. There were discussions regarding the preparations for the Autumn Ministerial Meeting, noting that the United States was still awaiting instructions on the Agenda. It was agreed that if the agenda could not be resolved, the Secretary General would raise the issue during the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives on October 22. Additionally, the United Kingdom representative requested early replies to administrative arrangements for the London meeting. The group also exchanged views on the future work programme in PO/76/116 in preparation for the upcoming meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives. Finally, the group took note of suggestions regarding the format and content of the paper to be submitted to Ministers and potential modifications by the Secretary General. The next meeting of the Staff Group was scheduled for October 25, 1976.
