> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1976 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the timing of a draft advance press release for the Spring 1976 Ministerial Meeting. The Chairman refers to a previous note dated 6th April 1976, which initially suggested a release date of Friday, 11th June 1976. However, following a request from the United States representative at a meeting on 5th May 1976, it was agreed that the release should be moved earlier to Tuesday, 8th June 1976, at 4 p.m. Brussels time. Canada, Germany, and the United States have already confirmed their participation in the simultaneous release. The Chairman requests that other participating countries inform him by Wednesday, 26th May, which other nations plan to make simultaneous releases.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE SPRING 1976
MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Members will recall my note dated 6th April, 1976(1), in which it as suggested
that a draft advance press release for the N.G Ministerial Meeting should be
made in Brussels on Friday, 11th June, 1976, at 3 p.m., local time, with
simultaneous additional release in Capitals of those participating countries who
so desire.

2. At the meeting of the Staff Group held on 5th May, 1976, the United States
representative said that his authorities would find it more convenient to make
this release somewhat earlier. It was therefore agreed, subject to confirmation
by some members, that the simultaneous release should take place on Tuesday, 8th
June, 1976 at 4 p.m., Brussels time. At the Staff Group meeting on 10th May,
1976, Canada, Germany and the United States confirmed that they would make such
a release.

3. I would be grateful to be informed, not later than Wednesday, 26th May, which
other participating countries plan to make simultaneous releases.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPC(Staff Group)N(76)29, 6th April, 1976
