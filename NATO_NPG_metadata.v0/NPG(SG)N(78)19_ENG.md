> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, March 20, 1978, at 3 p.m. The meeting's primary objectives are to prepare for the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting, including a discussion of the Ministerial Agenda, and to discuss Short-Term Measures in Theatre Nuclear Force Improvements. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references two working papers: NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)1(Revised) and NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)3.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions a discussion on 'Short-Term Measures in Theatre Nuclear Force Improvements', which refers to the deployment and improvement of nuclear weapons within the European theatre, a core component of extended deterrence and tactical nuclear sharing.

Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 20th March, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Spring, 1978 NPG Ministerial Meeting, including further
discussion of the Ministerial Agenda(1);

(b) discussion of Short-Term Measures in Theatre Nuclear Force Improvements(2);
(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)1(Revised), 25th January, 1978
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)3, 10th March, 1978
