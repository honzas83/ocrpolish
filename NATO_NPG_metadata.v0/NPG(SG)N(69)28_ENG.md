> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, March 24, 1969. The agenda items for discussion include a revised paper on theatre nuclear strike forces prepared by Canadian and United Kingdom representatives, and follow-on action regarding the ADM Time Factors paper resulting from a meeting of Permanent Representatives held on March 18, 1969. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
