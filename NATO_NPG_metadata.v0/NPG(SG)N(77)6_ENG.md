> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, January 24, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the revised draft Status of Work Report and to address any other business. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels. It references the revised draft Status of Work Report, identified as NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)1(Revised), dated January 18, 1977.
