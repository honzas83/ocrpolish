> [!abstract] 
> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on 13th March, 1972
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on 13th March 1972. The group discussed the circulation of Nuclear Planning Committee Working Papers, specifically the Draft Report on the Turkish Study (NPG/WP(72)1). They reviewed an initial draft report on an illustrative study on battlefield use, agreeing to several text changes and requesting a revised text of Part III from the International Staff. The meeting also touched upon upcoming studies regarding consultation facilities and the Mediterranean Maritime Study. Additionally, the Danish representative requested that delegations attending the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Copenhagen provide security certificates for their staff by 24th March. The group concluded by scheduling their next meeting for 15th March 1972.

NUCL. PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on 13th March,
1972

DOCUMENTATION
1. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who said that the first of the NPC
Working Papers for examination by Capitals and subsequent consideration by
Permanent Representatives had already been circulated (NPG/WP(72)1), dated 9th
March : Draft Report on the Turkish Study) and that three more would probably be
distributed during the course of the week.

ILLUSTRATIVE STUDY ON BATTLEFIELD USE - INITIAL DRAFT REPORT
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who recalled the previous
discussions on the Initial Draft Report, and referred to the International Staff
Working Paper(1) and to the re-draft(2) of part II of the Working Paper issued
during the weekend; he invited further comments;

(b) took note of the suggestions made and the views expressed in the ensuing
detailed discussion, and agreed to a number of changes in the text;

(c) invited the International Staff, in the light of the discussion, to prepare
and circulate a revised text of Part III with a view to continuing the
discussion at the next meeting.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)5, 2nd March, 1972
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(72)2B, 10th March, 1972

ITEMS FOR EARLY STUDY
3. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Chairman who said that he hoped that Staff Group
Working Papers on Consultation facilities and on the Mediterranean Maritine
Study would be available during the next few days for examination by the Staff
Group.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE HPG MINISTERIAL MEETING IN COPENHAGEN
4. The STAFF GROUP:
took note of a statement by the Danish representative who requested Delegations
attending the NPG Ministerial Meeting in Copenhagen to send him, if possible
before 24th March, group Security Certificates for their staffs.

NEXT MEETING
5. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Wednesday, 15th March, 1972, at 3 p.m.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
