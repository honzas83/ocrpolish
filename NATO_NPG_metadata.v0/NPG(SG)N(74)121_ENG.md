> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, January 6, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft report to the Nuclear Planning Committee (NPC) activities, examine the status of the Technological Study, discuss the Phase II Report, and review preparations for the Spring 1975 Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents, including NPG(Staff Group)N(74)116, NPG/VP(74)9, and NPG(Staff Group)N(74)119.

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)121
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 6th January,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the draft report to the NDAC on NPC activities(1);
(b) to examine the status of the Technological Study;
(c) to discuss the Phase II Report(2);
(d) to review preparations for the Spring 1975 Ministerial Meeting(3).
(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)116, 28th November, 1974
(2) NPG/VP(74)9, 4th December, 1974
(3) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)119, 13th December, 1974
