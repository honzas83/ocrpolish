> [!abstract] 
> # Notice: Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, May 13, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to review NPG/WP(74)4 in light of comments from the Chairman of the Military Committee and guidance from capitals, to review NPG/WP(74)5 considering recently proposed changes and capital guidance, and to continue preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)53

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 13th May, 1974 at
3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to review NPG/WP(74)4 in the light of comments by the Chairman, Military
Committee(1) and of guidance that may have been received from capitals;

(b) to review NPG/WP(74)5 taking into account the recently proposed change(2)
and the guidance that may have been received from capitals;

(c) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) PO/74/52, 6th May, 1974

(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)50, 3rd May, 1974
