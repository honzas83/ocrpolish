> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting of the Staff Group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Friday, August 6, 1976, at 10:30 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to conduct a final discussion on the terms of reference for the NIKE HERCULES Study. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references the document NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)6 dated August 3, 1976.
