> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, January 31, 1977, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft Terms of Reference for the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study and to discuss participation in WINTEX 77. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD

26th January, 1977
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(77)8
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 31st January,
1977 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss draft Terms of Reference for the Maritime Nuclear Warfare
Study(1);

(b) to discuss participation in WINTEX 77.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)9, Annex C, 6th December, 1976
