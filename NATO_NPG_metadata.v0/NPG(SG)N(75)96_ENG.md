> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 13th November 1975. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th November 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of this meeting is to discuss the future work of the Staff Group. The notice was signed by M. J. Filson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
