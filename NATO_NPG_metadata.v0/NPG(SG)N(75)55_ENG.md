> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels on Monday, 14th July, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group met on July 14, 1975, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to address several key agenda items. The group discussed follow-up actions from the ministerial meeting in Montreux and preparations for the autumn ministerial meeting. Key points of discussion included the status of comments from the United Kingdom and Germany regarding the Follow-On Use 'Vor' Programme - Initiation of Phase III. The Staff Group also agreed to decide at their next meeting whether the Air Defence Illustrative Study should be included on the autumn ministerial agenda. Additionally, Denmark, the United Kingdom, and the United States agreed to form an open-ended ad hoc working group to study questions regarding the warning of NATO's intentions. Furthermore, the group decided to postpone their visit to SHAPE (Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe) to September due to thes departure of several members. The document also lists future meeting dates for July 28, August 18, and August 25, 1975.

any comments received from their authorities;
