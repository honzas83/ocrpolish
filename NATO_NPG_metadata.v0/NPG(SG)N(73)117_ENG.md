> # Mention of NDAC in the Communiqué for the December Ministerial Meetings
> 
> The Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice regarding the inclusion of a short paragraph on the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee (NDAC) Ministerial session in the blanket communiqué to be issued at the conclusion of the December Ministerial meetings. The drafting of this communiqué is being managed by an ad hoc drafting group chaired by Mr. Humphreys, the Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning and Policy. The Chairman, H.B. Seim, suggests that Ministers who participated in the NDAC meeting will be asked to approve the paragraph pertaining to the NDAC. H.B. Seim plans to submit draft language to Mr. Humphreys and requests comments from members on Monday, 19th November. The attached draft paragraph specifies that the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee, comprising the Defence Ministers of Belgium, Canada, Denmark, Federal Republic of Germany, Germany, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States, held its annual meeting to review the work of the Nuclear Planning Group over the last year and its future activity plans.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
MENTION OF NDAC IN THE COMMUNIQUE FOR
THE DECEMBER MINISTERIAL MEETINGS
Note by the Chairman
In conformity with past practice, it is expected that a short paragraph on the
NDAC Ministerial session will be inserted in the blanket communiqué to be issued
at the conclusion of the December Ministerial meetings. Actual drafting of this
communiqué would be done by an ad hoc drafting group chaired by the Assistant
Secretary General for Defence Planning and Policy, Mr. Humphreys. When the
entire communiqué is considered at the end of the series of meetings, those
Ministers who participated in the NDAC meeting will be asked to approve the
paragraph pertaining to the NDAC.

2. I plan to submit to Mr. Humphreys some draft language on the NDAC along the
lines of the attached text and would appreciate at our meeting on Monday, 19th
November, any comments you may have.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
