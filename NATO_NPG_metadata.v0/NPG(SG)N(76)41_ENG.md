> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, May 17, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting, including the drafting of a transmission letter regarding preliminary national views on the United States Theatre Nuclear Forces paper. Additionally, the meeting will continue discussions on the presentation of newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation. The notice is signed by N.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document discusses preparations for a Ministerial Meeting regarding 'preliminary national views on the US Theatre Nuclear Forces paper', which directly relates to the deployment and sharing of nuclear weapons in the European theatre.
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The document mentions the discussion of 'Newly Developed Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation', which concerns the internal mechanisms for the execution and consultation of nuclear operations.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 17th May, 1976,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting, including a
draft letter of transmission concerning preliminary national views on the US
Theatre Nuclear Forces paper(1);

(b) to continue the discussion on the presentation of Newly Developed Procedures
in the Field of Nuclear Consultation(2);

(c) to discuss any other matters.

(Signed) N.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)VP(76)4, 13th May, 1976
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)2, 30th March, 1976
