> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, June 6, 1983, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to consider a draft report by Permanent Representatives on WINTEX-CIMEX 83, as well as any other business. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references the document NPG(Staff Group)WP(83)4 dated May 17, 1983.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Military-Operations-and-Capabilities/Multilateral-Military-Exercises — The document explicitly lists as a principal subject for the next meeting a 'Draft report by Permanent Representatives on WINTEX-CIMEX 83(1)', which refers to a multilateral military exercise.
