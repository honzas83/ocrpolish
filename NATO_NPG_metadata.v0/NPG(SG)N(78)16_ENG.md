> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 6, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss preparations for the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting, including a further review of the Ministerial Agenda. The notice also allows for the consideration of any other business. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 6th March, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Spring, 1978 NPG Ministerial Meeting, including further
discussion of the Ministerial Agenda(1);

(b) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)1(Revised), 25th January, 1978
