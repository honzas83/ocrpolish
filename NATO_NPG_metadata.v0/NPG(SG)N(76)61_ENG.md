> # Follow-Up Action to the Ministerial Meeting at NATO Headquarters, Brussels on 14th and 15th June 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on July 7, 1976. The primary focus was the following up on actions from the Ministerial Meeting held on June 14-15, 1976. The group examined and amended Draft Terms of Reference for a study on the role and numbers of dual-capable aircraft, requesting the International Staff to publish the agreed text. Additionally, the group agreed to examine the Draft Terms of Reference for a study on the levels of NIKE HERCULES sites once United States views were available. The meeting also noted a report by the Canadian representative regarding comments made by Ambassador Menzies in his farewell speech to the Council. Furthermore, the North Atlantic Council's Phase III Study Team meeting was scheduled for July 22-23, 1976, and the report by the Danish representative on the training of Ministers in nuclear decision-making was noted.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday,
7th July, 1976

FOLLOW-UP ACTION TO THE MINISTERIAL MEETING AT NATO HEADQUARTERS, BRUSSELS ON
14TH AND 15TH JUNE 1976

1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) noted a statement by the Chairman that the meeting of Permanent
Representatives to discuss this subject would take place on Wednesday, 14th July
at 3.30 p.m.;

(b) examined and amended the Draft Terms of Reference for a NPG Study on the
Rôle and Numbers of Dual-capable Aircraft(1) and requested the International
Staff to publish the agreed text(2) for consideration at the meeting of
Permanent Representatives;

(c) noted that the Netherlands representative could only accept the inclusion of
certain sections of CMCN-70-76 in Draft Terms of Reference ad referendum to his
Authorities;

(d) agreed to examine the Draft Terms of Reference for a NPG Study of Levels of
NIKE HERCULES Sites(3) as soon as the United States views on this subject were
available.

(1) Annex B to Addendum to NPG(Staff Group)VP(76)5, dated 24th June, 1976
(2) Addendum to NPG/WP(76)3, dated 8th July, 1976
(3) Annex A to Addendum to NPG(Staff Group)VP(76)5
