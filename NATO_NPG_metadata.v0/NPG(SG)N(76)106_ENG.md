> # NPG Ministerial Meetings
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a detailed record of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial meetings. It includes a list of all Ministerial meetings held from 1957 to 1976, listing the host cities and countries, such as Washington, Ankara, The Hague, and London. The document also provides a tally of the number of meetings hosted by each participating country as of December 1, 1976, with the Federal Republic of Germany, United Kingdom, and United States each hosting three meetings. It further outlines the projected participation of permanent members (Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, United Kingdom, and United States) and rotating members (Canada, Netherlands, Norway, Belgium, Belgium, Denmark, Greece, and Turkey) based on an agreement on NPG membership. The rotating members follow a schedule where they spend 18 months 'in' and 18 months 'out' of the group. The document references the membership agreement NDAC/D(69)4.

Note by the Secretary
For easy reference by Staff Group members, I attach the following information:
(a) a list of all the Ministerial meetings of Nuclear Planning Group which have
so far taken place, together with a note setting out the number of times each
participating country has acted as host;

(b) a diagram showing, for the period 1976-1979, country participation at NPG
Ministerial meetings on the basis of the agreement on NPG membership(1).
