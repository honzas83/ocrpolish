> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(82)19
> 
> This document, issued by the North Atlantic Council, North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO), serves as a corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(82)19. It announces that the meeting of the Staff Group of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) is postponed from its originally scheduled date of Monday, May 17, 1982, to Tuesday, May 18, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The document was issued in Brussels, Belgium, on May 15, 1982.
