> # Transport of Classified Documents to Bergen
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the transport of classified documents. Mr. Bittner, a member of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section, is scheduled to carry classified papers from Brussels to Bergen on June 10, 1974, and return with them on June 13, 1974. Delegations wishing to utilize this arrangement are requested to contact Mr. Bittner and submit their papers in a closed, clearly marked package, ensuring the packages are as light as possible. The papers must be delivered to him no later than 10:30 a.m. on Monday, June 10, 1974.

TRANSPORT OF CLASSIFIED DOCUMENTS TO BERGEN
Note by the Secretary
Mr. Bittner of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section (Tel. ext. 2481),
will carry classified papers from Brussels to Bergen on 10th June and will
return with them on 13th June. Delegations wishing to take advantage of this
arrangement are requested to contact Mr. Bittner and to send him their papers
not later than 10,30 a.m. on Monday, 10th June, 1974 in a closed package clearly
marked for easy identification. Packages should be as light as possible.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
