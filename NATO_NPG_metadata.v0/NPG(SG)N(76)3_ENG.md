> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the Meeting of NPG Ministers in Hamburg
> 
> This document, dated January 9, 1976, outlines the composition of the International Staff delegation accompanying the Secretary General to the 18th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers in Hamburg. The notice, issued by the Secretary of the NPG Staff Group, M.H.G. Wilson, lists various officials including the Secretary General, Mr. J.M.A.H. Luns, and the Assistant Secretary General for Defence Planning & Policy, Mr. D.C. Humphreys. The delegation includes specialized roles such as the Director of Nuclear Planning, Nuclear Planning Staff Officers, the Head of the NATO Press Service, and interpreters. It also includes administrative support staff, such as personal assistants and secretaries. The document serves as an official record of the personnel assigned to support the NPG Ministers' meeting in January 1976.
