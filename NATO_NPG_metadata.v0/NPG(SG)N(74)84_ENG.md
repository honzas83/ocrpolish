> # Communicating NATO's Intention to Use Nuclear Weapons
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on Monday, August 5, 1974. The primary focus of the meeting was to discuss a draft report by Permanent Representatives regarding a study on Communicating NATO's Intention to Use Nuclear Weapons. The Staff Group agreed that the study, along with comments from the Nuclear Planning Group, should be sent to the Nuclear Delegation Advisory Committee (NDAC) for endorsement and subsequently to the Defense and Politics Committee (DPC) after approval by NPG Ministers. The group took note of various views and suggestions and agreed to several changes and additions to the text. The Chairman was invited to circulate a revised version of the Working Paper based on these discussions. Additionally, the group scheduled its next meeting for Wednesday, September 4, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Bruxelles, on Monday, 5th
August, 1974

COMMUNICATING NATO'S INTENTION TO USE NUCLEAR WEAPONS

1. The Staff Group:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced the draft(1) of a
possible report by Permanent Representatives on a study on Communicating NATO's
Intention to Use Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) agreed that the Study, together with some comments by the NPG, should be
sent, after approval by NPG Ministers, to the NDAC for endorsement and
subsequently to the DPC;

(c) took note of the views and suggestions expressed in the ensuing discussion,
and agreed to a number of changes and additions to the text;

(d) invited the Chairman to circulate a revised version of the Working Paper,
amended in the light of the discussion.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP

2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) agreed to hold its next meeting on Wednesday, 4th September, 1974, at 3 p.m.

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
