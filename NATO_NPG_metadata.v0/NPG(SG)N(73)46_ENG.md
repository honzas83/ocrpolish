> [!abstract] 
> # Cancellation of Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated June 1, 1973. It informs the Staff Group members that the meeting scheduled for Monday, June 4, 1973, has been cancelled because the Chairman is on emergency leave. The Secretary, M.H.G. Wilson, states that members will be notified of the date of the next meeting at a later time. The notice originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

C.E
1st June, 1973.
NOTICE
NPG(STAFF GROUP)N(73)46
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP ( STAFF GROUP )
CANCELLATION OF MEETING
Note by the Secretary
In view of his absence on emergency leave, the Chairman has asked me to inform
you that the Staff Group meeting(1) scheduled for Monday, 4th June, 1973 is
cancelled. Staff Group members will be notified later of the date of the next
meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)45, 30th May, 1973
