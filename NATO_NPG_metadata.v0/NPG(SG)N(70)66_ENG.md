> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Wednesday, September 16, 1970, at 10:15 a.m. and 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the revised draft of the Special ADM Guidelines, which were prepared by the International Staff in light of previous discussions on September 3 and 4, 1970, and to discuss a draft agenda for the upcoming Ministerial meeting in Ottawa. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 16th
September, 1970 at 10.15 a.m. and at 3 p.m.

The principal objects of the meeting will be to discuss the revised draft(1) of
the Special ADM Guidelines, prepared by the International Staff in the light of
the Staff Group discussion on 3rd and 4th September, and to discuss a draft
agenda(2) for the Ministerial meeting in Ottawa.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(70)5(4th revise), 8th September, 1970
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(70)4, 8th September, 1970.
