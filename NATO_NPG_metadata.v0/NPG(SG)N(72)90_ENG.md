> [!abstract] 
> # Composition of the International Staff Delegation to the NPG Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document, dated 12th October 1972, provides a list of International Staff members who will attend the 12th Meeting of Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers to be held in London on 26th and 27th October 1972. The delegation includes high-level officials such as Secretary General J.M.A.H. Luns, Assistant Secretary General D.C. Humphreys, and Director of Nuclear Planning H.B. Seim, along with executive secretaries, interpreters, and administrative support staff. The note was signed by M.H.G. Wilson of the Executive Secretariat.
