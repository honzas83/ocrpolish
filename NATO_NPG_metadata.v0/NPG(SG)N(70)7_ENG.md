> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 19th February 1970. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 23rd February 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss a tentative list of topics for exploration by the Staff Group. The notice is signed by N.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium.
