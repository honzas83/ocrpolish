> # Draft Agenda for the Ottawa NPG Meeting
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for a Ministerial session of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) of NATO, to be held at the Centennial Conference Centre in Ottawa, Canada, on October 29th and 30th, 1970. The agenda items include the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, political guidelines for the initial tactical use of nuclear weapons, and special political guidelines for the possible use of atomic demolition munitions. The document is accompanied by a note from the Chairman, H. B. Seim, based in Brussels, Belgium, stating that the draft will be discussed at the Staff Group level on September 16th, 1970. The agenda specifies discussion leaders for certain topics, such as Germany and the United States for theatre nuclear strike forces and Italy for atomic demolition munitions, and further includes sections for any other business, the future work programme, an agreed minute, and a final communique.
