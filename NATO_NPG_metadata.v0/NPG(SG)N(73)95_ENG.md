> # Notes of Meetings Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on 17th September and 18th September, 1973
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held meetings on September 17 and 18, 1973, at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The group discussed the distribution of Working Papers NPG/WP(73)8, NPG/WP(73)9, and NPG/WP(73)10 regarding ADM Guidelines and Consultation Procedures and Facilities. The group also reviewed a draft advance press release and requested that nations indicate their acceptance of the text and timing of its release by October 19, 1973. Regarding early combined use, the group took note of the second revision of a draft report by Permanent Representatives and agreed to circulate the amended text as an NPG Working Paper for a meeting on October 4, 1973. The group discussed scheduling a meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives for October 18, 1973, to address business from the previous meeting and allow non-participating countries to express views on the Ministerial agenda. Additionally, the group confirmed a meeting for September 28, 1973, where the Chairman would provide a general review of the NPG's mission and activities, open to members of delegations and NATO Military Authorities. Finally, the group agreed to hold its next meeting on September 24, 1973.

MEETING OF NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES
3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) discussed a question raised by the Chairman whether a meeting of the NPG
Permanent Representatives should be scheduled towards the end of October in
order to complete any business arising from the meeting on 4th October and to
enable countries not participating in the Ministerial Meeting to express views
on subjects on the Ministerial agenda;

(b) agreed that, if required, this meeting should be held on 18th October, 1973.
REVIEW OF NPG ACTIVITIES
4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) confirmed its decision to hold a meeting on Friday, 28th September, 1973 at
10.15 a.m. at which the Chairman would give a general review of the NPG's
mission and activities;

(b) in view of the general interest of this briefing, agreed that the meeting
would be open to members of delegations and the NATO Military Authorities who
were not members of the NPG Staff Group but who had a need to know about the
activities of the NPG.

NEXT MEETING
5. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 24th September, 1973 at 3 p.m.
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
