> [!abstract] 
> # Note by the Secretary Regarding SACEUR's Nuclear Planning
> 
> This document is a formal notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 5th February 1969. It serves as a correction to the document NPG(Staff Group)N(69)11, which was dated 31st January 1969. Specifically, the Secretary requests that the name 'Ruefle' be inserted after the word 'Captain' in the second line of the text on the cover sheet. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium, under the authority of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group.
