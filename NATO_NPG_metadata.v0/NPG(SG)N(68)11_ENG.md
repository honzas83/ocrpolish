> [!abstract] 
> # Note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal note from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting to be held on Monday, October 28, 1968, at 2:45 p.m. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss various matters that were raised during the meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Ambassadors which took place on Thursday, October 24, 1968. The note is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from the NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) will take place on
Monday, 28th October, 1968 at 2.45 p.m. It is proposed to discuss various
matters raised at the meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Ambassadors which
was held on Thursday, 24th October, 1968.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
OTAN/NATO
Brussels, 39
