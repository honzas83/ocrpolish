> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday, 3rd July, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on July 3, 1974. The group continued its examination of the draft study regarding the communication of NATO's intention to use nuclear weapons, noting general agreement on the procedure for preparing the Study, a Permanent Representatives' report, and a Summary of the Study. The Chairman was invited to prepare a revised version of the draft Study for re-examination on July 18. Additionally, the group took note that the Chairman intended to circulate the first draft of a Permanent Representatives' report on the Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR) Illustrative Air Defence Study. The meeting notes also record the cancellation of meetings scheduled for July 8 and July 15, and the confirmation of the next meeting date for July 18, 1974.

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday,
3rd July, 1974

COMMUNICATING NATO'S INTENTION TO USE NUCLEAR WEAPONS

1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) continued its examination of NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7 and took note of the
views expressed during the discussion of the form and content of the draft
Study;

(b) noted general agreement with the procedure to be adopted (preparation of the
Study by the Staff Group followed by the preparation of a Permanent
Representatives' report and probably a Summary of the Study);

(c) invited the Chairman to prepare a revised version of the draft Study, taking
into account the suggestions made during the discussions, and to circulate it in
time for detailed re-examination on 18th July.

DRAFT REPORT ON SACEUR'S ILLUSTRATIVE AIR DEFENCE STUDY

2. The STAFF GROUP:
took note that the Chairman intended to circulate the first draft of a Permanent
Representatives' report on SACEUR's Illustrative Air Defence Study next week.
