> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, dated December 10, 1968. It announces that the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group is scheduled for Wednesday, December 11, 1968, at 3:30 p.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to continue the discussion regarding ADM Time Factors. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium, under the auspices of NATO.
