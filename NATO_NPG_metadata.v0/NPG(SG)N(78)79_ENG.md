> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, November 27, 1978, at 11 a.m. and 2.30 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with additional time allocated for any other business. The notice references several previous documents, including USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126, NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)8, and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)76. The document was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 27th November,
1978 at 11 a.m. and 2.30 p.m.

The principal subjects to be considered will be:
(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1);
(b) any other business.
