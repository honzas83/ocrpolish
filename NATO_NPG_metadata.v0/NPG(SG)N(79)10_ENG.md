> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 19, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines and to make preparations for the Spring 1979 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The notice also includes a list of reference documents related to the General Political Guidelines and the Ministerial Meeting. The document was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th February,
1979 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1);

(b) preparations for the Spring 1979 NPG Ministerial Meeting(2);

(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
