> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group, dated 10th September 1969, announcing that the next meeting will take place at NATO Headquarters on Monday, 15th September 1969, at 3 p.m. The principal subjects of discussion will include the revised agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting scheduled for 11th and 12th November 1969, and the draft terms of reference for follow-on tactical use. The notice is signed by H.G. Wilson and references other documents, such as DN(74)18 and NPG(Staff Group)N(69)61.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document mentions the discussion of 'draft terms of reference for follow-on tactical use', which directly relates to the mechanisms and consultation for the use of tactical nuclear weapons in Europe.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group will take place at
the NATO Headquarters on Monday, 15th September, 1969, at 3 p.m.

The principal subjects of discussion will be :
(a) the revised agenda for the NPG Ministerial Meeting to be held on 11th and
12th November, 1969 (1);

(b) draft terms of reference for follow-on tactical use (2).
(Signed) by H.G. WILSON
