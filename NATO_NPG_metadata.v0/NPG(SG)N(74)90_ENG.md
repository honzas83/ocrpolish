> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, 12 September 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting, to continue discussions on the CNI Study and the draft Permanent Representatives' report on it, and to consider the CNI portions of the draft report on the Air Defence Illustrative Study. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

10th September, 1974
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(74)90
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 12th September,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting;
(b) to continue discussion of the CNI Study(1), and of the draft Permanent
Representatives' report(2) on it;

(c) to consider further the CNI portions of the draft report on the Air Defence
Illustrative Study(3).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)7(3rd revise), 11th September, 1974
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)10(Revised), 13th August, 1974
(3) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)8(Revised), 5th August, 1974
