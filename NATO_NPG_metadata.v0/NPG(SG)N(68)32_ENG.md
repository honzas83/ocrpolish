> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group. It announces that the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, January 7, 1969, at 10:00 a.m. Additionally, the notice mentions that at least five experts from capitals will be attending the meeting. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium, under the auspices of NATO.
