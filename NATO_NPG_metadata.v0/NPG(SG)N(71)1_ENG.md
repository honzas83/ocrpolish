> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, January 11, 1971, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives for discussion during the meeting will include a review of the Staff Group's tasks for the coming months, the continued examination of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)'s potential participation in Exercise WINTEX 71, and the appointment of note-takers for the Chairman of the Military Committee, the Supreme Allied Commander Europe (SACEUR), and the Supreme Allied Commander Atlantic (SACLANT). The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO 1110 Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 11th January,
1971 at 3.00 p.m.

The principal matters for discussion will be:
(a) review of Staff Group tasks for the coming months;
(b) continuation of examination of possible participation by the NPG (Staff
Group) in Exercise WINTEX 71(1).

(c) note-takers for the Chairman of the Military Committee, SACEUR and
SACLANT(2).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO 1110 Brussels.
