> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 110 Brussels, on Wednesday, 15th March, 1972
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on March 15, 1972, to address several key agenda items. The group noted the issuance of NPG Working Papers regarding the status and initiation of Phase II of Follow-on Use Studies, and noted that a working paper on Consultation Facilities and Procedures was ready for circulation. Regarding the Illustrative Study of Battlefield Use, the group reviewed an International Staff re-draft of Part III and agreed to further changes and re-drafting in light of the discussion. The group also decided that the Consultation Facilities working paper should be examined before final agreement on the Illustrative Study report. The group further reviewed and agreed to the wording of a draft advance press release for the NPG Spring Ministerial Meeting, suggesting it be sent to Capitals for review with a potential release date of May 12, 1972. Finally, the group scheduled its next meeting for March 17, 1972.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 110 Brussels, on Wednesday,

DOCUMENTATION
1. The STAFF GROUP
(a) noted that the following NPG Working Papers had been issued and could now be
sent to Capitals for examination with a view to consideration by Permanent
Representatives at their meeting to be held on 15th April:

(i) NIG/VP(72)2, 7th March, 1972
Status of Work on Follow-on Use Studies
(ii) NPC/VP(72)4, 14th March, 1972
Initiation of Phase II of Follow-on Use Studies
(b) noted that the Staff Group Working Paper on Consultation Facilities and
Procedures, (NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)7, 15th March, 1972), was ready and would be
circulated the next morning.

ILLUSTRATIVE STUDY OF BATTLEFIELD USE – INITIAL DRAFT REPORT
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who recalled the previous
discussions on the initial draft report on the Illustrative Study of Battlefield
Use,(1),(2), and introduced an International Staff re-draft of Part III(3); he
invited comments;

(1) NPC(Staff Group)WP(72)5, 2nd March, 1972
(2) NPC(Staff Group)N(72)28, 10th March, 1972
(3) NPC(Staff Group)N(72)30, 14th March, 1972
