> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 111C Brussels, on 2nd, 5th and 6th May, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a series of meetings in Brussels on May 2, 5, and 6, 1975, to coordinate administrative and strategic planning. The group confirmed a meeting of HPC Permanent Representatives on May 12, 1975, and agreed on an agenda covering the draft agenda for the HPC Ministerial Meeting in Monterey and observations on the Nunn amendment. The United States representative noted that a US paper on the Nunn amendment would likely not be circulated before the meeting. The Canadian representative emphasized the interest of non-participating NPG countries in matters discussed at meetings where they were not present. The group also reviewed a revised draft report on Phase II of the Work Programme on Follow-on Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons, agreeing to several changes in form and content. The International Staff was tasked with preparing a further revision of the Working Paper for discussion on May 14 and 15, with the goal of preparing a final NPG Working Paper for approval by Permanent Representatives on June 3. Finally, the group scheduled its next meetings for May 14 and 15, 1975.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO headquarters, 111C Brussels, on 2nd, 5th and
6th May, 1975

SECURITY

1. The STAFF GROUP:

took note that all persons present were cleared to the security level of the
matters to be discussed.

MEETING OF HPC PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES OF MONDA. 12TH MAY

2. The STAFF GROUP:

(a) confirmed that a meeting of HPC Permanent representatives on Monday, 12th
May at 5.30 p.m. was necessary;

(b) agreed that the Agenda for this meeting should cover the draft agenda for
the HPC Ministerial Meeting in Monterey(1), and the paper on observations on
MUNN(2).

AGENDA FOR HPC MINISTERIAL MEETING

3. The STAFF GROUP:

(a) took note of a statement by the United States representative who referred to
Item V of the Ministerial Agenda (discussion of MUNN amendment)

REPORT ON PHASE II OF THE WORK PROGRAMME

4. THE STAFF GROUP:

(c) took note of the remarks of the Chairman who introduced the revised
version(1) of the draft report on the Ad-Hoc Study Team report on Phase II of
the Work Programme on Follow-on Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons;

(b) examined the revised Working Paper during the course of this 2-day meeting
and agreed to a number of changes to its form and content;

(c) invited the International Staff, in the light of the views expressed, to
prepare with all speed a further revision of the Working Paper with a view to
re-opening the discussion at a meeting to be held on 14th May, and if necessary
on 15th May;

(d) expressed the hope that it would be possible, on 15th May, to prepare an NPG
Working Paper in both languages for examination in Capitals in time for
discussion and approval by Permanent Representatives at their meeting on 3rd
June.

NEXT MEETINGS

5. THE STAFF GROUP:

agreed to hold its next meetings at 2.30 p.m. on Wednesday, 14th May, 1975 and
Thursday, 15th May, 1975.
