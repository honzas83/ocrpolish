> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters on 22nd September, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters on 22nd September 1969. The group discussed the dates of future meetings for Permanent Representatives, noting that a proposed meeting on 25th September might be unproductive and requesting the Chairman to inform the Secretary General. They identified scheduling conflicts for the Danish Ambassador on 17th October and noted a meeting scheduled for 27th October. The agenda for October meetings was set to include the Anglo-German Report on Guidelines for Initial Tactical Use, Terms of Reference for Follow-on tactical Use, and Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces. Most delegations, except the United States, expressed a desire to provide national views on consultation prior to the November Ministerial meeting. Additionally, the Staff Group reviewed an explanatory statement regarding International Staff paper NPG(Staff Group)N(69)68 on further studies of the tactical use of nuclear weapons, focusing on the title, assumptions, and objectives. The International Staff was invited to prepare a new version of the paper by 25th September, including proposed arrangements for specific studies, responsibilities, and schedules. The next meeting of the Staff Group was scheduled for 29th September 1969.

Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters

DATES OF FUTURE MEETINGS AT AMBASSADORIAL LEVEL

1. The STAFF GROUP discussed the dates of future meetings of Permanent
Representatives and :

(a) noted that several representatives considered that the meeting of Permanent
Representatives to discuss consultation, proposed for Thursday, 25th September,
might be unproductive;

(b) requested the Chairman to draw the attention of the Secretary General to the
views expressed concerning the proposed meeting;

(c) noted that a meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives was scheduled for
Friday, 17th October, at 10.15 a.m., but that this date was not convenient for
the Danish Ambassador;

(d) noted that a meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives was scheduled for
Monday, 27th October, at a time not yet fixed;

(e) agreed that the agenda items for the meetings of the Permanent
Representatives in October should include :

- The Anglo-German Report (Guidelines for Initial Tactical Use)
- The Terms of Reference for Follow-on tactical Use
- Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces.
(f) noted that all Delegations, except the United States, desired an opportunity
to express national views on the subject of consultation at Ambassadorial level
prior to and in preparation for the Ministerial meeting in November.

FURTHER STUDIES ON THE TACTICAL USE OF NUCLEAR WEAPONS

2. The STAFF GROUP :
(a) took note of an explanatory statement by the Chairman regarding the latest
International Staff paper (1) on Further Studies;

(b) took note of the views expressed in the subsequent discussions, with
particular regard to :

- the title of the study
- assumptions and objectives (Part A)
- the overall outline of substantive work (Part B)
(c) invited the International Staff, in the light of the comments made, to
prepare a new version of the paper, if possible by 25th September, and to put
forward, if time permits some elements of a Part C regarding proposed
arrangements for the first specific studies, responsibilities and schedule.

NEXT MEETING
3. The STAFF GROUP :
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 29th September, 1969, at 2.45 p.m.
