> # Notice of the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, March 25, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss an initial draft of a Permanent Representatives' Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons, to examine a revised text of the draft report on the Military Committee Function in the Nuclear Consultation Process, and to examine a draft agenda for the NPG Spring Ministerial Meeting. Additionally, the meeting aims to continue preparations for the NPG Spring Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)30
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th March, 1974,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss an initial draft(1) of a possible Permanent Representatives'
Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military
Doctrine for The Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) to examine a revised text(3) of the draft report on the Military Committee
Function in the Nuclear Consultation Process;

(c) to examine a draft agenda(4) for the NPG Spring Ministerial Meeting;
(d) to continue preparations for the NPG Spring Ministerial Meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)5, 21st March, 1974
(2) NPG/Study/45, 8th March, 1974
(3) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)3(2nd Revise), 12th March, 1974
(4) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)4, 18th March, 1974
