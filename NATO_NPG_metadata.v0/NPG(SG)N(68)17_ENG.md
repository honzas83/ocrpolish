> # Note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> On October 30, 1968, the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a note to circulate draft documents following a meeting held on the same date. The documents being distributed include a Draft Report by the Nuclear Planning Group to the Nuclear Planning Committee (NDAC) and a Draft Agenda for the upcoming NDAC meeting scheduled for November 4, 1968. The Secretary specifies that if no objections are received by 4 p.m. on Tuesday, November 5, 1968, these documents will be considered final and circulated as official Nuclear Planning Committee documents. The note was signed by F. G. Jilsun and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

# Page 1

30th October, 1968

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Note by the Secretary

As agreed at the Nuclear Planning Group Staff Group meeting held on 30th
October, 1968, the following documents are being circulated immediately:

- Draft Report by the NG to the NDAC(1)
- Draft Agenda for the NDAC meeting on 4th November(2)

If, by 4 p.m. Tuesday, 5th November, 1968, no objections have been received,
these two documents will be considered as final and circulated as official NDAC
documents.

(Signed) F. "G. JILSUN

CTAN/N.A.T.C Brussels, 39.

(1) NG(Staff Group)NEF(66)2(2nd Revised)
(2) NG(Staff Group)NEF(66)2(revised)
