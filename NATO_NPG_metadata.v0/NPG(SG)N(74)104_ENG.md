> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, October 14, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss matters arising from the meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives held on October 10, and to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD

9th October, 1974
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(74)104
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 14th October,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss any matters which may have arisen as a result of the meeting of
NPG Permanent Representatives held on 10th October;

(b) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
