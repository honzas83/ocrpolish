> [!abstract] 
> # Meeting Schedule Up to Ottawa
> 
> This document provides a detailed meeting schedule and work timeline leading up to the Ottawa meeting. It specifies the dates and times for meetings of the Permanent Representatives and the NATO Staff Group, including briefings from SACEUR and STRIKEFORLANT. The work schedule is divided into five main areas: the Draft Concept, Provisional Guidelines, ADM Guidelines, the Agenda for Ottawa, and the Advance Press Release. For the Draft Concept, it details the distribution of a US-German draft paper and subsequent reviews. The Provisional Guidelines section outlines the reports from the NMA and the distribution of Danish input. The ADM Guidelines section lists the timing for discussions and the distribution of final documents by the International Staff (IS). The Agenda for Ottawa section describes the process for approval by 'silence procedure' and the distribution of the final agenda. Finally, the Advance Press Release section outlines thes distribution of draft and national approval processes. All meetings for Permanent Representatives are scheduled for 4 p.m., while Staff Group meetings are scheduled for 10.15 a.m. or 3 p.m.
