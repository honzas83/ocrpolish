> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a notice for its next meeting scheduled for Monday, October 1, 1973, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The purpose of the meeting is threefold: to continue the examination of draft reports prepared for the upcoming Ministerial meeting based on instructions from capitals, to continue discussions regarding a possible report on the Status of the Follow-on Use Work Programme, and to discuss the desirability of a second meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives on October 18, 1973. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several working papers, including NP(Staff Group)WP(73)6, NP(Staff Group)WP(73)8, NP(Staff Group)9, and NP(Staff Group)WP(73)10, NP(Staff Group)WP(73)11, and NP(Staff Group)WP(73)13.

27th September, 1973

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 1st October, 1973
at 3 p.m. The purpose of the meeting will be:

(a) to continue the examination of the draft reports(1) prepared for the next
Ministerial meeting, in the light of instructions received from capitals;

(b) to continue the discussion of a possible report on the Status of the Follow-
on Use Work Programme(2);

(c) to discuss the desirability of a second meeting of Nuclear Planning Group
Permanent Representatives to be held on Thursday, 18th October, 1973.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
