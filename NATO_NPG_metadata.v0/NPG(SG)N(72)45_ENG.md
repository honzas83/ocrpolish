> [!abstract] 
> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Tuesday, May 30, 1972, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to consider the results of the meeting of Nuclear Planning Group Ministers held on May 18 and 19, 1972, in Copenhagen, and to discuss the future work of the Staff Group. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
