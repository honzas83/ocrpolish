> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Friday, June 17, 1977, at 10:30 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the results of the Ottawa meeting, begin consideration of the next Ministerial meetings, and address any other business, including the date of the Autumn 1977 Ministerial meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 17th June, 1977
at 10.30 a.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the results of the Ottawa meeting(1);
(b) to begin consideration of the next Ministerial meetings;
(c) to discuss any other business including the date of the Autumn 1977
Ministerial meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPC/D(77)8, 9th June, 1977 (Agreed Minute)
