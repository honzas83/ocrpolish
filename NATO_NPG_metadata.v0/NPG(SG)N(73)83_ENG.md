> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled a meeting for September 4, 1973, at 3 p.m. to continue the preparation of reports for the Ministerial Meeting. The agenda for the meeting includes completing work on a draft report regarding consultation procedures and facilities, as well as continuing discussions on a revised draft report concerning early combined use. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous working papers and notices, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)7, NPG(Staff Group)N(73)79, NPG(Staff Group)N(73)82, and NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)10.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 4th September,
1973 at 3 p.m. The purpose of the meeting will be to continue preparation of
reports for the Ministerial Meeting, and in particular :

(a) to complete work on a draft report (1) on consultation procedures and
facilities;

(b) to continue discussion of a revised draft report (2) on early combined use.
