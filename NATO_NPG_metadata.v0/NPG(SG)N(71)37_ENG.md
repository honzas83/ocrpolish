> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Friday, March 19, 1971, at 10:15 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss Illustrative Studies and Release Requests. The notice is signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references two specific studies, NPG/Study(71)4 and NPG/Study(71)5.
