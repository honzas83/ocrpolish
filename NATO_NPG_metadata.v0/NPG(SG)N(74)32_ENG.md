> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a meeting scheduled for Thursday, March 28, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Room I-334. The primary objectives of the meeting are to examine a revised text of the draft report on the Military Committee Function in the Nuclear Consultation Process and to discuss an initial draft of a Permanent Representatives' Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons. Additionally, the meeting aims to finalize the date for the next meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives. The notice was signed by N.W.G. Sherwen, Acting Secretary, in Brussels.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 28th March,
1974, at 3 p.m. in Room I-334. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to examine a revised text(1) of the draft report on the Military Committee
Function in the Nuclear Consultation Process;

(b) to discuss an initial draft(2) of a possible Permanent Representatives'
Report on the Study on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military
Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons(3);

(c) to finalize the date of the next meeting of NPG Permanent
Representatives(4).

(Signed) N.W.G. SHERWEN
(Acting Secretary)
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)3(3rd Revision), 26th March, 1974
(2) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)5, 21st March, 1974
(3) NPG/Study/45, 8th March, 1974
(4) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)34, 26th March, 1974
