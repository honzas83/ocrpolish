> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) notifying members of the next meeting scheduled for Monday, December 16, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss matters arising from the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting held on December 10, and to hear initial observations and comments on the substance of the Phase II Report, including suggestions for its processing. Additionally, the meeting will address future work. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 16th December,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss matters arising from the NPG Ministerial Meeting held on 10th
December;

(b) to hear initial observations and comments on the substance of the Phase II
Report(1) and suggestions on procedures for its processing;

(c) future work.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
