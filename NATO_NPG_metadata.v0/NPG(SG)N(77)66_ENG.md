> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Wednesday, September 7, 1977, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the Study of Maritime Nuclear Warfare in the NATO area, as detailed in the reference document NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)8(Revised). The meeting will also address any other business. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
2nd September, 1977
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(77)66
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 7th September,
1977 at 3.00 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be: (a) to discuss
the Study of Maritime Nuclear Warfare in the NATO area(1); (b) to consider any
other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)8(Revised), 26th August, 1977
