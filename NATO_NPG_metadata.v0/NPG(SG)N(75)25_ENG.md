> # NPG(Staff Group)N(75)25
> 
> This document records the proceedings of the NATO Staff Group meeting. The group discussed the Draft Advance Press Release, noting the deadline for approval by May 28, 1975, and the United States representative's need for instructions from his authorities. The group also reviewed observations on WINTEX 75, noting a first draft report by the Permanent Representatives on nuclear aspects of the nuclear exercise, and suggesting that this report might lead to a revision of the Provisional Political Guidelines. The meeting also touched upon the future work programme, noting that a planning timetable for 1975 would be circulated. Additionally, the group addressed arrangements for the NPC Ministerial Meeting in Monterey, including invitations to SACEUR and SACLANT, and the United States representative's request for accommodation requirements and details on personnel attending the Symposium at Annapolis. The document concludes by scheduling the next meeting of Permanent Representatives for March 25, 1975, and the next Staff Group meeting for April 7, 1975.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS-RELEASE

3. The STAFF GROUP:

(a) noted the remarks of the Chairman on the Draft Advance Press Release(1); he
said that the deadline for approval was 28th May, 1975;

(b) noted the remarks of the United States representative who said that he was
waiting instructions from his authorities regarding the most suitable release
date;

(c) took note of some suggestions regarding the text of the Release.

OBSERVATIONS ON WINTEX 75

4. The STAFF GROUP:

(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced a first draft of a
possible report by the Permanent Representatives on the nuclear aspects of
WINTEX 75(2);

(b) noted that the Military Committee had already held an informal discussion on
this draft and that its comments would be circulated;

(c) took note of the views expressed in the ensuing discussion and, in
particular, of a feeling in the Staff Group that this report might lead to a
revision or a unification of the Provisional Political Guidelines;

FUTURE WORK PROGRAMME

5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the remarks of the Chairman who said that the Staff Group's
paper(1) would make a good basis for the preparation of the Secretary General's
Future Work Programme (item VII in the draft agenda);

(b) noted that an updated planning timetable for 1975 (on the lines of the annex
to NPC(Staff Group) N(74)119) would shortly be circulated(2).

ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE NPC MINISTERIAL MEETING

6. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note that the Chairman would prepare, for signature by the Secretary
General, the customary invitations to SACEUR and SACLANT to the Spring NPC
Ministerial Meeting;

(b) took note of the remarks of the United States representative who requested
that delegations should urgently reply to his request to know their
accommodation requirements in Monterey;

(c) took note of the further remarks of the United States representative who
said that his authorities would like to know the names and functions of those
persons who would have to leave Monterey immediately after the NPC meeting
because they were taking part in the Symposium at Annapolis;

(d) the United States representative also said that he hoped shortly to be able
to give some details concerning visits and activities during and before the
Monterey meeting.
