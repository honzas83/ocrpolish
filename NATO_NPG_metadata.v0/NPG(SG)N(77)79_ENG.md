> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, November 14, 1977, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft terms of reference for the Study of Maritime Nuclear Warfare in the NATO Area and to consider the revised text of the draft report to the Defence Planning Committee on Theatre Nuclear Force Modernization. The notice also allows for the consideration of any other matters. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
>

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL

10th November, 1977

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(77)79

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 14th November,
1977 at 3.00 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the draft terms of reference for the Study of Maritime Nuclear
Warfare in the NATO Area(1);

(b) to consider the revised text(2) of the draft report to the Defence Planning
Committee on Theatre Nuclear Force Modernization;

(c) to consider any other matters.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)11, 7th November, 1977
(2) NPG(Staff Group)IP(77)10(Revised), 7th November, 1977 and Addendum dated 9th
November, 1977
