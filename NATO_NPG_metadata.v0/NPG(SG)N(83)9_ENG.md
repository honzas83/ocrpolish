> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, March 28, 1983, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss the follow-up to the Ministerial Meeting and address any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references a previous document, NPG/D(83)2, dated March 24, 1983.
