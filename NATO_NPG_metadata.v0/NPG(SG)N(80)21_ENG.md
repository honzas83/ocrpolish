> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, April 28, 1980, at 2:15 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes preparations for the Spring 1980 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting, consideration of a draft report by Permanent Representatives on Nuclear Play in future WINTEX exercises, and a discussion of the draft of Section VIII of the General Political Guidelines. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It also references several working papers and documents, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(80)1(Revised), NPG(Staff Group)WP(80)나(80)2, and DPP/NPD/80/55.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 28th April, 1980
at 2.15 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Spring 1980 IPG Ministerial Meeting(1);
(b) consideration of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on Nuclear
Play in Future WINTEX Exercises(2);

(c) discussion of the draft of Section VIII of the General Political
Guidelines(3);

(d) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

iiATO, 1110 Brussels.
