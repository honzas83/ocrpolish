> # Date of the Next NPG Permanent Representatives' Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated March 26, 1974, regarding the rescheduling of the NPG Permanent Representatives' meeting. Initially, a suggestion was made to move the meeting from May 13th to May 20th, but a review of the Secretary General's calendar indicated that the week of May 20th is impractical. Consequently, the Staff Group is asked to consider alternative dates of Thursday afternoon, May 16th, or Friday afternoon, May 17th, at 3:30 p.m. The author notes that while SHAPEX is scheduled for those days, the sessions are expected to be held in the morning, thus avoiding conflict with an afternoon meeting at NATO Headquarters. The matter is proposed to be resolved at the upcoming Staff Group meeting on March 28th. The document was signed by H.B. Seim in Brussels.

NPG(Staff Group)N(74)34
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DATE OF THE NEXT NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES' MEETING
Note by the Chairman
At its meeting on Monday, 25th March, the Staff Group discussed a suggestion to
reschedule the NPG Permanent Representatives' meeting on Monday, 13th May at
3:30 p.m.

Noting that a number of Ambassadors would not be able to attend a meeting on
that date, the Staff Group agreed to look at the possibility of holding the
meeting on Monday, 20th May.

2. A subsequent review of the Secretary General's calendar indicates that it
would be impractical to schedule the meeting during that week. It is suggested,
therefore, that the Staff Group consider the possibility of scheduling the
meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives on Thursday afternoon, 16th May or
Friday afternoon, 17th May at 3:30 p.m. Although SHAPEX is scheduled for both of
these days (as well as for Wednesday, 15th May), it is understood that the
SHAPEX sessions would be held in the morning and would not necessarily conflict
with an afternoon meeting at NATO Headquarters.

3. It is proposed that this question be resolved at the Staff Group meeting on
Thursday, 28th March.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.
1110 Brussels.
