> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Meeting Notice
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, announcing a meeting scheduled for Monday, June 16, 1979, at 5 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines and specifically address Section IV regarding the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice includes several references to previous documents, including US/NATO DPD/CUT/HS/78-126 and various IPG (Staff Group) documents from 1978 and 1979. The meeting is organized under the North Atlantic Council's framework in Brussels, Belgium.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, '6th June, 1979
at 5 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) any other business.
