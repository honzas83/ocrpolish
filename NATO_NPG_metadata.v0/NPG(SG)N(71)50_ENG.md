> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meetings
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, April 26, 1971, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue preparations for the Mittenwald Meeting, which includes discussing possible actions regarding the Military Committee's report on the role of theatre strike forces. Additionally, the meeting will address arrangements for a visit by the Staff Group to SHAPE (Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe). The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETINGS
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 26th April, 1971
at 3.00 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be :

(a) to continue the preparations for the Mittenwald Meeting, including a
discussion of possible action (1) to be taken on the report by the Military
Committee on the rôle of theatre strike forces;

(b) to discuss arrangements for a visit by the Staff Group to SHAPE.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
