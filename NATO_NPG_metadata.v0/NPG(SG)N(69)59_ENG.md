> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) proposing an informal meeting on Friday, August 29, 1969, at 10 a.m. The primary purpose of the meeting is to discuss the draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) ministerial meeting scheduled for November 11 and 12 in Washington, as well as the outline draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Advisory Committee (NDAC) meeting scheduled for December 4 in Brussels. The NPG ministerial meeting agenda includes topics such as the tactical use of nuclear weapons, strategic use of nuclear weapons, and consultation on the use of nuclear weapons, with reports from the United Kingdom, Germany, the United States, and Belgium. The NDAC ministerial meeting agenda includes a progress report by the Nuclear Planning Group, tentative political guidelines, consultation on the use of nuclear weapons, and NPG membership. Members are also requested to be prepared to discuss possible dates for meetings at the Permanent Representative level in September and October.

Note by the Secretary

(attached as Annex B).
