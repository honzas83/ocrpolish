> # Draft Agenda for the Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group in Ministerial Session
> 
> This document is a draft agenda for a meeting of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) in Ministerial Session to be held at the Centennial Conference Centre in Ottawa, Canada, on October 29th and 30th, 1970. The agenda items include a briefing on the balance of strategic forces, led by the United States, and a discussion on the role of theatre nuclear strike forces. It also covers political guidelines for the initial tactical use of nuclear weapons, featuring reports from Permanent Representatives and NATO military authorities, as well as a document from Denmark. The agenda further includes a discussion on atomic demolition munitions led by Italy, follow-on studies, any other business, and the future work programme, concluding with an agreed minute and a communique. The meeting's discussion leaders for various sessions are assigned to specific member states, including Germany, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
