> # Notes of a Meeting Held on Monday, 8th November, 1971
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group met on November 8, 1971, to coordinate reports and agendas for upcoming high-level meetings. The group discussed and agreed upon changes to a draft report for the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee (NDAC) meeting scheduled for December 8, 1971, and decided to circulate the document as a Working Paper via the 'silence' procedure. They also reviewed options for reporting to the Defence Policy Committee (DPC) Ministers in December regarding the nuclear aspects of AD 70, agreeing that a report should be submitted via theNDAC. Furthermore, the group addressed follow-up actions from the Brussels NPG Ministerial Meeting, specifically approving the distribution of certain documents and follow-on use studies to Portugal. The meeting concluded by scheduling the next Staff Group meeting for November 15, 1971, in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held on Monday, 8th November, 1971
NPG REPORT TO THE NDAC
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) discussed the draft of a Report by the Nuclear Planning Group(1) to be
placed on the Agenda for the meeting of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee on
Wednesday, 8th December, 1971, and agreed to a number of changes to the text;

(b) agreed to circulate the document to capitals as a Working Paper to be
approved by the "silence" procedure with the deadline date of Monday, 22nd
November;

(c) agreed that, if comment or objections were received, they should be
discussed at the Staff Group meeting on 22nd November and, in the case where no
accord was reached, at a meeting of the NPG Permanent Representatives that might
be rescheduled for Wednesday afternoon, 24th November;

(d) agreed that the final NDAC Agenda document should be circulated during the
week of 22nd November.

AD 70 FOLLOW-UP ACTION – NUCLEAR ASPECTS
3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) reviewed the draft of a report(1) to be submitted through the NDAC to DPC
Ministers at their meeting in December; and agreed to some minor changes to the

circulated as an NDAC/DPC document during the week of 22nd November.

FOLLOW-UP ACTION TO THE BRUSSELS MINISTERIAL MEETING
4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) in the context of an initial discussion of follow-up action to the Brussels
NPG Ministerial Meeting(2), approved distribution to Portugal of the documents
mentioned in paragraphs 12 and 13 of the referenced Staff Group Notice;

(b) agreed, in addition, to distribute to Portugal copies of comments on the
follow-on use studies received from the NATO Military Authorities(3);

(c) deferred discussion of the remaining items to a later meeting.
