> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled a meeting for August 31, 1973, at 10 a.m. to continue the preparation of reports for the Ministerial Meeting. The primary objectives of the meeting are to hold a preliminary discussion of a draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial Meeting in The Hague and to consider the first draft of a report regarding the advantages and disadvantages of the early combined use of conventional and nuclear capabilities. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references two working papers, NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)11 and NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)10.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 31st August, 1973
at 10 a.m. The purpose of the meeting will be to continue preparation of reports
for the Ministerial Meeting, and in particular:

(a) to hold a preliminary discussion of a draft agenda(1) for the NP G
Ministerial Meeting in The Hague;

(b) to consider the first draft(2) of a possible report on the advantages and
disadvantages of early combined use of conventional and nuclear capabilities.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
110 Brussels.
