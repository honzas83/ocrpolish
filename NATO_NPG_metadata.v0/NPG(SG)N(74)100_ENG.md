> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Monday, September 30, 1974, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on the future work programme, finalize the draft Permanent Representatives' report on the CNI Study, and discuss the draft advance press release. Additionally, the meeting will focus on continued preparations for the Autumn 1974 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels.

ORIGINAL: ENGLISH 26th September, 1974
NOTICE NGP(Staff Group)N(74)100
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 30th September,
1974, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue discussion of the future work programme(1)(2);
(b) to finalize, if necessary, the draft Permanent Representatives' report(3) on
the CNI Study;

(c) to discuss the draft advance press release(4);
(d) to continue preparations for the Autumn 1974 NPG Ministerial Meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
----
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)95, 20th September, 1974
(2) Amended paragraphs will be circulated at the meeting
(3) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)10(Revised), amended version
(4) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)11, 26th September, 1974
