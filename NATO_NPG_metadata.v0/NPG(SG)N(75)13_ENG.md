> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary to the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of their next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, February 3, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the session is to discuss the future work programme of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG). The notice references previous documents NPG(Staff Group)N(74)119 and PO/75/5. The document was issued by N.H.G. Wilson in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 3rd February,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to discuss the NPG
future work programme(1).

(Signed) N.H.G. WILSON

NATC, 1110 Brussels.
