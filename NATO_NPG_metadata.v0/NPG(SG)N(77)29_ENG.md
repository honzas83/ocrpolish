> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Wednesday, April 6, 1977, at 10 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on observations regarding Exercise VINTEX 77, to discuss the draft agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting in June, and to address any other business. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels. It references the document NPG(Staff Group)WP(77)3(Revised), dated April 1, 1977.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 6th April,
1977 at 10 a.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion on the observations
on Exercise VINTEX 77(1),
(b) to discuss the draft agenda for the NPG Ministerial
meeting in June,
(c) to address any other business.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
