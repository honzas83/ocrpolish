> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Tuesday, 4th September, 1973
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group held a meeting on September 4, 1973, at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting began with membership updates, welcoming Mr. Woodworth of the United States Delegation and noting the upcoming arrival of Mr. Sunger from the Turkish Delegation. The group discussed the revised version of a report on the status of implementation of the Special ADM Guidelines, incorporating comments from the United States representative and agreeing to circulate the amended text as a Working Paper for Permanent Representatives. Additionally, the the group reviewed a revised version of the draft report on consultation procedures and facilities, specifically focusing on message handling and identification, and decided to circulate the basic Working Paper and Annex as separate NPG Working Papers. Finally, the meeting addressed a revised report on early combined use, focusing on the implications of its definition, and agreed to resume this discussion at the next meeting.

MEMBERSHIP
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) welcomed the new member of the United States Delegation, Mr. Woodworth;
(b) noted a statement by the Chairman that he had been informed by the Turkish
Delegation that the new Turkish representative, Mr. Sunger, would be taking up
his duties shortly.

IMPLEMENTATION OF SPECIAL ADI GUIDELINES
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) resumed discussion of the revised version of a possible report by NPG
Permanent Representatives on the status of implementation of the Special ADM
Guidelines(1);

(b) took note of the comments by the United States representative arising from
the review of the report carried out by his authorities and agreed to certain
changes to the text;

**CONSULTATION PROCEDURES AND FACILITIES**

3.  The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced a revised version(1)
of the portion of the draft report on consultation procedures and facilities(2)
dealing with message handling and identification;

(b) agreed to certain changes to the text;
(c) as previously agreed(3) invited the International Staff to circulate the
basic Working Paper and the Annex, as amended, as two separate NPG Working
Papers for consideration by NPG Permanent Representatives at their meeting on
4th October, 1973.

**EARLY COMBINED USE**

4.  The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who introduced a revised version of
a possible report by NPG Permanent Representatives on early combined use(4);

(b) took note of the points made in discussion which concentrated on the
implications of the definition of early combined use, and agreed to certain
changes to the text;

(c) agreed to resume discussion at its next meeting.
