> [!abstract] 
> # Postponement of Next Meeting of the Staff Group
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, issued on November 8, 1973, informing members that the meeting scheduled for Monday, November 12, is postponed until Monday, November 19, 1973, at 3 p.m. The postponement is due to conflicting meetings of the Council and the Defence Review Committee on November 12. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references a previous document, NPG(Staff Group)N(73)112, dated October 30, 1973.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

POSTPONEMENT OF NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP

Note by the Secretary

The Chairman has asked me to inform members of the NPG Staff Group that the
meeting scheduled(1) for Monday, 12th November, is postponed until Monday, 19th
November, 1973 at 3 p.m., in view of meetings of the Council and the Defence
Review Committee on 12th November.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(73)112, 30th October, 1973

1110 Brussels.
