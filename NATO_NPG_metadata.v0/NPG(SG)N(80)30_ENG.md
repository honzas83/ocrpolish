> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, June 9, 1980, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to conduct a follow-up to the Spring 1980 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial and to address any other business. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from the North Atlantic Council in Brussels, Belgium.
