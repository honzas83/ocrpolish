> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Friday, March 14, 1975, at 10 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to hold an initial discussion on the results of V'INTEX 75, to continue discussions on the preparation of a Permanent Representatives' report on the Phase II Report, and to review preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.C. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NFC(Staff Group)N(75)22

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 14th March, 1975,
at 10 a.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hold an initial discussion of the results of V'INTEX 75;

(b) to continue the discussion on the preparation of a Permanent
Representatives' report on the Phase II Report(1);

(c) to review preparations for the Spring NFG meeting.

(Signed) M.H.C. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPC/MP(75)9, 4th December, 1975
