> [!abstract] 
> # NDAC/NFG Membership Arrangements
> 
> This document, a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), outlines the principal documents that define the membership arrangements for the Nuclear Planning Group (NFG) and the Nuclear Planning Group Advisory Committee (NDAC). The Chairman, H.B. Seim, identifies three key documents: the NDAC/NFG Charter from December 7, 1966, an Agreed Minute of the Hague NPG Ministerial Meeting from April 22, 1968, which allows for the continuing participation of NFG alumni at the Permanent Representative and staff level, and an NDAC Decision Sheet from December 9, 1969, which describes current membership arrangements. All three referenced documents are classified as SECRET. The note was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
