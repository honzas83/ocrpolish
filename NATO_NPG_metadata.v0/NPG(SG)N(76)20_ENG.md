> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 8th March, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on March 8, 1976, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting covered several key agenda items. First, the group discussed Command, Control, and Communications (C3), deciding to renounce a planned briefing on the NATO Situation Centre due to regular crisis management courses. The group then addressed press arrangements for NPG ministerial meetings, with representatives from Canada and the United States emphasizing that host countries should have flexibility in managing press conferences to facilitate informed public discussion. The group also noted the problem of determining the place and date for the next NPG Ministerial meeting. Furthermore, the Staff Group reviewed and agreed to changes in the text regarding country request procedures for the Compilation paper on Nuclear Consultation Procedures. Finally, the group discussed CNI procedures for nuclear consultation, agreeing on the need for such procedures while noting that Belgium and Denmark reserved their positions. The meeting concluded with the group agreeing to hold its next meeting on March 15, 1976.

Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 8th
March, 1976

COMMAND, CONTROL AND COMMUNICATIONS
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the remarks of the Chairman who referred to the C3 (Command,
Control, Communications) briefings given on 3rd and 5th March and to the
proposal made at the previous meeting that the Staff Group should receive
another briefing on the NATO Situation Centre on 15th March, 1976;

(b) agreed that in the light of the regular crisis management courses, which
have most recently been announced to take place every six months (and which had
already been attended by several Staff Group members) the briefing on the NATO
Situation Centre could be renounced.

of the Staff Group held on 9th February, he had requested members to obtain
advice from their capitals concerning the handling of press conferences in

(d) noted general agreement with the views expressed.

NUCLEAR CONSULTATION – CNI PROCEDURES
