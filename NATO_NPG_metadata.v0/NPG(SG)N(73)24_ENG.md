> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 26, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary agenda items for discussion include the draft report on the Illustrative Study on demonstrative use and suggested changes, as well as the preliminary report on Exercise WINTEX 73. Additionally, the Secretary notes that a second meeting may be necessary and has provisionally reserved conference rooms for the afternoons of Wednesday, March 28, and Thursday, March 29, 1973. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 26th March, 1973,
at 3 p.m. The principal matters for discussion will be:

(a) the draft report(1) on the Illustrative Study on demonstrative use(2) and
suggested changes (3)(4);

(b) the preliminary report on Exercise WINTEX 73(5).

2. Members will recall that they agreed, at the last meeting, that it might
prove necessary to hold a second meeting next week. A conference room has
therefore been reserved provisionally for the afternoons of Wednesday, 28th
March, and Thursday, 29th March.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
