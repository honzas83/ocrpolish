> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Spring 1979 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document consists of a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) and a draft advance press release in both English and French. The note proposes that a press release be issued in Brussels on Friday, 20th April 1979, to announce the twenty-fifth Ministerial Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO. The meeting is scheduled to take place at Homestead Air Force Base in Florida on 24th and 25th April 1979, under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns. The Chairman requests that Staff Group members obtain national authority approval for the text and timing of the announcement and inform the Director of Nuclear Planning. The draft press release lists the participating countries as Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE SPRING 1979 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text, in English and French, of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NFG Ministerial meeting to be held at Homestead Air
Force Base in Florida on 24th and 25th April, 1979. The text closely follows the
language used in former press announcements prior to NFG Ministerial meetings.
As on past occasions, the release could be made a few days before the meeting
takes place; accordingly, it is suggested that an announcement be made in
Brussels on Friday, 20th April, 1979 at 4.00 p.m., local time, with simultaneous
additional release in the capitals of those participating countries which so
desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at a future meeting. Staff Group members of the countries
participating in the Ministerial meeting are requested after their initial
discussion to obtain their national authorities approval of the text of the
announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director, Nuclear Planning
accordingly. Information on whether national authorities plan to make a
simultaneous release in their capitals would also be appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER
1110 Brussels.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, the Netherlands,
Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom and the United States will hold its twenty-
fifth Ministerial Meeting at Homestead Air Force Base in Florida on 24th and
25th April, 1979, under the chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary
General of NATO.

PROJET DE COMMUNIQUE ANNONCANT LA REUNION

Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
de la Défense du Canada, des Etats-Unis, de l'Italie, le la Norvège, des Pays-
Bas, de la République fédérale d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie
tiendra sa vingt-cinquantième réunion au niveau ministériel les 24 et 25 avril
1979 à Homestead Air Force Base, Florida, sous la présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H.
Luns, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN.
