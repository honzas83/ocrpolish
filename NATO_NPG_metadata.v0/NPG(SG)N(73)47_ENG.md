> [!abstract] 
> # Time and Place of the Autumn 1973 NPG Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the Autumn 1973 NPG Ministerial Meeting. The Netherlands Authorities have invited the NPG Ministers to meet in The Hague. Three sets of proposed dates are provided: October 23rd-24th, October 30th-31st, or November 6th-7th. The Chairman, H.B. Seim, requests that Staff Group members from participating countries—including Canada, Germany, Greece, Italy, Norway, the United Kingdom, and the United States—contact their respective capitals to confirm acceptance of the invitation and provide instructions on preferred dates. The document originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

TIME AND PLACE OF THE AUTUMN 1973 NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

The Staff Group members are no doubt aware that the Netherlands Permanent
Representative has informed the other NPG Permanent Representatives of the
invitation of the Netherlands Authorities for the NPG Ministers to meet in the
Hague in the Autumn of 1973. With respect to timing, I have been informed that
the Netherlands Authorities suggest one of the following dates:

23rd – 24th October;

30th – 31st October;

or

6th – 7th November.

2. It is requested that Staff Group members of the countries who will be
participating in the Autumn 1973 meeting(1) contact their capitals to confirm
that the Netherlands invitation has been accepted by their Ministers and to
obtain instructions on the preferred dates for the meeting.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) Canada, Germany, Greece, Italy, Norway, the United Kingdom, the United
States.
