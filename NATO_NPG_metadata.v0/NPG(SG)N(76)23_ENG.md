> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 15th March, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on March 15, 1976, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The group discussed a draft report to the Nuclear Planning Group (NDAC) on NPG activities and decided to keep the paper on file without further processing. They also addressed the Country Request Procedures paper and the inclusion of country request procedurals in the compilation of nuclear consultation procedures, agreeing to reopen the discussion at the next meeting. Regarding nuclear consultation and CNI procedures, the group took note of a corrigendum to the working paper and agreed to several changes to the text, instructing the International Staff to prepare a new working paper in both official languages. The meeting also touched upon illustrative studies and the expectation of a paper on the omnibus presentation of illustrative studies. Finally, the group recalled the request from Ministers in Hamburg to provide a progress report on the Technology Study Team's findings, specifically regarding MIT findings, and scheduled their next meeting for April 5, 1976.

DRAFT REPORT TO THE NDAC ON NPG ACTIVITIES

discussion and of general agreement with the
form and content of the paper, whilst retaining
some reserves as to the actual text;
(c) agreed to keep this paper on the file but to
take no further action on it for the time being.
