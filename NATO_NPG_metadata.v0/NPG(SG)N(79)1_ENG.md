> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, January 15, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the next meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, as well as any other business. The notice references several previous documents, including USATATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126, NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)8, NPG(Staff Group)N(78)76, and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)81. The meeting is located in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th January,
1979 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1);
(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
