> # Briefing on Atomic Demolition Munitions
> 
> This document is a notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing a briefing on Atomic Demolition Munitions. The briefing is scheduled for Tuesday, February 25, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. in the Presentation Room at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The session is conducted by a team from CENTAG under the auspices of SACEUR. As agreed during the Staff Group meeting on February 12, members of the Group are encouraged to attend and may be accompanied by one additional person. The briefing is classified as ATOMAL, requiring security passes for all attendees. The Secretary will arrange these passes provided that the names of accompanying persons and confirmation of their ATOMAL clearance are provided in writing. Passes can be collected from Office No. 0119 in the Main Entrance Hall.
>
