> [!abstract] 
> # NPG Ministerial Meetings
> 
> This document, a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, serves as a detailed record of all Ministerial meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group from Spring 1967 to Autumn 1983. It includes a chronological list of 34 meetings, specifying the city and the host nation for each session. Additionally, the document provides a summary table counting the number of times each participating country has acted as host, hosting duties shared among the United States, Federal Republic of Germany, United Kingdom, United Kingdom, Netherlands, Canada, Canada, Belgium, Denmark, Italy, Turkey, Norway, and Portugal. It also notes the specific dates when Portugal, Luxembourg, and Spain joined the Ministerial level of participation. The document was issued by the NATO Secretary in Brussels, Belgium, on 25th November 1983.
