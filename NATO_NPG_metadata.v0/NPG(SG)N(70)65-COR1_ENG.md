> # Corrigendum to NPG(Staff Group)N(70)65: Meeting and Work Schedules
> 
> This document serves as a corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(70)65, dated 7th September 1970, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). It instructs the recipient to remove and destroy Page 2 of the original notice and replace it with the newly attached Page 2. The updated Page 2 provides a detailed meeting schedule up to the Ottawa meeting, specifying dates, times, and tentative agendas for both Permanent Representatives and the Staff Group. Agenda items include SACEUR Briefing, ADM Guidelines, Ottawa Agenda, Provisional Guidelines, Draft Concept, and STRIKEFORLANT Briefing. Meetings are scheduled from 14th September to 20th October 1970, with some meetings listed as 'if required' or 'if desired'.
