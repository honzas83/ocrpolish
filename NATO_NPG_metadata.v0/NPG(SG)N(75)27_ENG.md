> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, April 7, 1975, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss the Ministerial Agenda and the WINTEX 75 draft report. Additionally, the Chairman notes that discussions regarding the Phase II draft report and future Nuclear Planning Group work will be postponed to another meeting later in the week. The Chairman also mentions his participation in the WINTEX 75 Review at SHAPE on April 14, 1975.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 7th April, 1975
at 3.00 p.m.

2. The principal objects of this meeting will be the discussion of the
Ministerial Agenda and of the WINTEX 75 draft report. It is intended to discuss
the Phase II draft report and future NFG work at another meeting later in the
week. In this context I recall that I will be participating in the WINTEX 75
Review at SHAPE on Monday, 14th april, 1975.

(Signed) R.E. Shearer
1110 Brussels.
