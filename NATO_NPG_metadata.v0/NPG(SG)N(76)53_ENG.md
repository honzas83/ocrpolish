> # Preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on June 8, 1976, in Brussels to coordinate preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting. The group noted several documents regarding the Ministerial agenda, CNI procedures, and the future work programme, as well as the upcoming circulation of the draft Communiqué and Agreed Minute. Discussions focused on the submission of contributions on TNF, with updates provided by representatives from Germany and the United Kingdom, and the expectation of a United States paper on top-down control. Additionally, the group reviewed newly developed procedures in the field of nuclear consultation, debating whether the paper should be limited to scenario information for the Central Region or maintain a certain degree of complexity for realism. The United Kingdom representative suggested that Permanent Representatives should review the paper to ensure its substance is worthy of Ministerial attention. The meeting concluded with the plan for an informal briefing for representatives of countries not participating at the Ministerial level and the scheduling of the next meeting for June 21, 1976.
