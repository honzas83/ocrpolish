> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, January 22, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue the examination of the ACIANT Follow-on Study and to discuss a first draft of a possible report on it. Additionally, the meeting will focus on continuing discussions regarding matters likely to be examined at the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NP(Staff Group)N(73)5

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 22nd January,
1973, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to continue the examination of the ACIANT Follow-on Study and to discuss a
first draft(1) of a possible report on it;

(b) to continue the discussion of matters likely to be examined at the next
meeting of NPG Ministers.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NP(Staff Group)WP(73)1, 18th January, 1973
