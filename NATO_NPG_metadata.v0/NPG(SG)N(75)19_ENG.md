> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 3, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to hold a general discussion on the preparation of a Permanent Representatives' report on the Phase II Report, to review preparations for the Spring Nuclear Planning Committee meeting, and to discuss any other outstanding business. The notice was signed by F.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

# Page 1

NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
25th February, 1975
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)II(75)19
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 3rd March, 1975,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hold a general discussion on the preparation of a Permanent
Representatives' report on the Phase II Report(1);

(b) to review preparations for the Spring NFC meeting;
(c) to discuss any other outstanding business.
(Signed) F.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPC/A(75)5, 4th December, 1975
