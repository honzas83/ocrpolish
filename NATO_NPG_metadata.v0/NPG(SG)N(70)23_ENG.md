> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) notifying members of the next meeting scheduled for Monday, April 6, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary subjects for discussion during this meeting will include the draft agenda for the meeting of Ministers in Venice and the initial draft of the general statement on Airborne Alert and Missile Systems (ADMS). The note references previous documents NPG(Staff Group)N(70)14(Revised) and NPG(Staff Group)WP(70)1. The document originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
