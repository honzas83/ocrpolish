> # NPG Ministerial Meetings
> 
> This document, issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, provides a detailed record of all Ministerial meetings of the Nuclear Planning Group from 1957 to 1981. It includes a table listing 30 meetings, specifying the meeting number, the city/location and the host nation-state, the season and year of the1957 to 1981. It also includes a summary count of meetings hosted by each participating country as of January 12, 1982. The list identifies the United States, Federal Republic of Germany, and United Kingdom as the primary hosts, with others such as the Netherlands, Belgium, Canada, Denmark, Italy, Turkey, and Norway listing fewer hosts. It also notes that Greece, Portugal, and Luxembourg have not hosted any meetings. The document also mentions the first meeting with expanded membership in 1979 and the first ministerial level participation of Portugal and Luxembourg.
