> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, July 16, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to continue discussions on future Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) activities, specifically focusing on future meetings of NPG Permanent Representatives and operational concepts and doctrine. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references two other documents: NPG(Staff Group)N(73)62 and NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)8(Revised).
