> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Friday, December 15, 1978, at 10:30 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, as well as any other business. The notice references several previous documents, including USNATO/DPR/OUT/NS/78-126, NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)8, and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)76 and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)81. The document was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 15th December,
1978 at 10.30 a.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:
