> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Tuesday, 23rd July, 1974
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened on July 23, 1974, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to address several key administrative and documentation issues. The group discussed delays in the production of the Warsaw Pact Study and report (DPC/D(74)18) and noted the upcoming circulation of the draft agenda for the Autumn Meeting of NPG Ministers and the final report on WINTEX-7. Additionally, the group focused on the Communicating NATO's Intentions (CNI) study, deciding that the concluding paragraphs would serve as a summary and requesting a first tentative version of the Permanent Representatives' report. The meeting also reviewed a draft report by Permanent Representatives on the SHAPE Illustrative Study on Air Defence Use, agreeing to include considerations concerning the communication of NATO's intentions. The group concluded by scheduling its next meeting for July 29, 1974, and considering potential meetings in August.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Tuesday,
23rd July, 1974

DOCUMENTATION
1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the remarks of the Chairman who said that delays were being
experienced in the production of DPC/D(74)18 (Warsaw Pact Study and report)
which would now probably be circulated on 5th August; the silence procedure cut-
off date would be adjusted;

(b) noted that the following papers would be circulated in time for discussion
at the next meeting:

(i) NPQ(Staff Group)WP(74)9, 23rd July, 1974: Draft Agenda for the Autumn
Meeting of NPG Ministers;

(ii) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)80, 23rd July, 1974: concerning the final report on
WINTEX-7;

(c) noted that the Chairman intended to circulate the 2nd Revision of NFG(Staff
Group)WP(74)7 – the draft Study on Communicating NATO's Intentions (CNI) – not
later than Friday, 26th July.
