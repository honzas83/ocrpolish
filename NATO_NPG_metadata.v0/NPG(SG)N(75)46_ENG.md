> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of their next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Wednesday, May 23, 1975, at 10 a.m. in Brussels. The primary purpose of the meeting is to discuss final preparations for the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Committee Permanent Representatives, which is scheduled for June 3, 1975. The note is signed by H.H.G. Tilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Wednesday, 23rd May, 1975
at 10 a.m. to discuss final preparations for the meeting of NPC Permanent
Representatives to be held on 3rd June, 1975.

(Signed) H.H.G. TILSON
