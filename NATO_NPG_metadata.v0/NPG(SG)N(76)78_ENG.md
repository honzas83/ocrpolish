> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, September 13, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to hear comments from Dr. R.G. Schreffler, the first Chairman of the Staff Group, to undertake follow-on action on the Compilation paper, and to continue preparations for the Autumn Ministerial meeting. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

8th September, 1976
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)78
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 13th September,
1976 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hear comments by Dr. R.G. Schreffler, first Chairman of the Staff Group ;
(b) to undertake follow-on action on the Compilation paper(1);
(c) to continue preparations for the Autumn Ministerial meeting.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)IP(75)7(3rd Revise), 8th September, 1976
