> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, September 19, 1977, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss preparations for the Autumn Ministerial meeting and to consider any other business. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
