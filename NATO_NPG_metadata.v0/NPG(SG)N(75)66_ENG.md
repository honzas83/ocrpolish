> # Next Meeting and Visit to SHAPE
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming schedule for September 1975. The next meeting of the Staff Group is scheduled for Monday, September 8, 1975, at 3 p.m., with objectives including a briefing on satellites and theatre warfare by Dr. Kane of the TRW Corporation, continuing discussions on Phase III scope papers, and discussing nuclear release requests from NATO member countries. Additionally, the meeting will examine and approve the Autumn work schedule and dates for MFG Permanent representatives' meetings. The document also outlines the the visit to Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE) on Tuesday, September 9, 1975, including transportation arrangements via a provided bus from the UTC Press Entrance to the SHAPE Officers Club and back to NATO HQ. Members are reminded to bring their NATO passes and confirm their attendance with Colonel Julian by September 8.
>

Note by the Secretary
Next Meeting
1. The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 8th September,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be: (a) to hear a
briefing on satellites and theatre warfare by Dr. Kane (TRW Corporation); (b) to
continue the discussion of Phase III scope papers provided that the United
States paper is available; (c) to discuss initial observations on a short draft
paper(1) on nuclear release requests from NATO member countries; (d) to examine
and approve the Autumn work Schedule and dates for MFG Permanent
representatives' meetings(2).

Visit to SHAPE
2. The visit of the Staff Group to SHAPE will take place on Tuesday, 9th
September, 1975. SHAPE prefers that we arrive as a group and Staff Group members
are therefore requested, as far as is possible, to use the bus provided. Its
schedule will be as follows: (1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)8 (to be circulated) (2)
