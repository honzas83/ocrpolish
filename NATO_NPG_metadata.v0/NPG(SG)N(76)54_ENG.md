> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, June 21, 1976, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to review the results of the Ministerial meeting and to continue discussions on the presentational aspects of Newly Developed Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It also references a working paper, NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)2(Revised), dated May 31, 1976.
>

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD
NORTH ATLANTIC COUNCIL
16th June, 1976
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(76)54
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 21st June, 1976
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to review the results of the Ministerial meeting;
(b) to continue discussion of the presentational aspects of Newly Developed
Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation(1);

(c) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)2(Revised), 31st May, 1976
