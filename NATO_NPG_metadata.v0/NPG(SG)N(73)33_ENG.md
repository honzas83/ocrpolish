> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Thursday, April 26, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to note the adoption of the Draft Advance Press Release if no comments are received by the deadline, to discuss any other matters regarding the forthcoming meeting in Ankara, and to continue discussions on Future NPG Activity. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references previous documents NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)5(Revised) and NPG(Staff Group)N(73)28.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 26th April,
1973, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to note that the Draft Advance Press Release(1), if no comments are received
by 26th April, will be adopted;

(b) to discuss, if necessary, any other matters concerning the forthcoming
meeting in Ankara;

(c) to continue the discussion on Future NPG Activity(2).
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
