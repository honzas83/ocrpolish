> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting of the group. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, January 29, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue the examination of the ACLANT Follow-on Study and to discuss a revised draft of a possible report on the study. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 29th January,
1973, at 3 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to continue the
examination of the ACLANT Follow-on Study and to discuss a revised draft(1) of a
possible report on it.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)1(Revised), to be circulated
