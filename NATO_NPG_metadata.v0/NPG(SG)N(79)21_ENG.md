> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, May 21, 1979, at 5 p.m. The primary objective of the agenda is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with a specific focus on the initial discussion of Section VII, Consultation. The notice also allows for any other business to be considered. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents including US/ATC/DDP/OUT/'S/78-126 and various NPG(Staff Group) papers from 1978 and 1979.

NOTICE (Staff Group): (79)21

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 21st May, 1979 at
5 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including initial
discussion of Section VII, Consultation (draft to be circulated);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
