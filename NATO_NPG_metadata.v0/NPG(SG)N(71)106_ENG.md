> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Friday, September 24, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss suggestions arising from instructions received from capitals on recently issued Working Papers and to continue discussions on Working Papers concerning Unresolved Problems and the AD70 Follow-up. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(71)106
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 24th September,
1971 at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to discuss any further suggestions arising from instructions which members
may have received from capitals on the subject of the Working Papers recently
issued;

(b) in particular, to continue the discussion on the Working Papers on
Unresolved Problems(1), and on the AD70 Follow-up(2).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
