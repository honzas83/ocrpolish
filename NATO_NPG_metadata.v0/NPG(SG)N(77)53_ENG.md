> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Acting Secretary of the North Atlantic Council, issued on July 18, 1977, announcing the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Tuesday, July 26, 1977, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to examine the draft Permanent Representatives' report on the status of New Weapons Technology follow-up work, to discuss points arising from the Vice-Chairman's note on follow-up action to the Ottawa Ministerial Meeting, and to consider any other business. The notice is signed by N.L.C. Park and references documents NPG(Staff Group)VP(77)7 and NPG(Staff Group)N(77)50.

NPG(Staff Group)N(77)53
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place Tuesday, 26th July, 1977 at
3 p.m. The principal objects this meeting will be: (a) to examine the draft
Permanent Representatives' report(1) on the Status of New weapons Technology
Follow-up work; (b) to discuss any other points arising from the Vice-Chairman's
note(2) on follow-up action to the Ottawa Ministerial Meeting; (c) to consider
any other business.

(Signed) N.L.C. PARK
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)VP(77)7
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(77)50
