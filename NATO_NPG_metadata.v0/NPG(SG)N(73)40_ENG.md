> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice regarding the rescheduling of the next meeting to Wednesday, 23rd May, 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of this meeting is to discuss the results of the Ministerial meeting in Ankara. Additionally, a farewell lunch party is scheduled for the same day at 12.30 for 1 p.m. in the Salon des Ambassadeurs to honor Mr. S.R.C. Jones of the United Kingdom Delegation and several officers from SHAPE, including Group Captain V.N. Cramer, Colonel W. Altensburg, Lt.Colonel H.D. Wells, and Lt.Colonel R.C. Turner. Members of the Staff Group are requested to confirm their attendance for the luncheon by 4 p.m. on Tuesday, 22nd May.

# Page 1

9th May, 1975
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(73)40
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
Due to unforeseen complications, the next meeting of the Staff Group will take
place on Wednesday, 23rd May, 1973 at 3 p.m. instead of on Monday, 21st May as
originally planned. The principal object of the meeting will be to discuss the
results of the Ministerial meeting in Ankara.

2. On the same day, Wednesday, 23rd May, there will be a lunch party in the
Salon des Ambassadeurs at 12.30 for 1 p.m. to say farewell to Mr. S.R.C. Jones,
United Kingdom Delegation, and to Group Captain V.N. Cramer, Colonel W.
Altensburg, Lt.Colonel H.D. Wells and Lt.Colonel R.C. Turner from SHAPE. Members
of the Staff Group are requested to inform me before 4 p.m. on Tuesday, 22nd
May, whether they intend to take part in this luncheon.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON.
1110 Brussels.
