> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 9th July, 1973
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group met on July 9, 1973, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to address several strategic and administrative matters. The meeting included a welcome for Major Warren G. Robbins of the United States Air Force Academy and congratulations to Mr. F. Spinelli on his promotion to Minister. The group reviewed documentation including corrigenda to operational concepts and reports on Dr. Kissinger's speeches and news conferences. Discussions focused on theising of operational concepts and doctrine, specifically the circulation of a new version of NPG(Staff Group)WP(73)8. The group also addressed consultation procedures and facilities, noting informal contacts between the International Staff, SHAPE, the IMS, and SACLAINT. There were updates on reports regarding early combined use and the Warsaw Pact's politico-military strategy and military doctrine, with a team including representatives from the Netherlands, Canada, Denmark, Germany, and Norway. Norway's role in the study on the Communication of NATO's Intentions was also discussed, with the United States suggesting more participants. The group touched upon the conceptual analysis of nuclear deterrence and the implementation of ADM Guidelines. Finally, the meeting concluded with administrative arrangements for the Autumn NPG Ministerial Meeting in The Hague and the scheduling of the next meeting for July 16, 1973.

WELCOME
1. The STAFF GROUP: warmly endorsed the welcome extended by the Chairman to
Major Warren G. Robbins, Political Science Instructor at the United States Air
Force Academy, Colorado, presently on attachment at SHAPE.

CONGRATULATIONS
2. The STAFF GROUP: heartily seconded the congratulations offered by the
Chairman to Mr. F. Spinelli on his attaining the rank of Minister.

to the following papers which had been, or were about to be circulated:

(a) Corrigendum No 2 to NPG/D(73)5, dated 5th July: This corrigendum contains a
number of changes proposed by SHAPE to their report on operational concepts and
doctrine, issued on 7th June;

(b) NPG(Staff Group)N(75)58, to be dated 4th July: This is the text of Dr.
Kissinger's speech given on 23rd April;
