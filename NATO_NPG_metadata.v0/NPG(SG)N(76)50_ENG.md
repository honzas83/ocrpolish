> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Tuesday, June 8, 1976, at 11 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to make final preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting and to further discuss the presentational aspects of Newly Developed Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references the document NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)2(Revised) and the archive code C-M(2008)0116(Inv).

NPG(Staff Group)N(76)50

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 8th June, 1976,
at 11 a.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to make final preparations for the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting,
(b) to hold a further discussion of the presentational aspects of Newly
Developed Procedures in the Field of Nuclear Consultation(1),

(c) any other business.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(76)2(Revised), 31st May, 1976
