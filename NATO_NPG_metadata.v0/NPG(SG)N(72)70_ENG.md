> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Tuesday, September 5, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft Ministerial agenda, finalize the working paper on Consultation, and examine and finalize the draft Status Report on Follow-on Studies. Additionally, the meeting will address other drafts if time permits. The notice also advises members that the Staff Group may need to meet again on Wednesday, September 6, and Thursday, September 7, 1972. The document is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Tuesday, 5th September,
1972, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss the draft Ministerial agenda(1);
(b) to finalise the working paper on Consultation(2);
(c) to examine and finalise if possible the draft Status Report(5) on Follow-on
Studies;

(d) to examine other drafts if available and time permitting.
2. Members should anticipate the probable need for the Staff Group to meet again
on Wednesday 6th September and/or Thursday 7th September.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
