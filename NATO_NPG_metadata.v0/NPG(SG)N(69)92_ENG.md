> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on 23rd December 1969, announcing that the next meeting will take place on Friday, 9th January 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary subject of discussion for this meeting will be further studies on follow-on tactical use, with reference to the working paper NPG(Staff Group)WP(69)11. The notice was signed by F.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Extended-Deterrence-and-Tactical-Nuclear-Sharing — The document explicitly mentions that the principal subject of discussion for the upcoming meeting will be 'further studies on follow-on tactical use', which refers to the tactical employment of nuclear weapons within the NATO alliance's shared strategic framework.
