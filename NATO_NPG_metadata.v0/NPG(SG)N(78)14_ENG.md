> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 27, 1978, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss preparations for the Spring 1978 Nuclear Planning Committee Ministerial Meeting, including a review of the Ministerial Agenda. The notice also allows for the discussion of any other business. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 27th February,
1978 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) preparations for the Spring, 1978 NPC Ministerial Meeting, including further
discussion of the Ministerial Agenda(1);

(b) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(78)1(Revised), 25th January, 1978
