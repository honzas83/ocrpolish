> # Notes of a Meeting Held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 4th July, 1977
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) convened on July 4, 1977, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels, Belgium, to address several key items. The group discussed the Vice-Chairman's note regarding actions required to implement decisions from the Ottawa Ministerial Meeting. Specifically, they requested the International Secretariat to draft a letter to the Military Committee regarding the further study of NIKE HERCULES, requesting the IMS Representative to check the distribution of the Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study, and tasking the International Secretariat and IMS to prepare a survey of past and ongoing NATO studies on new technology. The group also agreed that the Autumn Ministerial Meeting would take place in Bari, Italy, on October 11-12, 1977. Additionally, the Danish Member informed the group that Denmark would host the Spring 1978 Ministerial Meeting in Copenhagen between April 15 and May 15, 1978. Finally, the the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled their next meeting for July 26, 1977.

NPG(Staff Group)N(77)52

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held at the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 4th
July, 1977 at 3 p.m.

I. FOLLOW-UP TO THE OTTAWA MINISTERIAL MEETING

The STAFF GROUP:

(1) had an exchange of Views on "the Vice-Chairman's note in NPG(Staff
Group)N(77)50 summarizing and commenting on actions required to implement
decisions taken and recommendations made by NIG Ministers at the Ottawa meeting;

(2) with regard to paragraph 8 (NIKE HERCULES), requested the International
Secretariat to prepare the draft of a letter from the Secretary General to the
Chairman of the Military Committee, tasking the NATO Military Authorities with
the further study relating to NIKE HERCULES; Staff Group members who so desired
were invited by the Vice-Chairman to forward any contributions to this draft, as
they felt necessary;

(3) with regard to paragraph 11 (Maritime Nuclear Warfare Study), requested the
IMS Representative to ascertain whether this Study could be given advance
distribution;

(4) with regard to paragraph 12 (New Technology), requested the International
Secretariat, in collaboration with the IMS, to undertake steps in preparation of
the first draft of a survey and review of past and ongoing NATO studies and

II. DATE OF AUTUMN MINISTERIAL MEETING

The STAFF GROUP:

The STAFF GROUP:
