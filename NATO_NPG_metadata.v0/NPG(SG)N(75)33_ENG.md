> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Friday, April 25, 1975, at 10 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the third revision of the draft report on Exercise VINTEX 75, the revised version of the draft report by Permanent Representatives on the Phase II report, and preparations for the NPG Ministerial meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Willson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 25th April, 1975
at 10 a.m.

2. The principal objects of this meeting will be to discuss:
(a) the third revision of the draft report on Exercise VINTEX 75(1),
(b) the revised version of the draft report(2) by Permanent Representatives on
the Phase II report,

(c) preparations for the NPG Ministerial meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILLSON

1110 Brussels.
