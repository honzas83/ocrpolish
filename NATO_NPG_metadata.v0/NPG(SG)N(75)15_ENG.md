> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels on Monday, 3rd February, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group met on February 3, 1975, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels to address several administrative and strategic matters. The meeting began with a confirmation of security clearances for all attendees. The group welcomed new members, including representatives from Belgium, Denmark, and SACLANTREFEU. The a significant portion of the meeting was dedicated to the Nunn Amendment, where the group reviewed reports from the Military Authorities, SHAPE, and the Military Committee. Due to the Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe (SHAPE) report and SACEUR's evaluation being classified as ATOMAL, distribution was limited. The group agreed to hold an early meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives to determine how a report for the United States Secretary of Defense should be prepared and submitted. Additionally, the group discussed the upcoming NPG Spring Ministerial meeting to be held in the United States, with the United States representative being asked to provide further details on the date and place. The meeting concluded by scheduling a provisional date for the next Staff Group meeting on February 10, 1975, and confirming a meeting of Permanent Representatives on February 11, 1975.

report should address the broad lines of the problem without entering into too
much detail; it should also be considered whether the report should be
classified or unclassified;

(e) agreed, after discussion, that an early meeting of MTG Permanent
representatives on this matter was most desirable(2), and that members should
make every effort to obtain instructions from Capitals before the meeting.
