> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on March 12, 1974, announcing the next meeting scheduled for Thursday, March 14, 1974, at 2:30 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to conduct a preliminary discussion of a report produced by the Study Group on Warsaw Pact Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. The report under discussion is referenced as NPG/Study/45, dated March 8, 1974.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Thursday, 14th March,
1974, at 2.30 p.m. The principal object of this meeting will be to hold a
preliminary discussion of the report(1) by the Study Group on Warsaw Pact
Politico-Military Strategy and Military Doctrine for the Tactical Use of Nuclear
Weapons.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

(1) NPG/Study/45, 8th March, 1974
