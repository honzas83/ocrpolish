> # Revised Agenda for the Meeting of the Ministers of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee
> 
> This document is a revised agenda for the meeting of the Ministers of the Nuclear Defence Affairs Committee (NDAC) scheduled for December 3, 1969, at NATO headquarters in Brussels. The agenda includes a report by the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG), provisional political guidelines for the possible initial tactical use of nuclear weapons by NATO, and general guidelines for consultation procedures on the use of nuclear weapons. Additionally, the meeting will address Nuclear Planning Group membership and any other business, concluding with a communique. The document is accompanied by a note from the Secretary, D.H.C. Wilson, stating that the agenda will be considered final if no further changes are proposed by November 24, 1969.
>
