> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 12th July, 1976
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on July 12, 1976. The group took note of a briefing by SHAPE representatives regarding the updating of ROMEO procedures, which were set to become effective on October 1, 1976. The group also discussed preparations for the upcoming meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives, focusing on follow-up actions from the NPG-Ministerial Meeting held on June 14-15, 1976, and the importance of the Phase III Study. Additionally, it was noted that Annex A regarding NIKE HERCULES sites was not yet available. The United States representative announced that a meeting of the Phase III Study Team would take place on July 22-23, 1976, and recommended that a draft outline report on Phase III be sent to respective capitals. Finally, the Chairman suggested that further meetings of the Staff Group be cancelled unless the NIKE HERCULES paper became available unexpectedly.
