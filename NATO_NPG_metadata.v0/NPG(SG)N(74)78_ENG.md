> [!abstract] 
> # Farewell to Departing Members
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 19th July 1974. It informs members that an informal meeting will be held in Room I-334 at 5:15 p.m. on Wednesday, 24th July, to bid farewell to Colonel Maurice Minet, Mr. Sverre Skletnes, and Lieutenant Colonel Omno Oldigs. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
