> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(76)58
> 
> This document is a corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(76)58, dated 1st July, 1976. It serves to correct the date of the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) within the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO). The notice informs holders of the original document that the meeting will now take place on Wednesday, 7th July, 1976, at 3 p.m., rather than the previously scheduled Tuesday, 6th July. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
