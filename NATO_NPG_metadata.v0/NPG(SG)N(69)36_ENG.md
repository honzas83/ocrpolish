> # Notes of a Meeting Held at NATO Headquarters on Thursday, and Friday, 10th and 17th April, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held meetings at NATO Headquarters on April 10th and 17th, 1969, to discuss NATO's response to the initial tactical use of nuclear weapons by the Warsaw Pact. The group discussed the International Staff's document on Initial Use and requested a re-type of the document to facilitate further discussion. Additionally, the group noted a general agreement that the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives would take place on April 30th, 1969, pending confirmation from the Belgian Representative. The Staff Group also scheduled several upcoming meetings for April 14th, 15th, and 16th to complete their examination of the response paper and study the role of theatre nuclear strike forces.
