> # 25th Meeting of NPG Ministers - Transport of Classified Documents
> 
> This document is a notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the transport of classified papers for the 25th meeting of NPG ministers scheduled to take place in Florida. Mr. B. Tuccimei, the Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section, will serve as the courier, traveling from Saturday, 21st April to Thursday, 26th April 1979. Delegations are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and submit their classified papers by midday Friday, 20th April 1979. The notice specifies that papers should be packaged, clearly marked with the delegation's name or person and a serial number, and kept as light as possible. Furthermore, it explicitly instructs that unclassified material should not be burden the courier. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

25th MEETING OF NPG MINISTERS - TRANSPORT OF CLASSIFIED DOCUMENTS

Note by the Secretary

Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section (Tel. Ext.
2481) will carry classified papers for the meeting in Florida, leaving on
Saturday, 21st April and returning on Thursday, 26th April. Delegations wishing
to use the services of the Courier are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and to
send him their papers not later than midday Friday, 20th April, 1979.

2. Papers should be packaged and clearly marked with the name of the delegation
or person and a serial number. Packages should be as light as possible. Please
do NOT burden the Courier with unclassified material.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 1110 Brussels.
