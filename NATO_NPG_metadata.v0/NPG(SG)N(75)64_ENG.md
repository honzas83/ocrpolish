> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, September 1, 1975, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to hold an informal discussion on available Phase III scope papers, discuss the first draft of the Compilation of Nuclear Consultation procedures, and establish the procedure for preparing a paper on nuclear release requests from NATO member governments. Additionally, the meeting will address the Autumn work schedule and the requirements for Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives meetings. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(75)64
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 1st September,
1975, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to hold an informal discussion of available Phase III scope papers,
(b) to discuss the first draft(1) of the Compilation of Nuclear Consultation
procedures,

(c) to discuss the procedure for preparing a paper on nuclear release requests
from NATO member governments(2),

(d) to discuss the Autumn work schedule and requirements for NG Permanent
Representatives meetings.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(75)7, 20th August, 1975
(2) NPG/D(75)4; paragraph 4, 23rd July, 1975
