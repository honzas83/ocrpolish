> # Notes of a Meeting Held at NTO Headquarters on Monday, 17th March, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NTO Headquarters on Monday, 17th March, 1969. The primary focus of the discussion was the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, based on the working paper NPG(Staff Group)WP(69)4. The working group discussed the layout of the working paper, the presentation of issues as questions, the basic approach to the problem, and the need for a definition of theatre nuclear strike forces. Additionally, the Canadian and United Kingdom representatives offered to prepare a new outline paper for the next meeting. The staff group also noted an observation from the United States representative regarding anti-ballistic missiles, stating that while the Ambassador would not be making a statement on the matter at the meeting of Permanent Representatives on 18th March, the United States Delegation would be glad to forward questions to Washington. The group agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 24th March, 1969, at 2:45 p.m., with the principal item of discussion being theatre nuclear strike forces and the new outline paper.

Notes of a meeting held at NTO Headquarters on

THE ROLE OF THEATRE NUCLEAR STRIKE FORCES
Document : NPG(Staff Group)WP(69)4 dated 5th March, 1969.
1. The WORKING GROUP :
(a) discussed the lay-out of the working paper, with
particular reference to the need for background
information, and to whether this should appear
in the introductory chapters or in an annex;
(b) took note of the views of representatives with
regard to the presentation of issues in the
form of questions;
(c) noted the various views expressed concerning
the basic approach to the problem, and the depth
and detail required.
(d) noted the need for a definition of
theatre nuclear strike forces;
(e) accepted with appreciation the offer of the
Canadian and United Kingdom representatives
to prepare a new outline paper, hopefully in
time for the next meeting of the Staff Group;

2. The STAFF GROUP :

NEXT MEETING

3. The STAFF GROUP :
(a) agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 24th March, 1969, at 2.45 p.m.;
(b) agreed that the principal item of discussion should be Theatre Nuclear
Strike Forces and in particular the outline paper referred to above (paragraph
1(b)), if ready.
