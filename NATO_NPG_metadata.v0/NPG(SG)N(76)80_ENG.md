> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1976 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding a proposed advance press release for the twentieth Ministerial Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) in London on November 17 and 18, 1976. The Chairman, R.E. Shearer, suggests that the announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, November 12, 1976, at 4:00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is requested to discuss the draft text and timing of the release at its meeting on September 20. Members of participating countries are requested to obtain approval from their national authorities for the text and timing, and to inform the Director, Nuclear Planning, by October 15, 1976. The attached draft press release, provided in both English and French, identifies the participating ministers of Defence from Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, the Netherlands, Norway, Italy, Greece, the United Kingdom, and the United States, and notes that the meeting will be chaired by NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE AUTUMN 1976 MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Attached is the text, in English and French, of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting in London on 17th and 18th
November, 1976. The text closely follows the language used in former press
announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. As on past occasions, the
release could be made a few days before the meeting takes place; accordingly, it
is suggested that an announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, 12th November,
1976, at 4.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous additional release in the
capitals of those participating countries who so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 20th September. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Ministerial meeting are requested to obtain,
after their initial discussion, the approval of their national authorities for
the text of the announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director,
Nuclear Flaming, no later than Friday, 15th October, 1976. Information on
whether national authorities plan to make a simultaneous release in their
capitals would also be appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER

1110 Brussels.

ATTACHMENT to NPG(Staff Group)N(75)80

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

The Nuclear Flaming Group of NATO currently consisting of the ministers of
Defence of Canada, the Federal Republic of Germany, Greece, Italy, the
Netherlands, Norway, the United Kingdom and the United States, will hold its
twentieth Ministerial Meeting in London on 17th and 18th November, 1976, under
the chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

--------------------------------------------------------

PROJET DE COMMUNIQUE ANNONCANT LA REUNION

Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les ministres
de la Défense du Canada, des Etats-Unis, de la Grèce, de l'Italie, de la
Norvège, des Pays-Bas, de la République Fédérale d'Allemagne et du Royaume-Uni,
tiendra sa vingtième réunion au niveau ministériel les 17 et 18 novembre 1976 à
Londres, sous la présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secrétaire Général de
l'OTAN.
