> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, August 2, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The primary subject to be considered during this session will be 'Any Other Business'. The notice is signed by J.D. Martin and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
