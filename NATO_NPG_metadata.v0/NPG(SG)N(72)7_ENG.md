> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a notice announcing that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, January 24, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss an initial draft of a possible Permanent Representatives' report on the initiation of Phase II of the Follow-on Use Work Programme, as well as arrangements for a Staff Group visit to the United States. Additionally, the meeting will continue discussions on preparations for the Spring 1972 Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The document references two other documents: NFG(Staff Group)WP(72)1 and NFG(Staff Group)N(72)8.

MFG(Staff Group)N(72)7
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 24th January, at
3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be to discuss an initial draft
of a possible Permanent Representatives' report on initiation of Phase II of the
Follow-on Use Work Programme(1), arrangements for a Staff Group visit to the
United States(2), and to continue discussion of other steps to prepare for the
Spring 1972 NPG Ministerial Meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. Wilson
(1) NFG(Staff Group)WP(72)1, 20th January, 1972
(2) NFG(Staff Group)N(72)8, 20th January, 1972
