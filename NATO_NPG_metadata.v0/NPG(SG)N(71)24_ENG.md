> # Annex to NPG(Staff Group)N(71)24
> 
> The document is an annex to NPG(Staff Group)N(71)24, detailing the personnel requirements for several NATO member states. It lists current and future requirements for National Defence Advisory Committees (NDAC) non-ministerial and ministerial roles, as well as Nuclear Planning Group (NGP) non-ministerial and ministerial roles, and NGP/Study (Staff Group) positions. The member states listed include Belgium, Canada, Romania, Denmark, Germany, Greece, Italy, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The data is presented in tables showing the number of personnel required for each role category, and the number of personnel assigned to the umapped roles. Staffing levels are often marked as 'increase' or 'no change' in corresponding remarks. The document serves as a budgetary or organizational staffing requirement analysis for nuclear planning within the NATO alliance.
