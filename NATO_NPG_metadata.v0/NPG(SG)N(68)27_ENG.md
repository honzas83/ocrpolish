> [!abstract] 
> # Bibliography: MO-161 Soviet Bloc Strength and Capabilities
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of NATO, consists of a note by the Secretary and an annex containing a bibliography. The bibliography, specifically categorized under MO-161 Soviet Bloc Strength and Capabilities, lists several recommended readings on Soviet military strategy, including works by Herbert B. Dinerstein, Raymond L. Garthoff, and Thomas W. Wolfe. It also includes articles from the 'Survival' journal by authors such as Red Star. The document serves as a purpose of providing staff members with resources to better understand Soviet military policy and nuclear strategy during the Cold War era.
