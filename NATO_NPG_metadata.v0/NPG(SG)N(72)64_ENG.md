> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> The Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) has issued a notice regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, August 26, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to continue discussions on Consultation Procedures and Facilities, and to examine an initial draft by the International Staff on the United States' proposed revisions to the Concept Paper. Additionally, the meeting will address other matters raised at the previous session, subject to the availability of documents. The notice is signed by M.I.G. Wilson and originates from Brussels, Belgium. The document references several working papers and CTS papers, including NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)9, NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)13, PO/72/372, and DPC/D(70)59.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Monday, 26th August, 1972,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion on Consultation Procedures and Facilities(1);
(b) to examine an initial draft by the International Staff(2) on the subject of
the United States' proposed revisions(3) to the Concept Paper(4);

(c) to continue discussion of other matters raised at the last meeting, subject
to the availability of drafts and documents.

(Signed) M.I.G. WILSON
1110 Brussels
