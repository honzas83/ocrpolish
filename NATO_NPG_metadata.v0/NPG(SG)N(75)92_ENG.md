> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Wednesday, 22nd, and Friday, 24th October, 1975
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held meetings on October 22 and 24, 1975, at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. The primary focus of the first meeting was to resume discussions on the form and content of the draft report regarding arrangements for Phase III of the Follow-on Use Programme. The group agreed to a Corrigendum to be prepared by the International Staff for immediate circulation. During the second meeting on October 24, the group reached agreement on the text of the draft paper in light of the Corrigendum. The International Staff was then tasked with preparing an NPG Working Paper for examination and approval by NPC Permanent Representatives at their meeting on October 29, 1975. Additionally, the group discussed arrangements for a meeting in Hamburg, where the German representative provided details regarding ATOMAL clearance certificates, arrival and departure dates, and transport methods for delegations. The German representative also mentioned that authorities would assist in organizing cultural visits for delegation personnel. Finally, the group decided to cancel the meeting scheduled for October 27, 1975, and agreed that the Chairman would call for subsequent meetings as required.

SECURITY

1. The STAFF GROUP:
noted that all persons present were cleared to the security classification of
the matters to be discussed.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR PHASE III

Meeting held on Wednesday, 22nd October, 1975

2. The STAFF GROUP:

(a) resumed its discussions on the form and content of the draft report on the
arrangements for Phase III of the Follow-on Use Programme;

(b) took note of the views expressed and of growing agreement to the text of the
draft report;

(c) invited the International Staff, in the light of the views expressed, to
prepare a comprehensive Corrigendum for immediate circulation to members and
agreed to meet on Friday, 24th October, with a view to finalising the Working
Paper.

ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE MEETING IN HAMBURG
4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the German representative who made the following
points:

(i) group ATOMAL clearance certificates were still required from a number of
Delegations;

(ii) Delegations were requested to inform him not later than 31st October of the
dates of arrival and departure of their members and the method of transport;

(iii) Delegations arriving by official aircraft should give details of the type
of aircraft and its time of arrival;

(b) noted with appreciation the further remarks of the German representative who
said that his Authorities would be glad to help to organise cultural visits for
delegation personnel.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP
5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) agreed to cancel its meeting scheduled for Monday, 27th October, 1975;
(b) invited the Chairman to call for a following meeting as and when required.
