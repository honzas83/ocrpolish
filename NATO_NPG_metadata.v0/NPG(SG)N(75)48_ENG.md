> [!abstract] 
> # Transport of Classified Documents to California
> 
> This notice from the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) informs delegations that Mr. B. Tuccimei, the Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section, will be transporting classified papers from Brussels to California. Mr. Tuccimei is scheduled to depart on June 13, 1975, and is expected to return to Brussels on June 19, 1975. Delegations wishing to utilize this transport arrangement are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and submit their papers by the close of business on Thursday, June 12, 975, in a closed package clearly marked with the authorized person's name. The document emphasizes that packages should be kept as light as possible.

2nd June, 1975
NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(75)48
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
TRANSPORT OF CLASSIFIED DOCUMENTS TO CALIFORNIA
Note by the Secretary
Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section (Tel. ext.
2481), will carry classified papers from Brussels to California, departing 13th
June, and will return with them on 18th June, arriving Brussels 19th June.
Delegations wishing to take advantage of this arrangement are requested to
contact Mr. Tuccimei and to send him their papers not later than close of
business on Thursday, 12th June in a closed package clearly marked with the name
or names of the person(s) authorised to collect it. Pac:ages should be as light
as possible.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
