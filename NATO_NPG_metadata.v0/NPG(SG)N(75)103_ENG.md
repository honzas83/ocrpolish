> [!abstract] 
> # Agreed Press Release for the Hamburg NPG Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is an agreed press release regarding the eighteenth Ministerial Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) of NATO. The meeting, hosted by the Government of the Federal Republic of Germany, is scheduled to take place in Hamburg on January 21 and 22, 1976, under the chairmanship of NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns. The press release notes that the meeting was originally scheduled for November 20 and 21, 1975, but was postponed due to a change in the United States Secretary of Defence. The participating countries include Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The document specifies that the text should be released at 16:00 local Brussels time on December 8, 1975, at NATO Headquarters, with simultaneous releases planned for the Federal Republic of Germany, Turkey, and the United States.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
AGREED PRESS RELEASE FOR THE HAMBURG NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING

The following advance press release has been agreed among the seven countries
participating in the January NPG Ministerial meeting in Hamburg:

"The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the
United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the invitation of the Government
of the Federal Republic of Germany, hold its eighteenth Ministerial Meeting in
Hamburg, on 21st and 22nd January, 1976 under the Chairmanship of Mr. Joseph
M.A.H. LUNS, Secretary General of NATO. This meeting was originally scheduled
for 20th and 21st November, 1975 and had to be postponed because of the change
in the U.S. Secretary of Defence."

"Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les
Ministres de la Défense de la Belgique, du Danemark, des États-Unis, de
l'Italie, de la République Fédérale d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie
tiendra, à l'invitation du gouvernement de la République Fédérale d'Allemagne,
sa 18ème réunion au niveau ministériel les 21 et 22 janvier, 1976 à Hambour,
sous la présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. LUNS, Secrétaire Général de l'OTAN. Cette
réunion avait été prévue pour les 20 et 21 novembre, 1975 et avait dû être
adjournée à cause du changement du Secrétaire à la Défense des États-Unis."

2. Release of this text has been agreed for 16:00 local Brussels time on 8th
December, 1975 at NATO Headquarters. Simultaneous release is planned to be made
in the Federal Republic of Germany, Turkey and the United States.

(Signed) J.L. MENTZEL.
Deputy Chairman,
