> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, April 5, 1982, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the follow-up to the Colorado Springs Ministerial Meeting and to address any other business. Additionally, the notice informs Staff Group members that a reception in honor of Dr. Mentzel will be held in the Secretary General's dining room, next to the Salon des Ambassadeurs, starting at 5 p.m. following the meeting. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 5th April, 1982
at 2.45 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) follow-up to Colorado Springs Ministerial Meeting(1);
(b) any other business.
2. Staff Group members are reminded that after the meeting, a reception will be
held in honour of Dr. Mentzel in the Secretary General's dining room, next to
the Salon des Ambassadeurs, beginning at 5 p.m.
