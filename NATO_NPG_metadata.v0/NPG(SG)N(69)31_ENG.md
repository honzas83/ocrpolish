> [!abstract] 
> # Next Meetings of the Staff Group
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) detailing the schedule for upcoming meetings. As agreed during the meeting held on 31st March, the next meetings of the Staff Group are scheduled for Thursday, 3rd April 1969, at 10 a.m., Tuesday, 8th April 1969, at 2.45 p.m., and Thursday, 10th April 1969, at both 10 a.m. and 2.45 p.m. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
