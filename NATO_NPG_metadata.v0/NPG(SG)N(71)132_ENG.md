> [!abstract] 
> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Friday, December 3, 1971, at 10 a.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions regarding the preparation of illustrative studies and to consider the views of national authorities on the proposed dates for the Spring Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 3rd December,
1971, at 10 a.m. The principal objects of the meeting will be:

(a) to continue the discussion held on 15th November concerning the preparation
of illustrative studies(1);

(b) to take note of the views of national authorities on the proposed dates for
the Spring NPG Ministerial Meeting.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
