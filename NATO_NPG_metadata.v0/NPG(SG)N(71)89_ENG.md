> # Unresolved Problems Related to Tactical Nuclear Defence
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting on Friday, August 13, 1971, to address unresolved problems related to tactical nuclear defence. During the session, the Staff Group reviewed a draft report by the Permanent Representatives to Ministers and considered an alternative text proposed by the United Kingdom Representative. The group renewed their request for the Representative of the Military Committee to obtain views from Military Authorities on requirements for future nuclear weapons systems. Additionally, the International Staff was invited to prepare a revised draft of the report for circulation within a few days. The document concludes by noting that the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) was scheduled for Tuesday, August 17, 1971, at 3 p.m. in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

Notes of a meeting held on Friday, 13th August, 1971

UNRESOLVED PROBLEMS RELATED TO TACTICAL NUCLEAR DEFENCE

1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who recalled the discussion which
had taken place at the previous meeting of the Staff Group on Tuesday, 10th
August, on the first draft(1) of a report by the Permanent Representatives to
Ministers on the question of unresolved problems related to tactical nuclear
defence; recalled also that an alternative text had been proposed and handed out
by the United Kingdom Representative;

(b) continued their examination of this draft report, taking into account the
United Kingdom's proposed text, and the suggestions made during the further
discussion;

(c) renewed their request to the Representative of the Military Committee to
obtain from the Military Authorities their views on requirements for future
nuclear weapons systems;

(d) invited the International Staff, in the light of the views expressed during
the discussion to prepare a revised draft of the report, for circulation within
the next few days.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(71)80, 5th August, 1971
