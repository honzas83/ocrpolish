> [!abstract] 
> # Notice Regarding the Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on February 8, 1973. It informs members that the next meeting of the Staff Group, which was tentatively scheduled for Monday, February 12, was postponed until the following Monday, February 19, 1973, at 3 p.m. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
