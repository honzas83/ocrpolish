> [!abstract] 
> # Draft Agenda for the May 1971 Ministerial Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group
> 
> This document consists of a cover note from H. B. Seim and an annex containing a draft agenda for the meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) in Ministerial session to be held in Mittenwald on May 25th and 26th, 1971. The cover note proposes that the NPG Staff Group discuss the draft agenda on March 22nd to reach an agreement on a version to be forwarded to capitals. The draft agenda includes items on studies regarding the follow-on use of nuclear weapons, including status reports and regional studies on North Norway and Greece, and illustrative studies and sample release requests. It also covers the strategic use of nuclear weapons, featuring a US briefing on the balance of strategic forces and a concept for the role of Theatre Nuclear Strike Forces in the Allied Command Europe (ACE). The meeting's agenda concludes with any other business, the future work programme, an agreed minute, and a communique.

NOTICE NPG(Staff Group)N(71)18(Revised)
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT AGENDA FOR THE MAY 1971
MINISTERIAL MEETING
Attached is a revised draft of a possible agenda for the NPG Ministerial meeting
in May reflecting recent discussions. I propose that the Staff Group discuss the
draft at our meeting on Monday, 22nd March, with a view to reaching agreement on
a version that would be forwarded to capitals during next week. Delegations
would be requested to obtain national views in time to permit the Staff Group to
develop a final agenda at a meeting shortly after the Easter period, probably on
19th or 20th April.

(Signed) H. B. SEIM
NATO, 1110 Brussels.

one annex of one page.
