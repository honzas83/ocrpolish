> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> The Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) issued a notice for the next meeting scheduled for October 2, 1972, at 3 p.m. in Conference Room 4. The primary objectives of the meeting are to further examine Working Papers NPG/WP(72)8, 9(Revised), 10(Revised), 11(Revised), 14, and 15(Revised) in light of instructions from capitals. The meeting will also include a general discussion on the future of the Illustrative Study Programme and an examination of whether a Nuclear Directions and Control (NDAC) Ministerial Meeting should be held in December 1972.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP(STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place in Conference Room 4 on
Monday, 2nd October, 1972, at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will
be:

(a) a further examination of the Working Papers(1) in the light of instructions
received from capitals since the last meeting on Thursday, 28th September;

(b) a general discussion on the future of the Illustrative Study Programme;
(c) an examination of whether an NDAC Ministerial Meeting should be held in
December, 1972.

(signed) D.A.R. COOPER
(1) NPG/WP(72)8, 9(Revised), 10(Revised), 11(Revised), 14 and 15(Revised).
