> [!abstract] 
> # Notes of a Meeting Held at NATO Headquarters, Brussels, on Monday 2nd June, 1969
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at NATO Headquarters in Brussels on Monday, 2nd June 1969. The group discussed the scheduling of meetings in August, agreeing to plan their work to avoid meetings during that month. Additionally, the group reviewed revised draft terms of reference for the conduct of further analyses of the ADN problem. They suggested that specific contacts between S. CEIR and the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives should be moved from the terms of reference to the Secretary General's cover note to the Chairman of the Military Committee. The International Staff was invited to prepare a further revision of the paper based on these views. The meeting concluded with the agreement to hold the next meeting on Tuesday, 1st July 1969.

N TO CONFIDENTIAL NOTICE
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
Notes of a meeting held at NATO Headquarters, Brussels, 39 on Monday 2nd June,
1969

I. MEETINGS IN AUGUST
1. The STAFF GROUP :
(a) noted that a number of members of the Staff Group, including the Chairman,
would be present in August;

(b) agreed however to plan the work of the Staff Group with a view to avoiding
meetings in August.

II. FURTHER ANALYSES OF THE ADN PROBLEM
2. The STAFF GROUP :
(a) discussed the revised draft terms of reference (1) for the conduct of
further analyses of the ADN problem;

(b) suggested that paragraph 8 of DP/n(69)22(Revised) concerning S. CEIR’s
contacts with the NIG Permanent Representatives should not be included in the
terms of reference but taken up in the Secretary General’s cover note to the
Chairman of the Military Committee;

(c) invited the International Staff, in the light of the views expressed, to
prepare a further revision of the paper, for discussion at the next meeting of
the Staff Group.

III. NEXT MEETING
3. The STAFF GROUP :
agreed to hold its next meeting on Tuesday, 1st July at 2.45 p.m.
