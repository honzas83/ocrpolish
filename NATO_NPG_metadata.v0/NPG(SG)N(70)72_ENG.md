> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, September 28, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) meeting are to discuss three main items: a draft advance press release for the Ottawa meeting, an initial draft of a Permanent Representatives' Report on Provisional Political Guidelines, and ADM Guidelines. The notice is signed by M.I.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium.
