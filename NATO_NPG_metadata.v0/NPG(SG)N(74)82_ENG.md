> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, July 29, 1974, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss a draft agenda for the Autumn Meeting of NPG Ministers and to review a note by the Chairman concerning the final WINTEX 73 reports. The notice was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from the North Atlantic Council in Brussels, Belgium.

CONSEIL DE L'ATLANTIQUE NORD

26th July, 1974

NOTICE
NPG(Staff Group)N(74)

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 29th July, 1974,
at 3 p.m. The principal objects of this meeting will be:

(a) to discuss a draft agenda(1) for the Autumn Meeting of NPG Ministers ;
(b) to discuss a note(2) by the Chairman concerning the final WINTEX 73 reports.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATC,
Brussels.

(1) NPG(Staff Group)WP(74)9, 23rd July, 1974
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(74)80, 23rd July, 1974
