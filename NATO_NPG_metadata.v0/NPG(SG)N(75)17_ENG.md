> # NATO Political and Military Views on the Implications of the Nunn Amendment
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting in Brussels on 17 February 1975 to address the political and military implications of the Nunn Amendment. The group took note of a draft letter from the Secretary General to the United States Secretary of Defense regarding the Alliance's views on the amendment. It was decided that a new classified version of the letter would be prepared by the Defence Plans and Policy Division. Additionally, the group discussed the agenda for the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives scheduled for 20 February, which would include briefings on the Nunn Amendment and the Phase II Study. The Canadian representative raised questions regarding the initiation of Phase III. The group also noted that the NPG Ministerial meeting in the United States would take place on 16 and 17 June 1975, with the location yet to be decided. The agenda for the Spring meeting would likely include items left in suspense from the Autumn 1974 meeting and a report on VINTEX 75. The Staff Group agreed to meet again on 3 March 1975, unless a meeting is required sooner due to the results of the meeting of Permanent Representatives.

NATO POLITICAL AND MILITARY VIEWS ON THE IMPLICATIONS OF THE
NUNN AMENDMENT

1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the proceedings of a meeting at Deputy Permanent
Representatives level, chaired by the Assistant Secretary General for Defence
Plans and Policy, at which was discussed a draft letter from the Secretary
General to the United States Secretary of Defense on the subject of the Alliance
views on the implications of the Nunn Amendment;

(b) noted the views expressed, and the decision that a new classified version of
the letter, revised in the light of the discussion, should be prepared with all
speed by the Defence Plans and Policy Division in the form of a PO for
circulation in time for the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives to be held
on 20th February.

MEETING OF NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES ON 20TH FEBRUARY
2. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of the remarks of the Chairman who said that: Item I for the
meeting of Permanent Representatives on 20th February, would be the Nunn
Amendment implications, and would be classified ATOMAL; the briefing and
discussion period on the Phase II Study would follow, and would be at NATO
SECRET level only;
