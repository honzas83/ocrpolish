> [!abstract] 
> # Reference List of Follow-On Use Work Programme Documents
> 
> This document, issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), serves as a comprehensive reference list of the principal documents relating to the follow-on use work programme. It supersedes a previous list distributed in October 1971. The document is organized into Annexes A and B, which categorize documents by reference number and by subject respectively. Annex A lists the Document Series and Study Series, including reports on various regions such as North Norway, Macedonia, Western Thrace, and the Mediterranean Maritime area. Annex B organizes these documents by subject, including work programme arrangements, status reports, and follow-on use studies. The list includes detailed information on the reference numbers, dates, and classifications of each listed document, such as NPG/D(69)9, NPG/Study(71)1, and NPG/D(72)6. The document originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
> 
> ## Categories/Topics
> 
> #Doctrine-and-Strategy/Nuclear-Strategy-and-Strike-Planning — The document is a reference list of 'follow-on use work programme' documents, which includes specific studies on target areas (North Norway, Macedonia, Western Thrace, NORTHAG, CENTAG, and the Mediterranean Maritime area) and mentions 'letter/yield code' for several studies, indicating detailed nuclear strike planning and target selection.
