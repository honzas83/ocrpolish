> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a formal notice from the Acting Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting, which was originally scheduled for 2nd August, has been moved to Monday, 9th August 1971, at 3:00 p.m. The Acting Secretary, J. de Gaye, notes that the agenda for the meeting will be published in due course. The notice was issued from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
