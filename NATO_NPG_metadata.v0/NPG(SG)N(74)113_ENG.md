> [!abstract] 
> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 21st November 1974. It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th November 1974, at 10 a.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss possible agenda items for the upcoming December meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group.

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th November,
