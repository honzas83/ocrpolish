> # Notice NPG(Staff Group)N(72)24: Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, March 6, 1972, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue discussions regarding the initiation of Phase II of the follow-on use programme, incorporating results from informal contacts conducted the previous week. Additionally, the meeting will address other matters related to the preparations for the upcoming Spring meeting of NPG Ministers. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 6th March, 1972,
at 3 p.m. The principal object will be to continue the discussion of the
initiation of Phase II of the follow-on use programme, taking into account the
results of the informal contacts which took place last week, and other matters
connected with the preparations for the Spring meeting of NPG Ministers.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
