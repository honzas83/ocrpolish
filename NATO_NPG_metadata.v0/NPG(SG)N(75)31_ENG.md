> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, 21st April, 1975, at 3 p.m. The meeting's agenda includes the discussion of the second revision of the draft report on Exercise 'INTEX 75(1)', preparations for the NPG ministerial meeting, and any other matters. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Vilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
