> # Draft Agenda for Ministerial Meeting in May 1969
> 
> This document, issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on January 1st, 1969, outlines a draft agenda for a ministerial meeting to be held in London on May 29th and 30th, 1969. The agenda is divided into several key sections: tactical use of nuclear weapons, including political guidelines and Atomic Demolition Munitions, and NATO's response to the Warsaw Pact's initial tactical nuclear use. It also covers the strategic use of nuclear weapons, specifically the role of theatre nuclear strike forces, and consultation on the use of nuclear weapons. Additionally, the agenda includes items for any other business, a future work programme, an agreed minute, and a communique. The document was signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

DRAFT AGENDA FOR MINISTERIAL MEETING IN MAY 1969

At its meeting held on 27th January, 1969, the Staff Group agreed to discuss at
its next meeting the Agenda for the Nuclear Planning Group meeting at
ministerial level to be held in London on 29th and 30th May, 1969.
