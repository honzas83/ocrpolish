> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a formal notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 15, 1971, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on National Participation in Military Nuclear Planning, the North Norway Study, and Illustrative Studies and Sample Release Requests. Additionally, the meeting will include an oral critique of WINTEX 71. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 15th February,
1971, at 3 p.m.

The principal objects of the meeting, time permitting, will be to continue
discussion of:

(a) National Participation in Military Nuclear Planning(1).
(b) The North Norway Study(2)
(c) Illustrative Studies and Sample Release Requests(3)
(d) WINTEX 71 critique, introduced orally.
(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels
