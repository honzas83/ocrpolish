> [!abstract] 
> # Corrigendum to Notice NPG(Staff Group)H(75)74
> 
> This document is a corrigendum issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) of the North Atlantic Council, dated 23rd September 1975. It serves as a correction to Notice NPG(Staff Group)H(75)74, dated 23rd September 1975, requesting holders of the previous notice to delete footnote (2) and insert a new Footnote (2) referencing NPG(Staff Group)P(75):0, dated 17th September 1975, and its addendum dated 22nd September 1975. The document originated from Brussels, Belgium.
