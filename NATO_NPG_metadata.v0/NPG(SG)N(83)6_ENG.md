> # NATO and the Warsaw Pact Force Comparisons
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated 23rd February 1983. It announces a screening of a recently completed Force Comparisons video-cassette, which compares the military capabilities of NATO and the Warsaw Pact. The screening is scheduled to take place on Tuesday, 15th March 1983, at 9:30 a.m. in Studio Ismay. The event is intended for the benefit of members of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group and the Defence Resource Committee (DRC).
