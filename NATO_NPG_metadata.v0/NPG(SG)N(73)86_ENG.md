> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document serves as a notice for the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled for Monday, 10th September 1973, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to continue preparations for the Ministerial meeting, which includes the consideration of a proposed advance press release. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts.
