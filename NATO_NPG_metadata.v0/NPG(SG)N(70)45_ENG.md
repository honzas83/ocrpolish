> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the scheduling of the next meeting. The meeting is set to take place on Monday, May 25, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives for discussion will be the outcomes of the meetings of the Permanent Representatives held on May 12 and 20, and matters pertaining to the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers to be held in Venice on June 6 and 9. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 25th May, 1970 at
3 p.m. The principal subjects for discussion will be the outcome of the meetings
of the Permanent Representatives held on 12th and 20th May, and matters
connected with the forthcoming meeting of NFG Ministers to be held in Venice on
6th and 9th June.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
