> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, June 11, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, with a specific focus on Section IV, which concerns the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The notice also allows for the consideration of any other business. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references several previous documents, including USI:ATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126 and various NPG (Staff Group) notices.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 11th June, 1979
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including initial
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons (draft to be
circulated);

(b) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
