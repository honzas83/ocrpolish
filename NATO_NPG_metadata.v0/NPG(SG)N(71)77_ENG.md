> [!abstract] 
> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Acting Secretary, J. de Gaye, on behalf of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). It announces that the next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Monday, July 26, 1971, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the agenda are to discuss the spin-off from the Nuclear Planning Group Permanent Representatives' meeting held on Thursday, July 22, 1971, and to plan prospective Staff Group work between July and September 1971.

NPG(Staff Group)N(71)77
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Acting Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will be held on Monday, 26th July, 1971 at
3.00 p.m. The principal matters for discussion will be:

(a) spin-off from the NPG Permanent Representatives' meeting on Thursday, 22nd
July; and

(b) prospective Staff Group work between now and September.
(Signed) J. de GAYE
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
