> # Draft Advance Press Release for NPG Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document serves as a revised draft advance press release announcing the eighteenth Ministerial Meeting of the NATO Nuclear Planning Group (NPG). The meeting is scheduled to take place in Hamburg, Federal Republic of Germany, on November 20 and 21, 1975, under the chairmanship of Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luts. The Nuclear Planning Group currently consists of the Ministers of Defence of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The document also includes a French translation of the announcement. The correspondence accompanying the revised page requests that holders of the previous notice, NPG(Staff Group)N(75)80, dated October 1, 1975, replace page 2 with the attached revised version.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currentl consistng of the Ministers of
Defence of Belgium, Denmark,, the Federal Republic of Germany, Italy, Turkey,
the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the invitation of the
Government of the Federal Republic of Germany, hold its eighteenth Ministerial
Meeting in Hamburg, on 20th and 21st November, 1975, under the Chairmanship of
Mr. Joseph M.A.H. LUTS, Secretary General of NATO.

---
PRO ET DE COMMUNIQUE ANNOCANT LA REUNION
Le Groupe des plans nucleaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
de la D'ense de la Belgique, du Danemark, des Etats-Unis, de l'Italie, de la
Republique Federale d'Allemagne, du Royaume-Uni et de la Turquie tiendra,
l'invitation du gouvernement de la Republique Federale d'Allemagne, sa 18e
reunion au niveau ministériel les 20 et 21 novembre à Hamburg, sous la
presidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. LUTS, Secrétaire General de l'OTAN.
