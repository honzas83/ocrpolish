> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1971 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, dated 27 August 1971, proposing a draft advance press release for the upcoming NPG Ministerial meeting scheduled for 26 and 27 October 1971 in Brussels, Belgium. The Chairman suggests that the press release be issued in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday, 22 October 1971, with simultaneous release in capitals of the member states at their discretion. The document proposes that the Staff Group discuss the draft at a meeting during the week of 30 August 1971 to develop an agreed version for approval by the silence procedure. The draft press release itself specifies that the Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, consisting of Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Greece, Italy, the United Kingdom, and the United States, will hold its tenth half-yearly Ministerial meeting under the chairmanship of Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR THE AUTUMN 1971 MINISTERIAL MEETING
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text of a proposed advance press release concerning the
forthcoming NPG Ministerial meeting in Brussels on 26th and 27th October, 1971.
The text closely follows the language used in press announcements that were made
just prior to the Venice, Ottawa and Mittenwald meetings. It is suggested that
the release might be made in Brussels at 3 p.m. local time on Friday, 22nd
October, 1971, with simultaneous release in capitals at the discretion of the
country concerned.

2. It is proposed that the Staff Group discuss this draft at a meeting during
the week of 30th August with a view toward developing an agreed version that
could then be sent to capitals for approval by the silence procedure.

(Signed) H.B. SEIM.

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

As arranged at its previous meeting in Mittenwald, Germany in May 1971, the
Nuclear Planning Group of NATO, currently consisting of Belgium, Denmark,
Germany, Greece, Italy, the United Kingdom and the United States, will, at the
invitation of the Belgian Government, hold its tenth half-yearly Ministerial
meeting on 26th and 27th October, 1971, in Brussels, Belgium under the
chairmanship of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.
