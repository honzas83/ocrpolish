> # 23rd Meeting of NPG Ministers – Transport of Classified Documents
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the logistical arrangements for the transport of classified documents for the 23rd Meeting of NPG Ministers. Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section, will serve as the courier, traveling to Denmark from Monday, 17th April to Thursday, 20th April 1978. Delegations are instructed to contact Mr. Tuccimei and submit their classified papers by midday Friday, 14th April 1978. The notice further specifies that papers should be packaged, clearly marked with the delegation's name and a serial number, and kept as light as possible, while explicitly forbidding the inclusion of unclassified material in the courier's burden.

Note by the Secretary

Mr. B. Tuccimei, Head of the NATO Headquarters Cosmic Control Section (Tel. Ext.
2481) will carry classified papers for the meeting in Denmark, leaving on
Monday, 17th April and returning on Thursday, 20th April. Delegations wishing to
use the services of the Courier are requested to contact Mr. Tuccimei and to
send him their papers not later than midday Friday, 14th April, 1978.

2. Papers should be packaged and clearly marked with the name of the delegation
or person and a serial number. Packages should be as light as possible. Please
do NOT burden the Courier with unclassified material.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

1110 Brussels.
