> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) regarding the upcoming meeting scheduled for Monday, December 7, 1970, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting will be to discuss follow-up activities resulting from recent Ministerial meetings and to continue the examination of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)'s potential participation in Exercise VINTEX 71. The notice was issued in Brussels on December 4, 1970, and signed by M.H.G. Wilson.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 7th December,
1970 at 3.00 p.m.

The principal matters for discussion will be:
(a) follow-up activity stcoming from the recent Ministerial meetings;
(b) continuation of examination of possible participation by the NPG (Staff
Group) in Exercise VINTEX 71.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

1110 Brussels
