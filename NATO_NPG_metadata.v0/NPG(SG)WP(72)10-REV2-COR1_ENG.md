> # Corrigendum to Working Paper NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)10(2nd Revise)
> 
> This document is a corrigendum to the Working Paper NPG(Staff Group)WP(72)10(2nd Revise) issued by the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). It specifies a correction to the cover page of the previous document, requesting that holders of the paper insert the date '13th September, 1972' in the top left hand corner. The document is dated 14th September, 1972, and serves as a formal administrative update to the the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) records.
