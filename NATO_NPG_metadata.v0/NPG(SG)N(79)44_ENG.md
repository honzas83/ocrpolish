> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, November 19, 1979, at 3 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes follow-up action to the Ministerial meeting in The Hague, a discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines, and specifically Section IV regarding the Selective Use of Nuclear weapons, provided time permits. The notice is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from Brussels, Belgium. It references several other documents, including IPG/D(79)5, IPG(Staff Group) I(79)2, and DPP/IDP/79/55.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 19th November,
1979 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) follow-up action to the Ministerial meeting in The Hague(1);
(b) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(2), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear weapons(3) (time permitting);

(c) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
HATO. 1110 Brussels.
(1) IPG/D(79)5, 15th November, 1979
(2) IPG(Staff Group) I(79)2, 20th February, 1979
