> # Notice: Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing that the next meeting will take place on Friday, February 26, 1971, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to continue discussions on activities leading up to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial Meeting at the end of May, the North Norway Study, and illustrative studies and sample release requests. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Friday, 26th February,
1971, at 3 p.m.

The principal objects of the meeting, will be to continue the discussion of:

(a) Activities leading up to the NG Ministerial Meeting at the end of May
(b) The North Norway Study(1)
(c) Illustrative Studies and Sample Release Requests(2)

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON

NATO, 1110 Brussels
