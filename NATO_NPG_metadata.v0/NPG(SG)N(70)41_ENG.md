> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, May 11, 1970, at 3 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting is to discuss possible changes to the Report or 'Di's. The notice was signed by R.H.G. Wilson and originated from Brussels, Belgium. It references the document NPG/T(70)2(Revised), dated April 30, 1970, and also mentions the reference DN(74)18.
