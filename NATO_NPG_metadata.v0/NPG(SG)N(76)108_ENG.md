> # Follow-Up to NPG Ministerial Meeting and Future Work Programme
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held a meeting at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels on Monday, November 29, 1976. The primary focus of the meeting was the follow-up to the NPG Ministerial Meeting and the determination of a future work programme. The Staff Group noted that some delegations had completed and forwarded the form regarding the timing and priority of future tasks to the Chairman. Additionally, the International Secretariat intended to circulate a paper detailing top priority subjects for the upcoming NPC Ministerial Meeting in Spring 1977, along with suggestions for timing and a list of other suggested studies and activities. The Staff Group exchanged views on various aspects of its future work programme and agreed to consider the paper from the International Secretariat at its next meeting. The group concluded by agreeing to hold its next meeting on Monday, December 6, 1976, at 3 p.m. in Brussels.

FOLLO'-'UP TO NPG MINISTERIAL MEETING AND FUTURE 'ORK PROGRAMME

1. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) as informed by the Chairman that the form attached
to his note NGP(Staff Group)N(76)100 concerning
timing and priority of future tasks had been
completed and forwarded to him by some but not all
delegations;
(b) noted that the International Secretariat intended
to circulate very shortly a paper setting out
the top priority subjects to be discussed at the
NPC Ministerial Meeting in Spring 1977 and making
some suggestions with regard to timing; other
suggested studies and activities would be listed
at Annex;
(c) had an exchange of views on various aspects of its
future work programme and agreed to consider the
paper to be circulated by the International
Secretariat at its next meeting.

NEXT MEETING

2. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday,
6th December, at 3 p.m.
