> # Notice of Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice issued by the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) on May 21, 1974. It announces a meeting to be held on Wednesday, May 22, 1974, at 10 a.m. in Conference Room 3. The primary objective of the meeting is to review country reactions to the proposed new paragraph 14 in the draft report concerning the Function of the Military Committee in the Nuclear Consultation Process. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It references two other documents: PO/74/63 dated May 17, 1974, and NPG/WP(74)4 dated April 22, 1974.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
A meeting of the Staff Group will take place tomorrow, Wednesday 22nd May, 1974
at 10 a.m. in Conference Room 3. The object of this meeting will be to review
country reactions to the proposed new paragraph(1) 14 in the draft report(2) on
the Function of the Military Committee in the Nuclear Consultation Process.

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
