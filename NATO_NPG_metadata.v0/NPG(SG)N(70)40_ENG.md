> # Revised Draft Advance Press Release
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) dated May 5, 1970. It accompanies a revised draft advance press release concerning the upcoming June Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial meeting, as previously agreed upon during the Staff Group meeting on May 4. The Chairman, H. B. Seim, specifies that the press release is scheduled for distribution in Brussels at 3 p.m. on Friday, June 5, 1970, with the option for simultaneous release in national capitals of the member countries. The note further stipulates that if no comments or objections are received by the close of business on Tuesday, May 19, the proposed press release will be considered approved.

NFG(Staff Group)N(70)40
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
REVISED DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE
Note by the Chairman
Attached is the text of an advance press release concerning the June NFG
Ministerial meeting, as agreed at the NFG Staff Group meeting on 4th May. The
release would take place in Brussels at 3 p.m. on Friday, 5th June, 1970 with
simultaneous release in capitals at the option of the country concerned.

2. If no comments or objections are received by the Chairman by the close of
business on Tuesday, 19th May, the proposed press release will be considered
approved.

(Signed) H. B. SEIM
NATO, Brussels. 110
