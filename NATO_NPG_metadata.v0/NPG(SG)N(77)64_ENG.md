> # Draft Advance Press Release for the Autumn 1977 Ministerial Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group, proposing a draft advance press release for the upcoming twenty-second NPG Ministerial Meeting in Bari, Italy, on October 11 and 12, 1977. The Chairman suggests that the announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, October 7, 1977, at 4:00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous releases in the capitals of participating countries. The Staff Group is requested to discuss the draft text and timing of the release at its meeting on September 12, and members are asked to obtain national authority approval of the text and timing by September 23, 1977. The draft press release itself, attached as an attachment, lists the participating Ministers of Defence from Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, the Greece, Italy, the United Kingdom, and the United States, and notes that the meeting will be chaired by NATO Secretary General Joseph M.A.H. Luns.

NOTICE NPC(Staff Group)N(77)64

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE FOR
THE AUTUMN 1977 MINISTERIAL MEETING

Note by the Chairman

Attached is the text, in English and French, of a proposed advance press release
concerning the forthcoming NPG Ministerial Meeting in Bari (Italy) on 11th and
12th October, 1977. The text closely follows the language used in former press
announcements prior to NPG Ministerial meetings. As on past occasions, the
release could be made a few days before the meeting takes place; accordingly, it
is suggested that an announcement be made in Brussels on Friday, 7th October,
1977, at 4.00 p.m. local time, with simultaneous additional release in the
capitals of those participating countries which so desire.

2. It is intended that the Staff Group discuss the draft text and suggested
release timing at its meeting on 12th September. Staff Group members of the
countries participating in the Ministerial meeting are requested after their
initial discussion to obtain their national authorities' approval of the text of
the announcement and timing of the release, informing the Director, Nuclear
Planning no later than Friday, 23rd September, 1977. Information on whether
national authorities plan to make a simultaneous release in their capitals would
also be appreciated.

(Signed) R.E. SHEARER

NATO, 1110 Brussels.

ATTACHMENT to NFG(Staff Group)N(77)64

DRAFT ADVANCE PRESS RELEASE

The Nuclear Planning Group of NATO currently consisting of the Ministers of
Defence of Belgium, Denmark, the Federal Republic of Germany, Greece, Italy, the
United Kingdom and the United States will hold its twenty-second Ministerial
Meeting in Bari (Italy) on 11th and 12th October, 1977, under the chairmanship
of Mr. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secretary General of NATO.

-----------------------

PROJET DE COMMUNIQUÉ ANNONCANT LA RÉUNION

Le Groupe des plans nucléaires de l'OTAN qui comprend actuellement les Ministres
de la Défense de la Belgique, du Danemark, des Etats-Unis, de la Grèce, de
l'Italie, de la République Fédérale d'Allemagne et du Royaume-Uni tiendra sa
vingt-deuxième réunion au niveau ministérielle les 11 et 12 octobre 1977 à Bari
(Italie) sous la présidence de M. Joseph M.A.H. Luns, Secrétaire Général de
l'OTAN.
