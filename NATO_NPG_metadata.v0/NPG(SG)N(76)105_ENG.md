> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary regarding the next meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group). The meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, November 29, 1976, at 3 p.m. in Brussels. The primary objectives of the meeting are to consider follow-up actions resulting from the Nuclear Planning Group Ministerial Meeting and to discuss the future work of the Nuclear Planning Group. Additionally, the meeting will cover any other business that may arise.
