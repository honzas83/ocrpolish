> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Notes of a Meeting
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) held meetings on September 25th and 26th, 1975, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The group discussed several key items, including the draft ministerial agenda and the draft paper on nuclear calculation procedures compilation. The Canadian representative provided editorial changes to the International Staff, and the group agreed on several proposals and text changes. Additionally, the group reviewed a report from the Los Alamos Scientific Laboratory Panel on Tactical Nuclear Warfare. reviewed a paper on Warning Messages, and decided that the the paper should be made shorter. The International Staff, in consultation with Germany, the United Kingdom, and the United States, was invited to prepare a further draft of the paper on Warning Messages. The group also considered postponing the meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives planned for October 9th and agreed to discuss the next date for this meeting on September 9th. Finally, the group scheduled its next meeting for September 20th, 1975.

NEXT MEETINGS OF THE NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES

6. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who said that, in view of the
problems which had arisen in the preparation of the draft papers, currently
under consideration, it might be wise to postpone the meeting of NPG Permanent
Representatives at present planned for 9th October;

(b) agreed to discuss the date or dates of the next NPG Permanent
Representatives meeting(s) on Monday, 9th September.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP

7. The STAFF GROUP:
