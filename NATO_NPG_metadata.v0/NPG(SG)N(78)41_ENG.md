> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, June 12, 1978, at 3 p.m. The agenda for the meeting includes proposals from NATO Military Authorities for short-term measures to improve NATO's TNF, and further discussion of the revised draft Permanent Representatives report on follow-up action to the Frederikshavn Ministerial Meeting. The notice also allows for any other business to be discussed. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.
>

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 12th June, 1978
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) NATO Military Authorities' proposals for short-term measures to improve
NATO's TNF(1);

(b) further discussion of the revised draft Permanent Representatives report on
follow-up action to the Frederikshavn Ministerial Meeting(2) (if required);

(c) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS

NATO, 110 Brussels.

(1) NPG/Study/52. 23rd March, 1978 and NPG(Staff Group)N(78)40, 7th June, 1978
