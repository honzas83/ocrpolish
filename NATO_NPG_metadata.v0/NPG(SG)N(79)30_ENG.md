> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a note by the Secretary regarding the upcoming meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduled for Monday, July 30, 1979, at 3 p.m. The primary objectives of the meeting are to discuss the draft General Political Guidelines, specifically focusing on Section IV regarding the Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons. The note also allows for the general discussion of any other business. The document is signed by D.J. Roberts and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It includes several references to previous documents such as USNATO/DPD/OUT/NS/78-126 and various NPG(Staff Group) and DPP/IIPD documents.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)

NEXT MEETING

Note by the Secretary

The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 30th July, 1979
at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) discussion of the draft General Political Guidelines(1), including further
discussion of Section IV, Selective Use of Nuclear Weapons(2);

(b) any other business.

(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
