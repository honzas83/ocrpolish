> # Notes of a Meeting Held in the NATO Headquarters, 1110 Brussels, on Monday, 24th September, 1973
> 
> The Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Staff Group held a meeting on September 24, 1973, at NATO Headquarters in Brussels. The meeting began with the welcome of Colonel C. A. Zari, who replaced Colonel P. Caligo as the Italian representative. The group discussed several recently circulated papers, including those concerning the early combined use of NATO conventional and nuclear capabilities and the future work programme of the NPG. The Staff Group examined draft reports for ministers, noting that while there was general agreement on the text, most representatives lacked instructions. Amendments proposed by the German representative to Working Papers 9 and 10 were approved, and further examination of these papers was scheduled for the next meeting. Additionally, the group deferred discussion on the status report of the Follow-On Use Work Programme due to its potential impact on the Phase II Status Report. The Chairman confirmed a briefing on the mission and activities of the NPG scheduled for September 28, 1973, for personnel cleared to Cosmic Top Secret. The group also discussed the scheduling of a NPG Permanent Representatives meeting on October 4, 1973, and considered a second meeting on October 18, 1973. The next meeting of the Staff Group was scheduled for October 1, 1973.

EXAMINATION OF DRAFT REPORTS(1) TO MINISTERS

3. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who asked if there were any
amendments to be made to the five Working Papers;

(b) noted that although there was general agreement with the texts of the
Working Paper, most representatives did not yet have instructions;

(c) agreed to a number of amendments proposed by the German representative to
Working Papers 9 and 10;

(d) agreed to hold a further examination of the Working Papers at the next
meeting, and expressed the hope that all members would have received
instructions by then;

(e) agreed that the amendments approved at the present meeting and the next,
should be circulated as corrigenda in time for the meeting of NPG Permanent
Representatives to be held on Thursday, 4th October, 1973.

FOLLOW-ON USE WORK PROGRAMME STATUS REPORT

4. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who referred to his note(2) on a
first draft of a possible report on the status of the Follow-On Use Work
Programme for Permanent Representatives; he drew attention to a number of
editorial changes to be made;

(b) agreed, after a brief initial discussion, and because of the possible
bearing of this report on the Phase II Status Report, to defer further
discussion until the next meeting.

BRIEFING ON THE FUNCTIONS AND ACTIVITIES OF THE NPG

5. The STAFF GROUP:
(a) took note of a statement by the Chairman who confirmed that he would give a
briefing on the mission and activities of the NPG on Friday, 28th September,
1973, at 10.15 a.m. in Conference Room 16-A;

(b) noted that all national representatives, members of the International Staff,
the International Military Staff and Military Headquarters who had a need to
know and were cleared to Cosmic Top Secret would be welcome;

(c) agreed that this briefing would not constitute a formal meeting of the Staff
Group and therefore no other business would be transacted.

MEETINGS OF NPG PERMANENT REPRESENTATIVES

6. The STAFF GROUP:
recalling that the next meeting of NPG Permanent Representatives would take
place on Thursday, 4th October, agreed to examine at the next meeting of the
Staff Group the desirability of scheduling a second meeting of NPG Permanent
Representatives to be held on Thursday, 18th October, 1973.

NEXT MEETING OF THE STAFF GROUP

7. The STAFF GROUP:
agreed to hold its next meeting on Monday, 1st October, 1973, at 3 p.m.
