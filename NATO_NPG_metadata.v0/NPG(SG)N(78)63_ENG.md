> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, September 18, 1978, at 3 p.m. The meeting's agenda includes the completion of the draft Permanent Representatives' report on short-term measures, the draft agenda for the Brussels Ministerial Meeting, and items for the meeting of Permanent Representatives on September 28, 1978. Additionally, the group will hold an introductory discussion on the working draft of the Phase III report. The notice was signed by D.J. Roberts and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 18th September,
1978 at 3 p.m. The principal subjects to be considered will be:

(a) completion of the draft Permanent Representatives' report on short-term
measures(1);

(b) draft agenda for the Brussels Ministerial Meeting(2);
(c) items to be included on the Agenda for the meeting of Permanent
Representatives on 28th September, 1978;

(d) introductory discussion of the working draft of the Phase III report(3);
(e) any other business.
(Signed) D.J. ROBERTS
NATO, 11C Brussels.
