> [!abstract] 
> # NPG Ministerial Participation
> 
> This document, issued by the Chairman of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group), provides a detailed table of country participation at the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministerial level from 1970 to 1975. The participation table is based on the Nuclear Planning Group Advisory Committee (NDAC) agreement on NPG membership. The document is signed by H.B. Seim and originated from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium. It lists the participation of Belgium, Canada, Germany, Denmark, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
