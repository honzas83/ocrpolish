> # Next Meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group)
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) scheduling the next meeting for Monday, June 12, 1972, at 3:00 p.m. The primary objective of the meeting will be to continue discussions regarding preparations for the Autumn meeting of the Nuclear Planning Group (NPG) Ministers. Specifically, the meeting will focus on action to be taken regarding Consultation Procedures and Facilities. The notice is signed by M.H.G. Wilson and originates from NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

NPG(Staff Group)N(72)53
NUCLEAR PLANNING GROUP (STAFF GROUP)
NEXT MEETING
Note by the Secretary
The next meeting of the Staff Group will take place on Monday, 12th June, 1972,
at 3.00 p.m. The principal object of the meeting will be to continue discussion
of the preparations for the Autumn meeting of NPG Ministers(1), and in
particular the action to be taken on the question of Consultation Procedures and
Facilities(2).

(Signed) M.H.G. WILSON
NATO, 1110 Brussels.
(1) NPG(Staff Group)N(72)48, 1st June, 1972.
(2) NPG(Staff Group)N(72)52, 8th June, 1972.
