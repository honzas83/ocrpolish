> # Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) Next Meeting
> 
> This document is a notice from the Secretary of the Nuclear Planning Group (Staff Group) announcing the next meeting scheduled for Monday, February 24, 1969, at 2:45 p.m. The primary objectives of the agenda are to discuss Soviet initial use and the draft agenda for the upcoming Ministerial Meeting in May. The notice references two other documents: NPG(Staff Group)WP(69)2, dated February 3, 1969, and NPG(Staff Group)N(69)15, dated 5 February 1969. The document was issued in Brussels, Belgium, by M.H.G. Wilson.
